# What Recipients See on a Web Form

Read about how recipients start, complete, review, and submit a Web Form.

After you build and activate a form, you can share its URL by email or as a link on your website. Recipients open the link and select Start to complete the form.

For example, a new bank account creation form may display as:

![The first page of the Web Form where the recipient enters their personal and account information.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gmi1660583110357/page/images/wf-welcome-page1.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTIyNzMsInNoZWFmIjoiZ21pMTY2MDU4MzExMDM1NyJ9.EqakeXfkfpRS6QGeLf5CFm59iHEzZwyaBxTFwrE1sxw&_LANG=enus "The first page of the Web Form where the recipient enters their personal and account information.")

After completing the form, recipients review their responses on the Summary page. To edit a response, they select Back and go to the fields they want to change.

![The Summary page of a completed Web Form displaying all recipient responses for final review before submission.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gmi1660583110357/page/images/wf-summary-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTIyNzMsInNoZWFmIjoiZ21pMTY2MDU4MzExMDM1NyJ9.EqakeXfkfpRS6QGeLf5CFm59iHEzZwyaBxTFwrE1sxw&_LANG=enus "The Summary page of a completed Web Form displaying all recipient responses for final review before submission.")

Recipients select Submit and are taken to the agreement to sign.
