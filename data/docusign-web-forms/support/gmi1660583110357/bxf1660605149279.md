# Get a Web Form URL

Learn how to get the URL for a Web Form so you can email it to recipients or embed it on your website.

You can email a form URL to recipients or embed it on your website. You get the URL when you activate the form or select it from an activated form.

1. [Activate a Web Form](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gmi1660583110357&topicId=iri1669070601520.html&_LANG=enus).
2. Select Use at the top of the form.
3. In the Get URL tab, select the copy link.
4. Select Open Link  to view the form.

The form URL is copied to your clipboard and can be shared with recipients or embedded on your website.
