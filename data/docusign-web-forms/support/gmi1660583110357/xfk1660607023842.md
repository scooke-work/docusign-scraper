# Delete a Web Form from Your Account

Learn how to delete a Web Form from your eSignature account.

You can delete a form from your eSignature account. After deletion, the form is removed from your Envelope Templates list and can no longer be used to send new documents.

1. Go to the Templates page.
2. Select My Web Forms, Shared with Me, or All Web Forms and choose the form that you want to delete.

   Tip: If the form is open, select More Actions (...) > Delete Form.
3. Select More Actions (...) > Delete.

   The Confirm Form Delete window displays.
4. Select
   Confirm to delete the form.

The form is deleted from your account and removed from the Envelope Templates list.
