# Use Pre-Built Templates in Workflow Builder

Discover how pre-built templates in Workflow Builder provide ready-made blueprints to rapidly automate your business and agreement processes.

## What are workflow templates?

Workflow templates are pre-built workflows for specific use cases. You can use these specialized templates to quickly set up, customize, and publish workflows. With workflow templates, you can explore different scenarios for how Workflow Builder can automate your business processes. Use a template as is, or customize it to fit your specific needs.

## What is included in a workflow template?

Each workflow template comes with pre-built workflow steps that you can customize to adapt to your specific needs.

- The What's required section for each workflow identifies the resources required to use the workflow, including any Docusign products or extension apps. Some workflow templates use third-party extension app steps, such as Salesforce or Google Drive. Links to the Docusign App Center are provided for these.
- The What's included section lists supporting assets like web forms or Docusign eSignature templates that are included with the template. These assets are uploaded automatically to your account when you use the template.

## Where do I find workflow templates?

Workflow templates are presented in a gallery view in your Docusign IAM account. You can view the available templates through the following ways:

- Go to the Templates  page, and select the Workflow Templates view.
- Go to the Agreements page, and select the  Workflows view. Then select Create Workflow.

## What templates are available?

Docusign is developing and adding new templates all the time. The following table lists some of our most widely used templates. Be sure to check the gallery for the latest offerings.

| Template Name | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Know Your Customer | Collect information such as name and email from any type of participant, and verify their identity with Docusign ID Verification. |
| Mutual NDA with Google Drive | Send Nondisclosure Agreements securely with Docusign, which provides an eSignature that is enforceable and legally binding. Once the agreement has been signed by all parties, a copy is automatically stored in Google Drive. |
| Mutual NDA with OneDrive | Send Nondisclosure Agreements securely with Docusign, which provides an eSignature that is enforceable and legally binding. Once the agreement has been signed by all parties, a copy is automatically stored in OneDrive. |
| Account Change Request - Salesforce | Keep customer accounts up to date in Salesforce by allowing customers to submit and authorize their own address changes. |
| Create Customer in Stripe | Customize a web form for new customers, then use the data to draft and send an invoice through the Stripe platform. |
| W4 Tax Withholding with SharePoint | Have new employees complete and sign a 2024 W-4 Tax Withholding form. Automatically store a copy of the completed form in SharePoint. |
| RFI/NDA with Salesforce | Customize a web form for potential customers to request information about your business. Create and track each lead within Salesforce CRM. |
| Glyue Change of Address | Verify an account holder's identity to then search and update the banking core with the provided address information. |
| HR Offer Letter | Generate an offer letter and save a signed copy in OneDrive. |
| Wire Transfer | Route a wire transfer request after verifying a customer's identity. |
| Bank Account Opening | Gather information from a new customer to open a bank account. Verify the applicant's identity before sending an account application to sign. |
| Right to Work (UK) | Checks and validates passport information to check a person's legal eligibility to work in the United Kingdom and Northern Ireland. |
