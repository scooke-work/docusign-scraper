# Build Workflows to Automate the Agreement Process

Read about the process of building agreement workflows with Docusign Workflow Builder.

As a process builder, you design and build workflows to automate your agreement processes.
Workflow Builder empowers you to design and run workflows that combine steps from multiple Docusign apps and third-party extensions. Bring disconnected agreement
tools together in Workflow Builder's no-code workflow designer.

## Use case examples

Workflow Builder is ideal for high-volume, business-to-consumer (B2C) agreements. In these use cases,
the customer wants to implement processes quickly without engaging costly IT
services. Use cases of this nature are supported by the currently available Docusign applications and partner app extensions.

Here are some basic use case examples of how you could use Workflow Builder to automate your agreement
processes:

Opening a customer account
:   A credit union wants to design a digital and automated process to open a new account for a
    customer. The process will first verify the customer's identity, then
    present a form to gather information for the new account. Finally a
    pre-filled account application is presented to the customer for signature.

Adding a beneficiary to a customer's account
:   A wealth management firm needs a digitized process to help a customer add a
    beneficiary to their account. The customer completes a form to provide
    beneficiary data. From there, a completed beneficiary application is sent to
    the customer to sign. The completed application is archived in the firm's
    Google Drive file system.

New hire onboarding
:   A large enterprise firm wants an automated process to verify each new hire's
    identity and then send an employment offer letter.

Automated Contract Renewal
:   An organization wants to achieve zero-touch contract renewals. A CRM system checks for expiring agreements and, 30 days before expiration, triggers a workflow through an API call. The workflow seamlessly creates an Agreement Desk renewal request automatically and unattended, pulling in all necessary variables without any human intervention.

External Vendor Intake
:   A procurement team needs a streamlined way to accept incoming requests from outside vendors. An external, non-Docusign user clicks a public web link to initiate an interactive intake form. Once submitted, the workflow automatically generates a complete Agreement Desk request for the internal team to review.

## Workflow scenario

To illustrate what Workflow Builder can do for you, let's review a basic scenario for an automated and
fully digital agreement process. In this scenario we will use several of the available
Docusign applications and an external file archive app. The
agreement process will have the following steps:

Step 1: Gather information from your customer
:   Using Web Forms, your customer provides their personal information needed to complete your
    agreement.

Step 2: Verify your customer's identity
:   Docusign ID Verification verifies their government-issued identification matches the
    information they provided on the web form.

Step 3: Send documents to customer for signature with field data gathered from the customer
:   Docusign eSignature generates a document for your customer to sign. Fields in the document
    incorporate data gathered from the customer, generating a personalized and
    pre-filled document for their review and signature.

Step 4: Archive a copy of the completed agreement to your external storage system
:   The Google extension app saves a copy of the completed agreement in a Google Drive folder
    named for your workflow process.

Once you start the workflow, each step runs automatically at the right time and with
the right information.
