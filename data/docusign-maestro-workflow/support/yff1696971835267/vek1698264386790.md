# Managing Workflows

Read about how
process
builders and administrators can publish and manage workflows.

As a
process
builder, you can edit, duplicate, or delete your saved workflow drafts. You can
also publish, pause, and unpublish your workflows. In this section, you will learn how
to accomplish all of these tasks and more.

Docusign and Workflow administrators can manage all workflows on an account. However, if a workflow includes a third-party app step that uses a private connection, the administrator cannot edit that step. Only the connection owner can edit a configuration that uses a private connection.
