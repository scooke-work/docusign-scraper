# Frequently Asked Questions About Identity Claims

List of common questions and answers about identity claims in Workflow Builder.

## What are identity claims?

An identity claim is a set of verified information about a participant’s identity
generated during a Verify Someone's Identity step. Once created, the identity claim can be applied later in a Send
Documents for Signature step of the same workflow instance, allowing
the system to skip identity re-verification for the participant.

## What do identity claims solve?

Identity claims streamline the signing process by eliminating repeated identity checks.
If a participant verified their identity in a Verify Someone's
Identity step (using a supported method), the system will use
the existing identity claim to confirm their identity in the Send Documents
for Signature step. When a Send Documents for
Signature step confirms a participant's identity using the identity claim
from a Verify Someone's Identity step, the Certificate of
Completion (CoC) includes verification data from that step.

## How does Workflow Builder generate an identity claim?

Workflow Builder generates an identity claim automatically when a participant verifies their
identity in a Verify Someone's Identity step using a method
that supports identity claims.

## Can I skip the Verify Someone's Identity step if I already have an identity claim?

No. Only Send Documents for Signature steps can benefit from
identity claims. Verify Someone's Identity is the step that
generates identity claims.

## What happens if an identity claim expires?

When an identity claim expires, it can no longer confirm the participant’s identity. The
participant must go through the identity verification process again in the
Send Documents for Signature step.

## Can I re-use an identity claim in different workflow instances?

No. Identity claims are workflow-instance specific. They only apply to the same
instance in which they were generated.

## How do I know if my identity verification method is supported?

Check the [Supported identity verification methods](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yff1696971835267&topicId=vwg1731931235105.html&anchor=vwg1731931235105__section_qjs_pyj_vdc&_LANG=enus) section.

## What happens if multiple valid identity claims meet the method requirement?

Workflow Builder applies the newest valid identity claim that meets the verification requirements
in the Send Documents for Signature step. If multiple identity
claims match, the system always selects the one with the most recent validity period.

## Can an identity claim verify multiple participants?

No. Each identity claim is tied to one participant’s identity and verification data.

## Do identity claims create a Certificate of Completion (CoC) record for Verify Someone's Identity steps?

The CoC record only appears if a Send Documents for Signature
step is part of the process. To record information from a Verify Someone's
Identity step in the CoC, your process must include a Send
Documents for Signature step. In addition, the Send Documents
for Signature step must use a template with ID Verification configured.
The Send Documents for Signature and the Verify
Someone's Identity steps must use the same identity verification
configuration.
