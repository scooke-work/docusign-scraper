# Generate a Screenshot of a Workflow

Learn how to create a comprehensive visual record of your workflow by generating a high-quality PNG image.

Workflows can be extensive and difficult to review in their entirety online. Use the screenshot option in the workflow canvas to generate a PNG image of your entire workflow. This feature helps you create a comprehensive visual record for:

- Creating visual references for documentation or training materials.
- Sharing workflow visuals with team members or stakeholders.
- Archiving workflow states for compliance or audit purposes.

1. Edit or Preview the workflow you want to screenshot.
2. In the workflow more actions menu, select Take a Screenshot.
