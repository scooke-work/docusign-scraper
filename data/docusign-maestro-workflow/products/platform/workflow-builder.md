## Capabilities you can use in your workflows

- [Identity Verification](https://www.docusign.com/products/identify)

  Build a signer verification step into your agreement workflows.
- [Web Forms](https://www.docusign.com/products/web-forms)

  Capture data and dynamically populate content into agreements for signature.
- [eSignature](https://www.docusign.com/products/electronic-signature)

  Add electronic signatures to your agreement automation workflows.
- [App Center](https://www.docusign.com/products/platform/app-center)

  Extend your workflows with partner apps like Salesforce, Google, Microsoft, ServiceNow, and more.
- [Workflow Templates](https://www.docusign.com/products/platform/workflow-builder/workflow-templates)

  Get started quickly with our out-of-the-box templates for common agreement workflows.
- [Agreement Desk](https://www.docusign.com/products/agreement-desk)

  Kick off a workflow from a request to track and manage reviews and approvals
- [Document Generation](https://www.docusign.com/products/agreement-preparation)

  Use workflow data to automatically generate professional-looking documents leveraging dynamic templates.
