# A Smarter Docusign eSignature Experience, Powered by AI

Published Jan 13, 2026

---

Summary•3 min read

A reimagined Docusign eSignature experience, powered by AI, offers a faster, more intuitive way to prepare and send agreements by eliminating the friction of manual processes and boosting signer and sender confidence.

![A Smarter Docusign eSignature Experience, Powered by AI](https://images.ctfassets.net/9pvazpst9iwl/5rvGRu0l5m7oOvx9KgWIth/5a6df4bb917f3fc0bae55e88ae7d3a36/ds-ui-tagger-blog_1200x900.png?fm=avif&q=90&w=500)

- - [Get started](https://www.docusign.com/blog/smarter-esignature-experience-powered-by-ai#get-started)

Businesses run on agreements, but too often, the journey to a completed signature is a frustrating obstacle course.

For the people preparing agreements, the work is often slow and manual: finding and placing fields one by one, deciding what information belongs where, and double-checking details before hitting send. Every extra step can introduce friction, delays, and opportunities for error.

On the other side, signers are left to navigate dense legal language with little context. When something isn’t clear, progress stalls. Confusion leads to hesitation, and hesitation can lead to abandoned agreements.

All of this friction adds up. Deals take longer to close. Conversion rates suffer. Agreements shouldn’t slow business down, they should move it forward.

We’re introducing new features to Docusign eSignature to help remove the friction that slows down agreements. With AI-assisted features, we’re automating tedious document preparation, while a new signing experience provides more clarity and confidence.

- **Get agreements out faster:** Use AI-powered features to automate tedious work, while a modernized experience helps you send agreements with ease.
- **Drive conversion**: Give your signers the confidence they need to complete agreements quickly. Our new AI-powered features provide more clarity, leading to faster completion and a higher conversion rate.
- **Deliver with confidence**: Ensure your agreements reach the right people . We help you eliminate delivery risks by verifying recipient information.

### Automate tedious agreement preparation tasks

Save time and eliminate manual effort by using AI-Assisted Agreement Type Detection to surface relevant agreement-specific fields, and AI-Assisted Field Suggestions to auto-place signing and text fields. *Available February 2026.*

### Validate recipient contact details in real time

Pre-send Data Verification ensures your agreements reach the right people  by integrating with 3rd party databases to validate the accuracy and reachability of recipient email addresses and phone numbers before an envelope is sent. *Available March 2026.*

### Help signers understand key agreement terms

AI-Assisted Summary and Q&A provide signers with an AI-assisted overview of key highlights and details in an agreement to enable quicker agreement completion. Allow signers to get agreement-specific questions answered in real-time with the help of AI, so they can sign with confidence. *Available now.*
