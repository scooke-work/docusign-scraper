# Introducing AI-Assisted Agreement Summaries and Q&A in Docusign eSignature

Published Jan 13, 2026

---

Summary•3 min read

New AI-enhanced signing features in Docusign eSignature help signers understand agreements faster and with greater confidence, including plain-English summaries of key terms and natural-language Q&A.

Signing an agreement shouldn’t feel like decoding a foreign language. But for many people, dense legal text creates uncertainty, hesitation, and stress right at the moment when clarity matters most.

We know this is a real problem. Nearly 60%\* of people admit they’ve agreed to terms they didn’t fully understand, and almost half say complex contracts leave them feeling frustrated or anxious. Contrary to the myth that people just “scroll to the bottom,” only 2%\* of signers actually skip or skim agreements. Most people want to understand what they’re signing—they just don’t always have the tools to do so.

That’s changing.

With new AI-enhanced features in Docusign eSignature, signers will start seeing a clearer, more supportive signing experience designed to build confidence and reduce friction.

## AI-assisted agreement summaries

When you receive an agreement to sign, Docusign can now present a simple, plain-English summary of key terms. Instead of digging through pages of legal language, you’ll get a clear overview of key highlights—so you can review documents faster and feel confident moving forward. Nearly 75%\* of signers say they’d feel more confident with an AI-powered summary, and now it’s built directly into the signing experience.

## Ask questions, get direct answers

Every agreement comes with questions. Now, you can ask them directly. Using natural-language Q&A, signers can type questions like *“What happens if I need to cancel?”* or *“What are my payment obligations?”* and receive clear, agreement-specific answers—without searching through dense text or second-guessing your understanding.

Together, these updates are designed to remove unnecessary complexity from signing. Agreements are easier to understand, faster to review, and less stressful to complete—so you can sign with clarity and confidence.

These features are now available for all envelopes in English globally.

\**Source: OnePulse Survey of 1,000 Americans, December 2025*
