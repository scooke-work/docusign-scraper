## Introducing agreement intelligence with Docusign Iris

Docusign Iris has an expert-level understanding of complex agreement workflows and language, giving it the ability to provide you with comprehensive insights.

Here’s a closer look at how Iris stands out from other AI platforms:

- **Exclusive agreement datasets:** With diverse, specialized agreement data sets and deep agreement expertise, we’ve equipped Iris to deliver accurate and relevant agreement insights seamlessly within the Docusign user experience.
- **Data that stays in your control:** Iris, just like Docusign, is built with trust at the forefront to deliver next-generation AI solutions. Our consent-based approach to [AI data sets](https://www.docusign.com/blog/ai-powered-future-customer-control) gives you control over whether your data is utilized for AI training, and we prioritize protecting your information by aggregating and anonymizing data used for training purposes.
- **Enterprise-grade and tested**: Docusign IAM, powered by Iris, scales with your organization, supporting AI customization, seamless integrations, and global localization. With a flexible model infrastructure, you always benefit from the latest LLMs—tailored specifically for agreements—to drive real-world impact.

## How Docusign Iris works

Iris is already making significant strides in transforming agreement management for organizations of all sizes. Here are some examples of how Iris enhances your experience from sale to signature:

- [**AI-assisted review:**](https://www.docusign.com/blog/ai-assisted-review) Iris helps streamline contract review and negotiation processes, including reviewing agreements against pre-defined legal standards and suggesting AI-generated markups and clause language. This helps ensure that contracts are thoroughly analyzed for compliance and accuracy with your internal playbooks, reducing the time and manual effort previously required.
- [**Renewal management**](https://www.docusign.com/blog/how-to-simplify-contract-renewals)**:** Iris simplifies renewal tracking with automated notifications and pre-built reports—delivering actionable insights to ensure timely and efficient contract renewals. Now your business can easily prevent unwanted auto-renewals and use the data to identify cost-saving opportunities.
- [**AI-assisted data extractions**](https://www.docusign.com/products/platform/navigator)**:** With Iris, you can automatically identify and record critical data buried in your agreements. This standardizes your unstructured data which makes agreements more discoverable and reportable. With this data identified, you can boost efficiency for your most valuable teams and leverage newly identified data to make more strategic decisions

## More agreement AI capabilities coming soon

But we’re not stopping there: at Docusign Momentum ‘25, we unveiled exciting new AI advancements powered by Docusign Iris, which will be rolled out over the next few months.

### AI contract agents

[AI contract agents](https://www.docusign.com/blog/ai-contract-agents-future-agreement-management)enable you to automate repetitive and low-risk tasks like prioritizing contract requests and offer smart recommendations for contract actions like renegotiation.

### New AI capabilities in Docusign Agreement Manager

[Docusign Agreement Manager](https://www.docusign.com/blog/navigator-smart-manage-agreements), our centralized agreement repository, has gotten smarter over the past year, thanks to the latest AI advancements from Iris. Now you can easily [capture custom agreement data](https://www.docusign.com/products/platform/navigator) at scale, with just a few example contracts. No more tedious work to manually identify and record specific contract metadata, Iris can do the work for you with just a few clicks.

Another complex task you can now automate with Iris is tracking contractual obligations. New [obligation management capabilities](https://www.docusign.com/blog/obligation-management-docusign-iam) in Agreement Manager allow you to track key renewal, payment, and termination terms automatically, receive automated reminders, report on scheduled obligations, and manually capture obligations that are unique. Now you reduce the time spent tracking this information manually and say goodbye to missed obligations.

## Transforming agreement management with Docusign Iris

Applying AI to your agreement management process can elevate your understanding of current and future contracts. Here’s how:

### Boost revenue, cut costs with insights

Iris powers Docusign dashboards and AI-assisted data extractions that offer deep insights into your agreements. By analyzing patterns and trends within your contracts, Iris can help you identify key opportunities for growth. For example, it can help sales reps zero in on high-value accounts and start renewal conversations early—giving them more time to personalize outreach and boost retention.

### Close agreements in record time

AI-powered solutions streamline the preparation and negotiation phases by automating repetitive tasks like tagging fields and enforcing company standards. [According to Deloitte research](https://www.docusign.com/deloitte-agreement-study), organizations with more advanced Agreement Storage and Categorization, such as AI-enabled contract categorization, saw an average of 40% fewer deal delays.

Iris empowers users to self-serve contract-related questions through a generative Q&A experience and uses AI-driven automation to route agreements to the appropriate approvers—helping to prevent bottlenecks and keep workflows moving efficiently.

### Minimize legal and financial risk

Iris helps decode legal jargon and complex contract language, so you can avoid costly mistakes. Iris uses AI to analyze agreements, flag risky clauses, and recommend compliant language—helping your team stay aligned with playbooks. In fact, procurement teams with more advanced Review and Risk Evaluation capabilities are [69% more likely](https://www.docusign.com/deloitte-agreement-study) to report outperforming their goals than their peers with less mature systems.

Iris also helps you stay ahead of risk by surfacing key contractual commitments and potential anomalies for review.
