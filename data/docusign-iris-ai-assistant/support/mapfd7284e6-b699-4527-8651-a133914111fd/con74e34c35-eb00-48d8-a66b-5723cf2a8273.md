# Support for AI-Assisted Review in Agreement Desk ​Plans

Learn about the features and permissions that are available through Agreement Desk plans for AI-Assisted Review.

Agreement Desk is available in three Docusign IAM plans:

- IAM Professional
- IAM for Enterprise
- IAM for Sales

The plan you have determines the level of functionality available to you in AI-Assisted Review. The full set of AI-assisted review capabilities is available with Agreement Desk in the Enterprise and Sales plans.

With IAM Professional plans, AI-Assisted Review includes a simple save experience to make edits in Microsoft Word, and save them back to a request.

Access to AI-Assisted Review functionality is determined by a user’s playbook permissions. ​Playbook permissions are included with Agreement Desk Enterprise and Sales plans only. By default, Global Request Managers and Admins have View and Manage playbook permissions, respectively. Request editors can be granted View or Manage playbook permissions by a Global Request Manager or Admin. Users with permissions to View or Manage playbooks will also have the ability to use the Chat capabilities in AI-Assisted Review.
