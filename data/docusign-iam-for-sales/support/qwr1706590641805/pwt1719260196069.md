# Delete a Salesforce Connection to Your Docusign IAM Account

Learn to delete the Salesforce connection to your Docusign IAM account.

You can connect Salesforce organizations to your Docusign IAM account. When you connect to Docusign, you can
build workflows that read from and write to your Salesforce records. You can delete
a Salesforce connection if you no longer want to access that organization.

You can access the delete controls through your Salesforce organization or your Docusign account.

Use this procedure to delete a Salesforce connection:

1. Choose your login method:
   - Login from Salesforce:
     1. [Access the Docusign Setup page as a Salesforce
        administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus)
     2. Manage Connections and the IAM Launcher: Select
        Manage.
   - Login from Docusign:
     1. Open the [Production](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) or [Demo](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) App Center site.
     2. Select the Salesforce tile.
     3. Select Manage and choose Manage
        Connections.
2.
