# Install the Salesforce Application (Docusign App Center Instructions)

Learn to install the Salesforce app in your Docusign account and connect it to your Salesforce organization.

## Before you begin

[Purchase a Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) for Sales application](https://www.docusign.com/intelligent-agreement-management?utm_source=bing&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=GBL_XX_PRF_UPS_2410_SEMIAM&utm_term=docusign%20iam&campaignid=569731866&adgroupid=1178678316174006&location=84406&device=c&matchtype=e&extensionid=&creative=73667602015411&keyword=docusign%20iam&placement=&network=o&msclkid=3287ee64f2c01f69bcd686665f40e7da).

Important: Use these instructions to install the application from the Docusign App Center. Follow this link if you want to [install the app from your Salesforce organization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=&map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835&topicId=tsk91549a3b-f7f3-4b74-8e87-33ff33d198c0.html&_LANG=enus).

Docusign administrators can get extension applications from the Docusign App Center.

You can install the Salesforce app to connect your Docusign account with your Salesforce organization. As an administrator, log on to the Docusign App Center and install the Salesforce app for your account. Workflow creators can add Salesforce steps to read and write data, store documents, add merge fields, and more.

For more information about viewing, installing, and managing partner extension apps, see the  [Docusign App Center Guide](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ous1698169987748&topicId=ijy1698170251179.html&_LANG=enus).

Developers can review the [Extension apps 101 overview](https://developers.docusign.com/extension-apps/extension-apps-101/) in the Docusign Developer Center.

Tip: Wait at least 30 minutes after installing before you configure a Salesforce workflow step. Docusign indexes all Salesforce fields to make them available for use.

Use this procedure to install and connect the Salesforce app through the Docusign App Center:

1. Log on to your Docusign account as an administrator and open the appropriate App Center site:
   - [Docusign Production](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps)
   - [Docusign Demo](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps)
2. Select the Salesforce tile and select Install App when the page refreshes.

   The Install and authorize "Salesforce" app page opens.
3. Select Continue and select Connect Account when the page refreshes.

   The Connect a Salesforce account page opens.
4. Connect to your Salesforce organization:

   Important: [Salesforce Government Cloud customers](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=000386092&type=1) must use the Salesforce Production option.

   1. Select a Salesforce organization to connect to Docusign:

      - Salesforce Production (login.salesforce.com): Connect a Salesforce production or developer organization.
      - Salesforce Sandbox (test.salesforce.com): Connect to a Salesforce sandbox organization.

      ![A screenshot displays the Connect a Salesforce account with Salesforce Production selected.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/qwr1706590641805/page/images/Workflow_Builder_Connect_a_Salesforce_Account.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU4MjAsInNoZWFmIjoicXdyMTcwNjU5MDY0MTgwNSJ9.BLXfUeLVFOArJWjrivh4LHj3X7eHlxnpF-xq23pDa1A&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the Connect a Salesforce account with Salesforce Production selected.")
   2. Select Next.
   3. How would you like to connect this Salesforce account?

      - Private: Only accessible to you in Docusign.
      - Shared: Accessible by multiple users or the whole team in Docusign.
   4. Select Next.
   5. Name this Salesforce account: Keep or change the default name.
   6. Select Log In
5. Connect to your Salesforce organization. Salesforce does not ask for credentials if you are logged in to your organization.

   Important: Salesforce Government Cloud customers must use the Custom Domain option.

   - Standard domain: Enter your Salesforce credentials and select Log In.
   - Custom domain
     1. Select Use Custom Domain.
     2. Custom Domain: Enter your domain address and select Continue.
     3. Enter your Salesforce credentials and select Log In.

   The page refreshes. A message displays: Logged in to your Salesforce account.

You have completed the process. You have installed the Salesforce app and connected it to Docusign.
