# Use Workflow Builder with Salesforce

Learn to build Docusign workflows that update records in
your Salesforce organization.

## Introduction

With [Workflow Builder](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yff1696971835267&topicId=dnx1696972415150.html&_LANG=enus), you can connect Docusign workflow steps like Web Forms and eSignature templates to
third-party extensions. You can create workflows to automate your agreement process,
including reading data from and writing data to Salesforce records.

A typical agreement process consists of numerous steps to complete before and after
signing. Some of these steps can be addressed today by integrating multiple Docusign products, usually with expert assistance and developer
resources.

Workflow Builder offers a platform that integrates Docusign products with third-party apps like Salesforce. By
building workflows, you can digitize your
business processes without writing code.

You can configure the following workflow steps to integrate Salesforce and Docusign in your agreement process:

- Writeback to Salesforce: Update Salesforce records with
  data from your workflow.
- Read from Salesforce: Import data from Salesforce
  records to your workflow.
- Store Files in Salesforce: Use variables to name
  completed agreements and route them to the appropriate records.
- Use Salesforce Files: Add files from Salesforce records
  to workflows.
