# Uninstall the Salesforce Application

Learn to uninstall the Salesforce app.

The Salesforce app connects your Salesforce organization with your Docusign IAM account. You install and
authorize the Salesforce app from the Docusign App Center. You can
uninstall the Salesforce app if you no longer want to use it with your Docusign account.

Use this procedure to uninstall the Salesforce app:

1. Choose your login method:
   - Login from Salesforce:
     1. [Access the Docusign
        Setup page as a Salesforce administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus)
     2. Manage Connections and the IAM Launcher: Select
        Manage.
   - Login from Docusign:
     1. Open the [Production](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center) or [Demo](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center) App Center site.
     2. Select the Salesforce tile.
     3. Select Manage and choose Manage
        Connections.
2. Select Manage and choose Uninstall
   App.
3. Uninstall this app?: Enter UNINSTALL in
   the field and select Uninstall.![A screenshot displays the Uninstall this app dialog box with the word UNINSTALL in the field and the Uninstall button highlighted.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/qwr1706590641805/page/images/A-screenshot-displays-the-Uninstall-this-app-dialog-box-with-the-word-UNINSTALL-in-the-field-and-the-Uninstall-button-highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU5NjQsInNoZWFmIjoicXdyMTcwNjU5MDY0MTgwNSJ9.n6DWoq3ix_5DDYxd5jswn0l1BU3M22orFRNMYG5zbuo&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the Uninstall this app dialog box with the word UNINSTALL in the field and the Uninstall button highlighted.")

   Docusign removes the Salesforce app. The page
   refreshes.

You have completed the process. You have uninstalled the Salesforce app. Your workflows cannot read from or write to your Salesforce
organization.
