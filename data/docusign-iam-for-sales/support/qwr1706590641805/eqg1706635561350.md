# Complete a Salesforce Workflow Task

Learn to complete a task sent from a Docusign workflow.

Note: Standard users cannot view the progress of workflows in Salesforce. Salesforce administrators can [view failed workflows in the Docusign Logs page](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=qwr1706590641805&topicId=vqr1744835015673.html&_LANG=enus). Only Docusign administrators can [monitor the progress of all workflows](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yff1696971835267&topicId=vcp1698707326210.html&_LANG=enus).

Workflow Builder is a tool that your administrator uses to
automate your contract process. Docusign sends you an email when
you receive and complete workflow tasks. You complete tasks by entering contract
data, signing documents, and more.

Use this procedure to complete a workflow task:

1. Open the email from Docusign titled Review and
   complete workflow.
2. Select Review.
3. Complete all required workflow tasks and select Next for each completed stage.

You have completed the task. You have completed a workflow task. If
you are involved in later workflow steps, you receive additional messages from Docusign.
