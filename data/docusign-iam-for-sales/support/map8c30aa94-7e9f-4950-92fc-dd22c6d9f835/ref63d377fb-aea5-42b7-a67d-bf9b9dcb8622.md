# Pre-Built Agreement Desk Statuses Included in the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution

Learn about the pre-built Agreement Desk statuses that install in your Salesforce
organization from the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution.

You can install the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution in your
Salesforce organization. This solution installs a series of pre-built Docusign Agreement Desk statuses in your Salesforce organization.
Each status represents a common action, like Legal Review or Ready for Signature.

These statuses are functional and do not require additional configuration.
Installing these statuses gives you a quick way to implement Docusign workflows in your Salesforce organization.

The following table displays the pre-built Agreement Desk statuses that install with
the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution:

## Agreement Desk status list

| Status name | Description |
| --- | --- |
| New (default) | A new request has been created. |
| Agreement Generated; Ready for Business Review | An agreement has been generated and needs sending for customer review. |
| New Agreement Uploaded | A new document has been uploaded. |
| External Customer Review | An agreement has been sent for customer review. |
| Legal Review | An agreement must be reviewed by the legal team. |
| Ready for Signature | An agreement has been approved and can be sent to the customer for signing. |
| Waiting for Signature | An agreement has been sent but not signed. |
| Closed | An agreement has been reviewed and signed. |
