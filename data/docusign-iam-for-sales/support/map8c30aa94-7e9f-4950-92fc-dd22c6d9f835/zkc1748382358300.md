# Search for Agreements with Agreement Manager for Salesforce

Learn to search for agreements in Agreement Manager for Salesforce.

Important: This feature is only available to IAM for
Sales customers. Contact [Docusign support](https://support.docusign.com/s/?language=en_US&_gl=1*74elk4*_gcl_au*MTY5ODQyMjA2MC4xNzY5NjM1OTA1LjE5ODM3MTQzNjguMTc2OTYzNjIwNC4xNzY5NjM2MjA0) or [Docusign
sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) for more information.

The Docusign Completed Agreements component displaysAgreement Manager on Salesforce records. You can use Agreement Manager to [Search for your agreements in
Salesforce](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=qzl1712244340883.html&_LANG=enus).

Use this procedure to search for agreements in Agreement Manager for Salesforce:

1. Open a record and look for the Docusign Agreements
   component.

   Note: A Salesforce administrator may have placed the
   component on a tab labeled Docusign Agreements or
   something similar. The component features the word
   Completed at the top.
2. Try keywords, phrases, or a question: Enter search terms
   in the field.

![The Completed section of a Salesforce record displays a search field with Fontara and MSA entered.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835/page/images/The-Completed-section-of-a-Salesforce-record-displays-a-search-field-with-Fontara-and-MSA-entered-2.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU3MzgsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwOGMzMGFhOTQtN2U5Zi00OTUwLTkyZmMtZGQyMmM2ZDlmODM1In0.0EiOBATvTGXDBrTtQwFtUVgy2obFIpD-Don5vBnKd2A&_LANG=enus "The Completed section of a Salesforce record displays a search field with Fontara and MSA entered.")

3. Press [Enter] to see all results, or select a result
   from the dropdown menu that displays beneath.

   Agreement Manager displays completed agreements sent from this record and its
   child objects. Agreement Manager does not display results for parent objects, or
   agreements sent from Docusign CLM or your Docusign web account.

You have completed the process. You have searched for
completed agreements from this record and those of its child objects.
