# Pre-Built Document Templates Included in the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution

Learn about the pre-built document templates that install in your Salesforce organization
from the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution.

You can install the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution in your
Salesforce organization. This solution installs a series of pre-built Docusign document templates in your Salesforce organization. Each
template represents a common use case, like creating and sending an NDA or MSA.

These templates are functional and do not require additional
configuration.

Installing these templates gives you a quick way to implement Docusign workflows in your Salesforce organization. You can
customize these templates to fit your exact use cases.

The following images display the contents of the pre-built templates that install
with the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution:
