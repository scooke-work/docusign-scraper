# Open an Agreement with Agreement Manager for Salesforce

Learn to open agreements from Agreement Manager for Salesforce.

Important: This feature is only available to IAM for Sales customers. Contact [Docusign support](https://support.docusign.com/s/?language=en_US&_gl=1*74elk4*_gcl_au*MTY5ODQyMjA2MC4xNzY5NjM1OTA1LjE5ODM3MTQzNjguMTc2OTYzNjIwNC4xNzY5NjM2MjA0) or [Docusign sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) for more information.

The Docusign Agreements component displays Agreement Manager on Salesforce records. You can use Agreement Manager to open an agreement from the Docusign Agreements component.

Use this procedure to open an agreement from the Docusign Agreements component:

1. Open a record and look for the Docusign Agreements component.

   Note: A Salesforce administrator may have placed the component on a tab labeled Docusign Agreements or something similar. The component features the word Completed at the top.
2. Select an agreement in the File Name column:

   ![A screenshot displays the Docusign Agreements component with an agreement highlighted.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835/page/images/Agreement_Manager_select_agreement.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU3ODMsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwOGMzMGFhOTQtN2U5Zi00OTUwLTkyZmMtZGQyMmM2ZDlmODM1In0.Qx9sog2ek59xs_7ctDOT4CRtMF34J9zinBeHHifr1Dg&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the Docusign Agreements component with an agreement highlighted.")

   The agreement opens in another browser tab. If you are not logged in to your Docusign account, the Docusign Log In page
   opens in another browser tab. Enter your Docusign credentials
   and the agreement opens.

You have completed the process. You have opened an agreement from the Docusign Agreements component.
