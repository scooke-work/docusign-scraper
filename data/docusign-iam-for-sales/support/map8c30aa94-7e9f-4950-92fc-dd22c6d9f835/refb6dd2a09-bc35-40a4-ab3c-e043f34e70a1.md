# Pre-Built Agreement Desk Web Forms Included in the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution

Learn about the pre-built web form that installs in your Salesforce organization
from the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution.

You can install the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution in your
Salesforce organization. This solution installs a series of pre-built Docusign Agreement Desk web form templates in your Salesforce
organization. Each template represents a common use case, like creating and sending
an NDA or MSA.

These templates are functional and do not require additional
configuration.

Installing these templates gives you a quick way to implement Docusign workflows in your Salesforce organization. You can
customize these templates to fit your exact use cases.

with the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution:
