# Pre-Built Workflows Included in the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution

Learn about the pre-built workflows that install in your Salesforce organization from the
Docusign IAM for Sales Solution.

You can install the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution in your
Salesforce organization. This solution installs a series of pre-built Docusign workflows in your Salesforce organization. Each workflow
represents a common workflow use case, like creating and sending an NDA or MSA.
These workflows are functional and do not require step connection or
field mapping. Installing these workflows gives you a quick way to implement Docusign workflows in your Salesforce organization.

You can customize these workflows to fit your exact use cases. You can also duplicate
them to make new workflows that are similar to the original. And you can
export these workflows to quickly add a workflow to a different organization. For
example, you can build and test a workflow in your sandbox before exporting it to
production.

The following tables display the contents of the pre-built workflows that install
with the Docusign IAM for Sales Solution:

## Workflows included:

- Agreement Desk Request: MSA Submitted
- Gen and Send NDA (Account Object)
- Embedded Signing NDA (Account Object)
- Agreement Desk Request: MSA Ready for Signature
- Agreement Desk - MSA: Request Closed with Data writeback
- Agreement Desk Request: MSA New Agreement Uploaded
- MSA with Legal Request (Opportunity Object)
- General Legal Request (Opportunity Object)
- General Legal Request (Account Object)
- All Agreement Desk - Envelope Completed (ALL) > Request Closed
- All Agreement Desk: Envelope Sent - Status Change to Waiting for Signature
- All Agreement Desk - Add Owner and Groups
