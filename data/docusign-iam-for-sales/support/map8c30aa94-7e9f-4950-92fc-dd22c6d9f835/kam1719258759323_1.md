# Reconnect a Salesforce Organization to Docusign

Learn to fix connection problems by reconnecting your Salesforce organization toDocusign.

You can connect Salesforce organizations to your Docusign IAM account. When you connect to Docusign, you can build
workflows that read from and write to your Salesforce records. If you experience
problems with your connection, you can reconnect Docusign and
Salesforce.

You can access the reconnect controls through your Salesforce organization or your Docusign account.

Use this procedure to reconnect Salesforce with your Docusign IAM account:

1. Choose your login method:
   - Login from Salesforce:
     1. [Access the Docusign
        Setup page as a Salesforce administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus)
     2. Manage Connections and the IAM Launcher: Select
        Manage.
   - Login from Docusign:
     1. Open the [Production](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) or [Demo](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) App Center site.
     2. Select the Salesforce tile.
     3. Select Manage and choose Manage
        Connections.
2.
