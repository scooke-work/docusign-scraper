## Agreement Desk for Salesforce reports overview

Salesforce reports filter specific data sets from your organization to create structured, readable insights. You can build reports that highlight specific information, such as displaying all customers with expiring contracts. Salesforce reports eliminate manual searching and automate the delivery of business-critical metrics.

You can build custom Salesforce reports to filter and display your [Docusign
Agreement Desk for Salesforce requests](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835&topicId=con007504df-80b7-41f7-aa5f-1daa72bcc257.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about using Docusign Agreement Desk in Salesforce to streamline your agreement process.").

## Example

You create a report with columns for Docusign Agreement Desk Requests:
ID and Status. You add rows for
Created Date and Owner.

You add a filter with Show Me: All Docusign Agreement Desk
requests, Created Date: Current FY, and
Record Type > equals > Opportunity.

You run the report. Salesforce displays all Opportunity-based Agreement Desk requests
from the current fiscal year.
