# Use Filters to Display Agreements in Agreement Manager for Salesforce

Learn to filter the agreements that display in Agreement Manager for Salesforce.

Important: This feature is only available to IAM for
Sales customers. Contact [Docusign support](https://support.docusign.com/s/?language=en_US&_gl=1*74elk4*_gcl_au*MTY5ODQyMjA2MC4xNzY5NjM1OTA1LjE5ODM3MTQzNjguMTc2OTYzNjIwNC4xNzY5NjM2MjA0) or [Docusign
sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) for more information.

The Docusign Completed Agreements component displays Agreement Manager on Salesforce records. You can use
Agreement Manager to search for your Docusign
agreements in Salesforce. You can also use [filters to narrow search results](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=fjb1712255517076.html&_LANG=enus), making it easier to locate the
agreements you want to see.

Use this procedure to filter the agreements that display in Agreement Manager for Salesforce:

1. Open a record and look for the Docusign Agreements
   component.

   Note: A Salesforce administrator may have placed the
   component on a tab labeled Docusign Agreements or
   something similar. The component features the word
   Completed at the top.
2. Try keywords, phrases, or a question: Enter search terms
   in the field.

   ![The Completed section of a Salesforce record displays a search field with Fontara and MSA entered.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835/page/images/The-Completed-section-of-a-Salesforce-record-displays-a-search-field-with-Fontara-and-MSA-entered-2.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU3NDIsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwOGMzMGFhOTQtN2U5Zi00OTUwLTkyZmMtZGQyMmM2ZDlmODM1In0.-nUU2EetfwMT0RtWrxvN9Id0USTAZbQuL-1nA5Y_KX4&_LANG=enus "The Completed section of a Salesforce record displays a search field with Fontara and MSA entered.")
3. Press [Enter] to see all results, or select a result
   from the dropdown menu that opens beneath.

   Agreement Manager displays completed agreements sent from this record and its child objects.
   Agreement Manager does not display results for parent objects, or agreements sent from
   Docusign CLM or your Docusign web
   account.
4. Use the filters to refine the list of search results. Expand a filter, select a
   value, and select Apply.

   For example, use the Agreement Type filter to locate services
   agreements:

   ![A screenshot of the Agreement Type filter that shows Services Agreement is selected.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835/page/images/Filter-search-results-by-Type-filter_MSA-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU3NDIsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwOGMzMGFhOTQtN2U5Zi00OTUwLTkyZmMtZGQyMmM2ZDlmODM1In0.-nUU2EetfwMT0RtWrxvN9Id0USTAZbQuL-1nA5Y_KX4&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Agreement Type filter that shows Services Agreement is selected.")

   Services agreements display in the Docusign Agreements
   component.
5. Continue to apply filters to refine the agreement list table as needed. Select
   All Filters to access additional filters.

You have completed the process. You have used filters with
your search to display the agreements you want to see in Agreement Manager for Salesforce.
