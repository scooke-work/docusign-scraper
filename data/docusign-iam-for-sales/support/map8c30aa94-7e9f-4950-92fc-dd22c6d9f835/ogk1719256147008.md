# Create Multiple Salesforce Connections

Learn to connect multiple Salesforce organizations to your Docusign IAM account.

You can connect multiple Salesforce organizations to your Docusign
IAM account. When you connect multiple organizations, you can
build workflows for more than one Salesforce organization. Docusign does not limit the number of Salesforce organizations you
can connect to Docusign.

Example: You connect two production organizations and one
sandbox to your Docusign account. You build workflows
for each organization. Each Salesforce organization has access to fields from the
same Docusign account.

Use this procedure to connect multiple Salesforce organizations to your Docusign account:

1. Choose your login method:
   - Login from Salesforce:
     1. [Access the Docusign Setup page as a Salesforce
        administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus)
     2. Manage Connections and the IAM Launcher: Select
        Manage.
   - Login from Docusign:
     1. Open the [Production](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) or [Demo](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) App Center site.
     2. Select the Salesforce tile.
     3. Select Manage and choose Manage
        Connections.
2. Select + New Connection.
3. Configure your new connection:
   1. Connect a Salesforce account: Choose
      Salesforce Production or
      Salesforce Sandbox and select
      Next.

      ![A screenshot displays the Connect a Salesforce account with Salesforce Production selected.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835/page/images/Workflow_Builder_Connect_a_Salesforce_Account.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU1NDUsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwOGMzMGFhOTQtN2U5Zi00OTUwLTkyZmMtZGQyMmM2ZDlmODM1In0.Ht6tuUGyzD3EW2sdZWXmsb_fkMIz1RlQXu99BuBPeZQ&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the Connect a Salesforce account with Salesforce Production selected.")
   2. How would you like to connect this Salesforce
      account?: Choose Private or
      Shared and select
      Next.

      Important: If you log on from
      Salesforce, you must choose the Shared
      option.
   3. Salesforce Connection Name: Enter a descriptive
      name in the field and select Log In.
   4. Connect to your Salesforce organization. Salesforce does not ask for
      credentials if you are logged in to your organization:
   - Standard domain: Enter your Salesforce credentials
     and select Log In.
   - Custom domain
     1. Select Use Custom Domain.
     2. Custom Domain: Enter your domain address and
        select Continue.
     3. Enter your Salesforce credentials and select Log
        In.

   The page refreshes. A message displays: Logged in to your
   Salesforce account.

You have completed the task. You connected an additional Salesforce
organization in your Salesforce app. You can build workflows for the additional
organization.
