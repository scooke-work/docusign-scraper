# Customize Agreement Desk for Salesforce Columns

## Before you begin

Important: This feature is only available to IAM for
Sales customers. Contact [Docusign support](https://support.docusign.com/s/?language=en_US&_gl=1*74elk4*_gcl_au*MTY5ODQyMjA2MC4xNzY5NjM1OTA1LjE5ODM3MTQzNjguMTc2OTYzNjIwNC4xNzY5NjM2MjA0) or [Docusign
sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) for more information.

Agreement Desk is a central platform for streamlining your entire agreement process
within Docusign. You can use Agreement Desk to submit and manage
requests, negotiate and edit agreements, and use AI to automate contracts. When you
add the Agreement Requests component to your layouts, users can review, manage,
sign, and send agreements without logging on to Docusign.

You can customize component columns to view and act on request details like Title,
Owner, Source Record, ID, and more. When you customize your column view, the
following rules apply:

- Your changes do not affect other users.
- Your custom settings remain when you log off and on.
- Your settings apply to all records and objects that contain the Embedded Agreement Requests
  component.

Note: Your settings *do not* remain if you empty
your browser cache, use a new browser, or open an incognito session.

Use this procedure to customize component columns to view and act on details of
displayed requests:

1. Open a record and look for the Embedded Agreements Desk component.

   Note: A Salesforce administrator may have placed the
   component on a tab labeled Agreement Desk or
   something similar. The component features the word
   Requests at the top.
2.    The Customize columns dialog box displays.
3. Select the switch next to a column name to display or hide a column. You can
   search for columns by entering column names in the Find
   columns field.
   - Owner: View the name of the request owner. The owner
     is assigned by the request submitter.
   - Source Record: View the Salesforce object that the
     request was created from. You can select the link to open the record.
   - ID: View the unique system ID for the Agreement Desk
     request record in Salesforce.
   - Status: View the current stage of an Agreement Desk
     request, like In Review, Signed, Canceled, and so on.
   - Last Activity At: View the date and time of the most
     recent interaction with a request, like the date it was edited or sent for
     signature.
   - Due Date: View the date by which the owner must
     take action or complete the request.
   - Request Type: View the category of the Agreement Desk
     request, like MSA, NDA, SOW, and so on.
   - Created At: View the date and time that the Agreement
     Desk request was first created.
   - Submitter: View the name of the person who created
     the Agreement Desk request.

   The switches move to the right when you turn them on. They
   move to the left when you turn them off.
4. Select Save.

   Embedded Agreement Requests component.

You have completed the process. You have changed the columns that display in the Embedded
Agreement Requests component.
