# Edit the Name of a Salesforce Connection

Learn to edit the name of a Salesforce organization that's connected to your Docusign IAM account.

You can edit the name of a Salesforce organization that's connected to your Docusign IAM account. Editing the name of an
organization is useful when you have connected more than one Salesforce organization
to Docusign. Editing a name does not change the name of your
Salesforce organization; only how it displays in the app.

You can access the controls from your Salesforce organization or your Docusign account.

Use this procedure to edit the name of a Salesforce connection:

1. Choose your login method:
   - Login from Salesforce:
     1. [Access the Docusign Setup page as a Salesforce
        administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus)
     2. Manage Connections and the IAM Launcher: Select
        Manage.
   - Login from Docusign:
     1. Open the [Production](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) or [Demo](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) App Center site.
     2. Select the Salesforce tile.
     3. Select Manage and choose Manage
        Connections.
2. Select the three-dot menu icon ![A screenshot displays three dots stacked vertically.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835/page/images/A-screenshot-diplays-a-threedot-menu-icon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU1NDcsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwOGMzMGFhOTQtN2U5Zi00OTUwLTkyZmMtZGQyMmM2ZDlmODM1In0.ZuThDNCR-HqOxQQTRHw8Uk-TBGq8qW1mR7AAiqsggO4&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays three dots stacked vertically.") next to a connection and choose Edit Connection
   Name.
3. Edit the name in the Connection Name field and select
   Save.

   The page refreshes. A message displays: Successfully edited
   connection.

You have completed the process. You edited the name of a Salesforce
connection. This edit does not change the name of
your Salesforce organization; only the name of the connection.
