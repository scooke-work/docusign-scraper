# Prepare Your Salesforce Organization to Start Docusign Workflows

Learn to prepare your Salesforce organization so that administrators can link Salesforce
flows to Docusign workflows.

Important: Do not follow these steps if you already have
Docusign Apps Launcher v7.4 or higher installed. Only follow these
steps to add a Start Docusign Workflow Builder workflow action to
an organization with v7.3 or lower installed.

Before you begin
:   - [Purchase a Docusign
      Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) plan](https://www.docusign.com/intelligent-agreement-management?utm_source=bing&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=GBL_XX_PRF_UPS_2410_SEMIAM&utm_term=docusign%20iam&campaignid=569731866&adgroupid=1178678316174006&location=84406&device=c&matchtype=e&extensionid=&creative=73667602015411&keyword=docusign%20iam&placement=&network=o&msclkid=3287ee64f2c01f69bcd686665f40e7da).
    - [Install Docusign Apps Launcher version 7.4 or
      higher](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=ogj1586134254571.html&_LANG=enus).
    - [Install the Salesforce Application from the Docusign App Center](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yff1696971835267&topicId=bgb1697668738892.html&_LANG=enus).
    - [Assign Workflows Create permissions to your Docusign user](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yff1696971835267&topicId=gbb1696973048215.html&_LANG=enus), or be an account administrator.
    - [Build a workflow to be started by your Salesforce
      flow](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=qwr1706590641805&topicId=ovh1741282680990.html&_LANG=enus).

You can link Salesforce flows to Docusign workflows by adding a Start Docusign
Workflow Builder workflow action to your Salesforce flow.

When a flow starts, Salesforce starts your linked Docusign workflow. Linking flows with
workflows builds an automation chain that creates agreements from Salesforce and Docusign data.

Before you can link flows and Docusign workflows, you must prepare your Salesforce
organization.

Use this procedure to prepare your organization:

1. As a Salesforce and Docusign administrator, [Disconnect and reconnect Docusign and Salesforce](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=ntr1586134210309.html&_LANG=enus).

   Important: Disconnecting removes Docusign access from all Salesforce users. Disconnecting
   does not remove users from your connected Docusign
   account.
2. [Open Docusign Apps
   Launcher](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus).
3. Go to Docusign Setup >  User Management.
4. [Re-add your Docusign
   users](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=uwf1586134275195.html&_LANG=enus).

   Important: Users must [re-activate their Docusign access in Salesforce](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=vbw1648698462634&topicId=ymi1586134213877.html&_LANG=enus). After re-activating, users can start Docusign workflows.

You have completed the task. You have prepared your Salesforce
organization. You can add a Start Docusign Workflow Builder workflow
action to a Salesforce flow. [Prepare your users for
starting workflows from Salesforce](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835&topicId=bmk1744912007661.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to prepare your Salesforce users so that they can start Docusign workflows from Salesforce.").
