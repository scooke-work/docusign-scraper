## What is Agreement Desk?

Agreement Desk is a centralized workspace designed to simplify and
accelerate the pre-signature phase of the agreement lifecycle. It provides a single
location for all requests related to your agreements. Part of the unified
Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform, it centralizes request submission,
helps with negotiation, and streamlines approvals.

Through AI-Assisted Review, Workflow Builder workflow automation, and reusable fields that can be used
across products, Agreement Desk speeds up routine tasks.

Agreement Desk creates a central place to manage your agreement requests. You can use this space
to manage and collaborate on requests. When reviewers complete requests, Agreement
Desk helps send agreements for signature and even trigger other business
processes.

You can use Agreement Desk to accomplish the following tasks:

- Submit and manage requests.
- Negotiate and edit in-process agreements.
- Use AI to automate sales, procurement, and business contracts.

## Why use Agreement Desk in Salesforce?

Many Salesforce users prefer to act on their agreements entirely from Salesforce.
When you add the Docusign Agreement Requests component to your
layouts, users can search for, sort, filter, and open requests without logging on to
Docusign.

Agreement Desk only displays requests created from the record that you are viewing and its
related objects. For example, when viewing the Acme record, Agreement Desk only
displays requests sent from Acme and its related records.

## Key features and benefits

You can use the Docusign Agreement Desk component to perform the following
actions:

- [Search and
  filter](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835&topicId=tsk79fb118f-2fa3-4e60-965a-efb9edd7c2c8.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to search and filter the requests that display in the Docusign Agreement Requests component."): Find request titles from your connected Docusign account. Use data points from your requests like
  *submitter* and *request type* to narrow your search.
- [Open
  requests](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835&topicId=tsk151c1542-bdf4-4c8e-ba20-37affb7adc7e.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to open a request from the Agreement Requests component to view or act on."): Select requests in the Docusign
  Agreement Requests component to open and act on them in your Docusign account.
- Monitor request progress: Track the progress of your requests as they move
  from initiation to completion.
- Column sort: Arrange your requests by categories like
  Title, Request Type,
  Owner, and so on.

You can accomplish these tasks directly from Salesforce records, without logging on to your Docusign account.
