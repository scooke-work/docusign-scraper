# Duplicate a Workflow

Learn to quickly build new workflows by duplicating existing workflows.

## Before you begin

You can duplicate workflows to quickly build similar workflows.

Example: You have a workflow called Salesforce
MSA. The workflow is designed to generate and send MSA agreements
from Salesforce records. You want to build a similar workflow to create and send NDA
agreements from Salesforce records. Rather than building a new workflow, you select
the Duplicate option from your Salesforce
MSA workflow menu. Docusign instantly creates a
duplicate workflow named COPY Salesforce MSA. All steps,
fields, and settings from the original workflow are copied to the duplicate
workflow. You rename the imported workflow Salesforce NDA, make
a few changes, and publish it.

Note: Steps, settings, and field
mappings from the original function in the duplicated workflow. You are not
required to re-map fields or rebuild steps.

Use this procedure to duplicate a for Salesforce workflow:

1. As a Docusign administrator, go to Agreements > Workflows.
