# Check Salesforce Logs for Failed Agreement Manager Actions

Learn to check the status of failed Agreement Manager for Salesforce actions.

Important: This feature
is only available to IAM for Sales customers. Contact [Docusign support](https://support.docusign.com/s/?language=en_US&_gl=1*74elk4*_gcl_au*MTY5ODQyMjA2MC4xNzY5NjM1OTA1LjE5ODM3MTQzNjguMTc2OTYzNjIwNC4xNzY5NjM2MjA0) or [Docusign sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) for more
information.

In Salesforce, you access
Agreement Manager by adding the Docusign
Agreements component to your layouts. Approved
users can interact with Docusign agreements on
your records without leaving Salesforce.

If errors occur with
Agreement Manager actions, you can view the logs in
Salesforce.

Use this procedure to check the status of failed Agreement Manager actions:

1. Select the Docusign Logs tab.

   The Docusign Logs page opens.
2. Select a log from the Docusign Log column.

   The log details page opens for that item.

You have completed the task. You viewed the details of a failed Agreement Manager action.
