# Refresh the Salesforce Connection to Your Docusign Account

Learn to refresh the connection between Salesforce and Docusign after
you add fields or objects to your Salesforce organization.

You can connect Salesforce organizations to your Docusign IAM account. When you create a connection, you can build workflows that
read from and write to your Salesforce records. If you add Salesforce fields or
objects, perform a refresh so that the Salesforce app can access the new
content.

You can access the refresh controls through your Salesforce organization or your Docusign account.

Use this procedure to refresh the Salesforce content available to the app:

1. Choose your login method:
   - Login from Salesforce:
     1. [Access the Docusign Setup
        page as a Salesforce administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus)
     2. Manage Connections and the IAM Launcher: Select
        Manage.
   - Login from Docusign:
     1. Open the [Production](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) or [Demo](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) App Center site.
     2. Select the Salesforce tile.
     3. Select Manage and choose Manage
        Connections.
2. You have completed the process. You refreshed the connection to your
Docusign IAM account. New Salesforce fields or objects are now available for use in
Docusign workflows.
