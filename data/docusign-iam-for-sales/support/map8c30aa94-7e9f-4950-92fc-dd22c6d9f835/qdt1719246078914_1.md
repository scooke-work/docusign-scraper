# Update the Salesforce Application

Learn to update the Salesforce app to access new features.

## Before you begin

- [Purchase a Docusign
  Intelligent Agreement
  Management (IAM) for Sales application](https://www.docusign.com/intelligent-agreement-management?utm_source=bing&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=GBL_XX_PRF_UPS_2410_SEMIAM&utm_term=docusign%20iam&campaignid=569731866&adgroupid=1178678316174006&location=84406&device=c&matchtype=e&extensionid=&creative=73667602015411&keyword=docusign%20iam&placement=&network=o&msclkid=3287ee64f2c01f69bcd686665f40e7da).
- [Install the Salesforce Application from the Docusign App Center](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835&topicId=jxo1706295810553.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to install the Salesforce app in your Docusign account and connect it to your Salesforce organization.").

Docusign continues to release new versions of the Salesforce
application. To access new features, update your version of the app.

Tip: Wait at least 30 minutes after installing before
you configure a workflow step. Docusign must index all Salesforce
fields to make them available for use.

You can access the upgrade controls through your Salesforce organization or your Docusign account.

Use this procedure to update your version of the Docusign Salesforce app:

1. Choose your login method:
   - Login from Salesforce:
     1. [Access the Docusign
        Setup page as a Salesforce administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus)
     2. Manage Connections and the IAM Launcher: Select
        Manage.
   - Login from Docusign:
     1. Open the [Production](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) or [Demo](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center/extensionapps) App Center site.
     2. Select the Salesforce tile.
     3. Select Manage and choose Manage
        Connections.
2.
