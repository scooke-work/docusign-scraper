# Prepare Your Salesforce Organization to Use Agreement Manager

Learn to prepare your Salesforce organization to use Docusign Agreement Manager.

Important: This feature is only available to IAM for
Sales customers. Contact [Docusign support](https://support.docusign.com/s/?language=en_US&_gl=1*74elk4*_gcl_au*MTY5ODQyMjA2MC4xNzY5NjM1OTA1LjE5ODM3MTQzNjguMTc2OTYzNjIwNC4xNzY5NjM2MjA0) or [Docusign
sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) for more information.

Important: Do not follow these steps if you already
have Docusign Apps Launcher v8.4 or higher installed. Only follow
these steps to add the Docusign Agreements component in an
organization with v8.3 or lower installed.

Before you begin
:   - [Purchase an Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) for Sales
      application](https://www.docusign.com/intelligent-agreement-management?utm_source=bing&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=GBL_XX_PRF_UPS_2410_SEMIAM&utm_term=docusign%20iam&campaignid=569731866&adgroupid=1178678316174006&location=84406&device=c&matchtype=e&extensionid=&creative=73667602015411&keyword=docusign%20iam&placement=&network=o&msclkid=3287ee64f2c01f69bcd686665f40e7da).
    - [Install Apps Launcher version 8.4 or
      higher](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=ogj1586134254571.html&_LANG=enus).

      Important: If you are already
      using Agreement Manager for Salesforce, upgrading to Docusign Apps Launcher v8.4 breaks your
      connection. After upgrading v8.4 to get the latest features,
      you must use the following controls to re-establish your
      connection to Docusign.

[Agreement Manager](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=adf1702945446135.html&_LANG=enus) is a smart repository for your Docusign agreements. You can use Agreement Manager
to store, manage, and analyze all of your agreements.

You can use Agreement Manager in your Salesforce organization by adding the Docusign Agreements component to your layouts.

To use Agreement Manager in Salesforce, add the Docusign Completed Agreements component to your layouts. This component displays your Docusign agreements and the controls to filter, search, and take action on them.

Before you can use Agreement Manager in Salesforce, you must prepare your organization.

Use this procedure to prepare your Salesforce organization to use Agreement Manager:

1. [Open Docusign Apps Launcher](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus).
2. Go to Docusign Setup >  User Management.
3. [Grant Agreement Manager access to your Docusign for Salesforce users](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835&topicId=eut1712157553977.html&_LANG=enus "Read about user and group management tasks, such as managing agreement access and granting bulk upload permissions.").

You have completed the task. You have prepared your Salesforce
organization to use Agreement Manager. You can add the Docusign Completed
Agreements component to your Salesforce layouts.
