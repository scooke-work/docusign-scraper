# Natural Language Search Queries

Read about how natural language search queries, including components of the
Search Results view.

Natural language (NL) queries offer a more intuitive and efficient way for you to
find relevant agreement information. Enter a question into the search field to and
receive a direct answer and a list of relevant agreements.

![A snippet of the view that shows an example search question to locate master service agreements with automatic renewal.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835/page/images/NL-Search_Example-question-with-dropdown.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTU3OTMsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwOGMzMGFhOTQtN2U5Zi00OTUwLTkyZmMtZGQyMmM2ZDlmODM1In0.ArLpNODZVCwJOx2pA68KR4b76vxz5OcL2C5AjQUVE9E&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the view that shows an example search question to locate master service agreements with automatic renewal.")

This type of search is more robust than traditional keyword searches. With this search,
you can:

- **Search in your own words:** NL processing supports a more intuitive and
  human-like search experience.
- **Find answers faster:** NL queries return search results and direct answers.
- **Make more impactful decisions:** Gain insights as you search, to inform strategies and
  build stronger relationships with clients and vendors.

Tip: Ask more complex questions and get direct answers along
with the search results in one query. For example, if you asked "What is the value of my
purchase agreements with Company A"? The search results would include:

- A direct answer to your question (the total contract value for your purchase
  agreements with Company A)
- A list of purchase agreements with Company A

## Components of NL search results

Unlike traditional searches, NL queries return both an answer to the question and a
list of relevant agreements.

## Supported attributes and data points

As you enter a question into the search field, a dropdown of "quick" answers displays. You can
select a quick answer or view the results for your question. The search supports
questions related to the following agreement attributes (fields) and data
points:

- Agreement Type
- Currency Code
- Effective Date
- Expiration Data
- Governing Law
- Party Name
- Renewal-related
  - Auto Renewal (Y/N)
  - Renewal Notice Period
  - Renewal Term
  - Renewal Type
- Termination for Cause - Notice Period
- Termination for Convenience - Notice Period
- Title
- Total Contract Value
