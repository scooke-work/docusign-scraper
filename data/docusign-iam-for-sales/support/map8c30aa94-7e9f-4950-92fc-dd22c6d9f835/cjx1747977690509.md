# A Broken Page Icon Displays on Salesforce Records instead of the Docusign Completed Agreements Component

Learn to fix the issue of the Docusign Completed Agreements
component displaying a broken page icon instead of
Agreement Manager agreements.

Important: This feature is only available to IAM for
Sales customers. Contact [Docusign support](https://support.docusign.com/s/?language=en_US&_gl=1*74elk4*_gcl_au*MTY5ODQyMjA2MC4xNzY5NjM1OTA1LjE5ODM3MTQzNjguMTc2OTYzNjIwNC4xNzY5NjM2MjA0) or [Docusign sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales)
for more information.

Agreement Manager agreements on the record.

## Resolution: Refresh Your Browser Page

Cause: This issue occurs intermittently, often when users go to another
window and return to the Salesforce page.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

Choose an option to refresh the Docusign Agreements
component on your Salesforce record:

- Refresh your browser page.
- Right-click the component and select Reload
  Frame.

You have completed the process. The broken page icon disappears and
the Docusign Completed Agreements component displays,
providing access to
Agreement Manager. You can use the Agreement Manager component to view and act on your Docusign agreements in without leaving Salesforce.
