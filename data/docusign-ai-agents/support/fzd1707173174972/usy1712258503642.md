# Introduction to AI at Docusign

Read about AI at Docusign, including the types of AI that Docusign uses, and how Docusign protects and uses your data.

Review the following topics to learn more about Docusign Iris.

## What is Docusign Iris ?

Docusign Iris is the AI engine that powers AI features across the agreement lifecycle, enabling you to create agreements faster, commit with greater confidence, and manage for maximum business impact.

With Docusign Iris, customer can:

- **Boost revenue and cut costs with agreement insights:** Uncover key opportunities and take action with AI-powered dashboards.
- **Close agreements in record time:** Speed up agreement prep and negotiation with AI-powered solutions that help you enforce company standards and simplify approvals.
- **Minimize financial and legal risk:** Empower teams to decode legalese, avoid missteps, and stay sharp on contractual commitments with AI.

Important: In all cases, AI should be considered a tool to assist you with your work. Any AI findings or AI-produced content should always be reviewed for accuracy.

## What types of AI does Docusign use?

Docusign uses both traditional AI and generative AI:

- **Traditional AI** focuses on analyzing existing data or content to deliver contextual information specific to that data.
- **Generative AI** can create new content, such as text, based on patterns learned from existing data.

By leveraging both types of AI, Docusign can deliver the best results for the task in our AI-assisted solutions.

To illustrate how this works, let’s look at Docusign Iris in AI Extension for CLM:

- A user uploads an agreement into CLM. Docusign Iris analyzes the agreement and provides useful insights about the agreement.
- AI is used to create a summary composed of key parts of the agreement, which allows users to quickly understand the content of an agreement and accelerate agreement review.
- AI is also used to extract important attributes from an agreement, such as effective date or document type. Understanding these attributes fuels key business and contract processes, improves contract discoverability, and generates better business intelligence from agreements.

By using both traditional AI and generative AI, Docusign Iris transforms static, unstructured agreement data into actionable insights.

## How does Docusign protect and use my agreement data when I use AI-assisted experiences in Docusign?

Docusign is committed to trust and transparency with AI. This includes safeguarding customer data when using Docusign Iris. Docusign's platform for AI follows robust data protection controls, including encryption and access controls.

Docusign does not use your data to improve its AI models unless both of the following are true:

- Your organization has agreed to share its data for AI training through your Docusign service agreement.
- The AI data controls setting is turned on in your account settings. For Docusign Insight, see the next FAQ.

To provide additional protection, Docusign aggregates and anonymizes data before it is used to train Docusign AI models.

Note: Data anonymization includes removing personal identifiers, such as:

- Names
- Phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Social security numbers (in the U.S.)
- National ID numbers

## Does the 'AI data controls' setting apply to data in Docusign Insight?

No. This consent does not apply to data in Docusign Insight because it is a single tenancy platform. Docusign Insight customers can modify or create new models using their own data for their Insight environment, but the data is otherwise not used to improve Docusign AI models.

See the  [Docusign Insight landing page](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=hac1706296665611&topicId=jny1706298930641.html) on the support site for more information about this product.
