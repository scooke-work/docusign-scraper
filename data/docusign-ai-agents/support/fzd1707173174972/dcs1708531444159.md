# AI Data Controls Setting FAQs

Read about the AI data controls setting, including how to turn the setting on or off, and frequently asked questions (FAQs).

As with other organizations that use AI, one important way Docusign ensures that our AI-enabled offerings provide the best experience, results, and value for our customers is by using their data, with their consent, to train and improve the AI models powering their experience.

The AI data controls setting gives additional control within the product to customers who have agreed to share their data for training Docusign AI models to deliver improved products. The AI data controls setting is an account-level setting. All data is aggregated and anonymized before being used for AI training.

Note: This documentation relates to the AI data controls setting. This setting is available to:

- Accounts with Agreement Manager
- Accounts enabled with the Agreement Manager Trial
- Accounts with Workflow Builder
- CLM accounts (excludes CLM.CM accounts)
- Other accounts where an organization has agreed to share anonymized data for AI training in its Docusign service agreement.

This documentation does not apply to developer, Sandbox, or demo accounts.

Important: To consent to sharing your data for training Docusign AI models, your organization (as the account owner) must first agree to share data through your Docusign service agreement.

- If your organization has consented to sharing data, the AI data controls setting is turned on by default (the toggle is in the purple position). This setting can be turned off at any time by your account administrator.
- If your organization has not consented to AI training, the AI data controls setting is turned off and disabled. - If the setting is disabled and you are interested in sharing your data for AI training, you must contact Docusign to provide consent.

The setting toggle is located on the AI Control Settings page under the Actions menu:

   The Docusign Home page displays.

   The Docusign Admin page displays.

   Note: The AI data controls setting is an account-level setting. If necessary, select the relevant account from the Accounts dropdown located at the top of the navigation pane:![A snippet of the Docusign Admin page that highlights the Accounts dropdown at the top of the pane on the left.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/fzd1707173174972/page/images/Docusign-Admin_Accounts-dropdown_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MDQwNDksInNoZWFmIjoiZnpkMTcwNzE3MzE3NDk3MiJ9.g0NQixWltuwoGsH8WwFTXMZHx2e7f-3t7ra34X5y4gQ&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Docusign Admin page that highlights the Accounts dropdown at the top of the pane on the left.")

   The AI Control Settings page opens. Use this page to turn the AI data controls toggle off or on.
4. Select the AI data controls toggle to change the setting.

5. Select the SAVE option.

## Access and Availability FAQs

## Why don’t I see the 'AI data controls' setting?

There are two reasons that you may not see the AI data controls setting.

- First, the AI data controls setting is available to account administrators. If you are not an account administrator, you will not see this setting.
- Second, the setting will be rolled out to all Docusign accounts over time. If the setting has not been rolled out to your account, your account's data will not be used to help improve Docusign AI models.

Account administrators will be notified when the setting becomes available in your account.

## How will I know when my account will support AI training?

Account administrators will be notified when the setting is available.

## Why can’t I turn the 'AI data controls' setting on?

Customers must agree to AI training in their Docusign service agreement for the AI data controls setting to be enabled and configurable by the account administrator. If your organization did not consent to share its data for AI training, the setting will be disabled and turned off.

AI training helps Docusign build more powerful and valuable AI-assisted experiences. If the setting is off and disabled but you want to participate in AI training, contact your Docusign Sales Representative.

## Data Usage and Privacy FAQs

## Why is Docusign asking to use my data to train AI?

Docusign asks to use customer data for AI training to improve our models and provide better AI-assisted experiences. To protect sensitive agreement information, the data used to train Docusign AI models will be aggregated and anonymized before it is used. Anonymization includes removing personal identifiers, such as names, addresses, bank account information, and social security numbers (SSNs)/national tax numbers.

## What data does the 'AI data controls' setting apply to?

The AI data controls setting applies to customer data and AI output, which may include:

- Content created, uploaded, entered, sent, received, or stored through Docusign
- AI-generated content, for example agreement summary, extraction of agreement effective date
- Corrections made to AI-generated content

Any data used to train Docusign AI models will be aggregated and anonymized before it is used. Anonymization includes removing personal identifiers, such as names, addresses, bank account information, and social security numbers (SSNs)/national tax numbers.

Note: Docusign only uses data from the sending account when data from Docusign eSignature is used for AI training.

## Why does Docusign have a 30-day waiting period before using data for AI training?

The 30-day waiting period is designed to give customers full control over their data. Before any data is anonymized and used for AI training, we allow time for customers to review their preferences and make any necessary adjustments.

The waiting period starts on the date that your organization agrees to share data through your Docusign service agreement.

## What do I do if I don’t want my data used for AI training?

Customers have complete control over whether their data is used to improve Docusign AI models. We only use data from customers who have agreed to AI training in their Docusign service agreement. Customers can withdraw consent at any time by turning off the AI data controls setting.

## I don’t have the 'AI data controls' setting. Is my data being used for AI training?

No. Docusign will not use your data to improve its AI models unless your organization has agreed to AI training in your Docusign service agreement *and* the setting is available and turned on in your account.

- If your organization purchased Docusign services via the Docusign Sales team, contact your sales representative to provide consent.
- If your organization purchased a plan on the Docusign website, contact [Docusign Support](https://support.docusign.com/s/contactSupport) to provide consent.

## What happens to my data if I turn the 'AI data controls' setting off?

When the setting is turned off, Docusign does not ingest your data for AI training. Any data previously aggregated and anonymized for AI training based on earlier consent is retained as it can no longer be linked back to your organization.

## When I buy a FedRAMP or GovRAMP plan, do I share data for AI training?

No. For FedRAMP or GovRAMP plans, your account does not share data for AI training.

For non-FedRAMP or GovRAMP plans, Docusign may ask to use your anonymized data for AI training. You can opt out at the time of purchase by telling your Docusign sales representative that you would like to do so. Account administrators can also turn off the AI data controls setting to opt out at any time.

## Does the 'AI data controls' setting apply to data in Docusign Insight?

No. This consent does **not** apply to data in Docusign Insight because Docusign Insight is a single tenancy platform. Docusign Insight customers can modify or create new models using their own data for their Insight environment, but the data is otherwise not used to improve Docusign AI models.

## Managing the Setting FAQs

## Who can change the 'AI data controls' setting?

Account administrators can manage this setting from the AI Control Settings page located under the Admin menu.

Note: If your organization did not consent to share its data for AI training in your Docusign service agreement, the setting is turned off and disabled.

## Will turning the 'AI data controls' setting off affect my use of Docusign’s products and services?

No. Turning the setting to off does not prevent you from using any of Docusign’s AI-assisted features. You can change this setting at any time.

## How does the 'AI data controls' setting work if I have multiple Docusign accounts?

This is an account-level setting determined by your Docusign service agreement and your account administrator. Account administrators can turn the toggle off or on without affecting the organization’s other accounts. Additionally, organization administrators can update the setting for accounts linked to the organization.

When a customer agrees to share data for AI training in their Docusign service agreement, the setting is turned on by default in all the customer's accounts that are governed by the agreement. If a customer no longer wants to share data for AI training, the setting must be turned off for each individual account. There are two ways to do this:

1. An account administrator turns off the AI data controls toggle in each account.
2. A Docusign organization administrator can use [Bulk Actions](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=rrf1583359212854&topicId=eec1583359153905.html) to update the AI data controls setting for multiple accounts. For more information, see the [Update Multiple Accounts Using Account Settings Import](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=rrf1583359212854&topicId=fuv1583359168663.html) support page.
