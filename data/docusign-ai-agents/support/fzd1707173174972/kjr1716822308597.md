# Commitment to AI Awareness

Read about how Docusign is committed to being clear and transparent about how Docusign uses AI in its products and services.

Docusign is committed to being clear and transparent about how Docusign uses AI in our products and services. This article explains how customers are informed about the role that AI plays in our products and services. The article also describes the controls customers have when using AI in our products and services.

| **Docusign's Commitment** | **How It Is Implemented** |
| --- | --- |
| Customers are informed when AI is used to surface existing content or data from documents. | Product documentation and in-product indicators in the user interface (UI) inform customers that this processing activity uses AI. |
| Customers are informed of the use of AI when it has authored or generated new content. |
| Customers are informed when their data may be used to train AI models. | Customers may consent to AI training in the  [Docusign service agreement](https://www.docusign.com/legal/terms-and-conditions/ai-attachment-docusign-services). Account administrators can then view and manage whether the account’s data is used to train AI models through the AI data controls setting. See the [AI Data Controls Setting FAQs](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fzd1707173174972&topicId=dcs1708531444159.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the AI data controls setting, including how to turn the setting on or off, and frequently asked questions (FAQs).") for more information. |
| Customers are informed that their data may be sent to a third-party AI service for processing. | Docusign’s [Subprocessor List](https://www.docusign.com/trust/privacy/subprocessors-list) notifies customers about the subprocessors used to provide services to customers. Customer data is *not* shared with third-party model providers to train their models for other customers. |
