# Docusign Workspaces

Read about how you can use Docusign
Workspaces to manage multiple agreements and their data throughout the
life of a transaction.

Workspaces can help you manage multiple agreements and their data
throughout the life of an agreement more easily.

Workspaces improves collaboration with others both within and outside of
your organization. Invite users to a workspace so they can add
and review documents, sign envelopes, and monitor overall progress. Depending on how
much interaction you want them to have, you can assign different level permissions.

With Workspaces, it is easy to view and complete envelopes and keep track
of notifications. When you send envelopes from Workspaces, signers
receive a single email that directs them to a centralized signing experience that lists
all notifications.
