# View the History of Workspaces

Learn how to view information about the history of a workspace.
You can view its creation date, the name of the user who created it, and details about
changes made to the envelopes that it contains.

View information about the life of a workspace, such as its creation
date and actions taken by users on it, through the View
history option.

1. Open a workspace by selecting
   Workspaces
    from the
   Agreements page.
2. Select View history from the overflow menu (the three
   stacked dots next to Get Signatures at the top of the
   page).

   Information about the life of the workspace displays in
   columns by date and time, user, action, and activity.
3. Select the X to close the history window and return to
   the workspace.
