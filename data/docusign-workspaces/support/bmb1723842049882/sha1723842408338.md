# Create Workspaces

Learn how to create a workspace, to manage documents and
collaborate with other users.

Create a workspace to manage
documents and collaborate with other users.

1. Select the Agreements tab.
2. Select
   Workspaces
    from the left
   navigation area.
3. Enter a name for the workspace and select
   Save.

   A window with your new workspace opens and
   presents you with options to get signatures, invite collaborators, or add
   documents.

## Next steps

Send your document for signature or request documents from
another user.
