# Download Documents from Workspaces

Learn how to save copies of documents from your workspace to
your local environment for storage.

When you want to retain a copy of documents in a workspace, you can
download them to your computer.

1. Open a workspace by selecting
   Workspaces
    from the
   Agreements page.
2. Select the overflow menu (the three stacked dots next to Get
   Signatures).
3. Select Download all.

   The Download dialog box opens.
4. (Optional) If you do not want to download all documents, select the option for the document
   type you want to download, and select Download.

The documents are downloaded as a ZIP file.
