# Publish a Workspace Template

Learn how to publish a Workspace template to make it available for use.

## Before you begin

After you finish building your Workspace template and adding all necessary items, you can publish it. Publishing makes the template visible so that you and other users can use it.

After [Creating a Workspace Template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bmb1723842049882&topicId=tskdd3dde17-2ad1-4d13-a0e2-eed0edf56201.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to create a Workspace template to standardize the content for your recurring projects."), select Publish.

The Workspace template is published and available to use.

Note: You can update a published template. Changes are saved automatically while editing.
