# Workspace Templates

Read about how Workspace templates help you standardize repeated processes by pre-defining envelopes, upload requests, and documents.

Workspace templates contain pre-configured envelope templates, upload requests, and documents for repeated use.

Using a template ensures consistency and reduces the setup time required for new Workspaces.

Administrators and accounts with the right permissions can use and create Workspace templates.
