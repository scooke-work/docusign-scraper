# Participant Portal

Read about how the participant portal enables workspace collaborators to access and
complete requests.

Tasks that you send to workspace collaborators, such as requests for documents or
signature, are viewed and acted on in the participant portal.

When you send tasks to a participant, the participant receives an email notification that
links directly to the participant portal. The portal displays all assigned tasks, with
due dates, if available, and includes links to take action.

The participant does not need a Docusign account to access the
participant portal to view or complete the assigned tasks. As tasks are completed, the
status of each is updated in the workspace for easy tracking.

As the preparer of a workspace, you can also add a personalized message to the participant portal, which appears at the top of the portal. The message appears at the workspace level and is visible to all recipients that use the portal.
