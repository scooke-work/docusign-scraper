# Change the Status of Workspaces

Learn how to change the status of a workspace from
Active to Closed when the activities
associated with it are complete.

A workspace can have a status of either Active
or Closed. When your agreement process is complete, you can
change the status to Closed to remove the workspace from the list of active Workspaces.

Note: If necessary, you can change the status back to
Active by using the same procedure.

1. Open the workspace that you want to close.
2. Select the overflow menu (the three dots next to the Get
   signatures button).
3. Select Change status > Closed > Save.

The status of the workspace is
Closed.
