# Batch-Send for Envelopes and Upload Requests

Read about how workspace preparers and managers send multiple
tasks, such as envelopes and upload requests, at one time by using batch send.

As a workspace preparer or manager, you can consolidate
multiple tasks, such as envelopes and upload requests, and send them as a batch. Batch
sending is efficient not only for you as a preparer or manager, but also for recipients.
When you send tasks in a batch, recipients receive a single notification that
consolidates the multiple tasks.

To batch-send envelopes and upload requests, you save them to the workspace instead of sending them immediately after creation. They
remain in draft status until you select them from the Workspaces
detail view and send them together.

For more information, see the topic [Send Multiple Envelopes and Upload Requests in a Batch](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bmb1723842049882&topicId=xix1741909658346.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to send multiple envelopes and upload requests together in a batch.").
