# Branding in Workspaces

As an administrator, you can customize the sending and signing experience to promote your brand. You can apply your brand logo and colors to email notifications and the participant portal.

When branding is applied to Signing, participants will see a customized email template that reflects your unique colors and company logo.

The Participant portal can reflect your logo, custom header background, text color, button background & text color.

External participant portal has three new notification templates (Activation, Additional Task, and Reminders ). Each one of these templates must adhere to the email branding that is specified by the Admin of the sending account.
