# Actions Permitted By Permission Level and Role

Read about the actions that users who are invited to Workspaces or
have documents shared with them can take, depending on their role.

## Roles

The role that individuals take when interacting with Workspaces
depends on their association with the Docusign account that contains the workspace.

Workspaces owner
:   The creator of the workspace.

Internal invitee
:   Individual invited to the workspace who is a member of the same
    Docusign account as the Workspaces owner.

External invitee
:   Individual invited to the workspace who is not a member of the
    same Docusign account as the Workspaces owner.

Internal or external non-invitee
:   Individual who is not invited to a workspace but is associated
    with the tasks or documents within the workspace. For example, an individual
    that is assigned an upload request.

## Permission levels

Individuals who are invited to Workspaces can
be assigned either **Manage** or **Participate** permissions.

Manage
:   The Manage permission level allows adding and removing participants from a Workspace.
    Only invitees that are members of the same Docusign account as the
    Workspaces creator
    can have this permission.

    Note: Managing tasks or envelopes within a workspace is restricted to the owner of those artifacts.

Participate
:   The Participate permission can be assigned to invitees
    that are either members or non-members of the Docusign account. This
    permission limits the actions that assignees can perform.

## Actions allowed by permission level and role

The following table lists the actions allowed for each permission level by role.

| Action | Workspaces owner | Internal invitee (Manage access) | Internal invitee (Participate access) | External invitee (Participate access) | Internal and External non-invitee |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Add users to the workspace | Y (Only those created by the owner) | Y | N | N | N |
| View envelopes | Y | Y (only envelopes received) | Y (Only envelopes received) | Y (Only envelopes received) | Y (Only envelopes received) |
| Sign envelopes | Y (Only envelopes received) | Y (only envelopes received) | Y (Only envelopes received) | Y (Only envelopes received) | Y (Only envelopes received) |
| Create envelopes | Y | Y | N | N | N |
| Send envelopes | Y | Y | N | N | N |
| Revise and void envelopes | Y (Only envelopes created by the owner) | Y (Only envelopes created by the invitee) | N | N | N |
| Upload documents | Y | Y | Y | Y | N |
| Send workspace documents in envelope | Y (Only documents created by the owner) | Y (Only documents created by the invitee) | N | N | N |
| Share documents | Y | Y | Y | Y (Only documents uploaded by the invitee) | N |
| View documents | Y | Y | Y (Only documents uploaded by or shared with the invitee) | Y (Only documents uploaded by or shared with the invitee) | Y (Only documents uploaded by or shared with the non-invitee) |
| Rename documents | Y | Y | Y (Only documents uploaded by the invitee) | Y (Only documents uploaded by the invitee) | N |
| Delete documents | Y (Only documents created by the owner) | Y (Only documents uploaded by the invitee) | Y (Only documents uploaded by the invitee) | Y (Only documents uploaded by the invitee) | N |
| View users | Y | Y | N | N | N |
| Add upload request | Y | Y | N | N | N |
| Batch-send tasks (envelopes and upload requests) | Y | Y | N | N | N |
| Send a message | Y | Y | Y | N | N |
| View workspace history | Y | Y | N | N | N |
