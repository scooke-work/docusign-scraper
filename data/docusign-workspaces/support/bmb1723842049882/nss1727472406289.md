# Share a Document in Workspaces

Learn how owners and managers of Workspaces share documents with
other users both within and outside of the organization. Users can be added as participants
to all documents in the workspace or be given
access to specific agreement items.

You can share a document in a workspace with
collaborators that have been invited or with users that are not a part of the
workspace.

1. Open a workspace from the
   Workspaces
    page.
2. Locate the document that you want to share and select
   Share from the overflow menu (the three stacked dots
   next to View).

   The Share document with others dialog box
   opens.
3. Enter the email address for the user that you want to add and select
   Share.

   The participant you added is now visible beneath People with access to
   this document in the Share document with
   others dialog box.
4. Close the dialog box and return to the workspace.

The user you added receives a notification with a link to
the document.

## Next steps

After sharing a document, you can assign actions to the
participant.
