# Messaging in Workspaces

Read about how you can collaborate with members of a workspace through messaging.

Messaging is a key collaboration tool offered by Workspaces.

Workspace preparers can exchange messages with other members of the workspace, including their external clients. External clients can access messaging through the participant portal link that is emailed to them and do not require a Docusign account.

Workspace members who receive an envelope or upload request can directly communicate with workspace preparers by sending and receiving messages through the participant portal.

Note:

Administrators can disable messaging for an account. For more information, see [Disable Messaging](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bmb1723842049882&topicId=tsk9d79ef25-a585-4793-a236-c39c76046824.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how administrators disable messaging for all users and workspaces in an account.").
