# Sharing Workspaces with Other Users

Read about how you can share a workspace and documents within a
workspace with other users by assigning roles.

You can invite users to collaborate in Workspaces to add
documents and complete agreement steps.

The role you assign to users determines the level they can interact with the workspace. You can assign one of two roles: Manage and Participate.

- Manage–Users can view all documents in the workspace, view
  completed envelopes, add and share documents, and invite other collaborators.

  Note: The Manage role can be assigned to internal users only. To
  have this role, users must be on the same account as the person who created the
  workspace.
- Participate–Users can view and complete agreement steps assigned to them. You can assign this
  role to both internal and external users.

  Note: External users
  are those who are not on the same account as the workspace creator. These users can have the Participate role
  only. When you invite external users to a workspace,
  the Participate role is presented as the only option.
