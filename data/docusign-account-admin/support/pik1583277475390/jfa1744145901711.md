# Cancel Your IAM Subscription

Learn how to cancel your IAM subscription, and switch to a free account.

As an administrator of an existing IAM plan, you can cancel your subscription.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.

   The Subscription and billing page opens.![The screenshot shows the Subscription and billing page for an IAM Professional account.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/IAM-Sub-and-billing-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MDcsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.UHKbCyQTJ_j3vBnZK8oQT-2Cck84eKXUVyWLMZab9Aw&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Subscription and billing page for an IAM Professional account.")
2. Select Manage Your Subscription > Cancel Plan.

   The survey page opens.
3. Choose a reason for cancellation, and select Next.

   The plan information page opens.
4. Review feature information, and select Next.

   The offer page opens.
5. Review the discount offer. To continue to cancel your subscription, select
   Next.

   The confirm cancellation page opens.
6. Review cancellation and refund information, and select
   Confirm.

Your subscription is canceled on the date shown. Your account is
automatically switched to a free account.
