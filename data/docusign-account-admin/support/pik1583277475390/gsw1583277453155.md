# Use a Brand Company Name in the Electronic Record and Signature Disclosure

Learn how account administrators customize the legal disclosure with a company name. The
company name is used in email notifications and in the Signing experience.

As an administrator, you can customize the legal disclosure
with a company name to be used in email notifications and in the signing
experience.

1. From the Accounts view, select Legal
   Disclosure.
2. With your ERSD enabled, select the option Use brand company
   name.

   ![Use the brand company name in the ERSD.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Enable-Brand-Company-Name-in-ERSD.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjU1NzAsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.AdD6lAZ9x-jxU6fYHUePfCSJ2OGUDhdFc4iIkQqp_Bw&_LANG=enus "Use the brand company name in the ERSD.")
3. Select Save.

   Your settings are saved. Whenever an envelope is sent from the account that uses a
   signing brand with a specified Company Name, the value is used in email notifications and
   in the Signing experience. If the signing brand does not specify a Company Name, the
   Account Name is used instead.
