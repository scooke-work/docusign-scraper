# eNote Credentials

Read how account administrators configure their accounts with eOriginal credentials so
that eNotes can be sent.

To configure your account to create and send eNotes, you must add your eOriginal credentials.
With these credentials in place, eNotes sent through your account are tamper sealed and
vaulted in your eOriginal vault.

To send eNotes, you must also create a Connect to eOriginal configuration. See [Connect for eOriginal](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=vob1727899215236&topicId=tsn1583277394951.html&_LANG=enus) for more information.

eNotes are sent for electronic signature using the Docusign REST API. For details about
sending eNotes and eMortgage integration, see the eMortgage Implementation guide.

eNote credentials are managed from the eNote Credentials page in the
account administration settings. From this page, you can add, edit, and delete eNote
credentials.
