# Switch Your IAM Plan

Learn how to upgrade or downgrade your IAM plan.

As an administrator of an existing IAM account, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan, and
add or edit feature capacity to your subscription.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   The Subscription and Billing page opens.![The screenshot shows the Subscription and billing page for an IAM Professional account.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/IAM-Sub-and-billing-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ4OTksInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.S21e-wxSWzy4sp21RgM-G1SrdbWMfMa3RNUn3RkOdmE&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Subscription and billing page for an IAM Professional account.")

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Your Subscription > Switch Plan.

   The Switch your plan page opens.
3. (Optional) Select the Annual or
   Monthly toggle to change your billing cycle.
4. Choose the plan that you want, and select Select
   Plan.

   If you are upgrading your plan, the Confirm your subscription
   change page opens. If you are downgrading your plan, the
   Edit your subscription page opens.
5. To upgrade a plan, review your changes, and select
   Confirm.

   Your plan is upgraded and available immediately.
6. To downgrade a plan:
   1. Review your plan and edit by selecting from available options.
   2. (Optional) Select the Annual or
      Monthly toggle to change your billing
      cycle.
   3. (Optional) Select Add Capacity or edit the
      feature capacity total to add additional capacity.
   4. Select Next. The Confirm your
      subscription change page opens.
   5. Review your changes, and select Confirm.
   6. Your plan is changed. The changes apply on your renewal date.

You have switched your IAM plan.
