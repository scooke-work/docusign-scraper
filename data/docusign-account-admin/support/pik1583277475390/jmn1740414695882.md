# View Subscription and Usage

Learn how to view your subscriptions, renewal periods, and the status of your product
usage.

As an account administrator, you can view subscription and usage information for
products and add-ons. Usage information includes number of envelopes sent, seats
allocated, and add-on usage. You can also view renewal period information below the
subscription ID.

Note: This feature is available only for eSignature customers who have
purchased plans from a Docusign sales representative.

1. In the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Subscription.
3. In Product Usage, view the amounts used and the total
   amounts purchased for each product.![The screenshot shows the Product Usage section of the Subscription and usage page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Sub-and-usage-SSVC.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MzAsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.c71i6L28XGGyviycVO8SbmbRUP-9h74Idfz-t8slyBI&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Product Usage section of the Subscription and usage page.")
4. Select Purchased Products to view the quantity of
   products and add-ons purchased in your subscription.![The screenshot shows the Purchase Products tab on the Subscription and usage page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Sub-and-usage-tab-SSVC.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MzAsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.c71i6L28XGGyviycVO8SbmbRUP-9h74Idfz-t8slyBI&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Purchase Products tab on the Subscription and usage page.")
5. (Optional) Depending on your account type and renewal period, select
   Manage Renewal or Contact
   Sales to increase your amounts.

   Renewal period information displays below the subscription
   ID.
