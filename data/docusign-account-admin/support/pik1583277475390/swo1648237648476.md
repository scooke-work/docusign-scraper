# Subscribe to Account Administrator Email Notifications

Learn how to subscribe to account administrator email notifications. Account
administrators can choose the types of notifications to receive by email.

You can choose the types of notifications to receive by email.

1. From the Accounts view, select
   Overview to view the Notifications section.
2. Next to Filters, select the Notification Settings icon.

   The Notification Preferences opens.
   ![The screenshot shows the Notification Preferences modal containing the notification types.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/af.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ4NzcsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.Ij7EIbp-o4gBlt53Z3Zuc7IqLUZjGk_GKWNtHmabyNY&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Notification Preferences modal containing the notification types.")
3. Select the checkboxes for notification types that you want to subscribe
   to.
4. Select Save.

Your notification subscription settings are saved.
