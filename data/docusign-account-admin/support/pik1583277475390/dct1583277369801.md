# Users and Groups Management

Read about how administrators manage individual users, groups of users, and permission profiles for an account through the Users and Groups section.

As an account administrator, you can manage individual users, groups of users, and permission profiles in the Users and Groups section.

Note: Docusign CLM enabled accounts manage users and groups for the account through your Docusign organization settings. Contact your Docusign organization administrator to manage users and groups at the organization level. For more information, see [Managing Groups](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?topicId=fza1583359190504.html&bundleId=rrf1583359212854&_LANG=enus) and [Managing Users](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?topicId=jni1583359185567.html&bundleId=rrf1583359212854&_LANG=enus).
