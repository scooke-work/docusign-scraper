# Change the AI Data Controls Setting for an Account

Learn how to change the AI Data Controls setting for your account to control data sharing for AI model training.

The AI Data Controls setting provides additional control within the product to customers who have agreed to share their data for training Docusign AI models to deliver improved products. The AI Data Controls setting is an account-level setting. All data is aggregated and anonymized before being used for AI training. For more details, see the [AI Data Controls Setting FAQs](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fzd1707173174972&topicId=dcs1708531444159.html&_LANG=enus) .

This setting is unavailable in some accounts. If the setting has not been rolled out to your account, your account's data will not be used to help improve Docusign AI models.

This setting is available for the following accounts:

- Accounts with Agreement Manager
- Accounts enabled with the Agreement Manager Trial
- Accounts with Workflow Builder
- CLM accounts (excludes CLM.CM accounts)
- Other accounts where an organization has agreed to share anonymized data for AI training in its Docusign service agreement.

This setting does not apply to developer, Sandbox, or demo accounts.

1. From the Accounts view, select AI Data Controls from the ACCOUNT section in the navigation pane on the left.

2. Select the toggle to change the setting for Allow my anonymized data to be used to improve AI-assisted experiences.

   - Turn off the toggle to revoke consent to Docusign using your data for AI training.
   - Turn on the toggle to consent to Docusign using your data for AI training.
3. Select Save.

Your change to the AI Data Controls setting is saved.
