# Undo Pending Changes to Your Subscription

Learn how administrators can undo subscription changes that are still
pending.

When you reduce the number of seats, the change is pending until the renewal date of
your current subscription. You can undo the changes while they are still
pending.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Your Subscription > Edit Subscription Details.

   For eSignature accounts, Manage Your Subscription is in
   the Edit subscription details section.

   The Edit your subscription window opens. ![The screenshot shows the Edit your subscription window with Undo Pending Change.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Edit-your-subscription-window-pending-change.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NDQsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.JGagPsQs4jcH3YQBxP-5q-HRZf5xVvwYoY4w16Fn6_A&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Edit your subscription window with Undo Pending Change.")
3. Select Undo Pending Change.

   A message confirms that you want to undo your subscription changes.
4. Select Yes, Continue.
5. Select Done to close the window.

The pending changes are removed.
