# Buy an Add-On

Learn how to purchase an add-on to your subscription.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Buy for the add-on.

   A window to purchase the add-on for your subscription opens. ![The screenshot shows the Add SMS delivery to your subscription window.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/addons3.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NjQsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.zEUOq6leQtBQixYBRXCgbbjoLAKmcSwbc3o3Y8Kj8Oc&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Add SMS delivery to your subscription window.")
3. Enter an amount to purchase using your payment information, and select
   Purchase.
4. Select Done on the purchase confirmation.

The add-on is enabled in your account. Usage information displays
in the add-on section on the Plan and Billing page.

Note: Enabling
the add-on service can take up to one minute. If you do not see your add-on enabled,
refresh your browser.
