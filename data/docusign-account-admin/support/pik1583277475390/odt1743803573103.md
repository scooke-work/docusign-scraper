# IAM Plans

Docusign
Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) is an AI-powered platform that
enables you to connect and optimize every step of your business processes that involve
agreements.

As a new Docusign customer or trial user, you can review plan options and
purchase an IAM plan from the Plans and Pricing page.

As an administrator on a Docusign eSignature account, you can upgrade to an IAM
plan on the Subscription and Billing page.

As an administrator on a Docusign IAM account, you can manage or upgrade your
plan, purchase extensions, and add feature capacity to your plan on the
Plan and Billing page.

Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.

Note: IAM Starter, IAM Standard, and IAM Professional plans are
currently available only for customers in the United States, Australia, Canada, Great
Britain, Germany, and France.
