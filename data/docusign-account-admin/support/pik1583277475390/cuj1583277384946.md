# Reports and Envelope Custom Fields

Read about how account administrators include custom field information for accounts in
envelope reports.

As an account administrator, you can use reports to create a custom envelope report that includes
envelope custom field information. When you add the envelope custom field column to a report,
all fields defined on your account will be listed in the envelope report, including those that
are not shown to envelope creators.
