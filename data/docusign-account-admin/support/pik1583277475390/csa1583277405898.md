# Add Custom Logos and Specify Brand Colors

Learn how administrators customize the sending experience with a custom logo and brand
color scheme.

As an administrator, you can customize the sending experience
with a custom logo and color scheme that is applied to envelopes sent from your
account.

1. From the Accounts view, select
   Brands.
2. Select the Sending tab, and select Edit Your
   Theme.
3. In the sending or email logo section, select the "x" to remove
   the existing logo.
4. Select Upload Logo and select your logo file. The logo should
   meet the following requirements:

   Maximum file size: 300 KB

   Dimensions (recommended): 296x76 pixels

   File formats: JPG, GIF, PNG
5. To specify colors:
   1. Select the color box of the Banner or Button color that you want to edit. The color
      controls open.

      ![The screenshot shows the color picker.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/sending-color-picker.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjUwNDgsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.BMmi1S_qrmYbI2eMpoh0vyA8GK7HpFZ--3a0gqenunU&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the color picker.")
   2. Select a color using the color picker, use the eye dropper to match a color value in your custom logo or other image, or enter the hexadecimal value for the color.
6. Use the Preview views to check what account members will see for
   the Home and Manage pages. Use the Email preview to view what your recipients will see
   when they receive a Docusign envelope.
7. To finish and apply your brand, select Apply Brand.

   Your changes are summarized on the Branding page and all account members will see your
   custom sending brand when they log in to their account.

Your customized brand is available to account members
when they log into their account.
