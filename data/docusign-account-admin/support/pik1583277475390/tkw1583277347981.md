# Define Sending Settings

Learn how account administrators can modify sending settings for documents sent from your
account.

As an administrator, you can modify sending settings for
documents sent from your account.

1. From the Accounts view, select Sending
   Settings.
2. Adjust your sending settings as needed.

   See the [Fields and Properties](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=xgg1583277350154.html&_LANG=enus "Read about how account administrators configure optional fields and properties in Sending Settings.") topic for descriptions of the sending
   settings.
3. Select Save.
