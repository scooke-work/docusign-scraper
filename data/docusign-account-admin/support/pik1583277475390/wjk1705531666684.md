# Reduce the Number of Seats in Your Subscription

Learn how administrators can reduce the number of seats in a subscription. Seat
reductions are applied on the subscription renewal date.

Administrators can reduce the number of seats and envelopes in a subscription.

Note: When an administrator removes a user from an account, the
total number of seats and envelope capacity in the subscription is unchanged.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Your Subscription > Edit Subscription Details.

   For eSignature accounts, scroll to the bottom of the page to view Manage Your
   Subscription in the Manage subscription details section.

   The Edit your subscription window opens. ![The screenshot shows the Edit your subscription window for an eSignature Standard subscription.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Edit-your-subscription-window.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NDAsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.UAJbcLeDJslazocZ-3TXGy6Sj2ooKtbmVynr9HCY6Q4&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Edit your subscription window for an eSignature Standard subscription.")
3. Select Edit Quantity.

   The Edit seats and envelopes window opens.![The screenshot shows the Edit seats and envelopes window.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Edit-seats-and-envelopes.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NDAsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.UAJbcLeDJslazocZ-3TXGy6Sj2ooKtbmVynr9HCY6Q4&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Edit seats and envelopes window.")
4. Select the - button to reduce the number of seats in
   your plan.

   Each seat includes 10 envelopes on a monthly plan or 100 envelopes on an
   annual plan. The total number of envelopes can be shared among all users.
5. Select Next.

   The Confirm change window opens.
6. Review changes to your envelopes and select Confirm
   Change.

   The What led to this change window
   opens.
7. Select a reason for changing the number of seats, and select
   Next.

   The Confirm your subscription window opens.![The screenshot shows the Confirm your subscription window with a eSignature Standard subscription.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Confirm-your-subscription-window.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NDAsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.UAJbcLeDJslazocZ-3TXGy6Sj2ooKtbmVynr9HCY6Q4&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Confirm your subscription window with a eSignature Standard subscription.")
8. Review your current subscription and your new subscription reflecting the new
   number of seats, and select Confirm.
9. Select Done to close the confirmation window.

Your changes are saved as pending. The seat reductions are applied
to your subscription on your subscription renewal date.
