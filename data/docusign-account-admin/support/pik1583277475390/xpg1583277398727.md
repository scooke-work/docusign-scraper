# Logs of Account Changes and Bulk Actions

Read about how account administrators review and download a history of changes to
accounts and the results of bulk actions. Audit logs capture events and changes made to accounts
such as settings, billing information, and plan type. Bulk actions logs provide details about
bulk actions such as envelope transfers and user uploads.

As an account administrator, you can review changes to an account and the progress or results
of bulk actions for a specific period of time from the Audit Logs and
Bulk Actions Logs. You can download the information as a
comma-separated value (CSV) file for detailed processing and analysis.

Review changes to an account for a specific date range from the Audit
Logs page. The audit logs include changes made to account settings, billing
information, plan type, and more.

Review a history of bulk actions from the Bulk Actions Log page. The
bulk actions logs provide details about and the progress of actions such as user uploads and
envelope transfers.
