# Agreement Rules Modification

Read about the actions that account administrators can take on agreement rules. From the
RULES page, administrators can edit, disable, and delete
agreement rules.

Administrators can edit existing rules from the RULES page of the
Agreement Rules area. From the Actions
menu, administrators can modify the conditions and actions associated with the rule,
disable the rule, or delete the rule. Disabled and deleted rules no longer affect
completed envelopes.
