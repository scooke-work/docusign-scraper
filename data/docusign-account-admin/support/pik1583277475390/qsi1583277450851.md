# Transfer Sent Envelopes

Learn to transfer ownership of sent envelopes from your account to another account
member.

You can transfer your own sent envelopes from within your Docusign eSignature account
view.

1. From your Docusign account, select the
   Agreements tab in the global header.
2. Locate any of your draft or sent envelopes that you want to transfer to another account
   member.
3. To transfer a single envelope:
   1. Select the action menu for the envelope and select Transfer
      Ownership.
   2. Select the new owner for the envelope.

      ![The screenshot shows the Transfer Ownership section.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/transferownership01.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjU1NDgsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.O-O634UWVed62TEqMJTWAitdcAN82NuDcYOF52WzvDo&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Transfer Ownership section.")
   3. (Optional). To add yourself as a Receives a Copy recipient, select Add
      Original Owner as Carbon Copy. The envelope displays in your Inbox after
      the transfer is complete, and you are added to the envelope routing order as a
      completed copy received recipient.
   4. Select Transfer.
4. To transfer multiple envelopes:
   1. Mark the check box for each envelope that you want to transfer.
   2. Select the overflow menu and select Transfer
      Ownership.

      ![The screenshot shows Transfer Ownership of multiple envelopes.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/transferownership02.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjU1NDgsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.O-O634UWVed62TEqMJTWAitdcAN82NuDcYOF52WzvDo&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows Transfer Ownership of multiple envelopes.")
   3. Select the new owner for the envelopes and select
      Transfer.

      The transfer is initiated. The envelopes may remain in your account for a brief
      time until the transfer is complete. Refresh your browser for an updated view of the
      transferred items.
