# Settings to Allow Comments on Documents and Envelopes

Read about how account administrators configure settings for users of an account to
comment on documents in envelopes. To facilitate transactions, administrators can allow comments
and specify how often users are notified when comments are received.

Comments can help users complete transactions by enabling recipients to ask and answer
questions about specific sections of documents in envelopes.

The settings for comments apply to all users of an account. You cannot change comments
settings for some users on an account and not for others.

Note: By default, comments are disabled for an
account. To enable comments and manage the settings for them, you must be an account
administrator with All Administration Capabilities. If the comments option is not available on
your account, contact Docusign Support to enable the feature.

For information about user preferences, see [Changing the Frequency of Comment Notifications](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?topicId=ded1578456661259.html&bundleId=jux1643235969954&_LANG=enus).

For information about envelope settings, see [Disabling Comments on an Envelope](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?topicId=ptb1578456497413.html&bundleId=gbo1643332197980&_LANG=enus).
