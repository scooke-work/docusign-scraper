# AI Tools in the Signing Experience

The AI-Assisted Summary and Extraction feature provides a concise overview of agreements and key details. Account administrators on Direct plans can disable this feature in account adminstration settings.

When users are signing documents, they can take advantage of the AI-Assisted Summary and Extraction feature. It provides a short summary of the agreement and displays the key agreement details.

The AI-Assisted Summary and Extraction feature is enabled by default for eligible customers and agreements to offer to their signers. Account administrators on Direct plans can disable this feature for users through the account administration settings.

Note:

- AI-assisted features might not be available on your account.
- AI-assisted features are currently only available in North America.

For more information about using the AI-Assisted Summary and Extraction feature, see the [View Key Agreement Information with the Docusign Signing AI Summary](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?language=en_US&bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=dvd1751051359819.html&_LANG=enus)﻿ topic.
