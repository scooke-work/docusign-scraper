# Transfer of Envelopes

Read about how administrators transfer sent envelopes to other users on the
account.

As an administrator, you can transfer your own sent envelopes from within your Docusign account view, and you can transfer envelopes belonging to other account members. To transfer
another user's envelopes, their envelopes must first be shared with you. See [Manage Shared Access to Envelopes](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=imk1656607611659.html&_LANG=enus "Read about how administrators manage settings for shared access to envelopes among users.") and [Envelope Sharing Between Account Users](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=uzg1583277438725.html&_LANG=enus "Read about how administrators permit users to see and take action on envelopes that belong to other users on the account.") for details on how to share envelopes.
