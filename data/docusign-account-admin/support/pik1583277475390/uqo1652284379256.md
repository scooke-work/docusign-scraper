# Disable Comments on Envelopes

Learn how account administrators disable comments on documents and envelopes. Senders
and recipients of envelopes sent before comments are disabled can still view comments they
are authorized to see.

Administrators can disable comments on envelopes for
users of an account.

Note: When you disable comments, senders and
recipients of envelopes that had prior comments can still view the comments and
replies they are authorized to see.

1. From the Accounts view, select
   Comments.
2. Clear Enable comments in envelopes sent from this
   account.
3. Select Save.

Account users cannot post comments on envelopes sent from
the account.
