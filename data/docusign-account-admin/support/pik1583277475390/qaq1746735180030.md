# View Your Organization Administrators

Learn how to view a list of the organization administrators associated with your account.

Account Administrators can view a list of all the organization administrators for the organization that their account is connected to. The list shows only the active full organization administrators.

Most accounts are managed by only one organization. Some accounts might be managed by one organization while the account subscription is managed by another organization. In these cases, organization administrators for both organizations are listed.

Note:

To view this feature, an organization administrator must open the Features page of the organization and enable Share Organization Administrator Contact Details in the features list.

1. From the Accounts view, select Account Profile.
2. View the list of organization administrators by name and email address.
