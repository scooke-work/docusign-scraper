# Increase the Number of Seats in Your Subscription

Learn how administrators can increase the number of seats and envelopes in a
subscription. Additional seats are applied to your subscription immediately.

As an administrator, you can increase the number of seats and envelopes in a subscription.
Additional seats are applied to your subscription immediately.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Your Subscription > Edit Subscription Details.

   For eSignature accounts, Manage Your Subscription is in
   the Manage subscription details section.

   The Edit your subscription window opens.![The screenshot shows the Manage subscription details section.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Manage-Sub-CTA-Experiment.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NDcsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.Ob0Q5AelS6WpG5FcCJpZORC19Zh3Cd4aa6eSvgBMiQM&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Manage subscription details section.")
3. Select Edit Quantity.

   The Edit seats and envelopes window opens. ![The screenshot shows the Edit seats and envelopes window.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Edit-seats-and-envelopes.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NDcsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.Ob0Q5AelS6WpG5FcCJpZORC19Zh3Cd4aa6eSvgBMiQM&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Edit seats and envelopes window.")
4. Select the + button to increase the number of seats in
   your plan. Each seat includes 10 envelopes on a monthly plan, or 100 envelopes
   on an annual plan. The total number of envelopes can be shared among all users.
5. Select Next.

   The Review your subscription changes window opens.
6. Review your current subscription and your new subscription reflecting the new
   number of seats.
7. Select Continue to Purchase.

   The Confirm your order window opens.![The screenshot shows the Confirm your order window.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Confirm-your-order.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NDcsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.Ob0Q5AelS6WpG5FcCJpZORC19Zh3Cd4aa6eSvgBMiQM&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Confirm your order window.")
8. Select Purchase.

   The purchase is completed and a copy of your receipt is sent to your
   email.
9. Select Done to close the confirmation window.

The additional seats are applied to your subscription
immediately.
