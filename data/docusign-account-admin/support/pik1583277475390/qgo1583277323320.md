# Multiple Docusign Account Administrators

Read about the importance of having two full administrators on an account. Administrators
are alerted when an account has only one full administrator. To designate another user as an
administrator, assign full administrative permissions to the user.

If you have a paid account with two or more users, each time you log in, Docusign verifies that you have more than one active full administrator on the
account. If your account has only one full administrator, an information banner displays at
the top of the page.

Having multiple full administrators maintains continuous account access if an administrator
leaves the company or is otherwise unavailable. You can review a list of users and update
their assigned permission profile from the [Users
page](https://admin.docusign.com/authenticate?goTo=users). For more information about updating user permissions, see [User Management](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=ixb1583277365937.html&_LANG=enus "Read about how account administrators manage the users in an account through the Users page. You can add, edit, close, reset passwords, and modify sharing for your users.").
