# Update User Email Addresses

Read about how organization administrators can bulk update user email addresses using a CSV file.

Docusign organization administrators with the Administrators and Users Administrators permission profiles with a claimed domain can update email addresses for users on their domain.

You can update email addresses for your users if the following conditions are met:

- The organization has claimed the domain. For more information, see the [Domains](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=bzr1583359141662.html&_LANG=enus "Read about Step 1 of the process to set up and enable SSO for your Docusign organization and additional information about reserving domains.") topic.
- The user's email address is on the domain. For example, if your domain is www.example.com, the user's email would be user@example.com.
- If the organization has more than one claimed domain, you can also update the domain of the user to match another claimed domain.

Note: You cannot change an email address for a user on a domain you have not claimed.

To change a user's email address, download and populate the sample CSV from Users > Bulk Update > Download Update template. The UserEmail column will remain unchanged and should contain the current user's email address.

Add the UpdatedUserEmail column, and enter the new email address you want to use. After you complete the import, the user's email address is updated.
