# Custody Transfer Rules

Read about how account administrators use the Custody Transfer page to manage rules that
automatically transfer ownership of envelopes from one user to another.

Docusign account administrators can add and manage rules to automatically
transfer ownership of envelopes between users in the same Docusign account
from the Custody Transfer page.

Note: The Transfer Custody feature must be enabled for your account to be able to
transfer ownership.

To transfer envelopes or templates manually, see [Transfer
Envelopes and Templates](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=vyz1583277376194.html&_LANG=enus "As an account administrator, you can manually transfer envelopes or templates between users. When you transfer ownership of envelopes or templates, the transferred items are removed from the original owner’s account and moved to the new owner's account. Transfer envelopes and templates from either the Users page or the Custody Transfer page.").
