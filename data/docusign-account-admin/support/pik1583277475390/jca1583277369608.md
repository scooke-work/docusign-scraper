# Signing Groups Administration

Read about how administrators can set up and manage Signing Groups and alternate emails
for the members of the group. Any member of the Signing Group can open and act on the document
fields addressed to the group.

Note: Signing Groups are incompatible with the Docusign mobile app. Users must sign in at [www.docusign.com](https://www.docusign.com/) to use the
feature. If you do not see Signing Groups, the feature might not be enabled for your account.
Contact Docusign Support to enable this feature.

The Signing Groups option allows account administrators to create and edit Signing Groups. A
Signing Group is used to send documents to a group of people, where any member of that group
can open and act on the document fields addressed to the group, even though signature and
other fields are not specifically assigned to a person.
