# Recipient Identification Using Bank Logins

Read about how recipients can use their bank logins to verify their
identity.

In addition to passports and driver licenses, recipients can use their bank logins to
verify their identity for access to envelopes. For example, recipients who need to
identify themselves can choose between a bank login, a driver license, a passport, or
KBA.
