# User Password Reset with the AI Agent

Read about how account administrators on digital plans can use the Docusign AI agent to reset user passwords, resend invitations, and reactivate users.

Account administrators can reset passwords for their users by using the Docusign AI agent. In account administration, you can open the AI agent and select one of the quick actions or type requests in natural language, such as "Reset password for Lisa Chin." The AI agent locates the user, confirms the name and email address, and sends the password reset email. The AI agent handles common edge cases, including misspelled names or email addresses, multiple matching users, and pending or closed users.

The AI agent also includes guided steps to resend invitations or reactivate users. If a user is managed through SSO, you are instructed to contact your IT department to make any changes.

This feature is currently available only to account administrators on digital plans, either paid or trial, in the US region and the English locale. End users cannot access this AI agent to reset their password.
