# Add a VAT or GST Number to Accounts

Learn how administrators in certain countries add a Value-added tax (VAT) or a Goods and
Services Tax (GST) number to their accounts to obtain tax exemptions for qualified business
purchases.

## Before you begin

Log in to your Docusign account as an
administrator, and select the Admin tab.

As part of billing, taxes are automatically collected at the point of checkout for accounts in
certain countries. For application of the reverse charge mechanism where applicable,
add your VAT or GST number to the billing information of your account.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Update Payment and Tax Details in the
   Billing Details section.
3. Enter your VAT or GST number in the Tax information
   (optional) field.

4. Select Update.

Your tax information is saved.
