# Try an Add-On for Free

Learn how to enable a free trial of an add-on to your subscription.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Go to the add-on section, and select Learn More.

   A window opens with trial add-on information.![The screenshot shows the Try Identity Verification window.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/addonstrial.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5NTksInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.-tILA48YriVZb7hrf3sd_3e3gg9jqCb3zx_C5Sr9-Dc&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Try Identity Verification window.")
3. Select Start Free Trial.

   The window confirms that the add-on is enabled.
4. Select Get Started.

The add-on is enabled in your account. Usage information displays
in the add-on section on the Plan and Billing page.

Note: Enabling
the add-on service can take up to one minute. If you do not see your add-on enabled,
refresh your browser.
