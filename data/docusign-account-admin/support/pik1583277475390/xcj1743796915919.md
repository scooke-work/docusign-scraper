# Add Seats, Envelopes, and Add-Ons for Plans Purchased from a Sales Representative

Learn how to add additional seats, envelopes, and add-ons to your existing subscriptions.

This feature is available to eligible global customers who have purchased an eSignature plan from a Docusign sales representative. If you don’t see the option to edit products directly in your account, it might be due to your subscription type or contracting region.

Note: Some customers may temporarily be unable to access product purchase and product usage information. Usage information will be available once the transition is complete within two business days.

If you need to add capacity immediately or for additional help, visit the Docusign [Support Center](https://support.docusign.com/) or contact your Docusign account team.

1. From the Account view, select Plan and Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Subscription.

   The Subscription and usage page opens.
3. Select Add More or Edit Products to view your current order details and add more quantity to a product.![The screenshot shows the Subscription and usage page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/SSAC_sub_v2.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MjQsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.gqM1g4oAqN9bZsJfu3Ic5D8YKay3NtlDqZCM7zwD7jY&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Subscription and usage page.")

   The checkout page opens.
4. Enter the quantity to add to your product. As you increase the number of envelopes or seats, your plan services will automatically adjust to match the changes.
5. (Optional) Expand the Your other products section to add quantities for any of your other products.![The screenshot shows the checkout page where you can add more quantity to a product.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/SSAC_checkout.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MjQsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.gqM1g4oAqN9bZsJfu3Ic5D8YKay3NtlDqZCM7zwD7jY&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the checkout page where you can add more quantity to a product.")
6. Review your new order summary. Select the certification checkbox to confirm that you are authorized to make this purchase for your account.
7. Select Submit to complete your purchase.

Your purchase is completed and will be available to use right away while your order is processed. Within 24 hours, your subscription and pricing updates will be fully processed, and you’ll be charged according to your current billing cycle settings.

For additional help, visit the Docusign [Support Center](https://support.docusign.com/) or contact your Docusign account team.
