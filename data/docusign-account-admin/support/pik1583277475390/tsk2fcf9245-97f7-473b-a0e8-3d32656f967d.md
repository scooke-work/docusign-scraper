# Turn Off the AI Tools for Signing Setting in Account Administration

Learn how to disable the AI tools for signing in Account Administration to prevent account users from accessing AI-Assisted Summary and Extraction features.

You can choose to turn off the Enable AI tools in signing for sent agreements setting for your account users. When you turn off this setting, your account users cannot view or access the agreement summaries provided by the AI-Assisted Summary and Extraction feature.

Note:

- This setting allows administrators to turn off the feature for envelopes sent from the account. For any envelope received by users on the account, AI features may still appear if the sending account has them enabled.
- Any new AI features introduced into the Signing experience for sent agreements after this setting is turned off are also managed by this setting and are not auto-enabled.

1. In the Accounts view, select Signing Settings.
2. ![The screenshot shows the AI tools setting to turn off the feature for signers.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/AI-tools-sign-settings.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjUzODIsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.jKmUjseEVF06ED9x7pRkfC7I4Fs2eolx4scyjb76zJY&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the AI tools setting to turn off the feature for signers.")In the AI Tools in the Signing Experience section, uncheck the Enable AI tools in signing for sent agreements setting.
3. Select Save Changes.

The change to the setting is saved. Your account users cannot view or access the agreement summaries provided by the AI-Assisted Summary and Extraction feature.
