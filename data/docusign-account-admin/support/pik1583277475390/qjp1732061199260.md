# Update Your Account Subscription

Learn how administrators can update account subscriptions including product quantity,
payment method, and billing information.

For account subscriptions that use automatic renewal, the current subscription will renew on
the scheduled date. As an account administrator, you can make the following changes
to your subscription:

- Edit the quantity of products in the subscription
- Edit the payment method, shipping information, and billing information

Note: Subscription management is not available for all customers. If you
must make changes to your subscription and this feature is not available, contact
Docusign Support. To add or remove seats, go to [Manage Seats in Your Subscription](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=kla1705531597804.html&_LANG=enus "Read about how administrators can change the number of seats and envelopes in their subscriptions.")﻿.

1. In the Accounts view, select Plan and Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Subscription.

   The Renewals page opens.
3. Select Manage Renewal > Edit Your Subscription.
4. Edit the quantities of each product, and select
   Next.

   ![The screenshot shows editing the quantity of envelopes in a subscription.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/renewals2png.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MzUsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.eH3m1tHdwq0fpG-R8At3EWYXyLp5cR_JEemk3gzmzKU&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows editing the quantity of envelopes in a subscription.")
5. (Optional) Edit the billing details including payment method, shipping details,
   and billing details, and select Next.

   ![The screenshot shows the sections where you can edit your billing information.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/renewals3png.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MzUsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.eH3m1tHdwq0fpG-R8At3EWYXyLp5cR_JEemk3gzmzKU&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the sections where you can edit your billing information.")
6. View the terms and conditions before submitting renewal updates.

   ![The screenshot shows the summary of the changes to your subscription.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/renewals4png.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MzUsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.eH3m1tHdwq0fpG-R8At3EWYXyLp5cR_JEemk3gzmzKU&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the summary of the changes to your subscription.")
7. Scroll down to the page and select the checkbox to confirm that you are the
   authorized user to submit changes.

   ![The screenshot shows the checkbox that you must select to authorize the changes.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/renewals4bpng.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MzUsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.eH3m1tHdwq0fpG-R8At3EWYXyLp5cR_JEemk3gzmzKU&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the checkbox that you must select to authorize the changes.")
8. Select Submit to make the changes to your
   subscription.

Your subscription is updated.
