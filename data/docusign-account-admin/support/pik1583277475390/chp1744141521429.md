# Upgrade from an eSignature Plan to an IAM Plan

Learn how to upgrade your eSignature account to an IAM plan.

As an administrator of an existing eSignature account, you can upgrade your account to an IAM
plan.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   The Plan and billing page opens.![The screenshot shows the Plan and billing page for an eSignature Standard account.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/esign-plan-and-billing-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ4OTUsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9._pvZCVR_hcoP6CL9qK5lTsjjfCoq1JeMrdPXRpZWBSc&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Plan and billing page for an eSignature Standard account.")

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Your Subscription  > Upgrade Plan.

   The Upgrade your plan window opens.
3. Select View All Plans.

   The Upgrade your plan page opens.
4. Select the IAM tab.
5. (Optional) View monthly plans by selecting the Monthly
   toggle.
6. Choose an IAM plan and select Buy Now.

   The Add billing details page opens.
7. (Optional) You can make changes to your billing options and user licenses for
   the plan. Select from the dropdown menus on the checkout page. Checkout will
   automatically update totals.
8. Select your payment method.
9. Select Purchase.

   The confirmation page opens.

You have upgraded to an IAM plan. An order confirmation is
sent to your email.
