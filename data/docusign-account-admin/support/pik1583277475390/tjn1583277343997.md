# Assign Brands to Groups

Learn how administrators assign brands to groups for group members to use when they
create envelopes and templates.

As an account administrator, you can assign brands to groups
for group members to use.

1. From the Accounts view, select Groups.
2. Locate the group that you want to assign a brand to by searching or scanning the list,
   and select it.
3. Select the Brands tab.
4. Select Assign Brand.

   The brands configured for your account are listed.
5. Select the checkbox next to the brands that you want to assign to the group, and select
   Assign.

All group members can now use the assigned brands on the
envelopes they send and templates they create.
