# Add Envelopes and Seats for Plans Purchased Online

Learn how administrators can add more envelopes and seats for annual or monthly plans.

As an account administrator, you can purchase more envelopes in increments of 100 for annual
plans and 10 for monthly plans. New envelopes are shared among seats. Each purchase
of 100 or 10 envelopes includes one seat.

Note: This feature is available only for Standard or Business Pro Digital
eSignature plans that were purchased online.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.

   The Plan and billing page opens. ![The screenshot shows the Plan and billing page for eSignature accounts.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/PnBUpgradePlanNewDesktop.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MjgsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.wBUwsVd46sxUG2IT_ww8ukbaexh4npBh9ffkSYxKaas&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Plan and billing page for eSignature accounts.")
2. Select Add Envelopes & Seats.

   The Add envelopes and seats window opens. This
   example screenshot displays an annual plan. For monthly plans, you can select
   envelopes in quantities of 10.![The screenshot shows an example adding 100 envelopes and one seat on an annual plan.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/addenvelopes2.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ5MjgsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.wBUwsVd46sxUG2IT_ww8ukbaexh4npBh9ffkSYxKaas&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows an example adding 100 envelopes and one seat on an annual plan.")
3. Select the number of envelopes and seats that you want to purchase.

   Choose a number in increments of 100. Every purchase of 100 envelopes includes
   one seat. The total number of envelopes can be shared among all users.
4. Review the amount due for your purchase, and select
   Purchase.

   The purchase is completed and a copy of your receipt is sent to your
   email.
5. Select Done to close the confirmation window.

The additional envelopes and seats that you have purchased are
applied to your subscription immediately.
