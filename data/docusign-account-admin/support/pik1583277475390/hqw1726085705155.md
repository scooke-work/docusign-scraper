# Access Account Administration Settings from Your Docusign Account

Learn how to access account administration settings from your Docusign account.

If you are an account administrator, you can use the Admin tab to navigate to your account administration settings.

1. Log in to your Docusign account as an administrator.

   Your account opens. The top navigation bar includes the Admin tab. ![The screenshot shows the Admin tab in the top navigation bar.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/access-acct-admin1.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ4NTYsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.a_S6cXnJvnsw5xhV46S8h9lXjH6bP3dbZB9jG5w3sBQ&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Admin tab in the top navigation bar.")
2. Select the Admin tab in the top navigation bar.

   The Overview page opens in the Accounts view. You can return to your Docusign account by selecting Home on the top navigation bar.![The screenshot shows the Overview page in the Accounts view when you have an organization and one or more linked accounts.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/access-acct-admin2.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ4NTYsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.a_S6cXnJvnsw5xhV46S8h9lXjH6bP3dbZB9jG5w3sBQ&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Overview page in the Accounts view when you have an organization and one or more linked accounts.")

   If you have an organization and one or more linked accounts, you can use the left tabs to navigate to the organization or the other accounts.

   If you have only one account and no organization, the navigation bar on the far left does not appear. In this case, you only have access to one account in your Accounts view. ![The screenshot shows the Overview page in the Accounts view when you have one account and no organization.
