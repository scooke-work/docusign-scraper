# Customize Themes for Sending Brands

Learn how administrators customize the logo and color scheme for a brand.

As an administrator, you can customize the logo and color
scheme for a brand.

1. From the Accounts view, select
   Brands.
2. Select the Sending tab.

   Your current Sending brand configuration displays.![The screenshot shows the sending brand customization section on the Brands page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Brands-sending.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjUwNDEsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.22VqkCR7yd2mrqdqviqLaJIK9iVgxMA63of0RnEXv2g&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the sending brand customization section on the Brands page.")
3. Select Edit Your Theme to open the brand customization view.
4. Make your logo and color changes, and select Apply Brand.

   ![The screenshot shows the sending brand customization view.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/Customize-a-Sending-Brand-window.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjUwNDEsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.22VqkCR7yd2mrqdqviqLaJIK9iVgxMA63of0RnEXv2g&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the sending brand customization view.")

Your sending brand is saved. Account users will see the
change when they log in or refresh their active browser session.
