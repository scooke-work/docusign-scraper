# Turn Off Account Discovery

Learn how to turn off account discovery so that your eSignature account is not found by
new Free or Trial users in a company’s domain.

As the account administrator, you can turn off account discovery in Security Settings by
disallowing your eSignature account to be searchable. When the setting is turned
off, no Free or Trial users in a company’s domain can view the Account Discovery
screen, and administrators will not receive any account discovery email requests to
join specific accounts from users.

Note: If you are an account administrator for multiple Docusign accounts, make sure that you switch to the appropriate
account before changing any security settings.

1. From the Accounts view, select Security
   Settings.
2. Scroll down to the Account Discovery section at the end of the page.
3. Uncheck Make my account searchable.

Your eSignature account is not searchable for account
discovery.
