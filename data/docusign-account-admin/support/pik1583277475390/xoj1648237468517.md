# Administrator Email Notification Subscriptions

Read about how administrators can subscribe to account administrator email notifications
so that they can stay up to date with account events and product news.

On the account settings Overview page, you can view all of your notifications. You can also
choose to receive these notifications by email. Subscribing to notifications keeps you
informed of the notifications and captures this information for you in an email.

You can select the types of notifications to receive by email. At any time, you can
change your selections or unsubscribe if you no longer want to receive notifications by
email.
