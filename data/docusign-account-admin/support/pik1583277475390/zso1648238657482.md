# Manage Your Subscription to Account Administrator Email Notifications

Learn how to update or discontinue your subscription to email notifications. Account
administrators can choose the types of notifications to receive by email.

You can change the types of notifications to receive or
unsubscribe from receiving notifications by email.

1. From the Accounts view, select
   Overview to view the Notifications section.
2. Next to Filters, select the Notification Settings icon.

   The Notification Preferences opens.
3. Select the checkboxes for notification types that you want to subscribe to.
4. Clear all of the checkboxes to unsubscribe from all notifications.
5. Select Save.

Your changes to your notification subscription settings are
saved.
