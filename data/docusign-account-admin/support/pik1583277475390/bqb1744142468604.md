# Manage Your IAM Plan

Learn how to edit your account subscription, add user licenses, upgrade your plan, and
change feature capacity.

As an administrator of an existing IAM account, you can manage and edit your account
subscription. You can add additional user licenses, upgrade your plan, and change
feature capacity.

1. From the Accounts view, select Plan and
   Billing.

   The Subscription and Billing page opens.![The screenshot shows the Subscription and billing page for an IAM Professional account.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pik1583277475390/page/images/IAM-Sub-and-billing-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ4OTcsInNoZWFmIjoicGlrMTU4MzI3NzQ3NTM5MCJ9.xPz7oE5KWsFvLt4_io1s0vFeB_lXcOW8jVEJMaR2ddM&_LANG=enus "The screenshot shows the Subscription and billing page for an IAM Professional account.")

   Note: The Plan and Billing page appears with the title Subscription and Billing for some account types.
2. Select Manage Your Subscription > Edit Subscription.

   The Edit your subscription page opens.
3. Edit your plan by selecting the following options:
   1. Select the Annual or
      Monthly toggle to change your billing
      cycle.
   2. If you have an IAM Standard Plan, select Upgrade
      Plan to view plan upgrade options.
   3. Select Add Capacity or edit the feature capacity
      total to include a new add-on or additional capacity to an existing
      feature.
4. Select Next.

   The Confirm your subscription change page
   opens.
5. Review your changes and select Confirm.

Your IAM plan has been updated. Changes apply on your
renewal date.
