## IAM FAQs

- ###

  Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) is a category of cloud software. While every organization depends on agreements for many of its vital day-to-day activities, the processes that manage them are often slow, manual, and error-prone, leaving critical business data locked in static, flat files. IAM helps organizations transform the way they create, commit to, and manage their agreements, helping improve efficiency, reduce risk, and unlock additional value from agreements.
- ###

  Docusign CLM is an enterprise application that helps organizations store, organize, and manage contracts, vendor information, standard clauses, and templates. It is also a core application within the Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform. IAM is a unified, cloud-based platform that connects every step of the agreement process—from creation and negotiation to signing and ongoing management—powered by one system of record, one security and compliance framework, and one AI layer in Iris. Together, CLM and IAM give your organization end-to-end visibility, streamlined workflows, and the ability to unlock real value from your agreements.
- ###

  eSignature is part of the Docusign IAM platform and included in all IAM plans.

  You can buy eSignature in a standalone [eSignature plan](https://ecom.docusign.com/plans-and-pricing/esignature) or as part of an [IAM plan](https://ecom.docusign.com/plans-and-pricing/iam).
- ###

  If you’re an existing eSign or CLM customer, nothing changes. You’ll continue to see new innovations deployed to your existing applications. Once IAM plans and applications are available in your region, you’ll have the opportunity to access new plans that include exciting new capabilities, with no migration required. During this time, you’ll still be able to purchase or renew with your current eSignature and CLM plan.

  New customers can continue to try [eSignature for free.](https://trial.docusign.com)

  All IAM plans and applications are currently available globally.
