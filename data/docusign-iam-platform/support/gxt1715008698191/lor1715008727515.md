# Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management

Read about Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM). See how it
helps organizations of all kinds create smarter agreements, commit to agreements
efficiently, and manage agreements to realize their full value.

Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) applications are
purpose-built for the needs of their target audiences and industries. Each application
comprises Docusign core capabilities and platform services.

- **IAM Core** Designed for you to customize your own solution. Use modular platform
  services, build your apps on top, or customize with an app from the Docusign App Center.
- **IAM for CX** Designed for product managers who want to improve conversion, reduce
  abandonment, and deliver a frictionless experience for customers.
- **IAM for Sales** Designed for sales teams to close deals faster by generating smart
  contracts and connecting the agreement process with CRM systems.

## Create, commit, and manage

IAM helps organization and business leaders manage their agreements flexibly and
dynamically. IAM is designed for professionals across sales, HR, legal, procurement,
product management, IT, and customer experience.

- Create agreements in a collaborative, automated, and integrated way with all
  business processes and systems.
- Commit, send, and sign securely, with identities verified quickly without days of
  waiting.
- Manage agreements and monitor how the organization and its suppliers and partners are
  meeting obligations, while mitigating risks. Answer questions like:

  - Did we get what we paid for?
  - Have we delivered on our promises?
  - Have we met our customers’ expectations?

## IAM platform services

The following IAM platform services underpin the IAM applications:

Docusign Agreement Manager
:   A smart repository that organizations use to store, manage, and analyze
    agreements from any source. Agreement Manager effectively transforms unstructured
    agreements into structured data. With Agreement Manager, you can easily find
    agreements, access vital information quickly, and gain valuable insights
    from your agreements through AI.

Docusign Workflow Builder
:   Helps you create flexible, customizable agreement workflows without writing
    any code. With Docusign Workflow Builder, you can configure custom
    workflows quickly, using Docusign capabilities like
    eSignature, ID Verification, and Data Verification with third-party apps to
    connect to your business processes.

Docusign App Center
:   An ecosystem of developers and partners, where you can discover, install,
    and connect a wide variety of apps to integrate your existing systems into
    Docusign IAM.
:   - Record customer information with HubSpot and Salesforce.
    - Trigger a new workflow with ServiceNow.
    - Extract agreement data with Quik!
    - Generate a payment subscription with Stripe.
    - Archive completed documents with Box, Dropbox, Google Drive,
      Microsoft OneDrive or SharePoint, and Prisidio.

## Extension apps

An extension app integrates third-party functionality into the agreement process by launching
requests to a third-party service or custom API from within Docusign.

The app is incorporated into a Docusign envelope or workflow at one or more
extension points, for example, in an envelope field or workflow step. When a
recipient completes the envelope field or workflow step, the extension app
functionality is triggered.

## Capabilities

Core capabilities uniquely address each step of the agreement process, with select
capabilities included in our applications to help you transform your business.

Web Forms
:   Build dynamic forms with no coding. Get agreements completed faster by
    pre-populating them with data from completed forms and from external systems
    through APIs.

Document generation
:   Create and update templates to generate custom, accurate, and
    professional-looking agreements quickly.

Identify
:   Verify signer identity seamlessly to protect important agreements and your
    customer experience.

eSignature
:   Give consumers, partners, employees, and vendors a convenient, fast signing
    experience for practically any type of agreement on almost any device.
    Custom branding and responsive signing provide more intuitive
    experiences.

CLM
:   Manage contracts more easily, faster, and with less risk.

Workspaces
:   A place to bring people, agreements, and information together for multi-step
    agreement processes.

## Example: Workflow for patient intake form at a large healthcare clinic

![Diagram that shows 6 steps that match the workflow for a patient intake form described in the following section.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gxt1715008698191/page/images/Example-IAM-Core-workflow.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjI2NjEsInNoZWFmIjoiZ3h0MTcxNTAwODY5ODE5MSJ9.7Mjam1AStBdVNcISHf9QQVJbi4pKfXYXcsF_nkWbddM&_LANG=enus "Diagram that shows 6 steps that match the workflow for a patient intake form described in the following section.")

1. **Docusign Workflow Builder** Clinic administrators initiate the intake process
   within Docusign Workflow Builder by entering the patient details.
2. **Web Forms** An email is sent to the patient with a link to complete the new patient web
   form. The patient enters personal information such as name, contact details,
   current medical conditions, and medication.
3. An envelope is created automatically with the new patient agreement documents using a **Docusign eSignature template**. The data provided by the
   patient in step 2 is merged into Docusign fields. The patient
   can review and sign the document on a mobile device.
4. An email notification is sent to the patient and clinic administrators saying registration
   is complete.
5. Workflow Builder **extension app** The agreement documents and data are sent to the organization's
   custom system of records for storage, data analysis, and automation.
6. **Agreement Manager** The agreement documents and data are saved in Docusign
   Agreement Manager so that they can be searched and analyzed, giving the organization key
   insights about its agreement process.
