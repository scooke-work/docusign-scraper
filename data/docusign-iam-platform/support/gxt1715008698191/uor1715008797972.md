# IAM Core

Read about how you can customize your own Docusign Intelligent
Agreement Management (IAM) solution through IAM Core. Use modular platform services, build
your own apps on top, or customize with an app from the Docusign App
Center.

Docusign IAM Core is an application built for organizations of any size in
almost any industry. IAM Core is a customizable solution that can be extended through
app extensions available through the Docusign App Center.

## Benefits

IAM Core offers multiple benefits that can help businesses in all industries streamline
agreement processes, while reducing risk and gaining insights into agreement.

Accelerate revenue
:   Empower customers to agree instantly, on almost any device, with secure
    signing solutions. Plus, automate customized agreement workflows across your
    essential business systems to accelerate deal closures.

Reduce risk
:   Minimize manual errors and improve data integrity with seamless two-way data
    syncing between Docusign and other applications. Maximize
    control and security with centralized user and account management.

Unlock agreement value
:   Transform unstructured agreement data into actionable insights through
    native AI data extractions, and uncover hidden opportunities within your
    agreements.

## Is IAM Core right for my business?

IAM Core supports objectives common to businesses in every industry, such as growing a retail
business, expanding a contingent workforce, and streamlining employee requests.

## What's included?

IAM Core comes in three plan levels:

Standard
:   The Standard plan offers access to eSignature features like envelopes and
    collaborative commenting, and includes Docusign Agreement Manager
    and Docusign Workflow Builder.

Professional
:   The Professional plan builds on core features with advanced communication
    and data collection capabilities.

Enterprise
:   The Enterprise plan provides advanced customization and security
    features.

All plan levels include the following capabilities and platform services:

Docusign Workflow Builder
:   Build customizable agreement workflows that extend beyond eSignature. Create
    and use workflows to automate end-to-end agreement processes without writing
    any code.

Docusign Agreement Manager
:   Store, manage, and analyze all of your agreements from a central repository.
    Improve contract intelligence by extracting contract elements with AI.

Docusign eSignature
:   Sign and end electronic documents easily and securely.

Docusign Workspaces
:   Simplify multi-step transactions by centralizing all agreements in a secure,
    collaborative hub.

## Example: Grow a retail business with new partners

IAM Core helps a retail business with multiple brands efficiently onboard retail partners. The
business uses IAM Core to cultivate relationships with those partners to expand its
business.

![Diagram that shows 5 steps that match the process for growing a retail business described in the following section.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gxt1715008698191/page/images/Grow-a-retail-business.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjI2ODMsInNoZWFmIjoiZ3h0MTcxNTAwODY5ODE5MSJ9.cxUc2GV1vFbWv94GnapijrIrXYOQf45MSlEMUIs_tbY&_LANG=enus "Diagram that shows 5 steps that match the process for growing a retail business described in the following section.")

1. The business uses **Web Forms** to gather essential data from new
   retail partners.
2. The data from Web Forms populates **Locked Templates** from which
   non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) are generated automatically.
3. **Expanded Branding** tailors NDAs for specific brands and **Workspaces**
   provides a hub for bringing the right people, documents, and forms together in
   one place.
4. When retail partners are ready to sign, **Workflow Builder** automates approval and
   signing workflows with no coding required.
5. After the NDAs are signed, **Agreement Manager** organizes the documents in a central
   repository and uses AI to extract key details for better vendor management.
