# IAM for CX

Read about how Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) for CX
streamlines your entire agreement process from a customer-centric perspective. IAM for CX
uses intelligent automation and industry-specific integrations to maximize your customer
reach and drive faster conversions.

Docusign IAM for CX streamlines the entire agreement process from a
customer-centric perspective. IAM for CX uses intelligent automation and
industry-specific integrations to maximize your customer reach and drive faster
conversions.

## Benefits

IAM for CX offers the following benefits:

Increase conversions
:   Optimize agreements for speed and security to boost conversion rates. Save
    customers time by pre-filling data, embedding forms into existing workflows,
    and tailoring steps based on customer attributes.

Minimize agreement friction
:   Empower customers to agree instantly, practically anywhere, on virtually any
    device. Reduce the risk of fraud while maximizing customer satisfaction and
    interoperability.

Serve customers better
:   Easily adapt agreement processes to business needs with no coding.

Build trust
:   Increase customer confidence that their data is protected through
    enterprise-grade security, controls, access, and organizational
    management.

Docusign Workspaces
:   Simplify multi-step transactions by centralizing all agreements in a secure,
    collaborative hub.

## Is IAM for CX suitable for my business?

Any customer-facing agreements can benefit from IAM for CX.

For example, you can boost conversion rates for self-service processes like membership
applications and account opening. By providing a seamless agreement process, IAM for
CX reduces friction that can cause customer abandonment.

IAM for CX can also save employees time when assisting customers with applications,
like event and venue contracts or services quotes. Employees can prepare agreements
more quickly through templates and document generation, giving them time to focus on
other critical tasks.

For more information, contact the Docusign Sales team through the [Connect with our sales team](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) page.

## What's included?

IAM for CX is based on the following core capabilities:

Web Forms
:   Streamline data collection and speed up signing in one interactive
    experience.

Multi-channel delivery
:   Close business faster with real-time notifications through SMS or WhatsApp.

Identity Verification
:   Use embedded in mobile-first signing to capture government-issued IDs and verify identity.
    AI-powered biometric identity checks confirm that IDs provided are genuine,
    signers are physically present, and that signers' faces match their
    IDs.

Docusign Workflow Builder
:   Build customizable agreement workflows that extend beyond eSignature. Create and use
    workflows to automate agreement processes without writing any code.

Docusign App Center
:   Access ready to install extension applications that integrate additional
    functionality.

Premium Data Verification
:   Verify complex data entered into your documents, such as information from a
    bank account and social security number, against external data sources in
    real time.

## Example: Loan application workflow

![Diagram that shows 7 steps that match the loan application workflow described in the following section.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gxt1715008698191/page/images/IAM-for-CX-example-workflow.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjI2ODcsInNoZWFmIjoiZ3h0MTcxNTAwODY5ODE5MSJ9.mGb1A32-afSQimErfZT2IJ4qlCM24duSYNhtWFV2xKQ&_LANG=enus "Diagram that shows 7 steps that match the loan application workflow described in the following section.")

1. **Workflow Builder workflow** A customer selects a button on a lender website to start
   a loan application.

   Because the lender customized its Docusign account branding, the agreement steps reflect the lender's logo and
   colors.
2. Customers are automatically redirected to the Docusign
   Identify platform to provide proof of identity using Docusign
   ID Verification.
3. A branching rule within the Workflow Builder workflow lets customers whose identity has
   been verified to move to the next step.
4. A step in the workflow extracts data from the customer IDs, including legal
   name, birth date, ID number, and expiration date. Some of this data is used to
   populate fields in later steps.
5. Customers who passed the identity verification are prompted to complete a Docusign web form with their application details. Some of the
   data collected through the identity verification process is automatically copied
   into the form.
6. **Docusign eSignature** After submitting the form, the
   customer is redirected to Docusign eSignature, to review and
   sign the application documents. The envelope is routed to the loan approval
   team. An email notification is sent to the customer and approval team to inform
   them of the approval status.
7. The agreement documents and data are sent through the Salesforce extension app
   to the lender's Salesforce instance for storage, data analysis, and additional
   automation.
