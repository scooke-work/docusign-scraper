# IAM for Sales

Read about the Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) for
Sales application and how it helps sales teams create, commit, and manage sales agreement
processes. Access links to related sources of information, including support and education.

Docusign IAM for Sales is a line of business and industry-specific
application for sales teams. IAM for Sales uses a unique set of capabilities to help
teams digitize their sales agreement process. AI extracts valuable information from
static documents, letting teams make smarter decisions and capture more value out of
deals.

## Benefits

IAM for Sales offers your sales team multiple advantages:

- Close more deals by enhancing seller productivity and removing friction
- Configure agreement workflows that automatically route to the right stakeholders
  based on agreement contents
- Generate agreements from tools you already use through seamless
  integrations
- Accelerate the signing process by reaching customers on their phones and
  tablets
- Identify opportunities to maximize revenue from existing customers and new
  prospects

## Is IAM for Sales right for my business?

IAM for Sales can help your sales, operations, or IT organization achieve the following goals,
regardless of your industry. Whether you are currently using Docusign eSignature or you are new to Docusign,
IAM for Sales can benefit your organization.

- Get a birds-eye view of your contract portfolio to answer questions like:
  - Which agreements renew this month?
  - What agreements include the clause "force majeure"?
- Automate workflows beyond the signing stage and connect Docusign with
  other applications
- Streamline the way agreements are prepared prior to signature
- Collaborate with sellers who work with Salesforce

## What's included?

IAM for Sales includes the following platform services:

Docusign Workflow Builder
:   Build customizable agreement workflows that extend beyond eSignature. Create
    and use workflows to automate agreement processes without writing any
    code.

Docusign App Center
:   Select ready to install extension applications that integrate additional
    functionality.

Docusign Admin
:   Centralize control while improving system-wide visibility and administrator
    efficiency.

Docusign Agreement Manager
:   Store, manage, and analyze your agreements from a central location. Improve
    contract intelligence by extracting contract elements with AI.

Data Verification
:   Verify signer-provided information, such as email address and phone number,
    in real time.

Docusign Monitor
:   Detect potential threats and protect agreements with around-the-clock
    activity tracking. Docusign Monitor is available as an
    extension.

Gen for Salesforce
:   Speed up the deal cycles with easily configurable agreements.
    Effortlessly merge customer, product and pricing data to generate sales
    documents.

## Example: Sales contract renewal workflow

![Diagram that shows 8 steps that match the sales contract renewal process described in the following section.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gxt1715008698191/page/images/Sales-contract-renewal-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjI2OTAsInNoZWFmIjoiZ3h0MTcxNTAwODY5ODE5MSJ9.vZI5Pa6vAHpBkDovxjqHVlxpwLKtB0r4d6_-3ULkgSg&_LANG=enus "Diagram that shows 8 steps that match the sales contract renewal process described in the following section.")

1. **Docusign Workflow Builder** An account manager enters customer details and
   launches the sales renewal agreement workflow.
2. **Docusign Web Forms** An account team is prompted to complete a Docusign web form with the customer details and contract
   renewal terms.
3. **Docusign ID Verification** An email is sent to the customer with a
   link to verify identity using Docusign ID Verification with
   Liveness.
4. **Docusign eSignature** When the customer's identity is verified, an
   envelope containing the renewal documents is automatically created using a Docusign eSignature template. The data provided by the account
   team in step 2 is merged into Docusign fields.
5. **Docusign eSignature** The customer automatically receives a
   notification with a link to review and sign the renewal contract.
6. **Docusign Workflow Builder** When the contract is signed, an email notification
   goes to the customer and account manager to inform them that the renewal is
   complete.
7. **Docusign Workflow Builder extension app** An extension application for Workflow Builder
   sends the contract documents and data to the custom system of records for
   storage, data analysis, and automation.
8. **Docusign Agreement Manager** The organization gains key insights into their
   agreement process. Agreement Manager can highlight non-standard terms, provide an
   average sales cycle, and present contracts by status.
