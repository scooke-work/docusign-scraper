# Take Action on In-Flight Envelopes From the Docusign Envelope Status Component

Learn to view or act on active envelopes using the Docusign Envelope
Status component.

A Docusign envelope is a digital container you build that contains
documents, recipients, and signature fields. When all recipients complete their assigned
fields, Docusign saves the completed contract.

Salesforce users can create and send envelopes from Salesforce records. The Docusign
 Envelope Status component displays the
status of all envelopes sent for signature for a Salesforce record.

You can also use the Docusign Envelope Status component to take
action on “in-flight” envelopes. In-flight envelopes have been sent for signature and
have not been completed or voided (canceled.)

Note: If you do not see the Docusign
 Envelope
Status component, your administrator must [add the component to the Salesforce layout](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=xkm1586134206424.html&_LANG=enus).

Status component to take action:
