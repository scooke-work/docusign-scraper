# Automatic AutoPlace Field Syntax

Here are the automatic AutoPlace fields you can assign to Salesforce roles.

You can add automatic AutoPlace fields to envelope template documents.

These fields merge Docusign fields into your envelope.

You do not need to create, configure, or modify any Docusign fields to use
automatic AutoPlace fields.

Here are the automatic AutoPlace fields you can use:

| Field Type | Signer 1 | Signer 2 | Signer 3 | Signer 4 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Signature | \s1\ | \s2\ | \s3\ | \s4\ |
| Initial | \i1\ | \i2\ | \i3\ | \i4\ |
| Optional Initial | \oi1\ | \oi2\ | \oi3\ | \oi4\ |
| Name | \n1\ | \n2\ | \n3\ | \n4\ |
| Company | \co1\ | \co2\ | \co3\ | \co4\ |
| Title | \t1\ | \t2\ | \t3\ | \t4\ |
| Date Signed | \d1\ | \d2\ | \d3\ | \d4\ |

Here are some sample signer and AutoPlace field combinations:

- **Signer 2 signature field**: Enter **\s2\** in the document.
- **Signer 3 company field**: Enter **\co3\** in the document.
- **Signer 1 date signed field**: Enter **\d1\** in the document.
