# Docusign eSignature for Salesforce User Overview

Learn about how Docusign Apps Launcher apps help you manage agreements
in your Salesforce organization.

Docusign Apps Launcher for Salesforce is a package of apps that work together
to generate and send agreements for signature.

- CLM for Salesforce: Build workflows and document generation
  configurations, store documents, and send agreements for approval or signature
- Gen for Salesforce: Select a button to generate agreements
- eSignature for Salesforce: Send completed agreements for electronic
  signature

This guide covers use of the Docusign eSignature for Salesforce app.
