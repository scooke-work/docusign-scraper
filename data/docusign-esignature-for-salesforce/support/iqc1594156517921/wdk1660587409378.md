# Send an Envelope from the Salesforce Mobile App

Learn how to send a Docusign envelope from your mobile device using
the Salesforce mobile app.

You can send a Docusign envelope from your mobile device using the
Salesforce mobile app.

Note: If you see an error message, your envelope will not send. Tell your
Salesforce administrator if either error message displays:

- At least one document is required.
- Failed to send documents. No recipient found. Contact your
  administrator for help.

Use this procedure to send a Docusign envelope from the Salesforce mobile
app:

1. Open a Salesforce record.
2. Select the Docusign eSignature custom button at the top.

   Note: The button may be hidden in the ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/iqc1594156517921/page/images/Salesforce-More-button.jpg?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTcyMzcsInNoZWFmIjoiaXFjMTU5NDE1NjUxNzkyMSJ9.klZlX0iw2_MF_DB52486CuagAg1NY9QvgF9AFLBurPY&_LANG=enus)
   More menu.
3. A sending experience displays:

   - Send now: The envelope sends without input from
     you.
   - Quick send: [Complete the quick send experience](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=iqc1594156517921&topicId=wji1611295987221.html&_LANG=enus).

You have completed the process. You have sent a Docusign envelope using the Salesforce mobile app. Docusign adds the signed document to the record when all recipients
complete the signature process.
