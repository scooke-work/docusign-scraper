# Envelope Creation Options: Documents Step

Learn about the options that you can use in the Documents step of the envelope
creation process.

As an envelope creator, you have many advanced options available to you. Each step of the
envelope creation process contains its own set of advanced options. In the
Documents step, you add documents to your envelope. These
documents form the basis of the envelopes your recipients sign. You can add
documents from Salesforce or from your device.

This guide demonstrates the advanced options that you can use in the
Documents step:

- [Upload documents from your device](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=iqc1594156517921&topicId=jme1682397601214.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to upload documents from your device to an envelope template.")
- [Rename an uploaded document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=iqc1594156517921&topicId=phb1596147423215.html&_LANG=enus "You can change the file names of uploaded documents in the envelope template by following these steps.")
- [Preview an uploaded document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=iqc1594156517921&topicId=lav1596147478944.html&_LANG=enus "You can preview uploaded documents in the envelope template by following these steps.")
- [Delete an uploaded document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=iqc1594156517921&topicId=uqf1596147449716.html&_LANG=enus "You can delete documents from an envelope template at any time.")
