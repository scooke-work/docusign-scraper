# Open Docusign Apps Launcher in Your Salesforce Organization

Learn to open the Docusign Apps Launcher for Salesforce package in
your organization.

Docusign Apps Launcher for Salesforce is a package you install from the
Salesforce AppExchange. The package contains the following applications:

- Docusign CLM for Salesforce
- Docusign CLM Essentials for Salesforce
- Docusign eSignature for Salesforce
- Docusign Gen for Salesforce
- Docusign Negotiate for Salesforce
- Docusign eSignature Apex Toolkit for Salesforce

When you install one of these apps in your organization, Docusign
installs all apps. Depending on the apps you purchase, you see a custom Docusign administrator dashboard when you open Apps Launcher.

Docusign Gen customers see administrator controls for
Gen and eSignature.

Docusign CLM customers see administrator controls for
CLM, Gen, and eSignature.

CLM Essentials customers see controls for CLM
Essentials, Gen, and eSignature.

Use this procedure to access Docusign Apps Launcher in your Salesforce
organization:
