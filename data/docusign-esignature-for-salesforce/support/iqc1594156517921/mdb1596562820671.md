# Step 1: View or Add Documents (Envelope Template Button)

One or more documents are already present in the envelope. These have been added to the
envelope template by your administrator. Use this procedure to view the documents or add
more.

Note: One or more documents may be present in
the envelope. These have been added to the envelope template by your administrator.
Use this procedure to include these documents or add more.

1. Open a Salesforce record (Account, Opportunity, and so on.)
2. Select the envelope template custom button in the upper-right of the
   page.

   The button is named by your administrator. It may be hidden in the arrow
   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/iqc1594156517921/page/images/Arrowhead.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTcyMDksInNoZWFmIjoiaXFjMTU5NDE1NjUxNzkyMSJ9.dGWQgTj4gDj6pf0488kX36hq6YKeoYI7GvZRt7olvQQ&_LANG=enus) menu.

   ![Send with envelope template button](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/iqc1594156517921/page/images/Send-with-Envelope-Template-button.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTcyMDksInNoZWFmIjoiaXFjMTU5NDE1NjUxNzkyMSJ9.dGWQgTj4gDj6pf0488kX36hq6YKeoYI7GvZRt7olvQQ&_LANG=enus "Send with envelope template button")

   The Documents page opens, displaying the documents that have been added by
   your administrator.
3. (Optional) Choose any option to upload or arrange documents for your envelope.
   You can add up to ten documents.

   - Upload: Select this button and follow the prompts
     to add more documents from your device
   - Add from Salesforce: Select this button to add
     documents from Salesforce Files
   - Drag documents to change their order in the envelope![Reorder uploaded files](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/iqc1594156517921/page/images/esigdecreorderfiles.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTcyMDksInNoZWFmIjoiaXFjMTU5NDE1NjUxNzkyMSJ9.dGWQgTj4gDj6pf0488kX36hq6YKeoYI7GvZRt7olvQQ&_LANG=enus "Reorder uploaded files")
4. Select the Next button in the upper-right of the
   page.

   You have completed step one.
