# Sign with Docusign as a Salesforce Experience Cloud User

Learn to sign a document from a record as a Salesforce Experience Cloud user.

Salesforce Experience Cloud users cannot send Docusign envelopes to recipients. However,
Experience Cloud users can use Docusign to sign documents themselves.

Use this procedure to sign a document as an Experience Cloud user:

1. Open a Salesforce Experience Cloud site and select a record.
2. Select a Docusign button. Your Salesforce administrator chooses the button
   name.
3. Select I agree to use electronic records and signatures
   and Continue.
4. Select Sign and complete the following fields:

   1. Full Name: Enter or edit your full name.
   2. Initials: Enter or edit your initials.
   3. Choose a tab to set your signature:
      - Select Style: Pick a signature style from
        a list
      - Draw: Use the cursor to draw your
        signature
      - Upload: Upload an image of your signature
        from your device
   4. Select Adopt and Sign.
5. Select Finish.

   A copy of the signed document appears in Notes &
   Attachments.

You have completed the process. You signed a document with
Docusign as a Salesforce Experience Cloud user.
