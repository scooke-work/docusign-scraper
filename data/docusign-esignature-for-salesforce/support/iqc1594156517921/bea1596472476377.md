# Step 1: Select Documents for Your Envelope

Step one of the signature process involves selecting the content that will be loaded into the Docusign eSignature for Salesforce envelope.

Step one of the Negotiate signature experience involves selecting the content that will be loaded into the Docusign eSignature for Salesforce envelope.

1. Open a Salesforce record (Account, Opportunity, etc.)
2. Select the Share button ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/iqc1594156517921/page/images/negotiateactionsarrow.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTcyNTgsInNoZWFmIjoiaXFjMTU5NDE1NjUxNzkyMSJ9.0i4cw53iAA6RP_YUhf9SwDwY4-xjiY-oZxaEH3B84fs&_LANG=enus) to the right of any file in the Docusign Files section.
3. Select Send for Signature from the drop-down menu. The Send with Docusign page opens.

   ![Send for eSignature](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/iqc1594156517921/page/images/Send-for-eSignature.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTcyNTgsInNoZWFmIjoiaXFjMTU5NDE1NjUxNzkyMSJ9.0i4cw53iAA6RP_YUhf9SwDwY4-xjiY-oZxaEH3B84fs&_LANG=enus "Send for eSignature")
4. Check the box to the left of each document to be included in the envelope. You can add up to ten documents.
5. **(Optional Steps)**: To add or rearrange content:

   - Upload: Select this button and follow the prompts to add more documents from your device
   - Add from Salesforce: Select this button to add documents from Salesforce Files
   - Drag documents to change their order in the envelope![Reorder uploaded files](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/iqc1594156517921/page/images/esigdecreorderfiles.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTcyNTgsInNoZWFmIjoiaXFjMTU5NDE1NjUxNzkyMSJ9.0i4cw53iAA6RP_YUhf9SwDwY4-xjiY-oZxaEH3B84fs&_LANG=enus "Reorder uploaded files")
6. Select the Next button in the upper-right of the page.

   The first step of the process is complete. Documents are added to the envelope. Move to [Step 2](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=dfp1596477842847.html&_LANG=enus).
