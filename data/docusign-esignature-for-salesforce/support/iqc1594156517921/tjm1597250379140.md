# Send Reminders for Active Docusign Envelopes

Learn to use the Send Reminder action for active envelopes from the Docusign Envelope Status component.

The Docusign
 Envelope Status section of a
Salesforce page displays the status of all envelopes sent for signature. The
Send Reminder feature helps you remind envelope
recipients to complete their assignments.

You cannot use the Send Reminder feature for
envelopes with a voided, declined, or completed status.

Recipients with pending tasks receive an email notifying: (sender name) sent you a
document to review and sign.

Use this procedure to send a reminder for an envelope from the Docusign
Envelope Status component:

1. Open a Salesforce record and locate the Docusign
   Envelope Status component.
