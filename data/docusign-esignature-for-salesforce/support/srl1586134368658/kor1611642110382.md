# Enable or Disable Docusign eSignature for Salesforce

Learn to enable Docusign as your eSignature app or disable it to use
another app.

You can disable the built-in Apps Launcher eSignature app and
use another app. You can also switch your eSignature app to Docusign
if another app is managing your signing experience.

1. Go to eSignature >  Settings.
2. Select an option:

   - Enable Built-In eSignature : Select
     Disabled. The button changes to
     Enabled.
   - Disable Built-In eSignature : Select
     Enabled. The button changes to
     Disabled.![A screenshot displays the eSignature Settings page with the Settings tab and Disabled switch highlighted.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/srl1586134368658/page/images/A-screenshot-displays-the-Enable-BuiltIn-eSignature-switch-set-to-Disabled.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTY2ODEsInNoZWFmIjoic3JsMTU4NjEzNDM2ODY1OCJ9.IEbnTHVRv-FxsCrSsdAWW99Npx3Dc4lkkIHh0L2GHo8&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the eSignature Settings page with the Settings tab and Disabled switch highlighted.")

You have completed the process. You have turned on or off the Docusign eSignature app. If turned on, Docusign
manages your Salesforce eSignature actions. If turned off, another app manages your
Salesforce eSignature actions.
