# Docusign eSignature for Salesforce Administrator Overview

Learn to configure, customize, and administer the Docusign eSignature
for Salesforce application.

Docusign Apps Launcher for Salesforce is a package of apps that work
together to generate and send contracts for review, approval, and signature.

Docusign eSignature for Salesforce
:   Send completed contracts for electronic signature.

Docusign CLM for Salesforce
:   Store documents, build workflows, build document generation configurations, and send for
    approval or signature.

Docusign Gen for Salesforce
:   Create a new contract with the selection of a button.

Docusign Negotiate for Salesforce
:   Send a contract for internal approval or external review.

You can use Docusign eSignature for Salesforce to streamline and automate
your Salesforce contract process. You can build templates that merge data from Salesforce
records to your contracts and send them to recipients for signature. When recipients finish
signing, Docusign uses writeback to update fields and add documents to
records. This guide covers administration of the Docusign eSignature for
Salesforce app, including the following topics:
