# Envelope Template Options: Place Fields Step

This guide demonstrates the options that you can use in the Place Fields step of the
envelope creation process.

As an envelope creator, you have many advanced options available to you. In the
Place Fields step, you add Docusign and Salesforce fields
to your envelope. Recipients use these fields to add data or signatures to your
agreement.

Fields step:
