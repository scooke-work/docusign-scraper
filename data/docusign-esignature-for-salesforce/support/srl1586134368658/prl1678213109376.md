# Rebuild Your Docusign Connection, Users, and Templates After Cloning or Refreshing a Salesforce Sandbox

Learn how to rebuild your Docusign connection, users, and envelope templates after
refreshing or cloning a Salesforce sandbox.

When you refresh or clone a Salesforce sandbox organization, Salesforce imports all settings
from your production organization. After the cloning or refreshing process, your
Docusign connection, users, and envelope templates remain linked to your Salesforce
production organization. To get your Docusign account working in your sandbox, you
must rebuild your Docusign connection, users, and envelope templates.

For more information about refreshing or cloning sandbox organizations, see these sites:

- [Refresh a Salesforce sandbox](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=sf.data_sandbox_refresh.htm&type=5)
- [Clone a Salesforce sandbox](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?language=en_US&id=sf.data_sandbox_clone.htm&type=5)

Use this procedure to rebuild envelope templates after you clone or refresh your
Salesforce sandbox organization:

1. [Disconnect the sandbox from Docusign and remove your
   Docusign users.](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=vli1610514116162.html&_LANG=enus)
2. [Connect a Docusign Demo account to your
   Salesforce organization.](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=uxq1614636863027.html&_LANG=enus)
3. [Rebuild your Docusign users.](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=dzl1586134206658.html&_LANG=enus)
4. [Clone your Docusign envelope templates from Salesforce
   production to your sandbox.](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?language=en_US&bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=uhn1664569454728.html&_LANG=enus)

You have completed the process. You have rebuilt your Docusign connection, users, and envelope templates after refreshing or cloning your Salesforce
sandbox organization.
