# The “Select an Account” Page Prevents Me From Connecting Docusign and Salesforce

Learn to fix this account connection issue.

I cannot connect Docusign to my Salesforce organization. The "Select an
Account" page returns every time. I do not see an error message.

## Resolution: Use Credentials That Are Only Connected to One Docusign Account

Cause: Your integration user's email address is connected to
more than one Docusign account.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. [Access the Docusign Setup page as a Salesforce
   administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus).
2. Select Log in to Docusign.
3. Enter Docusign administrator credentials.

   Important: Ensure that the email address of this
   administrator is only connected to one Docusign
   account.
4. Enter Salesforce administrator credentials.
5. Select an Account: Choose a Docusign account and
   select Next.
6. Authorize: Select Allow
   Access and enter Salesforce administrator
   credentials.

   A message displays: Successfully connected and authorized to
   Docusign.

   You have completed the process. You have connected your Docusign
   account to your Salesforce organization.
