# Upload a Document to an Envelope Template

Learn how to upload documents from your device to an envelope template.

You can add up to ten documents to an envelope template.

Use this procedure to upload documents to an envelope template from your device:

1. Select the Upload button.
2. Follow the prompts to add a document from your device. You can add up to ten
   documents.

   Note: You can only upload one document at a time.
3. Select Save and Close.

   You have completed the process. You have deleted the uploaded
   document.
