# Envelope Template Options: Documents Step

This guide demonstrates the options that you can use in the Documents step of the
envelope template creation process. This includes: previewing an uploaded document, renaming
an uploaded document, and deleting an uploaded document.

In the Documents step of the envelope template creation process, you add
documents to your envelope template. These documents form the basis of the envelopes
your recipients sign. You can add documents from Salesforce or from your device.

This guide demonstrates the options that you can use in the
Documents step of the envelope template creation process.
The following options are available:

[Access the Documents Step of an Envelope Template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=qpa1620149203357.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to access the Documents step to add, edit, or delete envelope template files.")
:   Open the Documents step of an envelope template to change options

[Upload documents from your device](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=jme1682397601214.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to upload documents from your device to an envelope template.")
:   Add documents to an envelope template from your device

[Attach documents from Salesforce records](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=jng1682398197678.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to add Salesforce documents to an envelope template on the Documents page.")
:   Dynamically attach documents from Salesforce Files to your envelope
    template.

[Preview an uploaded document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=lav1596147478944.html&_LANG=enus "You can preview uploaded documents in the envelope template by following these steps.")
:   Preview what recipients see when they open your envelope.

[Rename an uploaded document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=phb1596147423215.html&_LANG=enus "You can change the file names of uploaded documents in the envelope template by following these steps.")
:   Change the file name of an uploaded document.

[Delete an uploaded document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=uqf1596147449716.html&_LANG=enus "You can delete documents from an envelope template at any time.")
