# Docusign CLM and Docusign eSignature for Salesforce Compatibility

Learn how you can use Docusign Gen and eSignature for Salesforce with your Docusign CLM
account.

The Docusign Apps Launcher package of apps contains Docusign CLM, Docusign Gen, Docusign Negotiate, and Docusign eSignature for Salesforce.

In the past, Docusign CLM users did not have access to the Docusign Gen and eSignature
for Salesforce apps in the CLM interface. This has changed.

**Why is eSignature for Salesforce available in CLM?**

CLM users can already send envelopes for electronic signature with the standard Docusign eSignature platform. The eSignature for Salesforce app opens up Salesforce-specific
signature functions that are unavailable to standard Docusign users.

**When should I use eSignature for Salesforce?**

- When you want to use Docusign Envelope Templates to deliver pre-built eSignature
  experiences
- When you want to add eSignature capability for Salesforce Experience Cloud
  users
- When you want to add eSignature custom buttons to Salesforce layouts
- When you do not want generated documents saved in CLM folders

**When should I use standard Docusign eSignature?**

- When sending for eSignature in a standard (non-Salesforce) CLM account
- When sending a file for eSignature from the CLM Files section of a Salesforce
  record

**What can Docusign CLM users do in eSignature for Salesforce?**

- Use the eSignature Configuration page on the Docusign Setup tab
- Create, add, edit, and delete eSignature for Salesforce Envelope Templates
- Enable a System Sender, which allows users who are not members of the Docusign account to send and sign envelopes
