# Step 8: Remove the Person Account Custom Button from the Accounts Layout

The eighth step of the process involves removing the custom button from the Salesforce
Account layout.

Use this procedure to remove the custom button from the Salesforce
Account layout.

1. Select the arrow ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/srl1586134368658/page/images/Arrowhead.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTY5MTIsInNoZWFmIjoic3JsMTU4NjEzNDM2ODY1OCJ9.RT1FJVBXF1mk0yHJtPBWrIAoseVr6byWqU1EXlYKgfM&_LANG=enus) next to the
   Person Account custom button that you built.
2. Select Delete from the dropdown menu.

   The are you sure? dialog box opens.
3. Select Delete from the dropdown menu.

   The are you sure? dialog box closes. The page
   refreshes.

You have completed step eight. Move to step nine to add the custom
button from the Person Account layout.
