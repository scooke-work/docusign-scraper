# Change the Default Language for Gen, Negotiate, eSignature for Salesforce, and CLM

Learn to use Translation Workbench to let users choose their default Salesforce
language.

You can use Translation Workbench to let your users choose their default
Salesforce language. Docusign app content displays in the selected
language.

You can use the following options to enable language support in your Salesforce
organization:

- [Enable Translations for Your Salesforce Organization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=ehh1586134268380.html&_LANG=enus)
- [Enable Translations for Your Salesforce User](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=yzs1586134269459.html&_LANG=enus)
