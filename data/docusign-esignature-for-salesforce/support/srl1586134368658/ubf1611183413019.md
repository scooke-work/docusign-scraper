# Customize the Email Subject and Message Seen by Recipients

You can customize the email subject and message to recipients by editing the envelope
template Recipients step.

Envelope templates have a pre-built email subject and
message for all recipients. You can customize the email subject and message to
recipients by editing the envelope template Recipients step.

Use this procedure to
change a message to recipients in an existing envelope template:

1. Customize any field:
   - Email Subject: Enter up to 100 characters of text
   - Email Message: Enter up to 2,000 characters of text
2. Select the Save and Close button at the top-right of the
   page.

You have completed the process. You have customized the recipient
message.
