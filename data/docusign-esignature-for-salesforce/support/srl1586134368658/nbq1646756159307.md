# Use the Docusign Envelopes Tab to View the Details of an Envelope

Learn how to view the details of envelopes sent from your Salesforce
records.

Docusign envelopes are bundles of information sent to recipients for
electronic signature. These include documents, recipients, and Docusign and Salesforce fields. You can use the Docusign
Envelopes page to view the details of envelopes sent from Salesforce
records.

Envelope entries include these details:

- Docusign Envelope name
- Email Subject
- Owner
- Salesforce Source ID
- Email Message
- Docusign envelope ID
- Sent status
- Remind signers via email
- Send reminder in N day(s)
- Remind every N day(s) until complete
- Expire/void Envelope
- Expire/void envelope in N day(s)
- Warn of expiration in N day(s) prior
- Created By
- Last Modified By

Use this procedure to view the details of a Docusign envelope in your
Salesforce organization:

1. Select the Docusign Envelopes tab.

   The Docusign Envelope page opens.
2. Select an envelope from the Docusign Envelope
   column.

   The Docusign Envelope details page opens for that
   item.

You have completed the task. You have viewed the details of
an envelope sent from a Salesforce record.
