# "This Page Has an Error. You Might Just Need to Refresh It" Displays When I Open Docusign for Salesforce Apps

Learn to fix the "This page has an error. You might just need to refresh it." error that
displays.

When I open Docusign apps in Salesforce, an error displays. The
error message reads: This page has an error. You might just need to
refresh it. Error in $A.getCallback() [TypeError: d is not a function] Failing
descriptor: {ltng:outApp}. I cannot open Docusign
apps in Salesforce.

## Resolution: Upgrade your version of Docusign Apps Launcher

Cause: You have Docusign Apps Launcher
version 5.4 or lower installed in your organization.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Check your Docusign Apps Launcher version number:
   1. Select the gear ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/srl1586134368658/page/images/salesforcegear.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg3NzYsInNoZWFmIjoic3JsMTU4NjEzNDM2ODY1OCJ9.N3J8IWSCinFkV93IWnLtwyq3REOqpNfiFWV8QeJ7x8Y&_LANG=enus)
      and choose Setup.
   2. Enter and select Installed Packages in
      the Quick Find field.
   3. Check your Version Number.
2. If your version number is 5.4 or lower, [upgrade Apps Launcher in your
   Salesforce organization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=nok1698959626724.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to upgrade the Docusign Apps Launcher package of apps in your Salesforce organization.").
3. Select Save.

   You have completed the process. You have upgraded the Apps
   Launcher package in your Salesforce organization. Docusign apps and services load normally.
