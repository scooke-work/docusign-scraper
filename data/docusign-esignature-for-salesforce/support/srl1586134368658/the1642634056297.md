# Step 4: Uninstall the Legacy Docusign eSignature for Salesforce App from your Salesforce organization

Learn how to uninstall the legacy Docusign eSignature for Salesforce app from your
Salesforce organization.

You have removed all legacy Docusign eSignature for Salesforce dependencies. You can now
uninstall the Docusign eSignature for Salesforce app from your Salesforce organization
using this procedure:

1. Open the Installed Packages page as an
   administrator:

   1. Select the gear ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/srl1586134368658/page/images/salesforcegear.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg4MjEsInNoZWFmIjoic3JsMTU4NjEzNDM2ODY1OCJ9.vVmoVM2biXM64w9ElEzT0kashGeV2s8xCYPkJUKUq6M&_LANG=enus) in the upper-right of the
      page.
   2. Select Setup.
   3. Enter Installed Packages in the Quick Find
      field.

   The Installed Packages page opens.
2. Select the Uninstall link next to the
   Docusign eSignature for Salesforce package
   name.

   The Uninstalling a Package page opens.
3. Scroll to the bottom of the page and select the Yes, I want to
   uninstall this package and permanently delete all associated
   components check box.
4. Select the Uninstall button.

   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/srl1586134368658/page/images/daluninstall.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg4MjEsInNoZWFmIjoic3JsMTU4NjEzNDM2ODY1OCJ9.vVmoVM2biXM64w9ElEzT0kashGeV2s8xCYPkJUKUq6M&_LANG=enus)

   Note: If you have
   remaining dependencies from the legacy eSignature for Salesforce app, an
   Unable to uninstall package message opens.
   Remove the remaining dependencies listed, and then repeat the
   instructions to uninstall the app.

You have completed the entire process. You have
removed all legacy Docusign eSignature for Salesforce dependencies, and have uninstalled
the app from your Salesforce organization. Your migration from the legacy app to the
current eSignature for Salesforce app is complete.
