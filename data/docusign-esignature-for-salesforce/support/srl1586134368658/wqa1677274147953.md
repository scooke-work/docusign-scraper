# A Spinning Wheel Icon Displays When I Try to Open Docusign Applications

Learn to fix the issue of a spinning wheel icon displaying when you try to open Docusign applications.

A spinning wheel icon displays when I try to open Docusign applications. Docusign apps and features never load.

## Resolution: Upgrade your version of Docusign Apps Launcher

Cause: You have Docusign Apps Launcher
version 5.4 or lower installed in your organization.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Check your Docusign Apps Launcher version number:
   1. Select the gear ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/srl1586134368658/page/images/salesforcegear.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg3NjAsInNoZWFmIjoic3JsMTU4NjEzNDM2ODY1OCJ9.AFfMjPsx_HW31eqokPk2XJLj80G07a7MmCTbq2Q2V1E&_LANG=enus) and choose
      Setup.
   2. Enter and select Installed Packages in
      the Quick Find field.
   3. Check your Version Number.
2. If your version number is 5.4 or lower, [upgrade Apps Launcher in your Salesforce organization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=nok1698959626724.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to upgrade the Docusign Apps Launcher package of apps in your Salesforce organization.").
3. Select Save.

   You have completed the process. You have upgraded the Apps Launcher package in your Salesforce organization. Docusign
   apps and services load normally.
