# Use the Disconnect Link to Disconnect and Reconnect Docusign and Salesforce

Use this procedure to disconnect and reconnect your Docusign account
from your Salesforce organization.

Occasionally, you must disconnect and reconnect your Docusign
connection. The following are reasons to disconnect and reconnect:

- You want to connect your Salesforce organization to a different Docusign account
- An administrator deleted your Docusign integration user
- [Salesforce applied enhanced domains to your organization](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=000393816&type=1)

Warning:
Docusign recommends that you
[disconnect your Docusign account
and preserve user settings with the Apps Launcher TroubleShooting tool](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=oks1610512362945.html&_LANG=enus). This option preserves your Docusign users and settings. After reconnecting, all Docusign users and permissions remain.

If you want to disconnect and reconnect without preserving user settings, you can
use the Disconnect link on the Docusign Setup page.
After reconnecting, you must rebuild all Docusign users and settings.

Use this procedure to disconnect and reconnect without preserving users and settings:

1. As a Salesforce administrator, [open Docusign Apps Launcher](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus).
2. Select the Docusign Setup tab.
3. Select Disconnect. ![A screenshot displays the Disconnect link highlighted on the Docusign Setup page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/A-screenshot-displays-the-Disconnect-link-highlighted-on-the-Docusign-Setup-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc2MzMsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.m4XJXWGfhfl3bnT0lyQVRhUWRHBjfdwGZ5OwPo9ebiI&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the Disconnect link highlighted on the Docusign Setup page.")

   The Disconnect Account and Remove Users dialog box displays.
4. Select Disconnect.

   Warning: Disconnecting Docusign from
   Salesforce disables Docusign for all Salesforce users. Disconnecting
   also deletes all Docusign user configurations. When you reconnect, you
   must rebuild all users and settings.

   The page refreshes. The Connect Docusign to Salesforce page
   displays.
5. [Reconnect Docusign and
   Salesforce](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=zfp1586134208988.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to connect your Salesforce organization to your Docusign account.").

You have completed the process. You have updated the
connection between your Salesforce organization and your Docusign account.
