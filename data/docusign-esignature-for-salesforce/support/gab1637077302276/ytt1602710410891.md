# Refresh or Reconnect a Salesforce Organization With Docusign CLM Federated Security

Learn about best practices for refreshing a Salesforce organization with Federated
Security enabled.

Salesforce organizations using CLM Federated Security should avoid taking these actions:

- Refreshing their organization
- Connecting their CLM account to a different Salesforce organization

If a Salesforce organization must be refreshed or re-connected, follow these steps
first (in order):

1. [Turn off Federated Security in your CLM
   account.](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=vbn1599207323944.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to disable the Federated Security feature in your Salesforce organization.")
2. Open the CLM **Other Sources** folder and rename the Salesforce folder:
   **Salesforce\_Old**.
3. Create a new **Salesforce** folder under Other Sources parent folder.

   ![Rename Salesforce folder.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/Rename-Salesforce-folder.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc4OTAsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.OnOlsgUBFgJtLKvh6rhOdmcKJoyeYCpEB7yq2b4JmUg&_LANG=enus "Rename Salesforce folder.")
4. [Perform a refresh of your Salesforce
   Sandbox](https://help.salesforce.com/articleView?id=data_sandbox_refresh.htm&type=5).
5. Add records to your Sandbox.
6. [Turn on CLM Federated Security](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=gqm1599194045432.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to enable standard Federated Security in your Docusign CLM account.").
7. Test your updates to ensure that all is working properly.

The process is complete. The Sandbox is refreshed and
Federated Security is properly applied to the new content. All archived content is found
in the **Salesforece\_Old** folder.
