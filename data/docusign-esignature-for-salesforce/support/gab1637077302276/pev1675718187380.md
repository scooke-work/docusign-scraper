# View the Documents of an Envelope on the Docusign Status Page

Learn how to view the documents of an envelope by accessing the Docusign Status page.

A Docusign envelope is a digital container you build that holds documents,
recipients, and signature fields. Recipients sign the envelope by completing the
fields assigned to them.

Salesforce users can create and send envelopes from Salesforce records. The
Docusign Status tab lets you view the status of your
Docusign envelopes.

Docusign Status details include: Docusign envelope ID, Sender
Name, Sender Email, Email
Subject, and Related Objects.

You can also view the documents of an envelope sent from a Salesforce record on the Docusign
Status page.

Use this procedure to view the documents of a sent envelope:

1. Select the Docusign Status tab.
2. Select an envelope from the Envelope # column.
3. Select View Envelope in the upper right.

   The envelope documents open in a preview window.

You have completed the task. You have viewed the documents
of an envelope sent from a Salesforce record.
