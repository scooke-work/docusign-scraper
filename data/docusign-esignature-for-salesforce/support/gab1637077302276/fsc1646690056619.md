# Access the Docusign Gen Templates Tab to Build and Edit Gen Templates

Learn to access the Docusign Gen Templates tab in
Salesforce.

Gen templates allow users to select a button on a record to instantly generate a contract. Use
this procedure to access the Docusign Gen Templates tab in
your Salesforce organization:

1. Select the Docusign Gen Templates tab.
2. Make any selection:

   - [New](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=zgw1621457735347.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to build Docusign Gen templates to automate your contract generation process."): Create a new Gen template.
   - [Change Owner](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=sut1646711991124.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to change the owner of a Gen template to a different Salesforce user."): Change the owner of a Gen template.
   - [Import](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=juw1586134252760.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about importing custom objects to your Salesforce organization."): Import up to 50,000 records at a time.
   - [Rename](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=gww1586134337518.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to change the name of a Gen template."): Change the name of a Gen template.
   - [Edit](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=xcs1646713533209.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to edit a Gen template."): Add or edit documents, Salesforce fields, and Docusign fields in a Gen template.
   - [Delete](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=mbd1586134342525.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to delete a Gen template."): Delete a Gen template.

   You have completed the task. You accessed the Docusign Gen
   Templates tab. From this page, you can create and edit Gen
   Templates.
