# Use Sync Domains to Rebuild Your Gen or eSignature Integration After a Salesforce Domain Name Change

Learn to fix domain integration issues using the Sync Domains feature.

## Before you begin

[Install Docusign Apps Launcher for Salesforce
v5.2](https://login.salesforce.com/packaging/installPackage.apexp?p0=04t3u000002wLcp)

You can change your Salesforce domain name to update your brand, adopt [Enhanced Domains](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=sf.domain_name_enhanced.htm&type=5), or enable partitioned domains. When
your My Domain name changes, your Salesforce-hosted URLs also change. You must
rebuild the connection to your Docusign account.

The Sync Domains feature of the Docusign
TroubleShooting tool rebuilds your connection to Docusign after a
domain change.

Use this procedure to rebuild your Gen, Negotiate,
eSignature, or eSignature Apex Toolkit
integration with the Sync Domains feature:

1. Access the Visualforce Pages page as a Salesforce
   administrator:
   1. Select the gear ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/salesforcegear.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc4MDAsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.Yv5UAZNj5Q1gC-e0oNwVZWcbaEgGHKAX1M5sSrOUSRw&_LANG=enus) and choose
      Setup.
   2. Enter and select Visualforce Pages in the Quick
      Find field.
2. Select TroubleShooting.![A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link on the Visualforce Pages screen.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/TroubleShooting-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc4MDAsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.Yv5UAZNj5Q1gC-e0oNwVZWcbaEgGHKAX1M5sSrOUSRw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link on the Visualforce Pages screen.")
3. Select Preview.

   The TroubleShooting page opens.
4. Sync Domains: Select Execute.

   The page refreshes. A message displays: Successfully synced domains on
   Docusign account.

You have completed the process. You have rebuilt the
Gen, Negotiate,
eSignature, or eSignature Apex Toolkit
integration with your Salesforce organization.
