# Use the Docusign Setup Page to Administer Your Docusign Gen Account

Learn how to use the Docusign Setup page to manage Gen and eSignature
users, components, templates, and more.

Docusign Setup is the main landing page for managing Gen and
eSignature for Salesforce users, components, templates, and more.

You access this page by [opening the Docusign Apps Launcher package in your Salesforce
organization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to open the Docusign Apps Launcher for Salesforce package in your organization.").

The Docusign Setup page displays the following resources:

- Home: Manage and configure the Gen and eSignature for
  Salesforce apps.
- [eSignature](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=xsw1586134361444.html&_LANG=enus): Configure your eSignature for Salesforce
  templates, buttons, and settings.
- [Document Generation](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?language=en_US&bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=zgw1621457735347.html&_LANG=enus): Create, edit, and delete Docusign Gen templates from this page.
- [User Management](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=zbp1629859390541.html&_LANG=enus): Add and manage Docusign Gen and eSignature users.
- [Components](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ysk1643667858834&topicId=xkm1586134206424.html&_LANG=enus): Customize layouts and experiences by adding
  custom components.
- Help: Learn how to get the most from Docusign
  Apps Launcher:
  - [Support Center](https://support.docusign.com/s/?language=en_US): Customers learn to use and
    troubleshoot Docusign products.
  - [Community](https://support.docusign.com/forum): Docusign customers
    ask and answer questions.
  - [Docusign University](https://dsucustomers.docusign.com/page/all-courses): Docusign customers build expertise.
