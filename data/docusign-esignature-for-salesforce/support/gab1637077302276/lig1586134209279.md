# Create a Gen Template with Microsoft Word

Learn to build a Gen template using a Microsoft Word document.

You can build Docusign Gen templates to automate your contract
generation process. Gen templates create agreements by merging Docusign and Salesforce fields to a Word document that you upload. When users select the Gen
button, Docusign generates an agreement based on the record data.

Important: To create a template, [grant Generator status to your Docusign user](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=doi1628107496987.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to edit the permissions of Gen, CLM, Negotiate, or eSignature for Salesforce users.").
