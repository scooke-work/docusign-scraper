# Enable Translations for Your Salesforce User

Learn how to change the display language of all Docusign Apps Launcher
apps.

You can change the display language of all Docusign Apps Launcher apps by changing the
language settings for your Salesforce user.

1. Access the Salesforce Language & Time Zone settings:
   1. Select the Salesforce user icon ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/salesforceusericon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc3NTEsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.THdM4E3bZfljM0CBLsARqW7a9nATMfFFiYq3gw2VVmI&_LANG=enus) and choose
      Settings.
   2. Select Language & Time Zone.
2. Language: Select a language from the dropdown menu.
3. Select Save.

   A message displays: Your settings have been successfully
   saved.
4. Empty your browser cache and restart the browser.

You have completed the process. Docusign Apps Launcher content displays in the language that you
chose.
