# Disconnect Your Docusign Account and Preserve User Settings with the Apps Launcher TroubleShooting Tool

Learn how to disconnect Docusign from Salesforce while preserving user
settings with the Docusign TroubleShooting tool.

Occasionally you must disconnect and reconnect your Docusign connection. The following are reasons to disconnect and
reconnect:

- You want to connect your Salesforce organization to a different Docusign account
- An administrator deleted your Docusign integration
  user
- Salesforce applied [enhanced domains](https://help.salesforce.com/s/articleView?id=000393816&type=1) to your organization

You can use the Docusign Apps Launcher TroubleShooting tool to reset your
integration safely.

Follow this procedure to use the Disconnect Account and Preserve User
Settings feature:

1. Access the Visualforce Pages page as a Salesforce
   administrator:
   1. Select the gear ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/salesforcegear.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.WJpVheNi3fqfRupVgp0m0gEK3m3R2YB0Ij0ZvBofxrU&_LANG=enus) and choose
      Setup.
   2. Enter and select Visualforce Pages in the Quick
      Find field.
2. Select TroubleShooting.![A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link on the Visualforce Pages screen.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/TroubleShooting-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.WJpVheNi3fqfRupVgp0m0gEK3m3R2YB0Ij0ZvBofxrU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link on the Visualforce Pages screen.")
3. Select Preview.

   The TroubleShooting page opens.
4. Disconnect Account and Preserve User Settings: Select
   Execute.

   A message opens: Successfully logged out from Docusign account. The
   Troubleshooting page displays the message: No Docusign Account
   connected.

You have completed the process. You have disconnected your Docusign account from your Salesforce organization. You can [re-connect Salesforce and Docusign](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?language=en_US&bundleId=ysk1643667858834&topicId=zfp1586134208988.html&_LANG=enus) or
connect to a different Docusign account.
