# Change the Owner of a Gen Template

Learn to change the owner of a Gen template to a different Salesforce user.

You can change the owner of a Gen template to a different user in your Salesforce
organization.

Use this procedure to change Gen template ownership:

1. Select Docusign Gen Templates.
2. Select the arrow ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/Arrowhead.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc4NTIsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.Zb0uYAbkY9qKrSd51Im2HWKuWQqpB2OP7iiyoSxB5So&_LANG=enus) next to a Gen template and choose Change
   Owner from the dropdown menu.
3. Add a new owner from the Change Owner dialog box:
   1. Search Users: Enter the name of a Salesforce
      user.
   2. (Optional) Send notification email: Select this
      check box to notify the user that they are the new owner of the Gen
      template.
   3. Select Submit.

   A message displays: (New Salesforce user) now owns the record
   for (Gen template name).

You have completed the process. You have given ownership of
the Gen template to a new Salesforce user.
