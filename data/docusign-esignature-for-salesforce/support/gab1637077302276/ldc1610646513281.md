# Create a Gen for Billing Template to Generate Invoices

Learn to create Gen for Billing templates to generate invoices from a Salesforce record
automatically or from a custom button.

You can configure Gen for Billing templates to generate invoices from a Salesforce record
automatically or from a custom button.

Important:

- You must have a [Salesforce Billing account](https://www.salesforce.com/products/sales-cloud/tools/cpq-software/) to create Gen for Billing
  templates.
- You must use the Invoice object when creating Gen for Billing
  templates.
- You can only create a Gen for Billing template with Microsoft Word. You cannot use the
  Docusign Agreement Cloud Editor.
- You can only create one automated template at a time.

Use this procedure to build a Gen for Billing template:

[Step 1: Add a Name, Data Source, and Template Editor](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=vgg1614494891133.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to set the main template details: template name, description, and Salesforce data source (Invoice.)")

[Step 2: Create Docusign and Salesforce Anchor Text Fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=irc1586134297835.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to create Docusign and Salesforce anchor text fields for your Gen template.")

[Step 3: Paste Anchor Text Fields Into Your Word Document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=ffk1586134297621.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to paste your Salesforce anchor text fields into a Microsoft Word document.")

[Step 4: Upload the Word Document to Your Gen Template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=gxl1586134297397.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to upload your Word template to use in your Docusign Gen template.")

[Step 5: Preview Your Template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gab1637077302276&topicId=fwi1586134297224.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to view a preview of what a generated document will look like.")
