# Disconnect Docusign and Salesforce and Remove Users with the Apps Launcher TroubleShooting Tool

Learn how to disconnect a Docusign account and remove users with the
Docusign TroubleShooting tool.

If you want to connect your Docusign account to a different
Salesforce organization, use Disconnect Account and Remove
Users. Docusign removes your connection to
Salesforce and deletes all Docusign for Salesforce users.

Important:
Disconnect Account and Remove
Users does not remove users from your connected Docusign account. This feature performs the following actions:

- Disconnects your Docusign account from your Salesforce
  organization
- Removes Docusign access from all Salesforce users
- Resets custom user settings
- Removes all permission set assignments
- Resets the integration user token stored in the connected CLM account

Follow this procedure to use the Disconnect Account and Remove
Users feature:

1. Access the Visualforce Pages page as a Salesforce
   administrator:

   2. Enter and select Visualforce Pages in the Quick
      Find field.
2. Select TroubleShooting.![A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link on the Visualforce Pages screen.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/TroubleShooting-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc3ODMsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.wp6k3VbtCkpZ99dK1PqMQcqwO0oV22xu4_dCGa6O0Vk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link on the Visualforce Pages screen.")
3. Select Preview.

   The TroubleShooting page opens.
4. Disconnect Account and Remove Users: Select
   Execute.

   A message opens: Successfully logged out from Docusign account. User custom
   settings and Docusign Apps Launcher Permission set assignments should be
   cleared shortly. The Troubleshooting page displays the message:
   No Docusign Account Connected.

You have completed the process. You have disconnected your Docusign account from your Salesforce organization. You can [connect Salesforce to a different Docusign
account](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?language=en_US&bundleId=ysk1643667858834&topicId=zfp1586134208988.html&_LANG=enus).
