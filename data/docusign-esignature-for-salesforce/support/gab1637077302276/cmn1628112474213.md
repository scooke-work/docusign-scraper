# Remove and Close Docusign Users

Learn to remove access and close Docusign Gen, CLM, Negotiate, and eSignature
users.

You can use the Remove and Close command to revoke a Salesforce user's
access to Docusign.

- Remove and Close removes a Salesforce user's access to
  Docusign apps and the connected Docusign account. This command does not delete
  Salesforce users.
- You can not remove and close your user while logged on.
- You can not remove and close the only eSignature administrator user.

Use this procedure to remove Gen, CLM, Negotiate, and eSignature access and close Docusign users in the connected account:

1. Select the check box next to each user you want to remove and close.
2. Select Remove and Close from the Add
   Users
   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/CLM-Files-Lightning-action-arrow.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc3MTIsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.17iKxAvDLAHEa1o7ULxZZuBEWwywZ_0uXPGyBAV2xsY&_LANG=enus) drop-down menu.
3. Select Remove.

You have completed the process. You removed a Salesforce user's
access to the Docusign Gen, CLM, Negotiate, and eSignature for Salesforce apps. You also
closed (removed) the user from the connected Docusign account. The user remains in your
Salesforce organization.
