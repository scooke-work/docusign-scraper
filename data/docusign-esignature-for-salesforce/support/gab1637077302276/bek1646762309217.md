# Use the Docusign Status Tab to View the Status of an Envelope

Learn how to use the Docusign Status tab to view the status of your Docusign
envelopes.

Docusign envelopes are bundles of information sent to recipients for
electronic signature. Envelopes include documents, recipients, and Docusign and Salesforce fields.

The Docusign Status tab lets you view the status of your Docusign envelopes. Envelope status entries include these
details:

- Envelope #
- Docusign envelope ID
- Sender Name
- Sender Email
- Email Subject
- Status
- Owner
- Sent
- Expires
- Completed
- Declined or Voided Reason
- Time to Complete
- Created By
- Last Modified By
- Related Objects

Use this procedure to view the status details of a Docusign envelope in your
Salesforce organization:

1. Select the Docusign Status tab.
2. Select an envelope from the Envelope # column.

   The Docusign Envelope details page opens for that
   item.

You have completed the task. You viewed the status details of an
envelope sent from a Salesforce record.
