# Delete a Gen Template

Learn to delete a Gen template.

When you delete a Gen template, remove that
template's buttons from your Salesforce layouts. Use this procedure to delete a Gen
template:

1. Select Docusign Gen Templates.
2. Select the arrow ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/Arrowhead.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc4NjcsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.wlb4aKWAGDnNA39PnnHI1Pc4ZCbHnLEbXHGGNz2f3e0&_LANG=enus) next to a Gen template and choose Delete
   from the dropdown menu.

   A dialog box displays: Are you sure you want to delete this Docusign
   Gen Template?
3. Select Delete.

   A dialog box displays: Docusign Gen Template was
   deleted.

You have completed the task. You have deleted a Gen
template. Remove this template's buttons from your Salesforce layouts.
