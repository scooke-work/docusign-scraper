# Access the Apps Launcher TroubleShooting Tool

Learn how to open the Docusign Apps Launcher TroubleShooting tool in your Salesforce
organization.

## Before you begin

You need Salesforce administrator and Docusign administrator
permissions.

Administrators can use the Docusign Apps Launcher TroubleShooting tool to fix issues with Docusign apps. The TroubleShooting tool is available in Docusign Apps Launcher version 2.5 and higher. Salesforce
administrators with Docusign administrator permissions can use it.

Use this procedure to open the Docusign Apps Launcher
TroubleShooting tool:

1. Access the Visualforce Pages page as a Salesforce
   administrator:
   1.       Setup.
   2. Enter and select Visualforce Pages in the Quick
      Find field.
2. Select TroubleShooting in the Label
   column.![A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link highlighted on the Salesforce Visualforce Pages screen.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/gab1637077302276/page/images/oas1598330401772.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTc3NzIsInNoZWFmIjoiZ2FiMTYzNzA3NzMwMjI3NiJ9.mrLETb8CrHNS3Wxo4R2GEThAJpp3aNrCLn6lwitffZE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link highlighted on the Salesforce Visualforce Pages screen.")
3. Select Preview.

You have completed the process. You have opened the Docusign Apps
Launcher TroubleShooting tool. You can use this tool to execute account-wide changes.
