# Generate an Invoice with Gen for Billing

You can select a Docusign Gen for Billing custom button on a Salesforce record to
generate an invoice. The generated invoice is automatically saved to the Notes and
Attachments section of the Salesforce record.

## Before you begin

Your administrator must grant Docusign
Generator permissions to your Docusign user.

You can select a Docusign Gen for Billing custom button on a Salesforce record to generate an
invoice. The generated invoice is automatically saved to the Notes and Attachments
section of the Salesforce record.

1. Find a Gen for Billing custom button:
   1. Log into Salesforce as a user with Docusign Gen
      Generator permissions.
   2. Open a Salesforce Invoice record.
   3. Select a Gen for Billing button on the page layout.

      Note: The button may be hidden under the arrow
      menu.![A screenshot displays an arrow menu opening to a button labeled Fresh Software Sales Contract.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/bpg1586134377111/page/images/Select-Gen-custom-button.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTYwNzQsInNoZWFmIjoiYnBnMTU4NjEzNDM3NzExMSJ9.FoEXe45jGBvBxonjFOpNb-AIM8JBmVGKOHPXsExxqaw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays an arrow menu opening to a button labeled Fresh Software Sales Contract.")

      The New Documents Generated and Saved page opens.
2. Optional Steps: Choose any optional steps:

   - Generated Documents: Uncheck the boxes of any
     documents that you don't want to send.
   - Preview: Select this link to see a preview of the
     generated document.
   - Download: Save a copy of the contract to your
     device.
   - **[Send for Signature](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=tco1611350907305.html&_LANG=enus "You can send documents for eSignature using the built-in Docusign for Salesforce app in the Docusign Apps Launcher package.")**:
     Start the eSignature process.
   - **[Deliver By Email](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=iyu1615180590827.html&_LANG=enus "You can generate an invoice from a custom button and email it to recipients.")**: Send
     an email with these attachments to up to 5 recipients.
   - Done: Take no further action and close the
     page.

You have completed the process. You have generated a new
invoice and taken optional steps. The generated invoice displays in the record's Notes
and Attachments section.
