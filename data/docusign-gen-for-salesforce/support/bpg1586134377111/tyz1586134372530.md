# Download a Document from Docusign Files

Learn how to download documents from the Docusign Files section of a
Salesforce record.

Use this procedure to download documents from the Docusign Files
section of a Salesforce record.

Select Download from the dropdown menu.

A banner opens: Successfully downloaded (file name).

You have completed the process. You have downloaded a
copy of the document from Docusign Files to your device.
