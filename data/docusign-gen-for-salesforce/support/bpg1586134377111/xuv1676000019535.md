# Generate a Document from the Salesforce Mobile App

Learn to use the Salesforce mobile app to generate a document from a Salesforce record.

## Before you begin

Your administrator must grant Docusign
Generator permissions to your Docusign user.

Docusign Gen for Salesforce lets you
generate documents from Salesforce records. Gen creates content by merging Docusign and Salesforce fields into uploaded documents.

You can
select a Gen custom button on a record and instantly generate contracts, invoices,
and more.

You can send this content for Docusign review or
signature.

You can use the Salesforce mobile app to
generate a document from a Salesforce record using Docusign Gen.

Use this procedure to send a Docusign envelope from the
Salesforce mobile app:

1. Open a Salesforce record.
2. Select a Docusign Gen custom button. The button may be hidden in the ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/bpg1586134377111/page/images/Salesforce-More-button.jpg?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTYwMzcsInNoZWFmIjoiYnBnMTU4NjEzNDM3NzExMSJ9.g4n79xiHbkE6N0G8t7udvZcIZfYVLbXZ3WvQIVbcU1g&_LANG=enus)
   More menu.

   A spinning icon displays progress. A message opens: New
   Documents Generated & Saved
3. Select an option:

   - Send for Signature: Create a Docusign envelope
     containing this document.
   - Done: Return to the record.

You have completed the process. You have generated a
document from a Salesforce record using Docusign Gen on the Salesforce mobile
app.
