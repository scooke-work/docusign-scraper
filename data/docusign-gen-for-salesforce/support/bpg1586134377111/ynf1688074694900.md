# Generate Documents From Salesforce Records

Learn to generate a new document from a Salesforce record using Docusign Gen for
Salesforce.

You can use Docusign Gen to generate a document from Salesforce. You select a Gen custom button
on a record and instantly generate contracts, invoices, and more. Gen saves the
generated document in the record's Notes & Attachments. You can
download the document or send it for review or signature.

There are a number Gen experiences you can use. Each experience is based on a template built
and named by your administrator.

:   Generate a document from a record with the standard Gen experience and take no further
    action.
