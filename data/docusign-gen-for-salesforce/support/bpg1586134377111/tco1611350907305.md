# Send an Envelope Using a Standard Send With Docusign Template in Docusign Apps Launcher

You can send documents for eSignature using the built-in Docusign for Salesforce app in
the Docusign Apps Launcher package.

You can create and send an envelope using a standardSend with DocuSign button.

The envelope starts out blank,
with no pre-filled documents, recipients, or eSignature fields present.

The
envelope creation process has three stages:

- [Step 1: Add Documents or Templates (Send With DocuSign Button)](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=pzf1617644386542.html&_LANG=enus "Use this procedure to add documents or templates to a standard Send with DocuSign envelope.")
- [Step 2: Add Recipients to Your Envelope (Envelope Template)](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=dfp1596477842847.html&_LANG=enus "Use this procedure to add recipients to the envelope. You can add recipients from your Salesforce organization, from Docusign or by entering a name and email address.")
- [Step 3: Add Docusign and Salesforce Fields and Send Your Envelope (Send With Docusign Button)](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=xmx1596487118055.html&_LANG=enus "The third step of the process involves placing Docusign and Salesforce fields into the document text.")
