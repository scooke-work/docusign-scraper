# Add, Edit, and Delete Files in the Docusign Files Section of a Salesforce Record

Learn how to add, edit, and delete files in the Docusign Files
section of a Salesforce record.

You can manage your Docusign files on Salesforce records with the
Docusign Files section.

You can add files by uploading documents to the Docusign Files section from
your device.

You can also add files from the Notes & Attachments section of a
Salesforce record.

You can use these options to add, edit, and delete files in the Docusign
Files section:

- [Generate a Document and Add it to Docusign Files](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=blu1586134216052.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to add files to the Docusign Files component after generating them with Gen.")
- [Upload a File to Docusign Files](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=eto1586134371953.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to upload files from your device to the Docusign Files section of a Salesforce record.")
- [Download a Document from Docusign Files](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=tyz1586134372530.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to download documents from the Docusign Files section of a Salesforce record.")
- [Copy a File from Docusign Files to Notes & Attachments](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=vpk1586134372796.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to copy documents from Docusign Files to the Notes & Attachments section of a Salesforce record.")
- [Upload a New Version of a Document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=qsm1586134374562.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to upload a new version of a document in the Docusign Files section of a Salesforce record.")
- [Rename a File in the Docusign Files Component](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=hdb1586134375876.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to rename a file in the Docusign Files component of a Salesforce record.")
