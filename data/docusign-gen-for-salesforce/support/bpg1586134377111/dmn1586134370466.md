# Docusign Gen for Salesforce Overview (User)

Learn to use Docusign Gen to generate and send agreements for review
or signature.

Docusign Gen for Salesforce is an application in the Docusign
Apps Launcher for Salesforce package. Docusign Apps Launcher is
a unified bundle of Docusign apps: Docusign Gen, CLM,
Negotiate, and eSignature for Salesforce. Administrators configure and manage apps and
features from a centralized platform in Salesforce.

Docusign Gen for Salesforce is a document generation app. Your
administrator builds Gen templates and adds custom buttons to layouts. When you select a
button, Gen merges data from Salesforce records into your generated document. You can send
generated documents for review with Negotiate or send for signature with
eSignature for Salesforce.
