# Send a Document for Review or Approval with Negotiate

Learn how to send a document for review or approval with Docusign
Negotiate.

You can manage your Docusign files on Salesforce records with the Docusign
Files section. You can take basic actions like adding, editing, and deleting
files.

You can also send files for internal review or external approval.

You can find these actions in the Share button ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/bpg1586134377111/page/images/negotiateactionsarrow.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTYwODgsInNoZWFmIjoiYnBnMTU4NjEzNDM3NzExMSJ9.17EviLhpg3h7AqfDu6iNUxaqR5_Gw1vS5eEm5eouRP8&_LANG=enus) drop-down
menu.

This button is found to the right of any file in the Docusign Files section.

Here are options for sending content for review or approval from the Docusign Files section:

- [Create a Share Link to Send Docusign Files for Viewing, Downloading, and Editing](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=ypi1586134378833.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to send a file from Docusign Files using a share link. This link lets people with access to your organization view, download, and edit your Docusign files.")
- [Use External Review to Send Docusign Files to Recipients Outside Your Organization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=gqt1586134379833.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to send a document for external review from the Docusign Files section of a Salesforce record.")
- [Use Send for Approval To Send Docusign Files to Users in Your Organization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=bpg1586134377111&topicId=wkj1586134381776.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to send a document for internal approval from the Docusign Files section of a Salesforce record.")

Important: You must have [Docusign Negotiator permissions](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?topicId=dzl1586134206658.html&bundleId=izj1586134369853&_LANG=enus) to send files for approval or review
with Negotiate.
