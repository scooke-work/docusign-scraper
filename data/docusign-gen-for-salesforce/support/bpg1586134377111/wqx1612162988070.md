# Generate a Contract and Send It for Signature

You can generate a document with Docusign Gen and then send it for signature with
Docusign eSignature for Salesforce.

## Before you begin

Your administrator must grant Docusign
Generator permissions to your Docusign user.

You can generate a document with Docusign Gen for Salesforce and send it
for signature using Docusign eSignature for Salesforce.

There are two ways to send a generated document for signature:

Send with Docusign template
:   This is a three-stage sending experience built by Docusign. You add documents, recipients,
    and signature fields.

Docusign envelope template
:   This is a sending experience built by your administrator and linked to your
    Gen template. You may see documents, recipients, and signature fields in the
    envelope that have been pre-added by your administrator. You can add to or
    edit these elements.

Use this procedure to generate a document with Gen and send it for signature:

1. Open a Salesforce record.
2. Select a Gen button in the upper right.

   Note: Salesforce may hide
   the button under the arrow drop-down menu.

   ![Select Gen custom button](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/bpg1586134377111/page/images/Select-Gen-custom-button.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTYwMjQsInNoZWFmIjoiYnBnMTU4NjEzNDM3NzExMSJ9.QeSElQ2k4eNDgy0RIKdQGSuZg7kIIBZG5C55QnELW3Y&_LANG=enus "Select Gen custom button")

   The New Documents Generated & Saved dialog box
   opens. Salesforce saves the generated document in Notes &
   Attachments.
3. Select Send for Signature.
4. Follow the prompts to [complete and
   send your envelope](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=iqc1594156517921&topicId=wxo1617594601811.html&_LANG=enus).

You have completed the process. You have generated a
document with Docusign Gen and sent it for signature with Docusign eSignature for
Salesforce.
