# Use a Gen for Billing Custom Button to Generate, Save, and Send an Invoice

Learn to generate an invoice with Gen for Billing and save, download, or send it for
signature.

You can generate a new invoice from a Salesforce record using a Gen for Billing button.

You select a Gen for Billing button on a record and Docusign generates an invoice using
data from the record. Gen saves the generated document in the record's Notes
& Attachments section. You can download the document or send it for
review or signature.

To use Gen for Billing, you need [Docusign Generator
permissions](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=doi1628107496987.html&_LANG=enus) and access to the Salesforce Invoices
object.

Use these procedures to generate, save, and send invoices with Gen for Billing:

The generated invoice is automatically saved to the Notes and Attachments section of the Salesforce record.")
- [Generate an Invoice With Gen for Billing and Email It to
