# Generate a Document with Docusign Gen

Learn how to generate a document from a Salesforce record using Docusign Gen for
Salesforce.

## Before you begin

Your administrator must grant Docusign
Generator permissions to your Docusign user.

You can use Docusign Gen to generate a document from Salesforce. The following happens when
you select a Gen button on a Salesforce record:

- Gen creates a document using data from the Salesforce record
- Gen saves the generated document in Notes & Attachments

You can view or download the generated document or send it for review or signature.

Important: You are the owner of the files you generate.
However, Salesforce displays a Docusign administrator as the Owner in
the record's Files related list. To find files you generated, visit Files >  Owned by Me.

Use this procedure to generate a document with Gen:

1. Select a Gen button on a Salesforce record. Your administrator chooses the button
   name.

   ![A screenshot of a Salesforce record displays a Gen custom button named Fresh Software Sales Contract under the arrow menu.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/bpg1586134377111/page/images/Select-Gen-custom-button.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTYwMTksInNoZWFmIjoiYnBnMTU4NjEzNDM3NzExMSJ9.hz0QixXqpwEG5zZq-PM33Zfz2pWV8w6K7n8X4uSdow0&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a Salesforce record displays a Gen custom button named Fresh Software Sales Contract under the arrow menu.")

   The New Documents Generated & Saved dialog box opens.
   Gen saves the generated document in Notes & Attachments.
2. (Optional): Take action on the generated document:

   Note: Your administrator can disable the Send with
   Docusign button.

   - Preview: See a preview of the generated document
   - Download: Save a copy of the document to your device
   - [Send for Signature](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=iqc1594156517921&topicId=gsh1675832324336.html&_LANG=enus): Send to a recipient for signature
   - Done: Take no action and close the dialog box

You have completed the process. You have generated a
new contract and taken any optional steps. Salesforce saves the generated document in the
Notes & Attachments section of the record.
