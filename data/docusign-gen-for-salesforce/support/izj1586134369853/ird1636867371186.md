# Create a Gen Template using the Person Account Object

Learn how to build a Gen template using the Salesforce Person Account
object.

Person Account is a part of the Account object.
Person Account objects store information about people by
combining certain account and contact fields into one record. The Person
Account object is available in Salesforce Business, Professional,
Enterprise, Performance, Unlimited, and Developer editions.

Note: Before you can use Person accounts, you must enable
them in your Salesforce organization. Visit the Salesforce help site to learn to
enable Person accounts.
