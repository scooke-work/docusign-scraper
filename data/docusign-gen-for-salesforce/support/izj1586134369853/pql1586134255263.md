# Docusign Gen for Salesforce Administrator Overview

Learn about how you can use and configure Docusign Gen in your
Salesforce organization.

Docusign Apps Launcher is a unified package of apps:

- CLM for Salesforce
- CLM Essentials for Salesforce
- eSignature for Salesforce
- Gen for Salesforce

These apps work together to get your users from contract generation to review to signature
with speed and efficiency.

This guide covers administration of the Docusign Gen for Salesforce app.
Administrators can use Gen to create templates for document generation. Gen templates merge
Docusign and Salesforce fields into contracts to create agreements.

You can add Gen template custom buttons to Salesforce object layouts. Salesforce users with
Gen access can select a Gen custom button on a record and instantly generate agreements. Users
save generated agreements to Salesforce records and send them for signature.
