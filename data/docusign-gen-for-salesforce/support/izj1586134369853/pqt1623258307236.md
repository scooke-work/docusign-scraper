# Start a Docusign Gen 30-Day Trial

Learn how to activate a free 30-day trial of the Docusign Gen
application.

## Before you begin

You must have the following to start a Gen trial:

- Salesforce administrator credentials
- Docusign administrator credentials
- [Docusign Apps Launcher installed](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=ogj1586134254571.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to install the Docusign Apps Launcher package of apps in your Salesforce organization.")
- [Connection to a Docusign account](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=zfp1586134208988.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to connect your Salesforce organization to your Docusign account.")

You can activate a free 30-day trial of the Docusign Gen for Salesforce
application. The trial has all the capabilities of the fully licensed Gen product.
You can start a Gen trial without contacting Docusign sales.

Note: Each Gen user must also have an eSignature user in your Docusign account. So if you're licensed for 100 Docusign eSignature users, you can add 100 trial Gen users.

Use this procedure to start your 30-day free trial of Docusign Gen for
Salesforce.

1. Select Document Generation in the left column.
2. Select Try it out.
3. Select Get Started.

   The dialog box closes. A message opens: Your Docusign Gen
   trial has started.
4. Select Allow Access.
5. Enter Salesforce administrator credentials.

   A message opens: Successfully connected and authorized to
   Docusign.

You have completed the process. You have activated your Gen trial.
You have 30 days to evaluate Gen at no cost. [Contact Docusign
Sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) to continue using Gen after 30 days.
