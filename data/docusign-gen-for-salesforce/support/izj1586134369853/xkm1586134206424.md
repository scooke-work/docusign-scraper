# Add Docusign Components to Salesforce Layouts

You can add Docusign components to your Salesforce layouts. Components give your users a space to view and interact with Docusign content on Salesforce records.

You can add Docusign components to your Salesforce page layouts. Docusign components display as sections on your Salesforce records. In these sections, users can view and interact with Docusign content without logging on to their Docusign account.

You can add the following components to your Salesforce layouts:

[Docusign Agreement Requests](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=map8c30aa94-7e9f-4950-92fc-dd22c6d9f835&topicId=tsk151c1542-bdf4-4c8e-ba20-37affb7adc7e.html&_LANG=enus)
:   View Docusign Agreement Desk requests on your records. You can use this component with Docusign Agreement Desk. Use Agreement Desk to view and act on your Docusign requests.

[Docusign CLM Files](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ysk1643667858834&topicId=dny1626816139155.html&_LANG=enus)
:   View and act on files and folders from your connected Docusign CLM account. You can use this component with the CLM for Salesforce app.

[Docusign CLM Obligations](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ysk1643667858834&topicId=tvw1659667572631.html&_LANG=enus)
:   View and act on your CLM obligations from a Salesforce record. You can use this component with the CLM for Salesforce app.

[Docusign CLM Tasks](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ysk1643667858834&topicId=lbi1628268219401.html&_LANG=enus)
:   View and act on tasks from your connected Docusign CLM account. You can use this component with the CLM for Salesforce app.

[Docusign Completed Agreements](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pwq1747797355782&topicId=cmv1746652068556.html&_LANG=enus)
:   View Docusign agreements on your records. You can use this component with Docusign Agreement Manager. Use Agreement Manager to view and act on your Docusign agreements.

[Docusign Envelope Status](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=srl1586134368658&topicId=all1586134265721.html&_LANG=enus)
:   Monitor the status of Docusign envelopes sent from Salesforce records. You can use this component with these apps:

    - Gen for Salesforce
    - Negotiate for Salesforce
    - eSignature for Salesforce

[Docusign Files](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=isj1586134263707.html&_LANG=enus)
:   Add files to a record that can be sent for Docusign signature or review. You can use this component with the Negotiate for Salesforce app.

[Docusign IAM Launcher](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=qwr1706590641805&topicId=tsk590e18e7-9dbc-418f-9fb8-0169f182eb3a.html&_LANG=enus)
:   Start Docusign workflows from your records. You can use this component with Docusign Workflow Builder. Workflow Builder reads Salesforce data and writes changes back to records when workflows complete.
