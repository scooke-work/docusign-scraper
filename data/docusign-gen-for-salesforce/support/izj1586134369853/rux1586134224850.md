# "You Must Have Docusign Generator Permissions to Perform This Operation" Displays When I Try to Create a Gen Template

Learn to fix the "You must have Docusign generator permissions to
perform this operation" error.

I am a Docusign administrator, but I cannot create a Gen template. When I
try, an error message displays: You must have Docusign generator permissions to
perform this operation.

## Resolution: Grant Generator Permissions to Administrator User

Cause: You have not granted Generator permissions to your
administrator user. Without Generator permissions, you cannot perform administrative
actions in Gen, including adding new users and creating templates.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

[Grant Generator permissions to your
user](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=dzl1586134206658.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to add Docusign Gen, CLM, Negotiate, and eSignature users and manage permissions on the user management page.").

You have completed the process. You have granted Generator permissions to
your administrator user. You can create templates and manage Docusign users.
