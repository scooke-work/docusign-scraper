# The Document Generator User Does Not Display as the Owner in Salesforce Files

Learn to find the Salesforce owner of documents generated with Docusign Gen.

Users do not display as the owner of documents they generate with Gen. When users view a
record's Files related list, they see the Docusign integration
user listed as the owner.

Users have trouble finding documents that they generated and are unable to determine
who generated them.

## Resolution: View the Document Owner in Salesforce Files

Cause: Docusign uses your integration
user's credentials to authenticate document generation.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Open Salesforce Files:
   1. Select the App Launcher grid ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/app-launcher-grid.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg1MDQsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.ho_GzUPvAAfrDalDFtaAbeJ-F99Ijq_xESkn1nM0Pn8&_LANG=enus).
   2. Enter and select Files in the search
      field.

   The Files Owned by Me page opens.
2. Select Owned by Me.

   You have completed the process. The list displays all documents
   that you have generated with Gen.
