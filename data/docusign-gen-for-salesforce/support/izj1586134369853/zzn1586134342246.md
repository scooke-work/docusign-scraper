# Add a Document to a Gen Template

Learn to add a document to your Gen template.

You can add multiple documents to your Gen template. When you upload several versions of
the same contract, your users can decide which version they want to generate. You can upload
Word documents or PDF files.

Use this procedure to add a document to your Gen template:

1. Select Upload Files and follow the prompts to upload a document
   from your device.

   A message displays: File was successfully
   uploaded.
2. Select Save and Close.

You have completed the process. You have added a
document to your Gen template.
