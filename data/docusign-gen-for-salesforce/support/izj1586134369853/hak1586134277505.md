# Check the Activation Status of a Docusign for Salesforce User

Learn how to check the activation status of a Docusign CLM, Gen,
Negotiate, or eSignature for Salesforce user.

Newly-created Docusign users must select an activation link before using Docusign apps in
Salesforce. You can check the activation status of newly-created users on the Docusign User
Management page.

Use this procedure to check the activation status of a Docusign CLM, Gen, Negotiate, or
eSignature for Salesforce user:

Check the Status column:

- Active: The user has activated their Docusign account
- Pending: The user has not activated their Docusign account

Tip: Pending
users should check their junk mail folder if they cannot find their Docusign activation email.

You have completed the process. You have verified the
activation status of your Docusign Users. Pending users must [activate their Docusign user accounts](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=iqc1594156517921&topicId=ymi1586134213877.html&_LANG=enus).
