# Docusign Fails to Connect to Salesforce When I Enter My Docusign Administrator Credentials

Learn to reset a browser session to connect Docusign and
Salesforce.

I am unable to finish connecting my Docusign account to my Salesforce
organization.

I enter Docusign administrator credentials, but the connection process
fails.

## Resolution: Clear the Docusign Login Session in Your Browser

Cause: The connection process can fail if you are logged on with
different Docusign credentials on another browser tab.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Clear your browser cache.
2. Log in to Salesforce as an administrator.
3. [Open Docusign Apps Launcher as
   an administrator.](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=etv1586134206174.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to open the Docusign Apps Launcher for Salesforce package in your organization.")
4. Follow these steps to [connect Salesforce and Docusign](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=pkr1586134231912.html&_LANG=enus).

   You have completed the process. You have cleared your browser cache and
   connected your Docusign account with your Salesforce
   organization.
