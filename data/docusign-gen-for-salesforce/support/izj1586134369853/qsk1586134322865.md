# Add Child Objects to an Agreement Cloud Editor Template

Learn to add up to four levels of child objects and two levels of related
lists.

Agreement Cloud Editor templates can contain up to four levels
of child objects and two levels of related lists. You can use the following combinations of
relation:

| Relation type | Object path |
| --- | --- |
| Root | Opportunity |
| 1: 1 | Opportunity >  Account |
| 1: 1: 1 | Opportunity >  Account >  Parent Account |
| 1: 1: 1: 1 | Opportunity >  Account >  Parent Account >  Owner |
| 1: Many | Opportunity >  Opportunity Contact Roles |
| 1: Many: 1 | Opportunity >  Opportunity Contact Roles >  Contact |
| 1: Many: 1: 1 | Opportunity >  Opportunity Contact Roles >  Contact >  Reports To |
| 1: Many: Many | Account >  Contacts >  Cases |
| 1: Many: Many: 1 | Account >  Contacts >  Cases >  Owner |

template:
