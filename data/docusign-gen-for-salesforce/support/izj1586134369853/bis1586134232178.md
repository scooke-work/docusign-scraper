# I Cannot Connect Docusign and Salesforce When the SpringCM File It App Is Installed

Learn to fix an organization that contains Docusign Apps Launcher and
the legacy SpringCM File It app.

I tried to connect Docusign and Salesforce. The connection failed.

My organization contains both Docusign Apps Launcher for Salesforce and
the legacy SpringCM File It app. I want to use both Docusign products in
the same organization.

## Resolution: Uninstall the Legacy SpringCM App or Docusign Apps Launcher

Cause: You cannot connect Salesforce to Docusign
when your organization contains Docusign Apps Launcher and the legacy
SpringCM File It app. Each Docusign product requires its own Salesforce
organization.

1. Choose one method for your existing Salesforce org:

   - Uninstall the legacy SpringCM File It app and install it in another Salesforce
     organization.
   - Uninstall Docusign Apps Launcher and install it in another
     Salesforce organization.
2. Follow the steps to [connect Salesforce and Docusign](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=zfp1586134208988.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to connect your Salesforce organization to your Docusign account.").

   You have completed the process. You have created dedicated organizations for
   each Docusign product.
