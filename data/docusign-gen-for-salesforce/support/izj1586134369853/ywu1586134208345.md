# Edit or Delete a Gen Template or Custom Button

Learn how to edit, delete, or change ownership for a Gen template or custom
button.

You can edit, delete, or change ownership for a Gen template or custom button. If you edit
the name of a Gen template, also edit the custom button name. If you delete a Gen template,
remove that template's buttons from your Salesforce layouts.

Use the following links to edit, delete, or change ownership of a Gen template:

- [Edit the Name of a Gen Template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=gww1586134337518.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to change the name of a Gen template.")
- [Edit a Gen Template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=xcs1646713533209.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to edit a Gen template.")
- [Delete a Gen Template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=mbd1586134342525.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to delete a Gen template.")

- [Edit a Gen Custom Button](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=tet1586134342760.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to edit the label or details of a Gen custom button.")
