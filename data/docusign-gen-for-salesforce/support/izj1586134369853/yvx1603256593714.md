# Activate Docusign Gen from a Salesforce CPQ Plus License

Salesforce CPQ Plus customers are given a Docusign Gen account that is activated by
following these instructions.

Docusign eSignature customers purchasing Salesforce CPQ Plus also receive a
Docusign Gen license. Your organization must meet the
following prerequisites to qualify:

- Your Salesforce organization has a Gen CPQ Plus license.
- Your Salesforce organization is connected to a paid, multi-user Docusign
  account (not a trial.)
- You do not have an existing paid Gen license.
- You have at least one Gen CPQ activation remaining.

Follow this link to [get started with your Gen CPQ Plus
license](https://support.docusign.com/s/articles/Getting-Started-with-DocuSign-Apps-Launcher-and-Gen-CPQ-Plus?language=en_US).

CPQ Plus customers matching the prerequisites can activate their Gen license by
following these steps:

1. Select Document Generation.
2. Select Activate.
3. Select Allow Access.

   The Salesforce login page opens.
4. Enter Salesforce administrator credentials.

   A message displays: Successfully connected and authorized to
   Docusign.

The process is complete. You have activated your Gen license. You
can create users, build templates, and add Gen buttons to your Salesforce page
layouts.
