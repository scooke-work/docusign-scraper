# Upload a New Version of a Word Document in Your Gen Template

Learn to update your Gen template Word documents.

When you add, edit, or delete Gen template fields, you must update the associated Word
document.

Example: You add the Opportunity ID field to
your Gen template. You download the Gen template Word document and paste the
Opportunity ID anchor text. You remove the old Word document from
your Gen template and upload the new version.

Use this procedure to update your Word document to reflect changes to your Gen
template:

1. [Edit the Gen template Upload Documents page as a
   Salesforce administrator](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=nyj1621662912687.html&_LANG=enus):
2. Select a document name.

   The document preview page opens in a new tab or window.
3. Select Download and [update
   your Salesforce data fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=ffk1586134297621.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to paste your Salesforce anchor text fields into a Microsoft Word document.").
4. Return to the Gen Upload Documents page and select
   X next to the old document version.![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/deletewordtemplate.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTY1NjYsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.tCAyIEN64lHGIpD6f1LwD4ce7URAX1ZDPb8Ykj4hgoU&_LANG=enus)

   A message displays: File was successfully
   deleted.
5. Select Upload Files and follow the prompts to upload the new
   version from your device.

   A message displays: File was successfully uploaded.
6. Select Save and Close.
7. Select Finish and choose Save in the
   Do you want to save changes? dialog box.

You have completed the process. You have uploaded a new
version of your Gen Word document.
