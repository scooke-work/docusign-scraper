# Access the Gen Word Template Upload Documents Step

Learn to access the Upload Documents step in a Gen Word template.

Follow this procedure to access the Upload Documents step of a Gen Word template:

Edit the Gen template Upload Documents page as a
Salesforce administrator:

1. Select the App Launcher grid ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/applaunchergrid.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTY1NDUsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.CGast1LQpH__x6pCbSh1vwBpa3NquZybBz_k3g-k7Q4&_LANG=enus) and choose View
   All.
2. Select Docusign Apps Launcher.
3. Select Docusign Gen Templates.
4. Select the arrow ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/Arrowhead.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTY1NDUsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.CGast1LQpH__x6pCbSh1vwBpa3NquZybBz_k3g-k7Q4&_LANG=enus) next to a Gen template and choose
   Edit from the dropdown menu.
5. Select the Upload Documents arrow. The arrow has
   no text until selected.

You have completed the process. You have opened the
Upload Documents step of a Gen template.
