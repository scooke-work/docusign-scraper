# My Gen for Billing Template Doesn't Generate or Send an Invoice When the Record Status Changes to "Posted"

Learn to fix the issue of Gen failing to generate or send invoices when the record
status changes to Posted.

My Gen for Billing template does not generate and send invoices when a record status changes
to Posted. When the status changes to Posted, nothing
happens.

## Resolution: Change the Salesforce Locale to German (Germany)

Cause: Your Salesforce locale is set to German
(Germany, EURO), rather than German
(Germany).

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Open your Salesforce user page:
   1. Select the gear ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/salesforcegear.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg0ODcsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.ileDeN8s8gCrLJto_-ABJ4MGa1LHx4fbZRNzJPGwpiw&_LANG=enus) and choose
      Setup.
   2. Enter and select Users in the Quick Find
      field.
   3. Select Edit next to your user.
2. Locale: Select German
   (Germany) from the dropdown menu.
3. Select Save.

   You have completed the process. You have changed your Locale from German (Germany,
   EURO) to German (Germany). When a record status changes to
   Posted, Gen generates an invoice and sends it
   for signature.
