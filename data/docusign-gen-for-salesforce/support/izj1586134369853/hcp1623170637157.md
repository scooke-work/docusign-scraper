# "Unable to Download the ContentVersion from Salesforce for the ID” Displays When I Generate an Agreement

Learn to fix the "Unable to download the ContentVersion from Salesforce for the ID"
error.

When users try to generate agreements, an error displays: Unable to download the
ContentVersion from Salesforce for the ID.

## Resolution 1: Create a Test Word Document for Your Template

Cause: Your Gen template's Word document contains Salesforce
merge fields that are not present in your Salesforce object.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Select the Docusign Gen Templates tab in
   Salesforce.
2. Follow these instructions to [create
   a new Gen template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=lig1586134209279.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to build a Gen template using a Microsoft Word document.").

   Note: Do not add Salesforce fields on the Add Fields
   step. Upload a blank Word document on the Upload
   Documents step.
3. Select the Gen button on a record.

   You have completed the process. If an agreement generates, the
   connection between Docusign and Salesforce is working.
   Create a new Word template from a blank Word document. Only add merge
   fields to your Word document that are present in your Salesforce
   objects.

## Resolution 2: Upgrade Docusign Apps Launcher

Cause: Your organization has an older version of Docusign Apps Launcher installed. Upgrade to the latest
version.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Check your Docusign Apps Launcher version number:

## Resolution 3: Use the TroubleShooting Tool to Disconnect and Reconnect Docusign

Cause: Your Docusign account is connected
to more than one Salesforce organization.

This is common when administrators create or refresh a Salesforce sandbox that is
connected to a Docusign account. The error displays when a
user selects a Gen button.

1. Open the [Docusign Apps
   Launcher TroubleShooting page](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=rag1597638195677.html&_LANG=enus "Docusign Apps Launcher TroubleShooting is a Salesforce page that allows administrators to troubleshoot and resolve issues with these Docusign apps.") in Salesforce.
2. Disconnect Account and Preserve User Settings:
   Select Execute.

   A message displays: **Successfully logged out from Docusign account.** The Troubleshooting page
   displays the message: **No Docusign Account
   Connected.**
3. Follow these instructions to [connect your Docusign
   account.](https://docs.docusign.com/supportdocs/dal-gen-admin-guide/Content/connect-salesforce-and-docusign.htm#zfp1586134208988).

   Important: Only connect a Docusign account
   that is not connected to another Salesforce organization.

   You have completed the process. You have disconnected your Docusign account from your Salesforce organization and
   reconnected. Users can generate agreements with Gen.
