# Step 5: Preview Your Template

Learn how to view a preview of what a generated document will look like.

Use this procedure to see what a generated document
looks like from this template.

Tip:
[This video explains the
step](https://bcove.video/3g7Ov8r).

1. Select Preview.
2. (Optional) Choose any option:

   - Preview: Review the document.
   - Download: Save a copy of the document to your device.
3. Select Next.

You have completed step five. Move to [Step 6: Add a Gen Custom Button to Your Salesforce Layout](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=ldc1586134295945.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to add a Gen custom button to your Salesforce layouts.").
