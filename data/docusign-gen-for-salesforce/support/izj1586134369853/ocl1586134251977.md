# I Cannot Uninstall the Docusign Gen Application

Learn to remove the Gen application by uninstalling Docusign Apps
Launcher and all dependencies.

I am trying to uninstall the Gen application. I cannot find it in Installed Packages.

## Resolution: Uninstall the Docusign Apps Launcher Package

Cause: Docusign Gen is not a standalone app. It
does not display in the Installed Packages list.

To uninstall Gen, uninstall the Docusign Apps Launcher package. Remove
all dependencies before uninstalling.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. [Remove users from Docusign
   Apps Launcher permission sets](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=pnw1623705527946.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to remove all users from Docusign permission sets.").
2. [Remove Docusign Apps Launcher
   components from Salesforce layouts](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=uxu1623708462949.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to remove all Docusign Apps Launcher components from Salesforce layouts.").
3. [Remove Docusign Apps Launcher
   custom buttons from your Salesforce layouts](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=cif1623740064281.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to remove Docusign Apps Launcher custom buttons from your Salesforce layouts.").
4. [Uninstall the Docusign Apps
   Launcher package from your Salesforce organization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=ump1623741000284.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to uninstall the Docusign Apps Launcher package from your Salesforce organization.").

   You have completed the process. You have removed the Docusign Apps Launcher package and all dependencies.
