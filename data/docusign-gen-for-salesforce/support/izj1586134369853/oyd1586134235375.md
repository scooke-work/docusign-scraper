# Docusign Creates Gen Templates That I Didn't Save

Learn to delete unwanted Gen templates.

I started building a Gen template but decided I didn't need it. I closed the browser tab
without saving the template.

When I opened the Gen Templates page again, Docusign had saved the
template that I discarded.

## Resolution: Delete Unwanted Gen Templates

Cause: Docusign auto-saves your Gen templates to
ensure that you don't lose work. Even if you close a template without selecting
Save and Close
Docusign saves it.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Select Docusign Gen Templates.
2. Select the arrow ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/Arrowhead.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg0NDYsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.lLBXgsXd6t0tB3_3QpeTCFEXx1SlyuRwLVvg3wzEm8Y&_LANG=enus) next to a Gen template and choose Delete
   from the dropdown menu.

   A dialog box displays: Are you sure you want to delete this
   Docusign Gen Template?
3. Select Delete.

   A dialog box displays: Docusign Gen Template was
   deleted.

   You have completed the task. You have deleted a Gen template. Remove this
   template's buttons from your Salesforce layouts.
