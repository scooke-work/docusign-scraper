# I See a Blank Screen When I Try to Grant Gen Permissions

Learn to enable Cross-Origin Resource Sharing to prevent blank screens when granting Gen
permissions.

I am a Salesforce and Docusign administrator, but I can't grant Gen
permissions to users.

When I try to grant Generator permissions, I see a white screen with no controls.

## Resolution: Enable Cross-Origin Resource Sharing (CORS)

Cause: You have not enabled [Cross-Origin Resource Sharing](https://help.salesforce.com/articleView?id=extend_code_cors.htm&type=5) in your Salesforce
organization.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Open the Session Settings page as an
   administrator:
   1. Select the gear ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/salesforcegear.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg0NjEsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.m3LSajUcKUt0l_JYkzH53xSFs4T8_H4rH3OiYOVgGec&_LANG=enus) and choose Setup.
   2. Enter and select Session Settings in the
      Quick Find field.

   The Session Settings page displays.
2. Select Add Domain.
3. [Paste your Salesforce Visualforce
   domain in the Domain field](https://developer.salesforce.com/docs/atlas.en-us.pages.meta/pages/pages_quick_start_external_iframe.htm).
4. Select Save.

   You have completed the process. Non-integration users can grant
   permissions to Docusign apps in your Salesforce
   organization.
