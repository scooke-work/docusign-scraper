# Refresh the Products Provisioned to Your Docusign Account with the TroubleShooting Tool

Learn to activate and update Docusign for Salesforce apps and settings
using the Refresh Products feature.

Administrators can use the Docusign Apps Launcher
TroubleShooting tool to fix issues with Docusign apps. The TroubleShooting tool is available in Docusign Apps Launcher version 2.5 and higher. Salesforce
administrators with Docusign administrator permissions can use it.

You can activate and update apps and settings in your Salesforce organization using the
Refresh Products feature.

- Switch any newly added products like Docusign Gen from
  Pending to Active
- Update the Account Products Last Checked custom setting
- Keep account feature settings up to date

Follow this procedure to use the Refresh Products feature:

1. Access the Visualforce Pages page as a Salesforce
   administrator:
   1.    2. Enter and select Visualforce Pages in the Quick
      Find field.
2. Select TroubleShooting in the Label
   column.![A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link highlighted on the Salesforce Visualforce Pages screen.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/oas1598330401772.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg0MDUsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.4eUI-uL6WQGBnBshVYqwnutcI5fwbKceGEUXpVRp1h0&_LANG=enus "A screenshot displays the TroubleShooting link highlighted on the Salesforce Visualforce Pages screen.")

4. Refresh Products: Select Execute.

   The page refreshes. A message opens: Successfully refreshed

You have completed the process. You have updated settings and
activated pending apps.
