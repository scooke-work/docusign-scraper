# I Cannot Connect Salesforce to Docusign

Learn to fix integration errors that occur when you try to connect Docusign and Salesforce.

I cannot connect Salesforce to my Docusign account. I [followed the standard connection procedure](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=zfp1586134208988.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to connect your Salesforce organization to your Docusign account.") but it
failed.

## Resolution 1: Enable Pop-Ups in Your Browser

Cause: If your browser is set to block all pop-ups, you cannot
connect your Salesforce organization to your Docusign account.

Without pop-ups enabled, administrators cannot enter their Docusign and
Salesforce administrator credentials.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Open your browser settings.
2. Enter exceptions for salesforce.com and
   docusign.com.

   You have completed the process. You have enabled exceptions for
   salesforce.com and docusign.com.

## Resolution 2: Confirm That Your Integration User Is an Administrator

Cause: The Salesforce administrator user that you use to connect Docusign and Salesforce is called the integration user. Follow this link
for more information: [Apps Launcher Integration User Best Practices](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=rhi1657226496755.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how best to manage the integration user that connects your Salesforce and Docusign accounts.")

If you revoke the administrator status of your integration user, the integration fails
and Gen does not work.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

Restore System Administrator status to the installing Salesforce user. Or use
different Salesforce administrator credentials to [reconnect the accounts](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=zfp1586134208988.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to connect your Salesforce organization to your Docusign account.").

You have completed the process. You have restored the access of the
integration user.

## Resolution 3: Log out of Any Active Docusign Sessions on Your Browser

Cause: You are logged into two different Docusign
accounts on the same browser.

Example: You log on to **Docusign Account 1**
as an administrator. On another tab, you try to connect Salesforce to **Docusign Account 2** with different Docusign
administrator credentials.

Result: The login session to Docusign Account 1 is
still active. This session prevents the administrator from connecting Salesforce to Docusign Account 2.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Log out of any Docusign accounts.
2. [Connect Docusign and
   Salesforce](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=zfp1586134208988.html&_LANG=enus "Learn to connect your Salesforce organization to your Docusign account.").

   You have completed the process. You have ended any active Docusign sessions and connected accounts.

## Resolution 4: Activate Your Expired Gen Trial as a Paid App

Cause: Your 30-day Gen trial has expired.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

Contact your Docusign sales representative to activate Gen as a
paid application.

You have completed the process. You have activated your expired Gen
trial.
