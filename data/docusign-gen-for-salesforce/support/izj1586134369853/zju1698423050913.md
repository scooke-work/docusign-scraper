# Docusign Does Not Email Recipients When I Generate Invoices from a Gen for Billing Template

Learn the workaround for invoices failing to send from a Gen for Billing
template.

I can generate invoices from a Gen for Billing template, but Docusign does
not email invoices to recipients.

## Resolution: Switch Your Salesforce Currency Locale to FR-Euro

Cause: Your Salesforce Currency Locale
field is set to fr-FR-Euro.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Edit the Company Information page as a
   Salesforce administrator:

   1. Select the gear ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/izj1586134369853/page/images/salesforcegear.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODIxNTg1MDAsInNoZWFmIjoiaXpqMTU4NjEzNDM2OTg1MyJ9.UfWeBSO--jTpGf3kGz9W8K79yYNzKVZ5p_hEsZq3uIE&_LANG=enus)
      and select Setup.
   2. Enter and select Company Information in
      the Quick Find field.
   3. Select Edit.
2. Currency Locale: Select French (France)
   - EUR.
3. Select Save.

   You have completed the process. You switched your Currency
   Locale settings from fr-FR-Euro to FR-Euro. Gen for Billing generates
   invoices and sends them to template recipients.
