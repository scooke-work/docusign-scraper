# Access the Docusign Gen Templates Tab to Build and Edit Gen Templates

Learn to access the Docusign Gen Templates tab in Salesforce.

Gen templates allow users to select a button on a record to instantly generate a contract. Use this procedure to access the Docusign Gen Templates tab in your Salesforce organization:

1. Select the Docusign Gen Templates tab.
2. Make any selection:

   - [New](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=zgw1621457735347.html&_LANG=enus): Create a new Gen template.
   - [Change Owner](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=sut1646711991124.html&_LANG=enus): Change the owner of a Gen template.
   - [Import](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=juw1586134252760.html&_LANG=enus): Import up to 50,000 records at a time.
   - [Rename](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=gww1586134337518.html&_LANG=enus): Change the name of a Gen template.
   - [Edit](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=xcs1646713533209.html&_LANG=enus): Add or edit documents, Salesforce fields, and Docusign fields in a Gen template.
   - [Delete](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=izj1586134369853&topicId=mbd1586134342525.html&_LANG=enus): Delete a Gen template.

   You have completed the task. You accessed the Docusign Gen Templates tab. From this page, you can create and edit Gen Templates.
