# The Generate Agreement with Docusign Action Failed in My Salesforce Flow

Check logs to see why your Generate Agreement With Docusign action
failed in Salesforce Flow.

I created a Salesforce Flow containing a Generate Agreement With
Docusign action. When my Flow triggered the action, Docusign did not generate a document.

## Resolution 1: Check Docusign Logs

Cause: Unknown. Check Docusign Logs to
find the issue.

Use this procedure to fix the issue:

1. Select the Docusign Logs tab.
2. Select a link in the Docusign Log column.

   You have completed the process. The Docusign Log details page
   opens for that item, displaying the cause of the error.

Cause: Unknown. Check Docusign Jobs to
find the issue.
