# Enable Agreement Desk AI Agents for Your Users

Learn how administrators turn on AI Agents inAgreement Desk.

## Before you begin

You must have: Docusign
administrator
(DS Admin) privileges or Agreement Desk settings
manager permissions privileges to turn on
Agreement Desk
for your account.

Enable AI Agents to gain assistance with request type selection, form completion, and request summaries. By default, this feature is turned off and can only be enabled by an administrator.

1. Select the Agreements tab in the top
   navigation.
2. Select Requests from the left
   navigation.
3. Select Settings.
4. Select Admin.

   The configurable Agreement Desk
   settings display
5. Select Turn on AI Agents

   The AI Contract Agents dialog box displays.![A screenshot of the Agreement Desk enable AI Agents option.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/mapf0ca9167-3825-4b36-a385-e98321f7adb5/page/images/ad-adm-enable-ai.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjI1NDEsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwZjBjYTkxNjctMzgyNS00YjM2LWEzODUtZTk4MzIxZjdhZGI1In0.yiZ1u24kSWCWzAmSkbu7P_RObMG0TUWa6CjfyqsRzWU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Agreement Desk enable AI Agents option.")
6. Select I agree in the confirmation dialog box.

Agreement Desk AI Agents are enabled for your users.
