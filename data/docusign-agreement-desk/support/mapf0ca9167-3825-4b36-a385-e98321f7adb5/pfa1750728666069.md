# Get Started with Administration for Docusign Agreement Desk

Learn some basics about the Docusign Agreement Desk features.

As an Agreement Desk administrator, you can customize Agreement Desk settings to fit your organization's needs. For example, you can create custom statuses for requests, access and customize your organization's Agreement Desk email address, and manage user permissions.

By default, Agreement Desk is turned off. For users to access it, you must turn it on. Because it's off by default, you can roll out Agreement Desk to your organization when you are ready.

Agreement Desk is a collaboration tool designed to speed up the intake, review, and finalize agreements. With it, you'll be able to manage the entire agreement process, from request intake to contract review, negotiation, and approvals, all within Docusign.

As an Agreement Desk administrator, you can customize Agreement Desk to suit your organization's requirements and manage levels of user interaction. For example, you can:

- Create request statuses to specific business processes.
- Build workflows to specific business needs by configuring unique request types with corresponding metadata and statuses.
- Control access to requests based on user roles and groups, ensuring data security.
- Specify whether request submitters receive notifications when their submitted requests change.
