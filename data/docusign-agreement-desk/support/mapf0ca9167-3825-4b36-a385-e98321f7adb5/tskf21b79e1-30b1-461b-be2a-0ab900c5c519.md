# Use Agreement Desk Reports

Learn how to gain valuable insights into agreement processes and performance. Access reports to understand how many requests are completed, how quickly, and where requests spent the most time.

Gain valuable insights into agreement processes and performance. Access reports to understand how many requests are completed, how quickly, and where requests spent the most time.

See the  [Agreement Manager Request Report Dashboards](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=mapf0ca9167-3825-4b36-a385-e98321f7adb5&topicId=con1fc88284-aff5-46c9-ac08-15db3839fce8.html&_LANG=enus) for detailed information about the request report dashboards.

Note: Reports pull information about closed requests. You can ensure that your reporting is accurate by marking completed requests as closed to ensure they are reflected in the reports.

**Agreement Desk request data in Docusign Reports**

The Request Reports in the Docusign Reports tab now has enhanced functionality that lets you apply filters, view reports, and download data.

In addition to the pipeline chart, Docusign Reporting provides deep insights into your overall requests. For more information, see the article.
