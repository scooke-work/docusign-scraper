# Configure a Custom Inbound Email Address for Messages﻿

Learn how to configure a custom email address for yourAgreement Desk to
manage incoming and outgoing messages effectively.

## Before you begin

Agreement Desk provides a dedicated email address for each account, such as
`requests.fontara@mail.docusign.net`. This address is used for all incoming
and outgoing messages, and is automatically set as the reply-to address for messages sent to
and from Agreement Desk.

If you want to use a custom email address from your own domain, for example
`agreements@yourcompany.com`, you can
configure Agreement Desk to use it.

Follow the instructions to configure Agreement Desk to use your custom email
address:

1. Set up forwarding from your custom email address to your
   Agreement Desk account.
   1. Set up email forwarding from your custom email address to your Agreement Desk email address in either Google Groups or Outlook
      Groups.
   2. Create a Google or Outlook email group with the
      address that you want to use (for example,
      `requests@fontara.com`). Ensure that the group
      allows you to add a user with an external
      domain.
   3. Add your default Agreement Desk email address (for example,
      `requests.fontara@mail.docusign.net`) to the group. Ensure that this
      email address is the only member of the group. To find the email address, see the
      topic [Locate Your Docusign Agreement Desk Email
      Address](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=mapf0ca9167-3825-4b36-a385-e98321f7adb5&topicId=xlg1741793314719.html&_LANG=enus "Learn where to find the default email alias provided by Agreement Desk.").
   4. Ensure that the group is set up correctly. Send
      an email to the new group and verify that it is
      forwarded to Agreement Desk.
2. Customize the default email address, specify the address you want to use in the
   Customize your email address section.
   1. Select Settings > Email.
3. In the Email Configuration section, you can edit the default
   email address and email name for outgoing email messages.

address.
