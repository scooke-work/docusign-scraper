# Enable Agreement Desk for Your Users with One-Click

Learn how administrators enable Agreement Desk for users to interact
with and create Agreement Desk requests.

## Before you begin

You must have Docusign administrator (DS Admin) privileges or
Agreement Desk settings manager permissions privileges to turn on
Agreement Desk for your account.

By default, Agreement Desk is turned off. Turn on Agreement Desk to begin using it.

You can turn on Agreement Desk from the Agreements Welcome page with
one click.

1. Select the Agreements tab in the top navigation.
2. Select Requests from the left navigation pane.

   If you are a DS Admin or have Agreement Desk settings
   permissions, the Turn on Agreement Desk  button
   displays.

   ![A screenshot of the Turn onAgreement Desk feature.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/mapf0ca9167-3825-4b36-a385-e98321f7adb5/page/images/ad-adm-1-click.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjI1MTIsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwZjBjYTkxNjctMzgyNS00YjM2LWEzODUtZTk4MzIxZjdhZGI1In0.P_zPR0ZK-oFG1ztG-3KxD3dXFb9TXxUS2f2EMk9almE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Turn onAgreement Desk feature.")
3. Select Requests from the left navigation.
4. Select Settings.
5. Select Admin.

   The configurable Agreement Desk settings display.
   ![A screenshot of the Agreement Desk configurable settings.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/mapf0ca9167-3825-4b36-a385-e98321f7adb5/page/images/ad-adm-set-turn-on.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjI1MTIsInNoZWFmIjoibWFwZjBjYTkxNjctMzgyNS00YjM2LWEzODUtZTk4MzIxZjdhZGI1In0.P_zPR0ZK-oFG1ztG-3KxD3dXFb9TXxUS2f2EMk9almE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Agreement Desk configurable settings.")
6. Select Turn on Agreement Desk.
7. Select Enable Agreement Desk in the
   confirmation dialog
   box
   to enable Agreement Desk
