# Locate Your Docusign Agreement Desk Email Address

Learn where to find the default email alias provided by Agreement Desk.

Your organization's account has a unique
Agreement Desk
email address to use
for
submitting email requests.
Users
with
this
email address
can send requests directly from their email inbox. You can locate
this email from the Settings menu.

1. Select
   Agreements
   in the top
   navigation.
2. Select
   Requests
   in the left navigation.

   The Requests dashboard displays.
3. SelectSettings > Email.

   The email address used for requests displays beneath EMAIL
   ADDRESSES.
