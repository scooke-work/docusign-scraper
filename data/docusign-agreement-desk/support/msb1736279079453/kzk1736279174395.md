# Manage Account Requests

Read about how users and administrators view Agreement Desk requests and
relevant information about them, as well as search and filter to find specific
requests.

Access the Agreement Desk
Agreements page by selecting Requests from
the left navigation area.

The Requests dashboard displays. The dashboard is a central hub for all requests for an
account. And from here, you can open and work with requests.

![A screenshot of the Requests page navigation menu.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/msb1736279079453/page/images/ad-newest-req-menu.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjIzMzAsInNoZWFmIjoibXNiMTczNjI3OTA3OTQ1MyJ9.GoBNzt4UzfH6v42l14jCZUm44nsaM8W8p7NZvhg8OyQ&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Requests page navigation menu.")

The dashboard give you a snapshot of requests and key information, including:

- **Title**: Enter text in the Title field.

  You
  can enter a text-only title of your choice. This title becomes the request's
  official title in Agreement Desk and throughout
  IAM.
- **Created at**: The date and time the request was created.
- **Due Date**: The targeted date for the request completion
- - **Last activity at**: The date and time of the latest action
    taken with the request.
- **Owner**: The owner of a request is assigned by the request creator.
- **Submitter**: The user who created the request. It may or may not be the
  current owner.
- **Request type**:The type of request, such as NDA or MSA.
- **Status** : A descriptive status of the request state:
  - Blocked
  - Business
  - HR Review
  - In Progress
  - Legal Review
  - Legal Review - Pending
  - Needs Work
  - New
  - Pending Approval
  - Ready for Signature
  - Waiting for Signature
- **Status type**: Indicates whether the request status is opened or closed. By
  default, only open requests display on the dashboard. You can use the search
  filters to display both open and closed or only closed requests.

By default, only open requests are displayed in the dashboard.

Note: Agreement Desk reports include only closed requests. To ensure
accurate reporting, it's a good practice to mark completed requests as
Closed. Closed requests marked as Open are not reflected in
the reports and will skew your results.
