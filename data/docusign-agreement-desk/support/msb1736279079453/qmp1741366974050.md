# Manage Agreement Communications

Learn how Agreement Desk messages work and how you can interact with
messages that are related to requests.

The Agreement Desk Messaging feature provides the ability to receive
and reply to messages.

Messaging in a request works just like the email that you use in your everyday life. You
can send a message, reply to a message, and forward a message. Recipients see the
message in their email inbox and can reply. The replies are captured in the request.

You can view the messages associated with a request. Refer to the Activity
Feed section of the Overview page.
