# How Does Agreement Desk Work?

Learn about how Agreement Desk works by reviewing a sample workflow that
demonstrates Agreement Desk features.

Agreement Desk is a centralized workspace designed to
simplify and accelerate the pre-signature phase of the agreement lifecycle. It
provides a single location for all your agreement work.

You can create new requests:

Directly in Agreement Desk
:   Start a new request directly within the platform's intuitive interface.

Submit through email
:   Email your request to a designated address, and Agreement Desk automatically
    transforms the email into a traceable task on your dashboard.

Once you create a request, you have a centralized hub for all subsequent actions.

- Easily collaborate on documents with your team
- Highlight and negotiate terms
- Send and receive approvals from various stakeholders
- Send the final document for signature

## Example workflow: new client contract

This scenario demonstrates how Agreement Desk works with
Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) features to complete an agreement
workflow.

This scenario follows an Account Executive (AE) as they work with their Legal team to
finalize a new client contract.

Request intake
:   An AE sends an email to Agreement Desk to request a new
    client contract. With the power of AI agents, Agreement Desk suggests what request type to select. It also helps complete the intake
    form based on information from the email and any attachments and
    documents.

Automated workflow
:   When the AE submits the web form, it kicks off a workflow. This
    workflow will automatically:

    - Change the request status to Legal Review
    - Add the Sales Director as a follower for visibility
    - Generate a draft contract document using Agreement Preparation and
      Document templates
    - Send the request for Approval to Legal and Finance

Collaboration and negotiation
:   The legal team is automatically notified of the new request.

    - An attorney reviews the document and uses in-app messages to email
      the revised contract to the client for their review
    - The client replies with their own changes and the changes are
      automatically captured in the request

Approvals and edits
:   An approval is sent to the finance team to get an extra discount approved.
    They use the Word Add-in to make final edits to the document before it’s
    ready.

Signature
:   The document is now ready for signature using Docusign eSignature.

Storage
:   Once signed, the final agreement is automatically stored in Docusign Agreement Manager, where key details are
    extracted for easy searching and reporting.
