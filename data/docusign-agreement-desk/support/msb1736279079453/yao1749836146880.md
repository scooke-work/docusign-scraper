# Permissions By License Type

Read about the permission levels and capabilities for free and full user licenses. The actions you can take with Agreement Desk requests and AI-Assisted Review (AIAR) playbooks differ by license type and level of permission.

In Agreement Desk, interaction with requests and [AI-Assisted Review](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=mapfd7284e6-b699-4527-8651-a133914111fd&topicId=confef1b68e-4d95-443d-a51c-7b04377874b9.html&_LANG=enus) playbooks differs by your
license type and permission level.

Agreement Desk offers two user license types: free and full. Each license type
has different levels of permissions, as outlined in these tables.

To submit an Agreement Desk request, the submitter needs to a user on a Docusign account used by Agreement Desk.

The user can be a Free User or Full License User.

For information about how to grant permissions to users, see the topic [Grant Agreement Desk Permissions to Users](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=mapf0ca9167-3825-4b36-a385-e98321f7adb5&topicId=tsk01779417-0383-47c8-b2a2-78f2a19df672.html&_LANG=enus).

For information about Docusign permissions, see the topic [Permission Profile Options](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=mfo1583277360424.html&_LANG=enus)﻿.
