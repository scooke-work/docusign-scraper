# Welcome to Agreement Desk

Read about Docusign Agreement Desk and how it can help accelerate your agreement lifecycle from initial request to final signature.

Note: Agreement Desk is offered to U.S. Docusign IAM customers on select plans. Contact your account team for
more information. To learn more, see the [Get from
request to signature faster](https://www.docusign.com/products/agreement-desk) article.

Agreement Desk is a collaborative hub to streamlines intake,
accelerates review, and centralizes approvals. With Agreement Desk,
agreements move from request to signature faster.
