# Create a Request through Email

Learn how to create anAgreement Deskrequest directly from your
email.

You can easily create an
Agreement Desk request from your email by sending
or forwarding an email, with or without an attachment, to Agreement Desk default or customized email address.

Note:

When submitting requests through email, only users within your account receive
confirmation email. External users do not receive this notification.

1. Send a new or forward an existing email addressed to the Agreement Desk email
   for your organization.

   See the topic [Locate your Agreement Desk Email Address](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=mapf0ca9167-3825-4b36-a385-e98321f7adb5&topicId=xlg1741793314719.html&_LANG=enus) for information.
2. Add a subject, body, and optionally attach documents (that are automatically
   added to the Documents list in the request) and send the
   email.

   Submitters that are in the account may receive the confirmation email with a
   link to complete the request intake form.

   Alternatively, users can go to the Requests  dashboard
   to view the new request, that may take a minute or so to show up.
3. Select your request to open it.

## Next steps

Update your request by assigning an owner, due date, and
creating an approval.
