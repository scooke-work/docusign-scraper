# Preview a Generated Document

## Before you begin

To see how your generated document will look, you can use the preview function. For the preview, you manually enter data for your sender fields. This data includes all agreement fields, custom fields, and third-party fields present on your document. You must provide a value for required fields.

Note: For third-party fields, actual field values are not available in the ATB preview. You can see how your document will look with sample data that you enter manually. But actual values from your connected account are available only when you generate an agreement from the template as part of a workflow created with Workflow Builder.

1. In your draft template, select Preview at the top right of the ATB.
2. Enter sample data to populate the sender fields. The experience is slightly
   different for document templates and envelope templates.

   - **Envelope Templates** present a form to first add the data and
     then display the generated document. Enter your data and then select
     Review.
   - **Document Templates** provide a side-by-side view with and
     Add field data on the left and your
     document on the right. Your document updates as you enter field
     data.
