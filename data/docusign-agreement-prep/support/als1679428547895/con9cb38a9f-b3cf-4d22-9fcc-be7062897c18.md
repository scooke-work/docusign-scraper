# Autoplacing Suggested Fields

AI autoplacement streamlines document template preparation by automatically identifying and positioning fields, reducing manual effort and accelerating template creation.

Autoplacing fields with AI streamlines the process of preparing document templates by
automatically identifying and positioning fields within your document. Instead of
manually dragging and dropping every data point, you can use AI-assisted field
suggestions to populate your template with the correct fields quickly.

## How autoplacement works

When you upload a DOCX file in the Agreement Template Builder (ATB) and select the AI icon, the AI scans the content for common agreement data points. It looks for specific text markers and contextual clues to suggest the best location for fields.

What are text markers? Text markers are optional placeholders that you can add to your
document to guide the AI toward precise field placement. While the AI can
detect fields automatically, using markers provides greater control over where specific fields are generated.

The AI recognizes common symbols such as:

- **Brackets:** `[[Effective Date]]`, `[[Customer Name]]`, `[[Sender Field]]`, or `[Sender Field]`
- **Braces:** `{{Signature}}`, `{{Date Signed}}`, `{Sender Field}`, or `{{Sender}}`
- **Less-than or greater-than sign:** `<Sender Field>`
- **Underlines**

By using these markers in your original document, you help the AI precisely map information to the correct fields in your Docusign account.

## Field-mapping logic

The AI evaluates field suggestions against three levels of your account data:

- **Agreement type fields:** Matches content to standard or custom fields already associated
  with the selected agreement type.
- **Account-level fields:** Matches content to fields that exist in your account but are not yet
  linked to the current agreement type.
- **New field suggestions:** If no existing field matches, the AI can suggest creating a new custom field (supporting Text, Date, or Number formats).

## Scope of AI autoplacement

- **Supported field types:** The AI primarily suggests Text, Number, and Date fields.
- **Multiple signers:** The AI places Signature and Date fields as Unassigned to prevent
  incorrect attribution. You can manually assign these fields to
  the correct recipient during the review process.

## Admin control

This feature is managed at the account level. Administrators can enable or disable AI-Assisted Field Suggestions for ATB on the Sending Settings page. When enabled, the feature is available to all users with permission to create document templates.
