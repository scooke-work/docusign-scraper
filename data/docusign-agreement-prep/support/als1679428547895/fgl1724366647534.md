# Why Can't I Use a Document Generation Template To Send an Envelope?

Review the reasons for why you are unable to send an envelope using a document
generation template.

When I prepare an envelope from a document generation template, the
SEND button is grayed out. I can't send the envelope.

Your eSignature account does not have the Document Generation Add-On. The template you are
using to send an envelope contains advanced functionality that requires the
add-on.

Your account administrator can
contact the Docusign Sales team to inquire about purchasing
the add-on. Visit [Contact
Sales](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales) for options to reach our team.
