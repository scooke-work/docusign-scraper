# About Agreement Preparation

Read about Agreement Preparation, a cornerstone of Docusign IAM that streamlines the creation of agreements across your organization.

Customer relationships are built on agreements. How they are prepared matters. Docusign Agreement Preparation enables customers to create and manage templates that result in professional agreements with accurate data—data that's connected across the entire agreement management process.

Agreement Preparation offers intuitive template creation, centralized template management, and integrations to external data sources to improve accuracy and efficiency.

Key capabilities:

- Create and manage templates using the Agreement Template Builder (ATB) or the Word Template Assistant (WTA)
- Connect data from preparation through the entire agreement management process with third-party systems connectivity and integrations.
- Generate professional-looking agreements with a single click.

Agreement Preparation streamlines how your organization creates agreements before they are sent for signature. Instead of manually copying content from old contracts, re‑entering data from multiple systems, or maintaining dozens of similar templates, Agreement Preparation gives you a centralized, connected way to build and manage templates and generate accurate, professional agreements at scale.

## What is Agreement Preparation?

Agreement Preparation focuses on the **"create" stage** of the agreement lifecycle: everything that happens between deciding an agreement is needed and sending it out for signature.

With Agreement Preparation, you can:

- **Create agreement templates** using intuitive, guided tools, including:

  - An online Agreement Template Builder (ATB) in the Docusign platform.
  - A Word-based plugin (Word Template Assistant) for teams who prefer working in Microsoft Word.
- **Manage templates centrally**, so you always know:

  - Which templates exist and where they are used.
  - That only current, approved language is available to users.
  - That updates are made in a single location and automatically reflected everywhere the template is used.
- **Generate agreements dynamically** that:

  - Pull in trusted data from systems of record like Salesforce or Microsoft Dynamics.
  - Apply approved, standardized language and formatting.
  - Use conditional logic and dynamic tables to reflect the details of each customer, product, or transaction.

## Why organizations use Agreement Preparation

Many organizations face challenges in agreement creation due to scattered templates, reliance on error-prone copy-pasting, inconsistent data sources, and difficulties in ensuring the use of approved language. These issues result in wasted time, inconsistent terms, and increased compliance risks.

Agreement Preparation is built to address these issues by offering:

- **Simplified template creation and administration** - Create templates for simple or complex scenarios, then manage them in one place instead of maintaining many near‑duplicate versions.
- **Faster agreement cycles** - Generate accurate, customer-specific agreements quickly, reducing manual editing and back‑and‑forth between teams.
- **Improved accuracy and compliance** - Use connected data and approved language so that agreements are consistent with corporate, legal, and regulatory requirements.

## Document generation

Using agreement preparation to build document generation templates, sets your organization up to produce professional-looking documents to send for signature. Document generation supports use cases that require customizing or personalizing documents before sending. The sender enters custom data using a data-entry form. The custom data is inserted seamlessly, generating finished documents that are highly customized. Signers receive the finished documents ready to review and sign.

There are two types of templates that you can build to power agreement preparation:

- Document templates - A reusable document with placeholders for data fields to customize the agreement, and recipient roles and fields. For more general information on document templates, see our [Document Templates](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=map332c3ebc-8fca-4fac-9b9f-c7206adc3985&topicId=conc8f06624-6ef6-40a3-bbc8-c5c7fe18746b.html&_LANG=enus) guide (Available for Docusign IAM plans only.)

- Envelope templates - A complete template for a Docusign eSignature envelope. They can contain multiple documents and document templates. For more general information on envelope templates, see our [Envelope Templates guide](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=xry1643227563338&topicId=dqj1578456412286.html&_LANG=enus)

Creating document generation templates is a core feature for Docusign IAM customers. Docusign eSignature customers can explore this functionality too and create eSignature templates with basic document generation features.

Note: All Docusign eSignature and Docusign IAM customers have access to create document generation templates. The Document Generation add-on for eSignature is required to send more than five trial envelopes from a document generation template that includes advanced features. The add-on includes additional functionality, such as [conditional content](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=als1679428547895&topicId=ziz1707785438108.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to show or hide content dynamically based on values entered in sender fields."), [dynamic tables](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=als1679428547895&topicId=zhi1724987563409.html&_LANG=enus "Read about dynamic tables. Dynamic tables are designed for agreements that include tables with a variable number of rows, depending on the data provided."), and [API sending](https://developers.docusign.com/docs/esign-rest-api/esign101/concepts/documents/document-generation/). For more information about the Document Generation for eSignature add-on, contact the  [Docusign Sales team](https://www.docusign.com/contact-sales).

## Use cases for agreement preparation

Document generation is a valuable solution for high-volume use cases that require customizing or personalizing documents before sending. The potential applications are vast. Here are a few use cases to illustrate the value of document generation.

Vehicle leasing
:   A leasing agent enters the customer's personal information and lease terms in a form to generate a fully customized lease.

HR offer letter
:   A large enterprise hires hundreds of people each quarter. An HR specialist generates offer letters customized with each prospective employee's information. Candidates receive personalized offer letters, helping them feel like valued individuals. The entry form for the custom data makes it fast and easy to enter each candidate's offer details accurately. If the HR specialist needs to send many offer letters at once, they can use bulk send. Each letter is customized with the offer details coming from the bulk send list.

Detailed contracts
:   A major corporation with complex contracts needs to ensure they are completed accurately. The data-entry form simplifies the sending process, guiding the sender through a list of all the data required to customize the contract.
