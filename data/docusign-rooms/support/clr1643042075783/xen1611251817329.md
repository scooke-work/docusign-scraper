# Share a Document To Make It Visible to Other Room Members

Learn how to give other room members access to your documents. Only you can see your
documents in Docusign Rooms unless you explicitly share them with others.

## Before you begin

Before you begin, you must own the document you want to
share or have a room role with Share any document
permission.

Note: Only you can see the documents you add to a room unless you explicitly share them
with others. However, there are exceptions. Documents are shared automatically
between room owners. Also, you might have a room role that shares documents with
other people automatically.

1. Open the room.
2. Select the DOCUMENTS tab.
3. Select the document to open it.
4. Select ![rooms-document-share-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsdocumentshareicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3MDgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.pKCSHqEE9jnM6O3IfvmQkUnTdGJTmqKMbTp908UnFFg&_LANG=enus "rooms-document-share-icon") in the toolbar.
5. Select the room member(s) to share the document with.

   ![rooms-document-share](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsdocumentshare.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3MDgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.pKCSHqEE9jnM6O3IfvmQkUnTdGJTmqKMbTp908UnFFg&_LANG=enus "rooms-document-share")

   Note: To revoke someone's
   access, clear the blue checkmark next to their name. If you see a gray
   checkmark next to someone's name, you can't remove their access because it
   was given to them by someone else.
6. Select DONE.
