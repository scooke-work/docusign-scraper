# Attach a Task List to a Room

A task list is a group of related tasks predefined by your company. For example, your company
can create a task list that contains documents and activities commonly needed for
commercial transactions. Defining task lists, and attaching them to rooms, ensures
process consistency across the company.

Depending on your company setup, task lists might auto-populate to rooms. All rooms include a
General task list where users can add individual
tasks.

Once attached to a room, task lists display on the TASKS tab in collapsible sections that show
the task name, assignee, and due date. If a document is assigned to the task, the
document name is included.

To attach a task list, you need a room role with permission.

1. Open the room.
2. Click the TASKS tab.
3. Click ACTIONS >  Attach task list.
4. Choose the task list.
5. Click ATTACH.

Note: To remove a task list, click ACTIONS >  Remove task list. A permission controls the ability to remove task lists.
