# Use Room History To Review the Audit Trail for a Transaction

Learn how to see what has happened in a transaction over time (who, what, when) in
Docusign Rooms.

The room HISTORY tab details what has
happened in the room over time (who, what, when, etc.). This is the audit trail of all actions
taken within the room.

Tip: You can also view the
history of many items in a room (such as documents and envelopes) by opening the item and
going to history.

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Open the room.
3. Select the History tab.

The room history displays. Use ACTIONS >  Export Room Summary to export the list to a PDF or CSV file.
