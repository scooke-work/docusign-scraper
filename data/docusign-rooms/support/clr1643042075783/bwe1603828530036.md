# Add a Task to a Room

If your room role has permission to add tasks, you can add new tasks to a room.

1. Open the room.
2. Click the TASKS tab.
3. Click ADD.
4. Choose which list you'd like to add the task to, then click
   NEXT.

   If you don't have permission to add tasks to lists, or there are no task lists in the
   room, you won't see this step and the task will be added to the
   General list after you save it.
5. Complete the detail, document, people, and reminder information for the
   task.
6. Click SAVE.
