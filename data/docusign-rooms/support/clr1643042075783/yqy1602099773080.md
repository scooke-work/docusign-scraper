# Close a Member Account To Delete It Permanently

Learn how to close a member account in Docusign Rooms. You can use
these steps to close and permanently delete a member account.

When you close a member's account, you delete it irreversibly. Before you can close the
account, you are prompted to transfer all the member's owned rooms.

Documents in the member's Inbox and My Docs folders are permanently deleted after account
closure, so they might want to transfer them beforehand.

Important:

When you close a member's Rooms account, it doesn't automatically close their
eSignature account. It depends on where the account was created or closed: in
Rooms or eSignature. If you create an eSignature account and have Rooms
provisioned, a Rooms user account isn't created automatically. However, if you
create a new user in Rooms, it will automatically create an eSignature
account.

Note: There isn't a "reopen" option after closing an
account. If you must re-add a member, you must add them again with a new account.
You can use the same name or email, but it's a brand-new account.

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
3. On the MEMBERS tab, select the account to close.
4. Select Close Account in the left navigation pane.

   Any rooms that require transfer are displayed. These are rooms the member owns or rooms
   that contain documents that transfer to new document owners.

   ![Rooms close account modal.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomscloseaccount.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE1NTgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.pwPZBXB3K8DSbU4ZlcEhUDLwWOyhk6kBX_EzBI4qBac&_LANG=enus "Rooms close account modal.")
5. For each room, select a new owner from the Select Member
   drop-down list. Use the drop-down list at the top of the page or at the office
   level to transfer multiple rooms to the same person.
6. Select CLOSE ACCOUNT.
7. Select CLOSE ACCOUNT to confirm.
