# Considerations When Inviting External People to a Room

Read about the importance of assigning external roles to people outside of your company
when inviting them to a room in Docusign Rooms.

When you invite someone to a room who does not belong to your company account, you need to give
that person an *external role*. People with external roles have limited permissions
and don't have access to administrative functions.

People with external roles display the EXT badge on their people
card.

![rooms-external-badge](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsexternalbadge.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MjUsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.2xvUHX6GWIOT0SDAyRhC3_DPyJQW__au2riWEbIIp1A&_LANG=enus "rooms-external-badge")

External roles also display the EXT badge in the role selection list.
When inviting people with external roles, you can choose a role that is equal to or
lower than your own role permissions.

external roles that are equal or lower to their role.
