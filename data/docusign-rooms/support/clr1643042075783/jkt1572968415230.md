# Use the Docusign Rooms Directory To Find Members of Your Company

Read about how the company directory is useful when locating coworker information in
Docusign Rooms, such as inviting someone to a room or adding an envelope recipient.

The directory is a read-only list of all the members of your company. To access the
directory, select your profile photo, then select Contacts > Directory.

![Directory tab.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/Directory.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NTMsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.Ne8SmI7ImWxUh8RCHoSWJxZOa3nt9lRbQMGN-C8X1ag&_LANG=enus "Directory tab.")

Select VIEW to view a member's name, role, and contact
information.

The directory is helpful in other areas of Rooms when you want to find a company member. For
example, when inviting someone to a room or adding an envelope recipient, click ![select contact icon.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/selectcontacticon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NTMsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.Ne8SmI7ImWxUh8RCHoSWJxZOa3nt9lRbQMGN-C8X1ag&_LANG=enus "select contact icon.") to open the directory and search members. You don't have to update the directory
since members manage this information for themselves.
