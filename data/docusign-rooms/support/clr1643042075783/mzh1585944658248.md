# Manage Room Detail Fields That Capture Contact Information

Contact fields display at the bottom of the Room Details settings page in a
CONTACTS section. Each contact type (Buyer, Agent, Service
Provider, etc.) has its own group of fields that allow your members to capture contact
information (name, phone, email, etc).

Contact fields are added differently than other detail fields. To add a new contact field,
select ADD NEW >  Contact Field.

![room-detail-admin-contact](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomdetailadmincontact.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE0ODUsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.840XrGxIJs4Pb8vUavMMnYBtKxeTtkTr5FHdrQRQ8dg&_LANG=enus "room-detail-admin-contact")

Select the Contact Field Type and the display name for the field. Then,
select SAVE to add it to room details.
