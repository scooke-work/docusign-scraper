# Connect Your Docusign Rooms Account to Dropbox, Google Drive, or Box

If your company uses Dropbox, Google Drive, or Box, enable
these integrations on your account so you can add documents from these cloud storage services
to Docusign Rooms.

1. Log in to your Rooms account.
2. Select your profile picture or initials in the upper right, then select
   Preferences.
3. Select Integrations.
4. Enable the cloud storage services to make available when adding documents to Docusign Rooms.

Any cloud storage providers you enabled now display when adding
documents.

![rooms-add-document-cloud](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsadddocumentcloud.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE1ODcsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.-XdN287MqpDXj-SKVK34UAwLs3cllY_hLY8kRnND6uQ&_LANG=enus "rooms-add-document-cloud")

You will be prompted for your login credentials for each service the first
time you select it.
