# Assign Form Libraries or Packets to an Office

## Before you begin

- This feature must be enabled on your company account. Contact your Account Team
  to enable this functionality for you.
- You need the following permissions enabled in your company role:
  - Manage form packets
  - Manage form libraries

Use this procedure when starting with an office. You can assign multiple form libraries or
packets to an office this way.

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the header.
3. Select the FORMS tab.
4. Select Add > Assign Office.
5. Click SELECT, then select the office you would like to
   work with.
6. In the Libraries field, select
   EDIT, then choose the libraries to assign to the
   office.
7. Select ADD.

Users see the filtered form lists immediately.
