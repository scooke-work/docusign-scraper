# Use Tasks To Keep Track of Transaction Activities and Due Dates

Read about how tasks keep your organized by tracking what items must be completed
before you can close a transaction.

Tasks help you keep organized. They keep track of the documents and activities you must
complete before you can close a transaction.

Select the TASKS tab within a room to view tasks applied to the
transaction.

![rooms-tasks-tab](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomstaskstab.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4MTQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.GBudLjcNg06n0VyuwC1J8DLyHkE93okpOdsSaRQOEBo&_LANG=enus "rooms-tasks-tab")

If you created a new room and already see tasks in the TASKS tab, your company uses templates
to pre-populate rooms with tasks.

Each task has an action that is required by the assignee. If a task requires review, it's
sent to the reviewer(s) when the assignee marks it complete.

Your responsibility on the task and your room role determine what you can do with tasks. For
example, an assignee can only edit a task if their room role has permission.

Tip: To check your permissions in a room, go to the
PEOPLE tab, click ![3-dot-people-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/3dotpeopleicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4MTQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.GBudLjcNg06n0VyuwC1J8DLyHkE93okpOdsSaRQOEBo&_LANG=enus "3-dot-people-icon") on your card, then choose Room role information. Expand the
Tasks section to view your task permissions.
