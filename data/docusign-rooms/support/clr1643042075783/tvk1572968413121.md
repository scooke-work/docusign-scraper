# Use Your Docusign Rooms Inbox

As a Docusign Rooms member, you have an inbox that nobody else in your company can see
or access. Read about the features of this inbox.

As a Rooms member, you have an inbox that nobody else in your company can see or access. You have an assigned email address for inbound emails to your inbox.

The Inbox tab displays a red circle with the number of new documents
in your inbox.

![Inbox tab.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsinbox.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3NDcsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.mmPk0m96rQhzXV2rYevN0uCy4ErwzTyO8F7o8AoWhwM&_LANG=enus "Inbox tab.")

Tip: You can customize your Inbox email address in Preferences > Inbox Details.

Documents can be sent to your inbox and then moved or copied elsewhere. In the upper left,
the Document Finder lets you search the inbox for a document by its name. Next to the Document
Finder is a drop-down menu that lets you filter documents.

Right-click a document to take action on it, such as edit, move, copy, or download. You can
also click a document to view or take action on it.

Note: Document permissions do not apply in your
inbox. Permissions only apply to documents in a room.
