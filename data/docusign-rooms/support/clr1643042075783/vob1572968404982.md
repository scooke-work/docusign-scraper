# Invite Someone to a Room to Collaborate on a Transaction

Learn how to add someone to a room to share documents, messages, and tasks with them in
Docusign Rooms. You can invite both internal and external people to collaborate in transaction
rooms.

## Before you begin

Before you begin, you need a room role with Add users
to rooms permission.

In a room, the PEOPLE tab displays
everyone who has access to the room. If you want to collaborate with someone who isn't already
in the room, you need to invite them in with an appropriate role. Their room role gives them
the features they need to work within the room and can be different than their company
role.

Note: Some people at your company may be
added to rooms automatically based on their role permissions.

These steps show you how to invite someone from the
PEOPLE tab of a room. You can also invite someone by clicking the
Invite link in the closing package.

1. Open the room.
2. Select the PEOPLE tab.
3. Select INVITE.

   The Invite to Room modal displays.

   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/invitetoroomRE.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MjAsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.tseWKQl0UG-HQmxlOb7j07XwM19lbu40wG7PdcGKeTs&_LANG=enus)
4. Locate or add the contact by doing one of the following:
   - Begin typing the person's email address in the Email field.
     Suggested contacts from the address book and directory display as you type.
   - If a contact isn't found, Invite the person to the room or
     Invite & Save to save the contact to your address book
     after you send the invite.
   - Select ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/selectcontacticon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MjAsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.tseWKQl0UG-HQmxlOb7j07XwM19lbu40wG7PdcGKeTs&_LANG=enus) to look up the person in your address book or the company
     directory.
5. Select the contact's Transaction side in the room.
6. For internal people, the person's company role displays in the Room
   Role field. Select a different role to give the person a different role in
   the room.

   Note: See the important considerations
   below when choosing a room role.
   - Select VIEW PERMISSIONS to review the detailed permissions
     the person will have in the room.
   - If you give someone a role other than their company role, they might have fewer
     permissions. They will see a banner at the top of the room, notifying them that
     their role is different.
   - For internal roles, you can select an equal or lower role than the invitee's
     company role.
   - Assign external roles to users outside of your company. These roles display
     EXT in the role selection list.
   - The badges SIMPLIFIED,
     COLLABORATE, or SIGNING display next
     to roles that can access the closing package with a unique view.
7. Select INVITE to send the email invitation.
