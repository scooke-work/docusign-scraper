# Add a Region

Learn how to add an office region in Docusign Rooms. Regions give administrators more
granular control over groups of offices so people can belong to or oversee entire
regions.

## Before you begin

You need the Manage company regions
permission enabled on your company role to complete these steps.

Regions are not required, but they add another layer
to your company hierarchy. Regions give administrators more granular control over groups of
offices so people can belong to or oversee entire regions.

1. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
2. Select the COMPANY tab.
3. Select Regions.
4. Select ADD REGION.
5. Type a name for the new region.
6. Select SAVE.
