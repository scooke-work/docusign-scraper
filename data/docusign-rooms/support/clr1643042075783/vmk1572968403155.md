# Get Started With Rooms

Read about getting started with transaction rooms in Docusign Rooms for Real Estate and
how you can create a room for a new listing or purchase, add relevant information, and monitor
transactions.

Rooms are where you will spend most of your time in Docusign Rooms. After you create a
room, you add tasks, documents, people, and envelopes with documents to be sent to
recipients. As the transaction progresses, you return to the room to monitor the state of
the transaction and keep track of the tasks and documents you need to complete and finalize
the transaction.

The Rooms tab lists all the rooms you created or have been invited to join. Depending on
your role and permissions, you may be automatically added to rooms. If you oversee offices
or regions, you'll see rooms for those locations.

Rooms are organized within tabs based on whether they are: Active,
in Review, or Closed. Click a room to open it.
If you have permission to create rooms, click NEW to create a new
room.

What you can do in a room is based on your room role and that role’s permissions. Your room
role was assigned to you when you were invited into the room or created the room.

Depending on your role, you might see the following tabs within a room: Details, Tasks,
Documents, People, Envelopes, Messages, and History.

![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsroomtabs.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjEzNDAsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.hrCGR2CwwArPrw718bBvZQ0llMgvU5Kll8NAAGWODYI&_LANG=enus)

Check the ACTIONS menu for a list of some of the actions you can
take in the room. The options available in this menu depend on your permissions and which
tab you are working in.
