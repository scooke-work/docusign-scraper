# Update Your Email Notification Preferences

Learn how to enable or disable email notification messages from Docusign Rooms.
Reviewing these settings helps you limit notifications to only those you want to
receive.

You receive an email notification when activities occur within your transactions in
Docusign Rooms. If you don't want to receive certain information, you can turn them off.

Note:

- Rooms sends some notifications automatically based on your role or responsibilities,
  such as when a task list is declined or a room is rejected. Because these items require
  your attention, you can't turn off notifications for them.
- As of September 2024, Rooms no longer sends notifications for completed and voided
  envelopes.

1. Log in to your Rooms account.
2. Select your profile picture or initials in the upper right, then select
   Preferences.
3. Select Notifications.
4. Enable or disable your Daily Digest email.

   This email summarizes the activity in all your rooms over the last 24 hours and gives
   you a preview of upcoming tasks and due dates. If no activity occurs in any of your rooms,
   you don't receive a Daily Digest email.
5. Select the check box next to the remaining notifications you want to receive.
6. Select SAVE CHANGES.
