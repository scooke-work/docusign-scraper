# Document Management in Docusign Rooms

Read about all the actions you can take on documents in Docusign Rooms.

Add your documents to store and manage them in Rooms. You can then instantly share them with
other members within Rooms or email them to individuals who do not yet have an account.
Document sharing is explicit, but your admin might have set up automatic document sharing for
certain roles in your company.
