# Change a Member's Company Role

## Before you begin

You need a company role with at least one of the following
permissions enabled:

- Edit role for members with lower access level
- Edit role for members with the same access level

A member's company role determines what they can do in Docusign Rooms and is their default
role when creating rooms or being invited to rooms internally.

If you have permission to edit a member's role, you can change it anytime. All
changes are tracked in role history.

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
3. On the MEMBERS tab, select the member whose role you are
   changing.
4. Select Role Information.
5. Change the member's role in the Company role
   field.

   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomschangecompanyrole.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE1MzQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.tqOY9jlYA6pzMb_caTXcSZfUXxWP2EJ1SAX6w_PWb7A&_LANG=enus)
6. Select SAVE.

If the new role affects the member's access to new or existing
rooms, you will receive a confirmation message after selecting
SAVE.
