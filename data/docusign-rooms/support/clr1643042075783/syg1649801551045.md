# Use Task List Templates To Standardize Tasks

Read about how task list templates help users keep track of documents and activities in
a room.

Task lists are checklists that help users keep track of documents and activities in a
room. Task list templates contain common transaction documents and activities and ensure
process consistency across the company.

Your company can use [room templates](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=clr1643042075783&topicId=doz1572968393138.html&_LANG=enus) to populate
new rooms with task lists automatically. Otherwise, users with permission to add task
lists to rooms can do so manually by selecting ACTIONS >  Attach task list on the room TASKS tab.
