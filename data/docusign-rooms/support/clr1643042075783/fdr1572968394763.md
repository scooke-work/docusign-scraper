# Change a Member's Access Level, Office, Region, or eSignature Permission Profile

## Before you begin

You need a company role with at least one of the following
permissions enabled:

- Manage members with lower access level
- Manage members with the same access level

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
3. On the MEMBERS tab, select the member whose information
   you are changing.
4. On the Member Information tab, update the member's
   access level, offices, regions, or eSign permission profile.
5. Select SAVE.
