# Manage Your Docusign Rooms Contacts

Read about how you can use the directory and address book in Docusign Rooms to quickly
access information for your internal and external contacts.

Access and manage your contacts directly within Rooms by selecting your profile photo, then
selecting Contacts.

Book.
