# Add Regions and Offices To Define Your Company Structure

Read about how administrators can add regions and offices to define your company
hierarchy in Docusign Rooms. When you add members, you select their access level and their
associated offices or regions, limiting their visibility to transactions outside their
areas.

Optionally add regions and offices to your company Rooms account to define a company
structure.

Regions are not required, but they add another layer to your company hierarchy. Regions give
you more granular control over groups of offices so people can belong to or oversee entire
regions.

Rooms provides one default office initially. You can rename this office or re-purpose it. If
you added regions, offices can belong to a region, or stand alone.

When you add members, you select their access level and their associated offices or regions.
For example, if you add a member with Region access level, you specify
the region(s) they will oversee. Members can belong to multiple offices or entire regions.

Note: Once you have added members to regions or
offices, you can't delete them. If you want to delete a region or office, you first need to
reassign members to a new region or office.
