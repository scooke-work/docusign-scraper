# Add an Office Location

Learn how to add an office location in Docusign Rooms. Offices and regions determine
what members can access in your company account.

## Before you begin

You need the Manage company offices
permission enabled on your company role to complete these steps.

One default office is provided initially, but
administrators can rename and re-purpose it. If you added regions, offices can belong to a
region, or stand alone.

1. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
2. Select the COMPANY tab.
3. Select Offices.
4. Select ADD OFFICE.
5. Type the office name.
6. Select the region the office is associated with from the drop-down menu.
7. Enter the office contact information.
8. Select SAVE.
