# Create Room Detail Fields To Track the Different Stages of a Room

Use the Company room status field in the ROOM
INFORMATION section of room details to define the different stages of a room.
For example, different statuses of a room might be New
Listing, Offer Made, Offer Received, Under Contract, and In Escrow. Users can change
the status throughout the transaction lifecycle.

Users see these statuses in:

- A drop-down menu on the room Details tab when setting the status of a room
- Search filters on the Rooms tab when searching rooms by status
- In the borrower's view to indicate the status of a
  loan
