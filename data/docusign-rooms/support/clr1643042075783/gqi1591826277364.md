# Email Documents Directly to a Room

Learn how to send an email attachment directly to the Documents tab of a room. Storing
transaction documents in Docusign Rooms helps you stay organized and prevents you from
emailing files back and forth.

You can send an email attachment directly to the
Documents tab of a room by including the room ID in the email
subject line. Storing transaction documents in Docusign Rooms helps you stay organized
and prevents you from emailing files back and forth.

Tip: To verify your Docusign Rooms inbound email address, select your profile
photo or initials in the Rooms header, then select Preferences >  Inbox Details.

1. Log in to Docusign Rooms.
2. Select the Rooms tab in the header.
3. Right-click the room you are sending documents to, then select Copy
   Room ID.

   ![rooms-copy-room-ID](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomscopyroomID.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3MzEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.iw1huXbGXK43S_8qc-rMWJuYIer7NcmcqALz3zTGDyg&_LANG=enus "rooms-copy-room-ID")
4. Open your email application and start an email message.
5. In the To line, type your Docusign Rooms email
   address.
6. Attach the documents.
7. Press Ctrl + V in the email
   subject line to paste the room number.
8. Type # before the room number, as shown in the example
   below.

   ![rooms-attach-document-for-email](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsattachdocumentforemail.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3MzEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.iw1huXbGXK43S_8qc-rMWJuYIer7NcmcqALz3zTGDyg&_LANG=enus "rooms-attach-document-for-email")
9. Send the email.

After a few moments, the documents display in the
Documents tab of the room.
