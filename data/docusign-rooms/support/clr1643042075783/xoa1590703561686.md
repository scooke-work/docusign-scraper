# Email Documents to Your Docusign Rooms Inbox

Learn how to send email attachments to your Docusign Rooms inbox. Once they arrive, you
can move them to rooms to share them with others.

You can send email attachments to your Docusign Rooms inbox. Once they arrive, move
them to rooms to share them with others.

Tip: Verify your Rooms inbound email address by
clicking your profile photo in the Rooms header, then selecting Preferences >  Inbox Details.

1. Open your email application and start an email message.
2. In the To line, type your Docusign Rooms email
   address.
3. Attach the documents.
4. Send the email.

After a few moments, the documents display in your
inbox.
