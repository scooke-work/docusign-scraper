# Configure Your Company Settings

Learn how to set up your Docusign Rooms company contact information, logo, currency,
privacy, and security settings. Administrators should complete this information as soon as your
company account is active.

## Before you begin

Before you begin, you need these permissions enabled on your
company role:

- Manage company account settings
- Manage company logo

As the company Rooms administrator, set up your basic company settings as soon as your
account is active.

1. Log in to your Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
3. Select the COMPANY tab.
4. Update your company information on the Company settings
   page.

   |  |  |
   | --- | --- |
   | Contact Information | Add your company name, website, support email address, and physical address. This information is visible to your users and the Docusign support team. |
   | Branding | Upload your logo to add your company branding to Rooms. Your logo replaces the Docusign logo in the upper right corner of the application. The logo also displays in other areas, such as email notification messages. |
   | Currency | Select the primary currency for your company. |
   | Privacy & Security Hide contents of room messages in email notifications | When this option is selected, message content does not display in room message email notifications. Users need to click a link to see the message in Rooms. |
5. Use the options in left navigation menu to continue setting up your company. Your
   access to these options depends on your permissions.
