# Considerations When Disabling Roles Already in Use

When disabling a role *already in use* at your company:

- If company members are assigned the role, Rooms will notify you that the role is in
  use. You will have the opportunity to choose a new role for these members to replace
  them all at once or jump to a list of members so you can edit their roles
  individually.
- Users can keep using the role in existing rooms. For example, users can select the
  role when inviting someone to a room or changing someone's room role.
- The role will not be available in new rooms.
- If you have task list templates that use the role, you don't have to update these
  templates right away. You can keep using them, but the next time you edit the
  template, Rooms will ask you to replace the disabled role with an enabled one.
