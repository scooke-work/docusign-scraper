# Remove a Detail Field From Company Rooms

Remove detail fields that your company doesn't use. Removing unused fields places
them in the library. This reduces clutter and makes it easier for users to find the
information they need.

Important: When you remove a field in Room
Details settings, you are removing it from all rooms. If the field is in use, you
can still remove it, and Rooms will retain any data that users might have added. If
you add the field back in later, users will see their data again.

To remove a detail field:

1. Log in to your Rooms account.
2. Go to Settings >  Company >  Room Details.
3. Select ![3-dot-people-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/3dotpeopleicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE0ODEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.OzgLyNRGZnzsaU_KpNL6P8sN5ZWJznW619iV56M1Vf0&_LANG=enus "3-dot-people-icon") for the field to remove.
4. Select Remove from details.

   ![room-details-admin-remove-field](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomdetailsadminremovefield.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE0ODEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.OzgLyNRGZnzsaU_KpNL6P8sN5ZWJznW619iV56M1Vf0&_LANG=enus "room-details-admin-remove-field")
5. Select REMOVE to confirm.
