# Lock a Member Account To Prevent Them From Logging in Temporarily

## Before you begin

You need at least one of the following permissions enabled
on your company role:

- Manage members with lower access level
- Manage members with the same access level

Lock an account when you want to prevent a member from logging into Docusign Rooms
temporarily. When the member tries to log in, they see the message "Your account is
currently locked." All of their rooms, room documents, and documents in their My
Docs and Inbox folders remain as-is.

Note:

Locking an account in Rooms only prevents the member from logging into Rooms.
They can continue to log in to their eSignature account.

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
3. On the MEMBERS tab, select the account to lock.
4. Type a reason for locking the account in the Locked
   Reason field.

   A reason is required.
5. Select LOCK ACCOUNT.

Members with locked accounts display Locked
in the STATUS column.
