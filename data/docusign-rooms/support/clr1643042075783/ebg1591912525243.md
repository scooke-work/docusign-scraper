# Send an Email Conversation to Docusign Rooms as a PDF Document

Learn how to save an email conversation as a PDF file by sending it to your Docusign Rooms email address.

To save an important email conversation, send the email
message to your Docusign Rooms email address with a special code in the subject line.
Docusign Rooms converts it to a PDF file and sends it to a room or your inbox.

Tip: To verify your Docusign Rooms inbound email address, select your profile
photo or initials in the Rooms header, then select Preferences >  Inbox Details.

1. Open your email application and start an email message.
2. In the To line, type your Docusign Rooms email
   address.
3. In the Subject line, type #room-ID
   #pdf, where room-ID is the room ID number
   displayed at the top of the room.

   To send the email conversation to your Docusign Rooms Inbox, exclude the room ID.
4. To add a title to the document, type a subject after #room-ID
   #pdf. For example, #152219 #pdf Disclosure
   Agreement.

   Important: Spaces are required between the
   #room-ID, #pdf, and the subject.

   ![rooms-email-convo](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsemailconvo.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3MzgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.krFPY_Vn0RBUaWuVAdbA9cba2S9mBijwZ45zm8VQZhw&_LANG=enus "rooms-email-convo")
5. Send the email.

   After a few moments, the PDF displays in the room
   DOCUMENTS tab or
   Inbox.
