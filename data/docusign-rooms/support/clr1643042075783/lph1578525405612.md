# Check Your Company Role Permissions

Learn how to check your company role and its permissions in Docusign Rooms. Reviewing
these permissions helps members understand what they are permitted to do in the
application.

Your Docusign Rooms administrator assigned you a company role when giving you access to the
system. Your company role determines what you can do in Docusign Rooms and is your
default role when you are invited to rooms.

Review your company role and permissions in your Rooms profile to understand what you
are permitted to do in Docusign Rooms.

You view the following information within your Rooms profile:

- Your role name
- The permissions associated with your role
- The history of changes to your role

Note: You can have a different role in a room than your
company role, and therefore, fewer permissions in that room.

1. Log in to your Rooms account.
2. In the upper right, select your initials or profile picture.
3. Select Preferences from the drop-down menu.

   ![rooms-preferences-select](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspreferencesselect.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MDEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.CjJ0caCFbnPEdi8CzXm4wbT9kUCtj8wdoRC1u327ZQo&_LANG=enus "rooms-preferences-select")

   The Profile tab displays.
4. Select VIEW INFO next to your role name in the
   Role field.

   The Role Information modal displays.
5. Select a section title to display the permissions enabled for your role.

   ![Full list of permissions for a user's role.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsroleinformation.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MDEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.CjJ0caCFbnPEdi8CzXm4wbT9kUCtj8wdoRC1u327ZQo&_LANG=enus "Full list of permissions for a user's role.")

   Your permissions display a green checkmark. Permissions you do not have
   are missing a green checkmark.
6. Select HISTORY to view changes made to your role.
