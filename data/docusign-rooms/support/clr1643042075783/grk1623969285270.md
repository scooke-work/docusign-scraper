# View Which Real Estate Forms You Are Sharing

Use the Preferences >  Integrations page to see who can access your forms, or has shared their form
access with you.

1. Select your profile photo or initials in the upper-right corner of the Rooms
   header, then select Preferences.
2. Select Integrations in the left menu.
3. Review the sharing information in the Docusign Forms
   Settings section.

   If you share forms within a listed form provider (you are sharing forms, or
   forms are shared with you), the shared icon ![rooms-preferences-shared-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspreferencessharedicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTcsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.fODVuYxRVOSol09rUQWexPkzgY6WVzzi0bgovShvi9A&_LANG=enus "rooms-preferences-shared-icon") displays in the Sharing column.

   ![rooms-preferences-form-settings](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspreferencesformsettings.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTcsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.fODVuYxRVOSol09rUQWexPkzgY6WVzzi0bgovShvi9A&_LANG=enus "rooms-preferences-form-settings")

Select ![rooms-preferences-right-arrow](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspreferencesrightarrow.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTcsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.fODVuYxRVOSol09rUQWexPkzgY6WVzzi0bgovShvi9A&_LANG=enus "rooms-preferences-right-arrow") for a form provider.
