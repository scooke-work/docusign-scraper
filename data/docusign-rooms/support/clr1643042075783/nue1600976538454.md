# Schedule an Overnight Process to Bulk Update a Member's Room Access

## Before you begin

You need a company role with at least one of the following
permissions enabled:

- Manage members with lower access level
- Manage members with the same access level

Follow these steps to update a member's room access in bulk. Each member can only have one
update scheduled at a time.

1. In Docusign Rooms, go to Settings >  MEMBERS.
2. Select the member.
3. Select Bulk Updates.
4. Select UPDATE ROOM ACCESS.

   ![rooms-bulk-member-updates](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsbulkmemberupdatesselected.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE1NDMsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.elZvOrb6MLd2xtXwHTOFmnptQoMMurB6qo183OvOi24&_LANG=enus "rooms-bulk-member-updates")
5. Choose one or both options:

   - Add the member to rooms (created by peers or subordinates) in their
     offices
   - Remove the member from rooms (to which they were previously auto-added)
     in offices they no longer belong to
6. Select UPDATE.

The update displays in the event list with the status
Job scheduled.
