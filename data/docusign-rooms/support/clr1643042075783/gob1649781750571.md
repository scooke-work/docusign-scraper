# Assign Offices to a Form Library or Packet

## Before you begin

- This feature must be enabled on your company account. Contact your Account Team
  to enable this functionality for you.
- You need the following permissions enabled in your company role:
  - Manage form packets
  - Manage form libraries

Use this procedure when starting with the form library or packet. You can assign multiple
offices to a library or packet this way.

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the header.
3. Select the FORMS tab.
4. Use the filters at the top of the page to show Libraries
   only, packets only, or
   Libraries and packets.
5. Select ![3-dot-people-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/3dotpeopleicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE1MDMsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.kwQDZA_EwneeMqgnBd25cSUQqwdXQgBFBCtIDB4-iAQ&_LANG=enus "3-dot-people-icon") next to a form library or packet in the list.
6. Select Edit offices.

   ![rooms-forms-admin-edit-offices](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsformsadmineditoffices.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE1MDMsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.kwQDZA_EwneeMqgnBd25cSUQqwdXQgBFBCtIDB4-iAQ&_LANG=enus "rooms-forms-admin-edit-offices")
7. Select Edit.
8. Select the offices to map to the packet or library.
9. Select USE SELECTED.
10. Select SAVE.

Users see the filtered form lists immediately.
