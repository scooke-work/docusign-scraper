# Review Individual Tasks

You will receive an email notification when a task requires review. Follow these steps to
review individual tasks.

Note: You don't need special permission to review tasks; any reviewer on a task can
review it. Some people at your company might have permission to review any task,
even if they aren't added as a reviewer.

1. Open the task(s) to review, using one of the methods below.
   - To review tasks one at a time: On your TASKS dashboard or TASKS
     tab in a room, click ![rooms-task-approve](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomstaskapprove.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NDEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.S6UfUyfcNeQdP9oHEgfu-qSXGvEVx2FHc7FgM6v21K8&_LANG=enus "rooms-task-approve") for a task to approve or ![rooms-task-decline](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomstaskdecline.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NDEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.S6UfUyfcNeQdP9oHEgfu-qSXGvEVx2FHc7FgM6v21K8&_LANG=enus "rooms-task-decline") to decline.
   - To review multiple tasks: In the room TASKS tab, select the
     circle next to the task(s) to review, then choose ![rooms-tasks-review-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomstasksreviewicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NDEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.S6UfUyfcNeQdP9oHEgfu-qSXGvEVx2FHc7FgM6v21K8&_LANG=enus "rooms-tasks-review-icon") in the toolbar.

   The task review page opens with the first task displayed.

   ![rooms-tasks-review-full-page](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomstasksreviewfullpage.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NDEsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.S6UfUyfcNeQdP9oHEgfu-qSXGvEVx2FHc7FgM6v21K8&_LANG=enus "rooms-tasks-review-full-page")
2. At Select One, choose whether to
   Approve or Decline the
   task.

   Note: If you are reviewing multiple tasks and would like to skip one, click
   SKIP to jump to the next task.
3. If you selected Decline, select who you'd like to send
   the task back to using the Return task to field (the
   assignee or a previous review level).
4. Add comments.

   If you are declining the task, comments are required.
5. Click SAVE AND NEXT to move to the next task, or
   SAVE AND COMPLETE to finish reviewing the final task
   and close the page.
