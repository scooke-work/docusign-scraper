# Disable a Room Template To Hide It From Members

## Before you begin

You need the Manage room templates
permission enabled on your company role.

Enable and disable room templates to control which templates are visible to your company. For
example, if you are working on a new template, you can disable it so it's hidden
from users when they create rooms.

1. In Docusign Rooms, go to Settings > COMPANY > Room templates.
2. Select the down arrow next to the template to disable.

   ![rooms-room-templates](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsroomtemplates.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE0NjgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.v6lnkJt5W4bwdUMj7_0j70qe7ZnFBP9mamiPJgPoXfA&_LANG=enus "rooms-room-templates")
3. Select Disable.

The Disabled indicator displays next
to the template.
