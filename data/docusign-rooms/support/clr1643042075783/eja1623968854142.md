# Give a Member Access to Your Real Estate Forms Anytime You Are Both in a Room

## Before you begin

Before you begin, you need the Share form
access permission enabled on your company role.

Use these steps to give a team member access to your forms
anytime you are both in a room.

1. Select your profile photo or initials in the upper-right corner of the Rooms
   header, then select Preferences.

   ![rooms-preferences-select](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspreferencesselect.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.C41iKPb_8fYc_E8pCqRE-mWaYgN1hY0rYSwJgct-Hfg&_LANG=enus "rooms-preferences-select")
2. Select Integrations in the left menu.
3. In the Docusign Forms Settings section, select the
   Shared link.

   ![rooms-preferences-form-settings-shared-link](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspreferencesformsettingssharedlink.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.C41iKPb_8fYc_E8pCqRE-mWaYgN1hY0rYSwJgct-Hfg&_LANG=enus "rooms-preferences-form-settings-shared-link")
4. Select the team member's name.

   ![rooms-preferences-share-form-access](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspreferencesshareformaccess.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.C41iKPb_8fYc_E8pCqRE-mWaYgN1hY0rYSwJgct-Hfg&_LANG=enus "rooms-preferences-share-form-access")

   Note:
   - ALL ROOMS is automatically selected in the
     drop-down list and is the only available option when sharing new
     access with a member.
   - You can change access for other (selected) members by choosing
     No Rooms or All
     Rooms from the drop-down list.
   - When you see LIMITED ROOMS, you gave this
     member access to your forms in specific rooms from the
     PEOPLE tab.
   - You can't grant access to specific rooms from this page (only
     No Rooms or All
     Rooms).
5. Select SHARE.
