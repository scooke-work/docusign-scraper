# Edit or Rename a Room Detail Field

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Go to Settings >  Company >  Room Details.
3. Expand each section and select any of the following that apply to the fields within:

   - Visible to all: When selected,
     people in the room with external roles can see the field.
   - Required to create: Agents must
     provide this information on the Create New Room page when they create a room.
   - Required to close: The field must be completed before a room
     can be submitted for review and approval.
4.    field label.

   You might want to edit a field label to reflect your company naming structure.
   However, for data mapping reasons, it is important not to change the meaning of a field
   when you edit the label. Upon saving, the new label displays on all new and existing
   rooms.
