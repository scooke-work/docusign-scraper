# Change the Transaction Side of a Room Owner

Learn how to change the room owner's transaction side (list, buy, or list and buy),
which also changes the side of the room.

## Before you begin

Before you begin, you need the Edit room
side permission enabled on your room role.

These steps change the transaction side of a room owner. By changing a room owner's side, you
also change the side of the room.

1. Open the PEOPLE tab in the room.
2. Select

    >  Side and room role.

   ![rooms-side-people](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomssidepeople.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MTAsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.AWLnnxGMXcyiPd-RPoYKNsBGvL3M7UkArLAiVDu0NGQ&_LANG=enus "rooms-side-people")
3. Change the side in the Transaction side field.
4. Select CONFIRM.
5. Select SAVE.
