# Unlock a Member Account

## Before you begin

You need at least one of the following permissions enabled
on your company role:

- Manage members with lower access level
- Manage members with the same access level

If an administrator locked a member's Docusign Rooms
account, unlock the account to allow them to access the application.

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
3. On the MEMBERS tab, select the account you want to
   unlock.

   Tip: To filter
   the member list to display only locked accounts, select the
   Locked Account filter.
4. On the Member Information page, select UNLOCK
   ACCOUNT.
