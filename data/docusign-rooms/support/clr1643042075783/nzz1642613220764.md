# Access Your Real Estate Forms From Your Docusign Rooms Account

If your company is provisioned for form access in Docusign Rooms, learn how to set up
your account to access the form libraries you subscribe to.

## Before you begin

Before you begin, your company must be provisioned for access to
Docusign forms.

Note:

If you don't see options to add a form provider to your account, or the form
libraries you are trying to access are not available, contact the Docusign Rooms
administrator at your company to request the form access you need. Your Docusign
Rooms administrator can contact your Docusign sales representative, account
manager, or support to enable form access.

If your company is provisioned for form access in Docusign Rooms for Real Estate, you can set
up your account to access the form libraries you subscribe to. To do this, you must
add the form provider on your Integrations page and provide your membership
credentials. Once your membership is validated, you can access the provider's form
libraries when adding documents to a room.

Note: Many libraries are available for validation through National
Association of REALTORS® (NAR). If you validate using the NAR
form provider, you will be validated for the libraries you have access to based on
your credentials. Some libraries are only available by validating with credentials
for that specific library.

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. In the upper right, select your profile picture or initials.
3. Select Preferences from the drop-down menu.
4. Select Integrations.
5. In the Docusign Forms Settings section, select
   ADD PROVIDER.

   ![rooms-forms-add-provider](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsformsaddprovider.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3NzgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.PANsLiE_M_ocmccbdfXfjYCEW27WualZvdg1czXmb-M&_LANG=enus "rooms-forms-add-provider")
6. Select the form provider to add.

   ![rooms-forms-providers](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/Rooms-form-providers.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3NzgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.PANsLiE_M_ocmccbdfXfjYCEW27WualZvdg1czXmb-M&_LANG=enus "rooms-forms-providers")
7. Enter your membership or form license credentials.

   If you are prompted for your last name, use the name on your real estate license. Docusign
   Rooms might pre-fill the name from your Docusign profile, but the form provider
   needs the name on your license to validate your membership.
8. Select VALIDATE.
9. If a license agreement displays, review the information, then select
   AGREE.

You can now access the provider's form library when
selecting ADD >  Docusign Forms in the Documents tab of a room.
