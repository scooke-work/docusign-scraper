# Add Documents in Docusign Rooms

Read about the different ways you can add documents to Docusign Rooms, depending on
whether you want to share them with others or keep them private.

There are two ways you can add documents in Docusign Rooms:

- *In a room* when the document pertains to a specific transaction. Your room role
  requires Add documents to rooms permission to add documents to the
  room.
- *In My Docs* when you want to keep the document in your personal document library.
  You don't need special permissions to add or manage documents in My Docs.

After you add documents, you can perform various actions on them, such as fax, email, move or
copy. The maximum file size is 35MB.

Note:

All documents imported into Rooms are copies of the original file. Updates to a document
stored on a cloud service will not automatically update the copy stored in Rooms.
