# Switch Between eSignature and Rooms

You use one set of credentials to access both eSignature and Rooms. Once logged in, you can switch back and forth between accounts easily.

1. Log in to either your eSignature or Rooms account.
2. In the upper right, click your profile picture.
3. Click Switch to eSignature or Switch to
   Rooms.
