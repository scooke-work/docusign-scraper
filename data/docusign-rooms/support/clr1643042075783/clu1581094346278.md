# Troubleshoot Your NRDS Information

Learn how to troubleshoot issues you might experience when validating your National
Association of REALTORS® (NAR) membership in Docusign Rooms for Real
Estate.

As a member of the National Association of REALTORS® (NAR), you can
access pre-tagged association forms within Docusign. You need to validate your
association memberships when accessing forms.

You'll need to provide:

- Your NRDS ID. This is a 9-digit number provided by NAR to
  verify your association memberships.
- Your Last Name, which is pre-filled with the last name on your
  Docusign profile. If you have a different last name on your real estate license,
  change this field to display the name on your license.
- An Association you belong to. If you belong to multiple
  associations, choose any association for validation purposes.

If the information you provided here doesn't match NAR records, you'll see an error message
after clicking VALIDATE.

![Error message stating the information you provided does not match NAR records.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsnrdserror.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3ODQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.uyWWeKGt6SfcX56zOinXicpIpj2c3OjeWZ21mToq2s0&_LANG=enus "Error message stating the information you provided does not match NAR records.")

The information in this topic will help you troubleshoot this issue.

## Possible Cause: You aren’t a member of the selected Association.

- Contact your local association to confirm your REALTOR® association
  membership information.
- Your brokerage might also be able to confirm your REALTOR® association
  membership information.
- If you aren't a member of the selected association but should have access to the
  association's forms, contact the local association and request they email [REForms@docusign.com](mailto:REForms@docusign.com) to resolve the issue on your behalf.

## Possible cause: The NRDS ID is incorrect, or there’s a mismatch on your last name.

- Click the Find your NRDS ID link to find your ID if you
  don't know your ID number. You can also click [here](https://login.connect.realtor/#!/forgotmember).
- Verify that the last name you are using to validate your association membership in Docusign is the same name on your real estate license. If the names don't match, we won't
  be able to validate your membership.
