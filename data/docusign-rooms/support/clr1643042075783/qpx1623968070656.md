# Share Form Access So Team Members Can Add or Edit Your Forms

Read about how sharing your form access helps you collaborate with team members on a
transaction.

Share your form access to collaborate with team members on a transaction. For example, sharing
forms with non-licensed agent users, such as transaction coordinators, allows these
people to add or edit forms on your behalf without requiring your credentials.

Note: Document access and form permissions apply to
shared forms as they do with other documents. Sharing your form access only gives
someone the ability to access your form libraries. They still need a room role with
permission to take action on them.
