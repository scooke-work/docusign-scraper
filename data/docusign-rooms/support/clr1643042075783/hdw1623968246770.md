# Share Your Real Estate Form Access With a Room Member

## Before you begin

Before you begin, you need the Share form
access permission enabled on your room role.

Use these steps to share your form access within the
context of a room. You have the option to share forms in the current room only or all
rooms you are in.

1. In Docusign Rooms for Real Estate, open the room where you'd like to share
   forms.
2. Select the PEOPLE tab.
3. Locate the member you'd like to share forms with, then select ![3-dot-people-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/3dotpeopleicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.nu5EEwS60uPOT88JuRErYKbva0xorjh2lutQeRAIJr0&_LANG=enus "3-dot-people-icon") > Share form access on their card.

   ![rooms-share-form-access](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsshareformaccess.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.nu5EEwS60uPOT88JuRErYKbva0xorjh2lutQeRAIJr0&_LANG=enus "rooms-share-form-access")
4. Choose the range of access you'd like to give this person.

   Only this room lets the member access your forms *in
   this room only.*

   Any room lets this person access your forms *in any
   room where you are a room member.*

   ![rooms-share-form-access-rooms](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsshareformaccessrooms.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.nu5EEwS60uPOT88JuRErYKbva0xorjh2lutQeRAIJr0&_LANG=enus "rooms-share-form-access-rooms")
5. Select SAVE.

The FORM ACCESS badge displays for
people in the room who have access to your forms.

![rooms-share-form-access-badge](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsshareformaccessbadge.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.nu5EEwS60uPOT88JuRErYKbva0xorjh2lutQeRAIJr0&_LANG=enus "rooms-share-form-access-badge")

A Docusign Forms Access Shared entry is also
added to the room HISTORY tab.

Tip: To change someone's access to your
forms, select ![3-dot-people-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/3dotpeopleicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3OTIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.nu5EEwS60uPOT88JuRErYKbva0xorjh2lutQeRAIJr0&_LANG=enus "3-dot-people-icon") > Change form access on their card. You can
change their access to No access, Only this
room, or Any room.
