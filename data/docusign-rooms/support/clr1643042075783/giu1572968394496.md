# Add a New Member Account

## Before you begin

You need a company role with at least one of the following
permissions enabled:

- Add members with lower access level
- Add members with the same access level

Note: Also see the video [How to Add Member Accounts](https://support.docusign.com/s/articles/DocuSign-Rooms-Add-Members-v6?language=en_US).

1. Log in to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Select Settings in the Rooms header.
3. On the MEMBERS tab, select ADD
   MEMBER.
4. Type the new member's first name, last name, and email address.
5. Select a role for the member.

   Each role is defined with specific permissions that enable or disable features in
   Rooms.
6. Select an access level.

   A member's access level determines *what they can see*, and their permissions
   determine *what they can do* in a room. For example, someone with Company level
   access can see all rooms in their company, but if they don't have Add
   documents to rooms permission, they can't add documents to those
   rooms.

   The access level options are:

   - Company - Members have access to rooms across all regions and
     offices, and if they have permission to manage members, they have access to members
     across the entire company. What they can do in the rooms and what they can do with
     members depends on their individual permissions.
   - Region - Members have access to rooms in the selected
     region(s), and if they have permission to manage members, they have access to members
     across their regions.
   - Office - Members have access to rooms in the selected
     office(s), and if they have permission to manage members, they have access to members
     across their offices.
   - Contributor - Members have access only to rooms they create
     and those they are invited into, and can't manage members because they don't oversee
     other members. However, contributors can belong to multiple offices.
7. If you selected a role that has Automatically added to new rooms
   permission, you will see an auto-add setting, Add to open
   rooms.

   - Leave this option cleared if you only want the member auto-added to new rooms going
     forward.
   - Select this option to add the member to open rooms in their offices. Rooms will
     schedule a room access update upon saving the new member.
8. Select an eSign permission profile.

   Your company eSignature admin can configure custom eSign permission profiles in eSign.
   This list contains the default eSign permission profiles, which are DS Admin, DS Sender,
   and DS Viewer, plus any custom eSign permission profiles that your company eSignature
   admin set up.
9. Select SAVE.

An invitation email is sent to the person you added. The member appears in the member list
with a paper airplane icon and a pending status.

The member must follow the instructions in the email to activate their Rooms account. After
they activate their account, the icon is updated to the default person icon or the member's
photo (if they upload one).

Note: You can re-invite an unactivated
member at any time by selecting the user, selecting Resend Invite,
and then selecting SEND INVITATION. You cannot change the name and
email here because that information is managed through eSignature Admin.

You can change
some information before you re-invite the member, such as access level and
role.
