# Read Your Messages

Find all your unread messages in the New Messages section of the Rooms
dashboard.

![rooms-dashboard-new-messages](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsdashboardnewmessages.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2NDgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.t3I2XnqGftv2X_lEzpsPyJmKHU2DhPN1EOcRSgXObNs&_LANG=enus "rooms-dashboard-new-messages")

Select a message to read it, or select View All to open a new page with
all your recent messages.

In a room, select the MESSAGES tab to access messages in that room.

If you have permission to read all messages in the room, you see ![rooms-messages-read-only-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsmessagesreadonlyicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2NDgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.t3I2XnqGftv2X_lEzpsPyJmKHU2DhPN1EOcRSgXObNs&_LANG=enus "rooms-messages-read-only-icon") for topics you aren't participating in. These topics are read-only.
