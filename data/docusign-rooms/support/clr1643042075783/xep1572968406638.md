# Remove or Restore Someone's Room Access

Learn how to control someone's access to a room in Docusign Rooms.

## Before you begin

Before you begin, you need a room role with at least one of
these permissions enabled:

- Manage any user's room access
- Manage invited user's room access

You might need to remove someone from a room if they shouldn't have access to it anymore.
When you remove a person's room access, you are only preventing them from entering the room.
The audit trail and all documents are preserved in their current status.

Tip: Use the Show removed
users filter on the People tab to show or hide removed people.

1. Open the room.
2. Select the PEOPLE tab.
3. Do one of the following:
   - In grid view, select ![3 dot icon.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/3dotpeopleicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MzIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.T3iFe4Rj-kC_9MVPWSiUPoDoKe7Ih4jiUJYRE0-h4Wo&_LANG=enus "3 dot icon.") under the person's name, then select Remove room access
     or Restore room access.
   - In list view, select Actions in the person's row, then
     select Remove room access or Restore room
     access.
4. Select REMOVE to confirm.

If you have the Show removed users filter enabled on the People tab,
removed people are grayed out and display a REMOVED badge.
