# Customize Your Docusign Rooms Email Address

Docusign Rooms issues you an auto-generated inbound email address so you can send
documents directly to your account. Learn how to personalize the email address so it's easier to
remember.

Docusign Rooms issues you an inbound email address so
can send documents directly to your Rooms account. This email address is usually part of your
name, followed by numbers. You might want to personalize the email address so it's easier to
remember.

1. Log in to your Rooms account.
2. Select your profile picture or initials in the upper right, then select
   Preferences.
3. Select Inbox Details.
4. Type inside the Inbound Email field to modify the email
   address.

   If the email address already exists, the system prompts you to create a different
   address.
5. Select SAVE CHANGES.
