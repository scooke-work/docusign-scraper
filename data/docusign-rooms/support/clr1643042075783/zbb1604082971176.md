# Complete a Task

If you are the assignee of a task, you are responsible for completing it.

If the task doesn't require a document, mark it complete when you are
done. On the TASKS tab, select the circle next to the task(s), then click ![rooms-tasks-mark-complete-icon](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomstasksmarkcompleteicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4MTgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.GPvUvtwiffcqeyP0RFtePtqI83AyU17izLWc75GKM-E&_LANG=enus "rooms-tasks-mark-complete-icon") in the toolbar.

If the task requires documents, you need to assign documents to the
task. The task will be marked complete automatically after you assign all the
necessary documents from the TASKS or DOCUMENTS tab.

- On the DOCUMENTS tab, first [add the
  document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=clr1643042075783&topicId=rqf1600180642906.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to upload documents to Docusign Rooms so people collaborating with you on a transaction can access your files. Sharing documents in a central location saves time and prevents you from emailing files back and forth.") to the room. Then, drag the document from the ROOM DOCS
  folder to the document's placeholder in the task list.
- On the TASKS tab, click the task to open it. Click the document placeholder
  under the Documents section, then choose the room
  document to assign to the task or upload a new document.

Completed tasks display a green checkmark.

![rooms-task-completed](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomstaskcompleted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4MTgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.GPvUvtwiffcqeyP0RFtePtqI83AyU17izLWc75GKM-E&_LANG=enus "rooms-task-completed")

Completed tasks that require review change to In Review status
and move to the reviewer(s).
