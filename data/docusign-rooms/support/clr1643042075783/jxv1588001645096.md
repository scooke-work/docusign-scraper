# Delete a Room To Remove It Permanently

Learn how to delete a room if you no longer need it. Deleting a room removes it
permanently and all of the room documents.

You can delete a room if you no longer need it. Deleting a room deletes it permanently. A
common reason for deleting a room is if you accidentally created a duplicate.

If you delete a room, you also delete all your documents in the room. Rooms won't
allow you to delete a room if other people own documents in the room or if there are
open tasks or envelopes.

Tip: Instead of deleting a room,
consider closing it instead. Closing a room retains documents, and you can reopen it
later if needed.

You need permission to delete rooms. The permission(s) you have determines when you
can delete a room.

- The Delete owned rooms permission lets you delete only
  rooms you own.
- The Delete rooms permission lets you delete any room
  where you are a room member with an internal role (including rooms you
  own).

To delete a room:

1. In your Rooms account, open the room you want to delete.
2. Click ACTIONS > Delete room.
