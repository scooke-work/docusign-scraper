# Role Permissions List

Reference a list of user role permissions available in DocusignRooms. Role
permissions let administrators control access to administrative functions and most actions
users can take in a room.

Below is a list of all permissions available in DocusignRooms. Some permissions may
not be available, depending on the role settings. For example, you cannot enable
company setting permissions to external roles.

## Room permissions

| Permission | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Create rooms | Users see the NEW button on the Rooms tab and can create a room. |
| Create room on behalf of others | Users can choose someone else (peers or contributors they oversee) as the room owner when creating a room. |
| Export room's history, details, and people | Users see the option in the room ACTIONS menu to export the room’s details, people, and history to a PDF or CSV file. |
| View all messages in the room | Users have read-only access to all message topics they aren't participating in. |
| Add users to rooms | Users see the INVITE button on the room's People tab and can invite people into a room. |
| Manage room owners | Users can designate people from your company as room owners, which automatically shares ownership of room documents. Note: Users with this permission do not have to be the room owner to designate people as room owners. |
| Submit rooms for review | Users can submit rooms for review that are owned by them or someone they manage. |
| Review and close rooms | Users can review and close rooms that are owned by them or someone they manage. |
| Re-open rooms | Users can re-open rooms that are owned by them or someone they manage. |
| Delete owned rooms | Users can delete rooms they own. |
| Delete rooms | Users can delete any room if they are a member of that room with an internal role. |
| Automatically added to new rooms | Auto-adds people to new rooms that their peers or subordinates create or join. This permission doesn't consider office locations when adding people to new rooms.  Note: Disabling this permission does not remove people from rooms. Once someone is added to a room (whether they are added automatically or not), removing their room access is a manual step [within the room](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=clr1643042075783&topicId=xep1572968406638.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to control someone's access to a room in Docusign Rooms.") or through a scheduled [bulk update](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=clr1643042075783&topicId=zyf1600976408469.html&_LANG=enus). |
| Automatically added to new rooms in their offices only | Auto-adds people to new rooms that their peers or subordinates create or join, but only within office locations they share.  Note: Disabling this permission does not remove people from rooms. Once someone is added to a room (whether they are added automatically or not), removing their room access is a manual step [within the room](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=clr1643042075783&topicId=xep1572968406638.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to control someone's access to a room in Docusign Rooms.") or through a scheduled [bulk update](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=clr1643042075783&topicId=zyf1600976408469.html&_LANG=enus). |
| Is hidden in rooms | Users are not visible on the People tab in rooms. |
| Copy room details | Users see a Copy room option in the room ACTIONS menu, which copies the room detail information to populate a new room. |
| Edit any user's room role | Users can change any room member's role. |
| Edit invited user's room role | Users can change the room role for people they have invited. |
| Edit room side | Users can change the side of the room. Note: This permission enables the EDIT button on the room Details tab, but users can only edit the Side field. |
| Manage any user's room access | Users can remove or restore any room member's access. |
| Manage invited user's room access | Users can remove or restore room access for people they've invited. |
| Can prepare and execute closing | Users can access and build closing packages for transactions. A CLOSING PACKAGE button is visible in the header of each room. |
| Send and retrieve documents from Mortgage Cadence Collaboration Center | This permission displays for internal roles if your company has the Mortgage Cadence integration enabled. Select this permission to allow users to add documents from Collaboration Center to a room and send room documents back to Collaboration Center. |
| Send and retrieve documents from Encompass | This permission displays for internal roles if your company has the Encompass integration enabled. Select this permission to allow users to add documents from Encompass to a room and send room documents back to Encompass. |

## Room details permissions

If both room details permissions are disabled, users do not see the room Details
tab.

| Permission | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| View room details | Users see the Details tab (view only) and see all detail fields that the company admin has set to Use. |
| Edit room details | Users see the EDIT button on the room's Details tab and can update any details field. Users with this permission also have permission to View room details. |
| Send room details to Lone Wolf | This permission displays if your company has the Lone Wolf integration enabled. Select this permission to allow users to send room details data to Lone Wolf. Note: Make sure the role also has the View room details and Edit room details permissions selected. These permissions allow members to see and edit the room DETAILS tab, where the Lone Wolf fields are located. |

## Documents and forms permissions

Viewing documents in a room is not controlled by a permission. Everyone in the room
can see the Documents tab, but they need access to each document to see it.

When setting permissions for forms, keep in mind there are
two types of form users:

- Users who manage forms (typically admin and
  manager roles)
- Users who add forms to rooms

Users who manage forms require the Manage form
packets or Manage form libraries permission
to access the Forms tab in Settings. These permissions are in
the Company Settings section. For all other roles, assign permissions based on how
these users will be working with forms. If no form permissions are enabled for these
roles, users cannot add forms to rooms.

| Permission | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Add documents to rooms | Users see the ADD button on the Documents tab and can add documents to the room, which they can share with people in the room. |
| Automatically share documents in the room with other users from the same office | When a user with this permission adds a document to an open room, the document is automatically shared with people who have access to the room and are in the user's office. |
| Automatically add all peers from the office as document owners | Peers are others in the same office or region who have the same access level as the user. When a user with this permission adds a document to an open room, the document is automatically owned by people who are peers of that user and have access to the room. |
| Add documents from form packets to rooms | Users see the ADD >  Docusign Forms option in a room's Documents tab, where they can add forms using form packets.  Note: This permission does not require Add documents to rooms permission to be enabled. |
| Add documents from form libraries to rooms | Users see the ADD >  Docusign Forms option in a room's Documents tab, where they can add forms using form libraries.  Note: This permission does not require Add documents to rooms permission to be enabled. |
| Delete signed documents | Users can delete signed documents from rooms. The user or a member they manage must own the documents. |
| Delete unsigned documents | Users can delete unsigned documents from rooms. The user or a member they manage must own the documents. |
| Share any document | Users can share documents they do not own (someone else shared with them). |
| Auto split documents | Users can auto split a document in the room Documents tab. Enabling this permission adds the Auto split option to the document right-click menu. |
| Share form access | Users can share access to their form libraries, allowing other members to add and edit their forms. |
| Create form templates | Users can create form templates in My Docs >  Form Templates, which can be used to pre-fill association forms. |

## Tasks permissions

There are two types of task users:

- Users who manage task list templates (typically admin and manager roles)
- Users who use tasks within rooms

Users who manage task list templates require the Manage task
templates permission to access Settings >  COMPANY >  Task list templates. This permission is in the Company Settings
section. For all other roles, assign permissions based on how these users will be
working with tasks within rooms.

| Permission | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Add tasks to rooms | Users can add ad hoc tasks to a room's General task list. |
| Add tasks to task lists | Users can add tasks to task lists that were created by others. |
| Edit tasks | Users can edit editable tasks. |
| Edit any task | Users can edit tasks in rooms (even if the task owner has not marked the task as editable). |
| Delete tasks | Users can delete deletable tasks. |
| Delete any task | Users can delete tasks (even if the task owner has not marked the task as deletable). |
| Review any task | Users can approve or decline any task on any task list (even if they aren't a reviewer on the task). |
| Manage documents on any task | Users can add or remove documents from any task. |
| Attach task lists to rooms | Users see the Attach Task List option in the room ACTIONS menu. |
| Remove task lists from rooms | Users can use the Remove Task List option in the room ACTIONS menu to remove task lists owned by others. (Users can already remove task lists they own.) |
| Submit task lists for review | Users can use the Submit Task List option in the room ACTIONS menu to submit task lists for review. |
| Auto-submit task lists | When users submit a room for review, task lists also submit for review if all tasks in the lists are complete. |
| Review task lists | Users can approve or decline task lists. Anyone with this permission can see all tasks on the task list but would need other permissions for tasks (like edit or delete) to take those actions. |
| Auto-approve task lists | When a room is approved, task lists auto-approve if all tasks in the lists are approved. |
| Apply room templates | Users can apply a room template when they create a room. |

## Member management permissions

| Permission | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Add members with lower access level | Users can add members with a lower access level than theirs and set those members' roles (in offices or regions they oversee). |
| Add members with the same access level | Users can add members with the same access level as theirs and set those members' roles (in offices or regions they oversee or belong to). |
| Edit role for members with lower access level | Users can change the role of a member with a lower access level than theirs (in offices or regions they oversee). |
| Edit role for members with the same access level | Users can change the role of a member with the same access level as theirs (in offices or regions they oversee or belong to). |
| Manage members with lower access level | Users can change the access level, office or region, and eSignature permission set for members with lower access levels than theirs. |
| Manage members with the same access level | Users can change the access level, office or region, and eSignature permission set for members with the same access level as theirs. |
| Remove members with lower access level | Users can close the accounts of members with a lower access level than theirs (in offices or regions they oversee). |
| Remove members with the same access level | Users can close the accounts of members with the same access level as theirs (in offices or regions they oversee or belong to). |

## Company settings permissions

When setting permissions for forms, keep in mind there are
two types of form users:

- Users who manage forms (typically admin and manager
  roles)
- Users who add forms to rooms

Users who manage forms require either the
Manage form packets or Manage form
libraries permission to access the Forms tab
in Settings. For all other roles, assign permissions (in the
Documents & Forms section) based on how these users
will be working with forms. If no form permissions are enabled for these roles,
users cannot add forms to rooms.

| Permission | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Manage company account settings | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Company settings to change the company name, contact information, logo, and other settings. |
| Manage company logo | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Company settings to change the company logo only. |
| Manage roles and permissions | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Roles and permissions to create, edit, and manage user roles that your company uses in Rooms. |
| Manage company regions | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Regions to add, edit, and delete any company regions. (Only unused regions can be deleted.) |
| Manage company offices | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Offices to add, edit, and delete any company offices. (Only unused offices can be deleted.) |
| Manage room templates | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Room templates to add, edit, and delete room templates. |
| Manage task templates | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Task list templates to create, edit, and delete task list templates for the regions and offices they manage. |
| Manage PDF split templates | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  PDF split templates to manage templates used when splitting PDF documents. |
| Manage room details | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Room details to configure room detail fields. They can also add contact fields. |
| Manage integration settings | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Integration Settings to manage your company integrations. |
| Manage email invite templates | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Email invite templates to manage email templates for room invitations. |
| Manage split document rules | Gives users access to Settings >  COMPANY >  Split document rules to create rules for automatically splitting a loan package into individual documents. |
| Manage form packets | This option is available if your company uses Docusign forms. It gives users access to Settings >  Forms to manage form packets. |
| Manage form libraries | This option is available if your company uses Docusign forms. It gives users access to Settings >  Forms to manage form libraries, such as assigning libraries to offices. |
