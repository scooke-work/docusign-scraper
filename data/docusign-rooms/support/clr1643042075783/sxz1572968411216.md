# Use Messages To Communicate With Other People Working on a Transaction

Learn how to send and manage in-app messages in Docusign Rooms. A transaction can have
several message threads active at once, allowing room members to connect on different
topics.

Room messages give you access to real-time conversations with room members. Start a message
when you want a single, continuous thread of discussion regarding a specific transaction. A
room can have several message threads active at once, allowing room members to connect on
different topics.

Find your messages in the room MESSAGES tab. Here, you see a list of
topics where you are a participant (you started the message or someone added you).
