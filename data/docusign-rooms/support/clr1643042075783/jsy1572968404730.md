# Add Docusign Forms to a Room

Learn how to add documents from your association form libraries to a room in Docusign
Rooms. When you add pre-tagged forms to a room, the forms populate automatically with data from
the room DETAILS tab.

If your company uses Docusign forms, you can add standard association forms to a room. When
you add pre-tagged forms to a room, the forms populate automatically with data from the room
DETAILS tab.

Your company administrator can give you access to form packets, making these documents easy
to find from the room DOCUMENTS tab.

Depending on the associations or form service providers you use, you might be prompted for
membership information when you add forms to a room. If you provide this information once
and your membership is validated, you won't be prompted for the information again unless
you've changed your membership. Rooms stores your membership information in Preferences >  Integrations.

To add a form document to a room:

1. Open the room.
2. Select the DOCUMENTS tab.
3. Select ADD >  Docusign Forms.
4. Select the name of the form provider.

   The National Association of REALTORS® icon accesses the form
   libraries for your supported local and state associations.

   ![rooms-forms-providers](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/Rooms-form-providers.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3ODIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.LWHC93Lvrj9HW-EuQHn5bBvRmzt12LP6_xygkVEeTPs&_LANG=enus "rooms-forms-providers")

   Note: If your company has
   company-provided forms, you'll see a Continue to Company Forms without
   validation link, which allows you to skip the validation process and go
   directly to your company forms. If your company uses association-provided forms, you
   might not have access to all forms if you skip validation.
5. Enter your membership information, then select VALIDATE.

   For example, to validate your NAR membership, enter your NRDS ID and select any
   association where you are a member.
6. If other people have shared their form access with you, select a member's name in the
   Forms from field to access their form libraries. Otherwise, leave
   your name selected to access your libraries.

   ![rooms-share-forms-select-owner](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsshareformsselectowner.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3ODIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.LWHC93Lvrj9HW-EuQHn5bBvRmzt12LP6_xygkVEeTPs&_LANG=enus "rooms-share-forms-select-owner")
7. Select the form library or packet from the left navigation pane.

   The right pane refreshes to show forms within the selected library or
   packet.
8. Select the form(s) to add to the room.
9. If a form template is associated with a selected form, a drop-down menu displays next
   to the form name. To use a template, select the template from the drop-down menu.

   ![rooms-form-template-in-row](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsformtemplateinrow.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE3ODIsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.LWHC93Lvrj9HW-EuQHn5bBvRmzt12LP6_xygkVEeTPs&_LANG=enus "rooms-form-template-in-row")
10. Select ADD SELECTED.
