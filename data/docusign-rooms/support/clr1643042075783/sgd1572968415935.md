# Add Your Personal Contacts to Docusign Rooms

Read about how to use the Docusign Rooms address book to manage your contacts outside
of the company.

Use the address book to manage your contacts outside of the company. To access the address
book, select your profile photo, then select Contacts > Address Book.

![Address Book tab.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/addressbook.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NTUsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.faE_sK0dtyT8TEMAJ_p13vA06-bXHMJssSmvAxD4D4E&_LANG=enus "Address Book tab.")

You have access to the address book when adding people to a room, so add contacts you work with regularly. You can add contacts here, but it might be easier to add new contacts to your address book when you add them to a room. When inviting someone new, you'll have a chance to save the person to your address book.

Note: Your Rooms and eSignature address books sync
contacts. When you update a contact in Rooms, it updates in eSignature, and vice versa.

## Share an address book contact

When you share a contact in your address book, all members of your company can see the contact in their address books, but they can't edit it.

When adding a new contact, enable Shared Contact to share it.

![rooms-share-contact](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomssharecontact.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NTUsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.faE_sK0dtyT8TEMAJ_p13vA06-bXHMJssSmvAxD4D4E&_LANG=enus "rooms-share-contact")

To share an existing address book contact:

- In grid view, select ![3 dot icon.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/3dotpeopleicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE4NTUsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.faE_sK0dtyT8TEMAJ_p13vA06-bXHMJssSmvAxD4D4E&_LANG=enus "3 dot icon.") under the person's name, then select Share or
  Unshare.
- In list view, select the drop-down menu next to EDIT, then select
  Share or Unshare.
