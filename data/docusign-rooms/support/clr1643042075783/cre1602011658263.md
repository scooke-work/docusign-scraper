# Download Documents

Learn how to download documents from Docusign Rooms. After you download a document, it's
no longer linked to the original document in your Rooms account.

In Docusign Rooms, you can download documents from a room.
In Docusign Rooms, you can download documents from a
room, your Rooms Inbox, or your My Docs folder. After you download a
document, it's no longer linked to the original document in your Rooms account. Any
changes you make to the downloaded document outside of Rooms is not reflected in
your document in the Rooms application.

If you download multiple documents, Rooms creates a ZIP file.

Note:

Pre-tagged real estate association forms within Docusign Rooms are protected and
can't be downloaded when they are blank. You will see a “Sample” watermark when
viewing these forms in certain areas of the application. This watermark doesn't
display on in-progress or completed forms.

1. In Docusign Rooms, navigate to the document(s) to download.

   - If the document is in a room, open the room and select the
     DOCUMENTS tab.
   - If the document is in your Inbox or My Docs
     folder, select either of those options in the Rooms header.
2. Move your mouse over the document(s), then select the circle icon to select
   them. A check mark displays when the document is selected.
