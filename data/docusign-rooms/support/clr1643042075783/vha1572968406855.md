# Designate Other People As Room Owners To Share Document Access

If you are a room owner, learn how to designate other people as room owners to share
documents with them.

If you are the owner of a room and have permission to manage room owners, you can designate
other people at your company as room owners. This is useful if you are working with someone
on a transaction and share responsibilities. Room owners share document ownership.
Otherwise, the person's room role determines what else they can do in a room.

Keep these considerations in mind when adding co-owners:

- There is no limit to the number owners you can have in a room.
- Co-owners must be members of the same company account.
- For real estate, co-owners must be on the same side of the transaction (buy or list
  side).
- Room owners can change other owners back to participants. If an owner is reverted to a
  participant, they no longer have access to anything in the room that wasn't explicitly
  shared with them.

To make someone a room co-owner:

1. Open the room.
2. Click the PEOPLE tab.
3. Do one of the following:
   - In grid view, click ![3 dot icon.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/3dotpeopleicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MzQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.541sLu03dkfvOYR1NuGjYiSTLJes0_D4ZywEMymDWtg&_LANG=enus "3 dot icon.") under the person's name, then click Access.
   - In list view, click Actions in the person's row, then click
     Access.

   ![The Access option on the people card menu.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspeoplemenuaccess.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MzQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.541sLu03dkfvOYR1NuGjYiSTLJes0_D4ZywEMymDWtg&_LANG=enus "The Access option on the people card menu.")
4. In the Access Level drop-down list, choose
   Owner.

   ![Choosing the Owner option from the Access Level drop-down list.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomspeopleaccesslevel.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE2MzQsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.541sLu03dkfvOYR1NuGjYiSTLJes0_D4ZywEMymDWtg&_LANG=enus "Choosing the Owner option from the Access Level drop-down list.")
5. Click SAVE.

A ROOM OWNER badge displays on the new
co-owner's people card.
