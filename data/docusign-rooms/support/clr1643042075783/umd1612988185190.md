# Why Do I See a “Sample” Watermark on My Association Forms?

Learn why some pre-tagged real estate association forms within Docusign Rooms are
protected with a sample watermark.

Some pre-tagged real estate association forms within Docusign Rooms are protected with a
"Sample" watermark, and you cannot download them when they are blank. Certain
associations require that we display their forms this way.

This watermark doesn't display on in-progress or completed forms. Once you begin filling
the form, the watermark disappears.

Note: After you add data to a form, then save and close it, the watermark might still
display on the document thumbnail. Refresh your browser, and the watermark will
disappear.
