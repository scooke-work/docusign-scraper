# Create a Form Template

## Before you begin

Before you begin, you need a company role with Create
form templates permission.

1. Log in to your Rooms account.
2. Select My Documents in the Rooms header.
3. Select the FORMS tab.
4. Scroll to the My Forms section.
5. Select NEW TEMPLATE.
6. Find the form you are creating a template for.
   1. In the first drop-down list, select whether to find the form in a
      DocuSign Form Library or a
      DocuSign Form Packet. (The options you see
      here depend on your permissions for adding forms.)
   2. In the second drop-down list, select the packet or library that
      contains the form.

      A form list displays after you make a selection.
   3. Select the form, then select USE.
7. On the form, fill in or select the fields that will be the same for every
   transaction, such as your name.
8. Select SAVE & CLOSE.

After you save the template, it is available in the My
Forms section. Select Templates to view templates associated with a form or the
Forms and templates filter to view all templates. Use the options in the
Actions column to edit, copy, or delete the template.

Note: Deleting a template only deletes the template. It does not remove your access to
the form.
