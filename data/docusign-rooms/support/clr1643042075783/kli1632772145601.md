# Update Your Name, Email Address, or Profile Photo

Manage your personal information in your Docusign Profile. Here you can update the information related to your Docusign account, such as
your login credentials, profile photo, name, signatures, and more.

1. Log in to your Rooms account.
2. In the upper right, select your profile picture or initials.
3. Select Manage Profile from the drop-down menu.

   ![rooms-manage-profile-crop](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsmanageprofilecrop.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE1OTgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.yFA2UWuaVRyoPzO3pCZaHjhTQk303RYK9TdTmcRxVHw&_LANG=enus "rooms-manage-profile-crop")
4. On the My Profile Information page, select the information to update.

   For example, to update your profile photo, select the profile picture area. Or, to update
   your email address, select Email Address.

   ![rooms-my-profile](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/clr1643042075783/page/images/roomsmyprofile.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjE1OTgsInNoZWFmIjoiY2xyMTY0MzA0MjA3NTc4MyJ9.yFA2UWuaVRyoPzO3pCZaHjhTQk303RYK9TdTmcRxVHw&_LANG=enus "rooms-my-profile")
5. Follow the prompts to update the information.
