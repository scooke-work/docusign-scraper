# Room Document Permissions, Ownership, and Sharing

Read about how document permissions, ownership, and shared settings determine what users
can see and do with documents in Docusign Rooms.

Docusign Rooms saves transaction documents in the room Documents tab. All users can
access the Documents tab, but what they can *see* and *do* with documents
depends on permissions, ownership, and shared settings.

Note: This information applies to room documents. Documents in your Inbox and My Docs folder
are only visible to you, so permissions and sharing don't apply to those
documents.

## Document role permissions

Permissions control what users can *do* with documents in a room. A few document
permissions are:

- Add documents to rooms
- Delete documents from rooms
- Share any document

For example, if your room role has Add documents to
rooms permission, you see an ADD button at
the top of the Documents tab.

Admins update role permissions in Settings >  COMPANY >  Roles and Permissions.

Note: You don’t need permission to take these actions on
documents (available from the document right-click menu):
Rename, Move,
Copy, Email,
Download, Print, and
Archive. The Create Envelope
option is also available if your eSignature permission profile allows you to send
envelopes.

## Document ownership and sharing

You own the documents that you add to a room. When you own a document, you can share
it with other people.

The document owner's name displays with the document in the room
Documents tab.

Only you can see your documents unless you explicitly share them with others.
However, there are exceptions. In these scenarios, documents are shared
automatically:

- There are multiple room owners.

  Documents are shared
  automatically between room owners. If one owner uploads a document, all
  other owners can see it.
- Your room role has any of the permissions below.
  - Automatically share documents in the room with users from the same
    office
  - Automatically add all peers from the office as document owners
  - Manage documents on any task (lets you see task documents in the room,
    even if those documents weren't shared with you)
