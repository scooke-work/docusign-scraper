# Email Documents From Your Docusign Rooms Account

Learn how to send documents in Docusign Rooms to an email recipient. Email is a fast way
to share documents with people who don't have a Docusign Rooms account.

Email is a fast way to share documents with people who
don't have a Docusign Rooms account. You can email documents from the Rooms
Dashboard or the Documents tab of a
room. You can also email documents stored in
My Docs or your
Inbox.

1. Log on to your Docusign Rooms account.
2. Locate and select the document or documents.
3. Right-click and select Email.
4. Type the recipient's email address in the Email
   field.
5. Type your message in the Email Message field.
6. Select SEND EMAIL.

The recipient receives an email containing your personalized message and a link to download
the documents. The download link is valid for 7 days.
