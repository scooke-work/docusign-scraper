# What's the difference between a company role and a room role?

You are assigned a *company role* when an admin adds you to your company account. This
role determines what you can do around your account. For example, your company role may
permit you to manage other members, company settings, or templates.

When you are invited to a room, the inviter assigns you a *room role*. Your room role
determines what you can do in that room. It might be the same as your company role, or
it could be a different role with fewer permissions.

When you are in a room, if your room role differs from your company role, you'll see a banner
at the top of the page. Click the role name link to check your permissions in the room.

Tip: One of the most frequent questions we get from new
users is, "why can't I see this, or take this action, in a room?" Most of the time, the
user has a different role in a room and, therefore, different permissions. Check your
role in a room if you notice changes.
