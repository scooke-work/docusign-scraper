# Use Form Templates To Pre-Fill Association Forms

Learn how to create and apply form templates. Form templates prevent you from having to
fill in the same information on a form each time.

If your forms contain some of the same information for each transaction (such as your name),
try creating templates to save time. Your form templates are only visible to you and
cannot be shared with others.

If you complete a form in a couple of different ways (for example, cash deal vs. finance
deal), you can create multiple templates for the same form. However, template names must
be unique.
