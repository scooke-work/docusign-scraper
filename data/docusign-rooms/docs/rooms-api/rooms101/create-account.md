# Creating a Rooms developer account

To use Docusign Rooms functionality, you need to create a company Rooms account. This account will contain all of your member (employee) accounts, any rooms you create, and all of the metadata associated with those members and rooms. As with the eSignature REST API, Rooms accounts can be created in either the *developer account* (demo) or *production* environment.

You can create a Rooms developer account for free, which you can use to see, experiment with, and develop for the Rooms API functionality. You can create a Rooms production account (which can use your Rooms developer account data) by purchasing one of the Rooms API Docusign products. Once you’re ready, you can promote your app from your Rooms developer account to your production account by completing the [Go-Live](https://developers.docusign.com/docs/rooms-api/go-live/) process.

You can create a new company Rooms API developer account using the following steps:

1. [Create an eSignature developer account](https://www.docusign.com/developers/sandbox?postActivateUrl=https://developers.docusign.com/&_gl=1*1d3gman*_gcl_au*ODczOTUxNTUwLjE3MjcyOTgwNDc.). Because Rooms integrates eSignature functionality, you must have an existing eSignature account before you can create a Rooms account.
2. Contact Docusign to request a Rooms account via support-re@docusign.com. Include the following information:
   - Your company’s name and address.
   - Your developer account ID. To find your **Account ID**, log in to your eSignature developer account, then select your account portrait to access your profile. Copy the **Account ID** value that appears directly below your name in the displayed drop-down menu.
   - Your API Account ID. To find your **API Account ID**, log in to your eSignature developer account and select **Admin**, then click [Apps and Keys](https://admindemo.docusign.com/authenticate?goTo=appsAndKeys). Copy your **API Account ID** value
3. After you have provided this information, Docusign will send you a confirmation email. When you receive it, go on to step 4.
4. [Log in to your eSignature developer account](https://apps-d.docusign.com) from an incognito browser window, select your portrait at the top right of the window, then select **Switch to Rooms**.
   Re-enter your developer credentials if prompted. On the **Link your Docusign eSignature and Rooms accounts** page, select **Sign up for a Rooms Account** at the bottom of the page, then select **Sign Up** to open the dashboard of your new Rooms account.

   ![](data:image/svg+xml;charset=utf-8,%3Csvg height='383' width='600' xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg' version='1.1'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

   ![Link your Docusign eSignature and Rooms accounts](https://images.ctfassets.net/aj9z008chlq0/3z3RsAvhCymwaUUlHjQRvY/590dc8f7527aae07b21bff1e89444c74/Link_page.png?w=600&h=383&q=50&fm=png)

   ![](data:image/svg+xml;charset=utf-8,%3Csvg height='341' width='600' xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg' version='1.1'%3E%3C/svg%3E)

   ![Sign up with your Docusign account](https://images.ctfassets.net/aj9z008chlq0/57UUI47ox5y75KeCfdWQnI/b7d08a785911053d88ba7480205d9f61/Sign_Up.png?w=600&h=341&q=50&fm=png)
5. Select **Accept Agreement** to accept the agreement.

You are now an administrator of the new account with access to both eSignature and Rooms functionality. Go to the [Rooms sign-in page](https://demo.rooms.docusign.com/?_gl=1*18k5ei6*_gcl_au*MTI0OTM5NDAwMi4xNzU4NzUwNzU4LjUyODkwODgzLjE3NjYwODM2MTMuMTc2NjA4MzYxMw..*FPAU*MTI0OTM5NDAwMi4xNzU4NzUwNzU4) to sign in to your new rooms account.
