# Docusign CLM Administration Overview

Read about the role of administrators in Docusign CLM. Administrators are responsible
for account management, user management, and other areas in CLM.

Docusign CLM is a secure document-management platform. You can use CLM to generate documents,
collaborate with stakeholders, and automate business processes.

Docusign CLM administrators are responsible for account management and user management.
Administrators can also maintain the following product areas:

- Product integrations (for example, Salesforce)
- Automated workflows
- Reporting
