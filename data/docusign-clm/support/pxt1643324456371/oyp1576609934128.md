# Component Packaging in Docusign CLM

Read about component packaging in Docusign CLM. Component packaging can be used in
Docusign CLM to move some account settings to a new account, making setup easier.

Docusign CLM account components can be packaged for transfer to other Docusign Accounts.
Packaging is often used to move configurations from one account to another. Administrators can
use Component Packaging to move components between UAT and Production environments.

Component packaging is meant to transfer components rather than a whole Docusign CLM account.
Packaging is not meant to migrate a mass amount of content, such as documents, from one
account to another.
