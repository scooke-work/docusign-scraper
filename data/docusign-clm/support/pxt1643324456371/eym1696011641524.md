# Bulk AI Data Review in Docusign AI Extension for CLM

Read about bulk AI data review in Docusign AI Extension for CLM, and how administrators
can validate AI-suggested attributes in bulk across multiple documents.

Note:
Docusign AI Extension for CLM is currently available only for new CLM
customers. Contact your account representative with questions or to purchase the
Docusign AI Extension for CLM add-on.

Docusign CLM can use artificial intelligence (AI) to extract key terms, or
attributes, from documents. This allows you to understand the agreement data in your
legacy or third-party agreements, and provides visibility to key stakeholders into
critical agreement information. This process also helps ensure that agreements are
discoverable and reportable.

Using bulk AI data review in Docusign AI Extension for CLM, you can now
validate attributes suggested by AI in bulk across multiple documents. This helps to
speed up onboarding and contract migration processes with bulk ingestion, analysis, and
extractions of key legal points from legacy agreements.

You must be an Administrator in CLM.DS to access the Bulk Documents and Data
Management experience in Docusign AI Extension for CLM.
