# Access a Public eForm in Docusign CLM.CM

Learn how to access public eForms in Docusign CLM.CM.

In Docusign CLM.CM, public eForms are published forms
that are available to the general public. Recipients can access, complete, and submit the
eForm through the internet.

Note:

- This topic applies to Docusign CLM.CM. To determine which kind of Docusign CLM account
  you have, see [Identify your Docusign CLM Account
  Type](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fsk1642969066834&topicId=xzt1631832579827.html&_LANG=enus).
- New eForms can no longer be made public. Public eForms that have already been
  published will continue to work without interruption.

1. Log on to Docusign CLM.CM as a Super Administrator.
2. Select Admin from the navigation header.

   The Docusign CLM admin panel displays.
3. Navigate to Automation Tools  >  eForms.
4. In the Access menu, select Show Public
   Links.

   The public links screen displays.
5. Copy the URL, and paste it into a web browser to access your eForm.
6. Complete all parts of the eForm, including the reCAPTCHA at the end of the form.
7. Select Save to submit the eForm.

   You have successfully accessed and completed a public eForm.
