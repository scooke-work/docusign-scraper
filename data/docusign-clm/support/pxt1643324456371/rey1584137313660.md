# eForms in Docusign CLM

Learn about eForms in Docusign CLM. eForms are custom HTML forms that administrators
can build into Docusign CLM. These forms provide a way to replace paper processes with web-based
forms.

Note: You can no longer make new eForms public. Published public eForms
will continue to work without interruption.

eForms are custom HTML forms that administrators can build into Docusign CLM. These forms
provide a way to replace paper processes with web-based forms. You can store any submitted
eForm in a folder, or include the eForm in an advanced workflow step. You can include fields
from your attribute groups in an eForm. Selecting attributes automatically generates the HTML
code for your eForm.

Administrators and Super Administrators can determine whether eForms are private or public.
Private eForms are available only to Docusign CLM users. Private eForms are typically used
within a company between users who have acces to Docusign CLM.

Public eForms are available to anyone with internet access. Public eForms are mostly used
between a company and its customers and vendors. Public eForms include a URL that can be
opened in a web browser. A Docusign CLM license is not required to complete a public
eForm.
