# Global Notifications in Docusign CLM.CM

Read about how to configure alerts in Docusign CLM.CM for users. Users can receive
alerts about folder and document activity, and choose a notification delivery method. Super
Administrators can enable additional notifications to receive alerts about user account and
external review activity.

Note: This topic applies to Docusign CLM.CM. To determine which kind of
Docusign CLM account you have, see [Identify your Docusign CLM
Account Type](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fsk1642969066834&topicId=xzt1631832579827.html&_LANG=enus).

Docusign CLM.CM users can receive alerts about folder and document activity, and choose a
notification delivery method:

- Email notification: Users can receive notifications by
  real-time emails or a single digest.
- Notification counter: Notifications will display as a
  number on the page header.

Docusign CLM.CM Super Administrators can enable notifications to receive alerts about user
accounts and external reviews:

- User account notifications: Administrators can receive alerts when
  users enable, disable, or change their Permission Profile.
- External review notifications: Administrators can receive
  alerts when a reviewer, CC recipient, or sender completes or cancels an external
  review.
