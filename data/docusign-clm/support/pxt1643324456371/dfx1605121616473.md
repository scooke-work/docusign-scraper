# Archive to Agreement Action in Docusign CLM

Learn about the Archive to Agreement Action in Docusign CLM. Administrators can use this
agreement action to automatically archive a document in CLM when specific conditions are
met.

The “Archive to” agreement action triggers archiving a document to CLM when specific envelope
conditions (e.g., custom fields or sender/recipient combinations) are met. Under Add
Actions, select **Archive to** and your CLM account type.![Agreement Actions in CLM 1](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pxt1643324456371/page/images/Agreement-Actions-in-CLM-1.jpg?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTkwMTMsInNoZWFmIjoicHh0MTY0MzMyNDQ1NjM3MSJ9.PYeRkeM5cVbT7NpLBL42K63erRd9cGqxEpMLlip613k&_LANG=enus "Agreement Actions in CLM 1")
