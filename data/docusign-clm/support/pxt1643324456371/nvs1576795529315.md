# Dashboards in Docusign CLM

Read about dashboards and dashboard widgets in Docusign CLM. Administrators and users
can use dashboards to consolidate and organize information.

In Docusign CLM, administrators and users can use dashboards to consolidate and organize
information.

Administrators can create shared dashboards to control the visibility of folders, reports,
and document-linked tasks. Shared dashboards can also be applied globally for an entire
Docusign CLM account.

Users can use personal dashboards and widgets for visibility on document information and
reports.
