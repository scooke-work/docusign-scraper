# Disable Two-Step Verification for Your User Profile in CLM.CM

Learn how to disable two-step verification for your user profile in CLM.CM.

If you enabled two-step verification for your user profile, you can disable the two-step
verification. This action does not impact other users in your account.

**Note**: Removing your account from your Google Authenticator app does not
turn off two-step verification for your CLM.CM account.

1. Log in to your CLM.CM account.
2. Select ![utility-menu](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pxt1643324456371/page/images/Utilitymenu.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTg1ODQsInNoZWFmIjoicHh0MTY0MzMyNDQ1NjM3MSJ9.NcN1WiyTIW53eaghYW9lmAG-K0yFkke2muLQnVAYnvU&_LANG=enus "utility-menu") in the top right corner.
3. Select **My Preferences**.
4. Select 2-Step Verification in the left pane.

   The 2-Step Verification page opens.
5. Select **Off** to disable two-step verification, and select
   Save.

   A dialog box displays, requesting additional information to confirm the
   action.
6. Enter your account password (and a verification code from the Google
   Authenticator app, if necessary), and select
   Continue.

The product disables two-step verification for your user
profile.
