# Two-Factor Authentication in Docusign CLM.CM

Read about two-factor authentication for login in Docusign CLM.CM. Super Administrators
can enable two-factor authentication in Docusign CLM.CM as an extra layer of login
security.

Super Administrators can setup and manage two-factor authentication for user accounts in
Docusign CLM.CM. This gives your organization more control over the level of login
security in Docusign CLM.CM. Administrators can enable and disable two-factor
authentication for the entire account.

Note: This topic applies to Docusign CLM.CM. To determine which kind
of Docusign CLM account you have, see [Identify your
Docusign CLM Account Type](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fsk1642969066834&topicId=xzt1631832579827.html&_LANG=enus).
