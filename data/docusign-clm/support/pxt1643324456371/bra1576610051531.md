# Limited Administrative Permissions with Delegated Permissions in Docusign CLM

Read about delegated permissions in Docusign CLM. Administrators can assign
permissions to users in Docusign CLM (or full subscribers in CLM.CM). Users with delegated
permissions have access to assigned administrative functions.

Administrators can assign limited administrative permissions to CLM users in
Docusign CLM. Users with delegated permissions have access to specific assigned
administrative functions.

Delegated permissions give your organization flexibility when managing work in
CLM. Permissions are grouped into administrative areas based on function. This can help
you assign roles within CLM. For example, you can enable functionality for users on
tasks like customized reports.

Note:

- In Docusign CLM, administrators can assign delegated permissions only to users
  with the CLM User permission profile.
- In CLM.CM, super administrators can assign delegated permissions only to users
  with the Full Subscriber permission profile.
- To determine the type of Docusign CLM account you have, see
  [Identify Your Docusign CLM
  Account Type](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fsk1642969066834&topicId=xzt1631832579827.html&_LANG=enus).
