# Building a Schedule and Rule to Trash Unwanted Documents in Docusign CLM

Read about building a schedule and rule to move canceled contracts to the trash in
Docusign CLM. Administrators can build these items to help ensure removal of unwanted
contracts.

In Docusign CLM, a smart rule lets you build "if-then" logic to automate actions
in your account. A scheduled rule is a smart rule that takes action on
files at specified dates and times. A schedule dictates the times and dates
that a scheduled rule runs.

Administrators can build a schedule and scheduled rule to move canceled contracts to the trash.
This process removes unwanted contracts. Your CLM account storage also gets reduced when
the files get emptied from the trash folder.

following tasks:
