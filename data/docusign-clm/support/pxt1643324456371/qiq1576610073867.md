# Task Management in Docusign CLM

Read about task management options available to administrators, managers, and users in
Docusign CLM.

The task management page is the central inbox for user tasks in Docusign
CLM. Users access their tasks by selecting Tasks in the navigation
header.

The following task management activities are available in Docusign CLM:

- [Enable the Updated Task Management Page in Docusign CLM](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pxt1643324456371&topicId=dtn1607108809778.html&_LANG=enus)

- [Show or Hide Task Management Columns in Docusign CLM](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pxt1643324456371&topicId=laj1576610075029.html&_LANG=enus)

- [Unassign a Task in Docusign CLM](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pxt1643324456371&topicId=iyt1576610076022.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to unassign a task in Docusign CLM.")
