# View My Preferences in Docusign CLM

Learn how to view and change your preferences in Docusign CLM.

You can view your preferences and user profile in Docusign CLM. Your preferences and user
profile indicate your permission levels, groups, and localization settings.

1. Log on to Docusign CLM.
2. Select My Preferences from the user profile menu.

   The My Preferences screen displays.
3. Select or make changes to your preferences, and view your profile on Docusign CLM:

   - Change your localization, region settings, and email signature in the
     General section.
   - View your user personas, permission profile, and groups in the
     My Profile section.
   - Change settings for notifications in the Notification
     Settings section.
4. Once all changes have been made, select Save.

   You have successfully viewed and made changes to your preferences in
   Docusign CLM.
