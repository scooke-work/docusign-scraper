# Email Documents in Docusign CLM

Learn how to send documents through email in Docusign CLM.

In Docusign CLM, you can send documents as email
attachments or share links to documents and folders. When you share a document through an
email, the action gets tracked in the document history. You can send Docusign CLM emails to
mailing lists or single users.

1. Log in to Docusign CLM.
2. Select Documents in the navigation header.

   The Documents view opens.
3. Select the checkboxes for documents to send by email, and select Send  >  Email.

   A page opens where you can complete fields for sending the email. The documents
   that you selected are displayed in the Attachments
   field.
4. Complete all required fields, and complete other fields as needed.

   Tip: To include additional documents as attachments,
   choose Select Items to navigate to other documents and select
   them.
5. Select Send.

The product schedules the email to be sent to the specified
recipients. The selected documents get included in the email as attachments.
