# Identify Your Docusign CLM Account Type

Learn how to identify your account type in Docusign CLM.

When you log on to Docusign CLM, you can identify your
account type. There are two account types:

- CLM.DS
- CLM.CM

1. Log on to Docusign CLM.
2. Select the profile icon.

   Your name and account type display.
3. Identify your account type:

   CLM
   :   Indicates that you are using CLM that authenticates through
       Docusign. Also known as CLM.DS.

   CLM.CM
   :   Indicates that you are using CLM that authenticates through
       SpringCM. Also known as CLM.CM.

   You have successfully identified your CLM account type.
