# Document Management in Docusign CLM

Read about document management in Docusign CLM. Users with access to a document can
perform actions to manage the document.

After a document gets uploaded to Docusign CLM, users with access to the document can manage
the document. Users can also keep documents organized by creating and managing folders.
Document management can include any of the following activities:

- Moving and copying documents between folders
- Editing documents and commenting in documents
- Sending documents to parties for review
- Assigning tasks for review
- Comparing document versions
