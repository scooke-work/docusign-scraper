# Document Upload and Download Limits in Docusign CLM

Read about document upload and download limits in Docusign CLM. Understand these limits
when uploading and downloading documents between your computer and Docusign CLM.

Documents can be uploaded and downloaded between your computer and Docusign CLM. The
following table outlines the document upload and download limits, based on the method of
transfer.

| Method | Maximum Document Size | Maximum Number of Documents | Maximum File Size with Multiple Documents |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Drag-and-drop | 1.5 GB | 20 | 1.5 GB times the number of documents uploaded, up to the limit of space left in the account |
| Download through the user interface | 1.5 GB | 20 | 1.5 GB times the number of documents downloaded |
| Salesforce widget drag-and-drop upload | 1.5 GB | 20 | 1.5 GB times the number of documents uploaded, up to the limit of space left in the account |
| Salesforce widget upload | 1.5 GB | 20 | 1.5 GB times the number of documents uploaded, up to the limit of space left in the account |
| SFTP upload or download | Unlimited, up to the space limit in the account (pending network connection and bandwidth) | Unlimited | Unlimited, up to the space limit in the account (pending network connection and bandwidth) |
| Inbound email | Limited to the size that the inbound client will allow (typically less than 10 MB) | Limited to the number of attachments you can add to an email | Limited to the size that the inbound client will allow (typically less than 10 MB) |
| Sync upload or download | Whatever can be successfully transmitted in 2 hours | Up to 5 concurrent transfers (upload or download) | Whatever can be successfully transmitted in 2 hours |
