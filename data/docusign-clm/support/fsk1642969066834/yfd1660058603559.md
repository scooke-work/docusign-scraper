# Access Docusign CLM

Learn how to log on and log off of Docusign CLM.

To access all of the features and documents in
Docusign CLM, users must log in using their Docusign credentials. Once you have
completed your tasks in Docusign CLM, you should securely log out through your user
profile. This ensures that none of your account information can be accessed by outside
parties.

Note:

- To see what CLM account type you have, see [Identify Your Docusign CLM Account Type](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fsk1642969066834&topicId=xzt1631832579827.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to identify your account type in Docusign CLM.").
- To access Docusign CLM from the app switcher, users must have a Docusign CLM
  account. Contact you Docusign CLM administrator if CLM does not display in
  the app switcher.

1. To log on to CLM.DS:
   1. In a web browser, navigate to [https://clm.docusign.com](https://clm.docusign.com/).

      Note: To log on to a Docusign CLM UAT or test
      environment, navigate to [https://clmdemo.docusign.com](https://clmdemo.docusign.com/).
   2. Enter your Docusign CLM email address, and select
      Next.
   3. Enter your Docusign CLM password, and select Log
      In.

      The Docusign CLM home page displays. You have successfully
      accessed your Docusign CLM account.
2. To log on to CLM.CM:
   1. In a web browser, navigate to [https://login.springcm.com](https://login.springcm.com/).

      Note: To log on to a CLM.CM UAT or test
      environment, navigate to [https://loginuat.springcm.com](https://loginuat.springcm.com/).
   2. Enter your CLM.CM credentials, and select Log
      In.

      The CLM.CM home page displays. You have successfully accessed
      your CLM.CM account.
