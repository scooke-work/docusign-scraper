# Preference Management in Docusign CLM

Learn about the My Preferences page in Docusign CLM. Users can use this page in CLM to
configure their account settings.

You can use the My Preferences page in Docusign CLM to configure your
CLM account settings.

Note: You will only be able to configure settings that your CLM
administrator has enabled for your Docusign CLM account.

Localization Settings
:   You can configure your language and region settings in your CLM account allowing
    you to manage you work in your native date and time formats. For more
    information about configuring localization preferences in CLM, see [Configure Your Localization Preferences in Docusign CLM](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fsk1642969066834&topicId=dts1576610029141.html&_LANG=enus "You can configure your language and region settings in your Docusign CLM account allowing you to manage you work in your native date and time formats.").

Email Settings
:   You can configure your email signature in your CLM account. For more information
    about configuring your email signature in CLM, see [Configure Your Email Signature in Docusign CLM](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fsk1642969066834&topicId=don1659213582980.html&_LANG=enus "You can configure your email signature in your Docusign CLM account.").

Tasks
:   You can configure the page you return to after completing a task in CLM. For
    more information about configuring your task preferences in CLM, see [Configure Your Task Preferences in Docusign CLM](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fsk1642969066834&topicId=zve1659728465428.html&_LANG=enus "You can configure your task preferences in Docusign CLM. You can instruct the product to return to your inbox after you complete a task in Docusign CLM.").

Notification Preferences
:   You can configure your notification preferences in CLM. For more information about
    configuring your notification preferences in CLM, see [Enable or Disable CLM Slack
    Notification (User)](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=dtv1597882236545&topicId=nqj1648676511957.html&_LANG=enus).
