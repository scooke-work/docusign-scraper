# Document Actions in Docusign CLM

Learn about the actions that can be taken on documents in Docusign CLM's document
library. This includes actions that enable moving, uploading, and deleting
documents.

Docusign CLM includes a suite of tools that enable document management. Users can take
advantage of several methods for uploading, moving, copying, and deleting documents.

Document management in Docusign CLM also enables users to download documents, or send and
share documents for review.
