# Configure Your Localization Preferences in Docusign CLM

You can configure your language and region settings in your Docusign CLM account
allowing you to manage you work in your native date and time formats.

You can configure your language and region settings in your Docusign CLM account. This
allows you to manage your work in your native date and time formats.

1. Log on to Docusign CLM.
2. Select My Preferences from the user profile menu.
3. Select a language from the Language dropdown menu.

   Note: The
   Language setting controls the language displayed in the user
   interface. By default, this field is set to the CLM account default.
4. Select a time zone from the Time Zone dropdown menu.

   Note: The Time
   Zone setting formats timestamps and notifications.
5. Select a region from the Region dropdown menu.

   Note: The
   Region setting formats dates and times. By default, this field
   detects your localization settings from your web browser.
6. Select Save.

   You have configured your localization settings.
