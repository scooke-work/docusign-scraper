# Configure Your Email Signature in Docusign CLM

You can configure your email signature in your Docusign CLM account.

You can configure your email signature in your Docusign CLM account.

1. Log on to Docusign CLM.
2. Select My Preferences from the user profile menu.
3. Configure your email signature in the Email Settings
   section.
4. Select Save.

   You have configured your email signature.
