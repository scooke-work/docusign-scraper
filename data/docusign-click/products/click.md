## What is a clickwrap?

A clickwrap is an industry-accepted term that refers to a simple method for [legally indicating consent](https://www.docusign.com/white-papers/the-effectiveness-of-clickwrap-for-legally-enforceable-agreements) to a set of terms such as terms of service and privacy policies by clicking "I agree" or similar process. Other use cases include account sign-ups and openings, software licenses and downloads, product trials, checkout pages and registration pages.
