# Review Data Verification Events

Learn how to use your Docusign account's envelope history and
Certificate of Completion to determine whether the data verification passed or failed.

Use your Docusign account's envelope history and Certificate of Completion
(COC) to determine whether the data verification passed or failed. The features support
these management tasks:

- Account envelope history
  - Determine if data verifications per envelope were successful to identify risks
  - Keep a trusted record of events in case of customer support, legal, or
    compliance needs
  - Store records for legal, litigation, or compliance purposes
  - Investigate technical and customer issues
- Document's Certificate of Completion
  - Manage customer inquiries and disputes
  - Respond to internal and external investigations
  - Use this information as evidence in legal, arbitration, or mediation
    proceedings
