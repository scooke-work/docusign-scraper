# Understand Docusign Extension Apps' Verification Definition

Learn how Docusign Data Verification partner apps works and what kind
of information is verified.

As an administrator or agreement preparer, you must understand the verification definitions
implemented by each Data Verification partner app. Doing so ensures that it suits your
business needs and business processes that use agreement data and associated
verification.

What is Verification definition?
:   Verification definition includes:

    - What is being verified: Required input information
    - Why it's verified: The criteria or logic that determines if an attempt to verify is
      successful

Where can I find Data Verification Apps information?
:   Review the app's information in the [Docusign App Center](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center). Each app has a feature
    summary. Additionally, there is an example for incorporating the app's features
    in a workflow.

What is Data Verification app coverage?
:   Coverage varies across with external verification services. The coverage can include items
    such as countries, geographic regions, jurisdictions, data types, data
    sources.
