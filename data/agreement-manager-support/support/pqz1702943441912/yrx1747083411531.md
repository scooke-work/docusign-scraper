# Custom Extractions

Read about custom extractions, including how these data points bring value to our organization.

Custom extractions unlock organization and industry-specific agreement data and insights beyond the standard Docusign-provided fields. This includes custom agreement types and custom fields. Once the AI detection is enabled for your custom agreement types and fields, these values are surfaced in your agreements.

Note: See the [Custom Agreement Type Extractions](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con6f74f3c5-e52b-4611-9d7b-8455c97725b4.html&_LANG=enus "Read about custom agreement type extractions, including their use, purpose, and entry points in Agreement Manager.") and [Custom Field Extractions](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=condece8d7e-2da8-4e5f-8ba4-3f0175c2a625.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the custom field extractions, including entry points for creating new fields and a review of the AI detection setup wizard.") topics to learn more about these custom extractions.

Custom extractions bring value to your organization:

- Value of increased productivity (hours saved)
- Value of unlocking data and insights
  - Boost productivity by tracking the data points in terms that are unique to your organization or industry
  - Simplify compliance monitoring with more consistent and precise agreement records
  - Enhance strategic decision-making with greater visibility into your agreement data

Important:

Customers do not need to consent to AI training to use this feature. When customers create custom
agreement types and fields, their data is used as **input** to the AI model to help generate
results for their use. This process does not involve AI training.

If a customer has given consent to sharing data for AI
training, anonymized data may be used for training Docusign AI models. This may
include anonymized data from custom extractions.

See the [Docusign FAQs for AI](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fzd1707173174972&topicId=uss1707173279973.html) topic for more information about how
Docusign uses data for AI training.

## Process overview

Standard, Docusign-provided agreement types, and fields are surfaced in
your agreements automatically during processing. Custom extractions let you extend
your agreement data and insights to include your own organization-specific data
points.

Through the use of custom extractions, your agreement data is transformed into actionable strategic insights:

![An infographic of the custom extraction process for mastering agreement intelligence with Agreement Manager.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Custom-extractions_process-orverview_inforgraphic.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwOTksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.c5ZKUh5ikeyOTNFDADltqZAuLBZN31UF3sndHL1KlIw&_LANG=enus)

1. **Define unique agreement types:** Categorize your document portfolio with custom types that reflect your specific business needs.
2. **Add targeted custom fields:** Capture unique data points by creating and adding custom fields to your agreement types. For example, create custom text, number, date, or dropdown fields to surface these attributes in your agreements.
3. **Enable AI detection:** Activate automated extractions for your custom types and fields. The
   AI model identifies data without manual entry. The definitions and
   examples provided during the setup are used to teach the AI detection to
   recognize your data points.
4. **Process documents to unlock data:** Reprocess historical documents or upload new ones to surface your custom extractions. Once the AI detection is activated, your custom extractions are surfaced in new documents automatically. However, reprocessing for historical documents is manually triggered when you activate the extraction.
5. **Drive strategy with strategic insights:** Use the expanded data points to drive strategic insights, including tailored reporting. For example, use the extracted data points to:

   - Identify cost saving opportunities
   - Simplify compliance monitoring
   - Make informed contract renewal decisions

## Use cases

Organizations can use custom agreement types and fields to meet their specific and unique use cases. For example:

- A financial services organization can monitor Loyalty Program Agreements more closely. The data surfaced in these agreements can be used to personalize customer outreach and boost retention.
- A medical supplies organization can track agreements with specific volume discounts and industry-specific compliance requirements.
- A human resource (HR) team can manage specific types of HR-related agreements. For example, independent contractor or gig worker agreements. The agreement data can be used to enhance workforce planning and ensure compliance with local labor laws.

## Why do we ask for examples?

When you set up custom extractions, you are prompted to provide examples, in addition to a text
definition. The definition tells the AI model what the data means and the examples
show how the data displays in your agreements.

![A screenshot of the AI detection setup wizard that prompts you to choose a method for providing examples.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Custom-extractions_AI-detection_Setup-Provide-examples.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwOTksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.c5ZKUh5ikeyOTNFDADltqZAuLBZN31UF3sndHL1KlIw&_LANG=enus)

Examples are important to the AI detection setup process:

- **Teach the AI model real patterns, not just the labels and field names.** Your examples should include the actual value plus the surrounding text for context. When you include the context, the AI model learns to detect a pattern, not just the field or attribute name.
- **Account for variation in language and layout.** The same custom extraction can appear in different sentences, clauses, tables, or forms. When you provide multiple examples, the AI model learns to generalize across different formats, document types, and writing styles.
- **Distinguish between similar concepts.** Examples make it easier for the AI model to detect the differences between closely related fields or attributes. For example, different dates or fee types may look similar but have different meanings.
- **Improve your accuracy score.** It is equally important to include example documents that do not contain the custom data. Including incorrect examples teaches the AI model to recognize when the data is not present.

## Why do we test for accuracy?

Before you activate the AI detection for custom extractions, the setup wizard prompts you to test for accuracy. This step is optional, however, it is highly recommended. Testing for accuracy helps you confirm that the AI model is finding the right data in your agreements.

![A screenshot of the setup wizard that prompts you to test the accuracy before activating the AI detection.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Custom-extractions_AI-detection_Setup_Test-for-accuracy.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwOTksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.c5ZKUh5ikeyOTNFDADltqZAuLBZN31UF3sndHL1KlIw&_LANG=enus)

Testing is important to the AI detection setup process:

- **Validates how the AI detection behaves on real agreements.** You can see where the custom data was correctly identified and where it was not. This gives you the chance to refine the AI detection and improve its accuracy before activating it.
- **Catches setup issues early.** Many accuracy issues are caused by vague or overloaded definitions or from poor examples. For example, the examples don't fully represent the data. Testing for accuracy provides feedback so you can refine your definitions and examples before activation.
- **Builds trust in your results.** Accuracy and guidance are key to achieving success with your custom extractions. Accurate agreement insights and reporting depend on being able to trust your data. The testing experience is designed to give you more confidence in your agreement insights and data.
- **Protect reporting and compliance workflows.** Validating the extraction quality reduces the risk of incorrect values flowing into your filters, dashboards, or compliance monitoring.

Once you are satisfied with the accuracy score and sample results, you can activate the AI detection for your custom extraction. Your new and selected historical agreements are processed for the custom data point.

## Custom extraction and AI detection limits

Custom extractions and AI detection are evolving based on feedback and usage patterns. Be sure to review these limitations before using custom extractions:

| **Limitation** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| **Limited AI detection** | AI detection is available for custom agreement types and fields. AI detection is not available for obligations or clause extractions. |
| **Custom field dependency** | Custom fields do not exist outside of an agreement types. Link these fields to a standard or custom agreement type. |
| **Limited custom field options** | Custom fields are limited to text, number, date, and dropdown options. |
| **Reporting limitations** | Custom extractions cannot be used to build custom reports. However, data from custom extractions shows in existing reports where relevant. |
| **Custom types (no AI detection)** | The limit for custom agreement types *without AI detection* is 200 types per account. When AI detection is not enabled, users can manually tag agreements with a custom agreement type. |
| **Custom types (with AI detection)** | The limit for custom agreement types *with AI detection* is 15 per account. |
| **Custom fields (no AI detection)** | The limit for custom fields *without AI detection* is 100 fields per account. When AI detection is not enabled, users can manually enter values for custom fields. |
| **Extraction limits** | Customers with IAM Professional, IAM Enterprise, and IAM for Sales plans can create up to 50 custom extractions per account:  - The limit represents the combined number of custom agreement types and fields with AI detection. - The limit can represent any combination of custom agreement types and fields that equal 50.  Consider these scenarios:  - Customers can create a maximum of 50 custom field extractions across standard agreement types. - Customers can create a maximum of 49 custom field extractions across one custom agreement type (with AI detection). The custom fields are also surfaced in other standard agreement types. - Customers can create a maximum of 35 custom field extractions across 15 custom agreement types. The custom fields are also surfaced in other standard agreement types. - Customers **cannot** create 34 custom field extractions across 16 custom agreement types   (with AI detection). **Reminder:** The limit for   custom agreement types with AI detection is 15 per   account.   Note: Without AI detection, customers can create up to 100 custom fields in total. Field values for custom fields without AI detection are populated manually during data review. |
| **Filters for bulk processing** | When locating agreements to refresh for custom data, do not use search queries to locate agreements. On-demand or bulk processing supports the use of filters to identify agreements to process for custom data. |
| **Numbers support** | There is no explicit support for number fields, such as currency, percentage, and unit of time. |
| **AI detection samples** | You can provide up to 10 example documents when setting up the AI detection. |
| **Document processing limit** | There is a 1,500 document limit when processing documents. This limit applies to both newly ingested documents and documents that require reprocessing for custom data. Tip: The 1,500 document processing limit is the "allowance per seat, per year" and is pooled at the account-level. For example, if an account has 100 users, the document processing limit for the account is 150,000 per year. |
| **No service level agreement (SLA) for processing jobs** | There is no SLA for processing times. You can view the status and details for processing jobs from the job monitoring hub. |
| **View processing usage** | Currently, there is no way to monitor the volume of processing at the aggregate level. |
| **Unable to cancel jobs** | Processing jobs cannot be canceled once started. |

Note: See the [Understanding Your IAM and eSignature Plan Allowances](https://www.docusign.com/iam/plan-allowances) page to learn more about processing limits by plan type.

## On-demand processing for custom data (Update)

Once custom agreement types and fields are enabled for AI detection, you can choose specific agreements to reprocess for new data. For example, if you have a number of *Miscellaneous* agreements that fall under a newly created custom type.

Filter the agreement list table to locate the agreements that you want to process for new data points. When you select one or more agreements, additional actions are displayed, including the Update option:

![A screenshot of the Completed Documents view that highlights the selected agreements that you want to refresh for custom data.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Custom-extractions_Refresh-Custom-Data-highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwOTksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.c5ZKUh5ikeyOTNFDADltqZAuLBZN31UF3sndHL1KlIw&_LANG=enus)

Expand the Update dropdown and choose to reprocess for Standard Data or Custom Data.
