# Agreement Manager for Contract Managers and Analyzers

Read about how analyzers and contract managers interact with Agreement Manager, including example job titles and use cases. This topic answers the question: How do contract managers and analyzers use Agreement Manager to do their jobs?

Contract managers and analyzers use Agreement Manager to understand their agreements. This includes reviewing, updating, and verifying suggested attribute values that were surfaced in your agreements. These users might also use the Renewals dashboard to analyze agreements for upcoming renewal opportunities.

Typical tasks include:

- Verify suggested attribute (or field) values for processed agreements
- Locate agreements for a specific customer when preparing a new agreement
- Filter agreements to gain insight into relationships with employees, customers, partners, and investors
- Manage parties to ensure party normalization
- Verify the number of expiring or renewing agreements to manage renewals and terminations
- Manage agreement obligations and obligation types
- Create agreement sets to organize and share sets of agreements with users and groups
- Create custom agreement types and fields that reflect your organization's specific agreements
- Kick-off and monitor agreement processing jobs
