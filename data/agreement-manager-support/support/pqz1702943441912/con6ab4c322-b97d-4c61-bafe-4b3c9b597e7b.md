# Docusign Agreement Manager for CLM

Read about Agreement Manager for CLM customers with IAM Plans.

Docusign Agreement Manager for CLM enables organizations to store, manage, and analyze agreements. The Agreement Manager for CLM integration automatically organizes CLM agreements data for easier discovery and analysis. Agreement Manager for CLM is a unified repository of both CLM and eSignature documents.

With Agreement Manager, you can:

- Centralize agreements and empower your most valuable teams to find what they need fast.
- Use AI to uncover cost-saving opportunities by harnessing agreement data.
- Use AI to organize agreements into parties for better portfolio analysis and visibility.

To learn more, see the documentation about [Docusign Agreement Manager](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=adf1702945446135.html&_LANG=enus).

Note:

- Docusign Agreement Manager integration is available only for CLM customers who have purchased an Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) Enterprise or Sales plan. For more information, contact your account representative.
- Agreement Manager leverages artificial intelligence (AI) to provide insight to your agreements. For complete information, see the documentation about [AI denotation in Agreement Manager](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=rsz1722973735168.html&_LANG=enus) and [AI Data Controls Setting FAQs](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=fzd1707173174972&topicId=dcs1708531444159.html&_LANG=enus) .
