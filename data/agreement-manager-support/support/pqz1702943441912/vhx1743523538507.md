# Capabilities by Agreement Manager Permissions

Look up the available capabilities for each Agreement Manager permission.

There are two primary options when granting access to agreements in an account:

- **View all agreements in this account:** this permission grants basic access to view and download all agreements in an account. This access is dependent on agreement-level access controls. For example, some agreements contain sensitive information and are not accessible for all users.
- **Organize and manage all agreements:** this permission grants the highest level of access to view, edit, download, and delete all agreements in the account. This permission also grants access to manage agreement sets and attributes, like agreement types and fields.

Important:

When organizations purchase an Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) plan with Agreement Manager, by default, all users can access their
own shared, received, sent, and uploaded agreements. However, specific Agreement Manager access is required to view, organize, and manage all the agreements in the account.

Restriction: While administrators automatically have
full access to all the Agreement Manager features, they do not have access to
view the agreements. Access is explicitly assigned to administrators for them to
view any of the agreements in the account. See the [Agreement Sharing and Downloads](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=hrr1722978886188.html&_LANG=enus "This topic answers the question: What are my direct sharing and download options for Agreement Manager documents, including the required permissions?") article to learn more about sharing agreements
with others.

## Agreement Manager access for "free participants"

*Free participants* can view their own completed agreements in Agreement Manager. These *free license* users have similar access to Agreement Manager features as those users with View all agreements permission. However, this role cannot use Agreement Manager features that rely on the account's repository and data sets.

For example, a "free participant" may have access to the Insights tab, but no data is loaded. The dashboards and reports are populated from the account's Agreement Manager data set. Access to this information requires Agreement Manager permissions:

- View all agreements in this account permission (minimum requirement)
- Organize and manage all agreements permission

Free participants can gain access to additional agreements through shared agreement sets. Depending on the assigned access level, users can view, edit, or manage the agreements in a shared set.

A full license and additional permission are required to use the majority of Agreement Manager features. See the [License Management in Account Administration](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=bhs1740099648152.html) topic to learn more about IAM plans and license types.
