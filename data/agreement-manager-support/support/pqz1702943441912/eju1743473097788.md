# Agreement Manager Help FAQs

Look up answers to frequently asked questions related to Agreement Manager, including capabilities by permission, agreement ingestion, and dashboard reports.

The purpose of this support topic is to provide answers to common Agreement Manager questions, including permissions, agreement ingestion, and dashboard reports.

## General

## Permissions and capabilities

## How many permission levels does Agreement Manager have?

There are two permission levels for granting access to agreements in an account:

1. **View all agreements in this account:** authorizes users or groups to view and download all agreements in the account (to which they have access).
2. **Organize and manage all agreements:** authorizes users or groups to view, edit, download, and delete all agreements in the account. This permission level also grants access to manage agreement sets and attributes, like agreement types and fields.

Note: There is a third permission that gives users access to bulk upload up to 1,000 files at a time to Agreement Manager. The Upload up to 1,000 files at a time toggle is turned on (blue) at the user-level.

Restriction: While administrators automatically have
full access to all the Agreement Manager features, they do not have access to
view the agreements. Access is explicitly assigned to administrators for them to
view any of the agreements in the account. See the [Agreement Sharing and Downloads](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=hrr1722978886188.html&_LANG=enus "This topic answers the question: What are my direct sharing and download options for Agreement Manager documents, including the required permissions?") article to learn more about sharing agreements
with others.

See the [User and Group Management](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=eut1712157553977.html) support topic to learn more about Agreement Manager permissions.

## What's the difference between the Agreement Manager permission levels?

The View all agreements in this account permission limits users and groups to viewing and downloading all the agreements in the account to which they have access.

The Organize and manage all agreements permission gives users and groups expanded capabilities, such as:

- Agreement set management
- Agreement type management
- Custom field management
- Duplicate party management

See the [Capabilities by Agreement Manager Permissions](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=vhx1743523538507.html) support topic to learn more about the capabilities to which each permission has access.

## Can administrators automatically access Agreement Manager agreements and functionality?

No. While administrators automatically have full access to all Agreement Manager features by default, they do not have access to view the agreements.

See the [Agreement Manager Permissions, Profiles, and Roles](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=eut1712157553977.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the available Agreement Manager permissions, including how users and groups are granted access to Agreement Manager agreements and example roles for each permission.") support topic to learn more about Agreement Manager permissions.

## Can I use Agreement Manager without assigned permissions?

Yes.

When organizations purchase an Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) plan with Agreement Manager, by default, all users can access their
own shared, received, sent, and uploaded agreements. However, specific Agreement Manager access is required to view, organize, and manage all the agreements in the account.

See the [User and Group Management](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=eut1712157553977.html) support topic to learn more about Agreement Manager permissions.

## Agreement processing

## How are agreement types identified during agreement processing?

Docusign AI models are designed to detect agreement types by considering the title and content of ingested and uploaded files. This includes recognizing synonyms and phrase variations.

See the [Agreement Type Management](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=uie1724446326038.html) support topic to learn more about agreement types.

## What is the limit for creating custom fields and agreement types?

Customers with IAM Professional, IAM Enterprise, and IAM for Sales plans can create up to 50 custom extractions per account. This includes any combination of custom agreement types and fields that are enabled with AI detection:

- The limit represents the combined number of custom agreement types and fields with AI detection.
- The limit can represent any combination of custom agreement types and fields that equal 50 - taking into account the customer agreement type limit.
- For custom agreement types with AI detection, the limit is 15 per account.

.

Messages are displayed when you reach the total number of custom agreement types and fields allowed per account.

Important: Without AI detection, customers can create up to 100 custom fields and agreement types in total. Field values for custom fields without AI detection are populated manually during data review.

## How long does it take to process my documents?

The processing time for agreement uploads, ingestion, and reprocessing is dependent on a number of variables. Processing jobs can take from hours to days, depending on:

- Number of documents being processed or reprocessed
- Size of the data set being extracted (standard versus standard plus expanded attributes)
- Number and size of jobs currently in the processing queue
- Increased activity in the processing queue due to period close dates

Yes. Hierarchies are created and managed from the Hierarchies tab in the party hub. You can also view and create hierarchies from the Related agreements pane on the Agreement preview page.

## Ingestion and access controls

## Can I restrict which historical agreements are ingested to Agreement Manager?

No. Currently, you cannot choose which Docusign historical agreements are auto-ingested into Agreement Manager. All historical agreements are ingested.

See the [General Limitations and Considerations for Agreement Manager](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=jyn1704248204281.html) support topic for more information.

## Can I use ingestion filtering to sync agreements from eSignature?

No. Ingestion filtering is not widely available. Customers must meet specific criteria and complete a request form before being considered for this functionality. Contact your account executive or account team to discuss this function further.

## Can I set access controls for ingested agreements?

Yes. You have the option of adding an agreement set to the sync configuration. Agreement sets are used to organize agreements into groups based on filters:

- Share sets with users or groups
- Limit access to the set agreements by assigning access levels

See the [Agreement Set Management](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=zed1730843285476.html) support topic to learn more about agreement sets.

## Can I bulk share documents with colleagues?

Yes. Agreements can be shared in bulk with agreement sets. See these support topics to learn more about agreement sets:

- [Create an Agreement Set](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=jay1736181359265.html)

Note: Only users that are assigned the Organize and manage all agreements permission can create and manage agreement sets. This toggle is turned on from the user or group profile page by an administrator.

Agreements can also be shared during the bulk upload process:

- Add to agreement sets
- Share files

Note: The bulk upload process is available to users with the Upload up to 1,000 files at a time permission. This toggle is turned on from the user profile page by an administrator.

## If I add more agreements to a set, are the "share" settings updated automatically?

Yes. Once you share an agreement set with other users or groups, they will have access to any agreements that are added in the future. Likewise, if a document is removed from an agreement set, it no longer displays in the agreement list for shared users or groups.

See these support topics to learn more about agreement sets and share settings:

- [Share an Agreement Set](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=pvb1736186431453.html)
- [Share an Agreement with Others](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=vyz1722979030156.html)

## Can I restrict users with "view" access from downloading agreements?

No. There is no special permission for restricting access to download documents.

## Are received agreements synced to Agreement Manager?

Yes. Your completed eSignature envelopes, whether you are the recipient or the sender, are automatically ingested into Agreement Manager. Both the sent and received agreements are ingested if the envelope is completed.

## Agreement List Table

## Can I display the envelope ID in the agreement list table?

Yes. The Envelope ID column is displayed in the agreement list table by default. If this column is not visible, customize the table so the Envelope ID column is displayed.

The envelope value is displayed for any documents that were ingested from completed eSignature envelopes.

See the [Display the Envelope ID in the Agreement List Table](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=sqs1748881628076.html) support topic for more information.

## Dashboards, reports, and subscriptions

## Can I view report data based on agreements in a set?

Yes. The Sets filter is available for all the insights and charts on the Agreements Dashboard view. Use this filter to view report data for the agreements in a selected set.

See the [View Report Data for Sets of Agreements](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tskf1577eb7-c826-4181-8a66-d0e9ff353b86.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to filter an agreement report to display data for a selected set of agreements.") support topic for more information.

## Can I subscribe to dashboard reports?

Use the Subscribe button to subscribe to standard and custom reports. Before setting up a subscription, be sure to customize the report so that the relevant data is displayed.

Tip: If you make changes to the report, expand the dropdown in the top-right corner and select Save As. The report is saved to the REPORT TYPE > Custom section located in the pane on the left.

After customizing the report, select Subscribe and complete the Create subscription form. See the [Subscribe to Receive Report Notifications](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=wfw1734545937270.html) support topic to learn more about report subscriptions.

## Can I update my email notification preferences?

Yes. Notification preferences give you control over the email notifications that you receive. By default, all the notification types related to the completed agreements in your account are enabled. See the [Customize Notification Settings for Your Completed Agreements](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk155b1ed7-7b45-43cd-be92-2a5279630c0f.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to customize your email notification settings from the Notification Preferences page.") topic to learn more about customizing your notification preferences.
