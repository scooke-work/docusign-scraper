# Agreement Manager for Contract Viewers and Preparers

Read about how contract viewers and preparers interact with Agreement Manager, including example job titles and use cases. This topic answers the question: How do contract viewers and preparers use Agreement Manager to do their jobs?

Contract viewers and preparers use Agreement Manager to locate specific agreement data to perform job duties. Example tasks by department include:

- Looking up a customer agreement (Support)
- Reviewing a vendor agreement (Procurement)
- Verifying payment terms (Accounting)
- Referring to purchased products to verify if discounts were applied (Sales)

See the use case topics in the Related Content section on the right to learn how to complete a few basic tasks using Agreement Manager.
