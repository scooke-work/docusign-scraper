# Test the Extraction Accuracy for Your Custom Agreement Types

Learn how to test the accuracy of the AI detection for your custom agreement types.

## Before you begin

This topic reviews the next step of the AI detection set-up process, *Test for accuracy*. The steps in this task are completed by administrators and contract managers.

Remember: Administrators automatically have access to configure agreement data. For non-administrator users, agreement type management tasks require Organize and manage all agreements access to Agreement Manager agreements and functionality.

Complete these topics before attempting this task:

- [Configure New Custom Agreement Types](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=gke1727282238086.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to create custom agreement types to support organizational use cases.")
- [Define Your Custom Agreement Types](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tske5fe1ad5-2832-4e6c-9177-f33a9ffade15.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to initiate the AI detection set-up process and complete the first step to define the custom type.")
- [Add Examples of Your Custom Agreement Types](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tskd38465c6-c8c0-4588-bfb3-fdb6916a1926.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to complete the second step of the AI detection set-up process, Add examples of this agreement type.")

Before you activate the AI detection for custom extractions, the setup wizard prompts you to test for accuracy. This step is optional, however, it is highly recommended. The results are a good indicator of the quality and accuracy that you can expect from the AI detection before activating it.

In the previous step, you selected documents that exemplify the custom agreement type. After you selected the Save Examples option, the AI detection set-up wizard advanced to the next step.

You are prompted to test the accuracy of the AI detection:

![A screenshot of the dialog box that states that AI detection is now available for your custom agreement type.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreement-Types-Manager_AI-detect-setup_Test-for-accuracy_dialog-box.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2MDMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.h5uUIBxzOpW79oGDCHVEgK2Dr-un4K32fiNgfa_emUw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the dialog box that states that AI detection is now available for your custom agreement type.")The Test for accuracy option is selected by default. Use this option to review how well the AI detection can find the agreement type in your documents.

1. Your AI detection is now available for the custom agreement type. Before
   activating the AI detection, Docusign recommends that you test it for accuracy.
   Select the Confirm option.

   Tip: Choose a broader set of documents to test
   whether the system can correctly recognize and differentiate similar
   agreement types. For example, can the system correctly identify a
   *non-short-term lease* while testing the AI detection of the
   *short-term lease* type? Did the system classify the documents as
   the same type, *short-term lease*?

   The Select relevant documents page opens. Choose
   documents to confirm if the AI detection can accurately locate the agreement
   type.
2. Locate the documents that you want to use to test the AI detection accuracy:

   ![A snippet of the Select relevant documents page that shows the search field and filter options to locate agreements for testing.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreement-Types-Manager_AI-detect-setup_Locate-documents_search-filter-options.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2MDMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.h5uUIBxzOpW79oGDCHVEgK2Dr-un4K32fiNgfa_emUw&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Select relevant documents page that shows the search field and filter options to locate agreements for testing.")

   1. Search for the relevant agreements. Enter a keyword or search string in the search field. The dropdown opens and search suggestions are displayed. Select a search suggestion or press the Enter key.
   2. Filter the agreement list table to find the relevant agreements. For example, filter by Sets, Status, Parties, Agreement Type, or AI Suggestions.

      Tip: Use the All Filters option to apply additional filters to the results table.

      Expand the filter, choose the relevant filter value, and select the Apply option.

   The results table updates and the documents that match the search criteria and applied filters are displayed.
3. Choose the documents that you want to use in the test.
4. Select the Start Testing option in the bottom right.

   The Agreement preview opens for the first document. The pane on the left shows the agreement type that the AI detection identified in the document.

   ![A screenshot of the Agreement preview page that highlights the agreement type that the AI detection identified for the document.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Agreement-Type_Test-for-accuracy_Confirm-accuracy-of-identified-types-in-selected-documents.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2MDMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.h5uUIBxzOpW79oGDCHVEgK2Dr-un4K32fiNgfa_emUw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Agreement preview page that highlights the agreement type that the AI detection identified for the document.")
5. Review the identified agreement type in the context of the document. Verify that the AI detection surfaced the correct value.
   - Select Yes if the AI detection identified the correct agreement type.
   - Select No if the AI detection did not identify the correct agreement type.

   Note: If a different agreement type is identified, a message is displayed:
   ![A screenshot of the message that displays when a different agreement type is identified.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Agreement-Type_Test-for-accuracy_Identified-other-type.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2MDMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.h5uUIBxzOpW79oGDCHVEgK2Dr-un4K32fiNgfa_emUw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the message that displays when a different agreement type is identified.")

   Review the document and locate the type. Select
   Yes or No to verify
   whether or not the AI detection was accurate.

   The Agreement preview page updates and the next document is displayed. Repeat this step to verify the identified agreement type in the remaining documents.
6. Select the Done option at the bottom of the pane. This option becomes active only after all the identified agreement types have been verified.

   The Results dialog box opens.

   ![A screenshot of the Results dialog box that shows the results of the accuracy test.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Agreement-Type_Test-for-accuracy_Results.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2MDMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.h5uUIBxzOpW79oGDCHVEgK2Dr-un4K32fiNgfa_emUw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Results dialog box that shows the results of the accuracy test.")

   Important: If the results do not meet your accuracy requirements, use the Refine definition and examples option to refine your definition and add more examples. Retest the AI detection. Continue refining the definition and examples until the AI detection achieves the expected results.

   The last part of the set-up process is activating the AI detection for the custom agreement type. Complete the [Activate Your Custom Agreement Types](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=cgn1740452596841.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to activate the AI detection for a custom agreement type and surface it in selected historical agreements.") task to activate the type and find it in your historical agreements.

You have successfully tested the AI detection accuracy for the custom agreement type.
