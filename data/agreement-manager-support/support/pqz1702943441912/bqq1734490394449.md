# View Obligations at the Party Level

Learn how to view obligations that are associated with a selected party or alias group.

The "party hub" displays a unified summary of all agreements and obligations that are tied to a party or alias group.

Note: The Obligations tab lists the payment, renewal, and termination obligations that were surfaced in your agreements. The list also includes manually created obligations. When creating an obligation, you can choose from a number of different types of obligations to track.

Access the party hub from various places within Agreement Manager, including:

- The PARTIES column on the Completed Documents view.
- The Details pane on the Agreement preview page.
- The NAME column in the Parties table.

This topic reviews the steps to view party obligations by selecting a name from the PARTIES column.

1. Log in to your Docusign account.

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select Agreements from the global header.

   The Docusign Agreements page opens.
3. Select Completed from the AGREEMENTS section in the navigation pane.

   The Agreement Manager homepage opens and the Completed Documents view is displayed.

   ![A screenshot of the Completed Documents view that highlights the search field and filters.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Navigator_Completed-documents_Search-and-filters-highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcxNjgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.IelfHx_1pND7VZ69bvtHDmNQx4LUXveYn91w4FHdq6g&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Completed Documents view that highlights the search field and filters.")
4. Perform a keyword search or use filters to locate the party that you want to review:
   - Enter the party name in the search field and select Enter.
   - Expand the Filters option and select the Parties filter. Search for and select the appropriate party, and select the Apply option.

   The table is updated to reflect the entered criteria or applied filter. The names in the Parties column are clickable and link directly to the party hub.

   ![A snippet of the Completed Documents page that highlights the Parties column as an entry point for the party hub.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Entry-point_Completed-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcxNjgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.IelfHx_1pND7VZ69bvtHDmNQx4LUXveYn91w4FHdq6g&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Completed Documents page that highlights the Parties column as an entry point for the party hub.")
5. Select a name from the Parties column.

   The party hub opens for the selected party or alias group. The Overview tab is displayed by default.
6. Select the Obligations tab:

   ![A snippet of the party hub that highlights the Overview, Agreements, Obligations, and Details tabs.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Overview-dashboard_highlighted-tabs.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcxNjgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.IelfHx_1pND7VZ69bvtHDmNQx4LUXveYn91w4FHdq6g&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the party hub that highlights the Overview, Agreements, Obligations, and Details tabs.")

   The Obligations tab opens. All the obligations tied to the selected party or alias group are displayed in the table.

   ![A screenshot of the party hub Obligations tab that shows a list of obligations that are tied to the selected party or group.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Obligations-tab.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcxNjgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.IelfHx_1pND7VZ69bvtHDmNQx4LUXveYn91w4FHdq6g&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the party hub Obligations tab that shows a list of obligations that are tied to the selected party or group.")
7. Apply additional filters to refine the obligations list as needed.
8. Select an obligation.

   The Agreement preview page opens and the obligation details are displayed for the selected obligation.

   ![A snippet of the Agreement preview page that highlights the details of the selected obligation.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Obligations_Agreement-preview.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcxNjgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.IelfHx_1pND7VZ69bvtHDmNQx4LUXveYn91w4FHdq6g&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Agreement preview page that highlights the details of the selected obligation.")
9. Review the obligation details as needed.
10. Return to the party hub. Select the Back arrow from the top-left corner of the page.

    You are returned to the party hub.
11. Return to the Agreement Manager homepage. Select the Completed option from the navigation pane on the left.

    The Agreement Manager homepage opens and the Completed Documents view is displayed. Your previously entered search criteria and applied filters are still in effect.
