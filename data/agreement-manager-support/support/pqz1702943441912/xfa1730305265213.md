# Agreement Manager Release Notes

Look up new features, bug fixes, and other changes for Docusign Agreement Manager.

Note: The features and enhancements referenced in this topic are available in Demo accounts, Production accounts, or both. Availability is dependent on the ramp schedule and can vary across regions and languages.

## Release Notes | May 21, 2026

These fixes, features, and product enhancements have been delivered since the last release.

## Introducing: Docusign Agreement Manager (formerly Navigator)

Docusign Navigator was renamed to Docusign Agreement Manager.

Agreement Manager is an AI-powered repository that centralizes signed agreements and uses Docusign Iris to turn them into structured, searchable data. From Agreement Manager, teams can quickly find, analyze, and act on the terms that matter most.

This change is focused on making the product name clearer and more descriptive. Your existing Agreement Manager features, usage, and workflows continue to work as they do today. No customer action is required.

## Introducing: Docusign Workflow Builder (formerly Maestro)

Docusign Maestro was renamed to Workflow Builder across the Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform.

Workflow Builder is the tool used to design and automate your agreement workflows. This tool helps you understand, build, and trigger multi-step agreement processes.

This change is focused on the name only. The underlying functionality and any existing entitlements for your workflows remain the same. You can continue using your current workflows without interruption.

## Advanced search queries

Advanced search queries give you more control over how results are matched and ranked. You can use a combination of Boolean operators, exact and proximity searches, boosting, and wildcards in a single query:

Enter the query string directly in the search field when you want to:

- Combine multiple conditions
- Control how closely words appear together
- Emphasize some terms over others
