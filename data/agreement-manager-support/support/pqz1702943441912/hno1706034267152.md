# Configure Docusign Apps in an Agreement Workflow

You can integrate Docusign apps to automate your agreement workflows and boost productivity.

Add apps from Docusign App Center when creating agreement workflows in Docusign Workflow Builder. With Docusign apps, you can customize and automate your agreement processes as described in the [Creating Workflows](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yff1696971835267&topicId=tco1697826811758.html&_LANG=enus) article.

Additionally, you can use the Google Docs app with Docusign CLM as described in the  [Google Docs for Docusign CLM](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=alb1642965087302&topicId=gcn1714403494382.html&_LANG=enus) article.

## Previously installed applications

If apps were previously installed from the Docusign App Center, you must uninstall and reinstall them. Due to versioning issues, backward compatibility is not currently possible.

Additionally, you must reconnect all Workflow Builder fields after upgrading.
