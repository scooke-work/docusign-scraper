# Monitor and Report on Obligations

Learn how to monitor and report on obligations from the Obligations dashboard, including subscribing to receive notifications. This topic answers the question: How do I report on my obligations?

The Obligations dashboard provides a visual summary of your agreement obligations through the use of bar and pie charts. After applying filters, you can further drill down to a detailed report view for any of the dashboard charts.

Note: Subscribe to receive daily report notifications about your agreement obligations. This feature is available from the detailed report view. Review the [Subscribe to Receive Report Notifications](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=wfw1734545937270.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to set up one-time or recurring email reports for yourself or others to receive notifications about your obligations, renewals, or agreements.") topic to learn more about this feature.

1. Log in to your Docusign account.

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select Insights from the global header.

   The Insights hub opens and the Overview is displayed.
3. Select Obligations from the navigation pane on the left.

   The Obligations dashboard opens.

   ![A screenshot of the Obligations dashboard that shows available reports and filter options.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Obligations-Dashboard.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Obligations dashboard that shows available reports and filter options.")
4. Filter the Obligations dashboard to locate upcoming obligations:

   ![A snippet of the Obligations dashboard that shows the filter options, including Due Date, Obligation Type, Parties, and Obligation Owner.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Obligations-Dashboard_Filter-options.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Obligations dashboard that shows the filter options, including Due Date, Obligation Type, Parties, and Obligation Owner.")

   - Expand the Due Date filter, choose a time period, and select Apply. Choices are: Today, Next 7 Days, Next 14 Days, Next 30 Days, and Next 6 Months.

     You can also select Custom Date Range and enter a specific date range. Choose from Between, Before, or After and complete the necessary date fields.
   - Expand the Obligation Type filter, choose the type of obligations
     that you want to view, and select Apply For example,
     filter for renewal, payment, or termination obligations.
   - Expand the Parties filter, choose the relevant parties and party role, and select Apply.

     ![A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows a selected party and party role options.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Obligations-dashboard_Report-view_Parties-filter_Party-role_expanded.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows a selected party and party role options.")

     Important: If you choose to filter the dashboard by Parties, you must select a Party role for the selected party or parties. You can select Obligated and Benefiting if you are unsure of the role.

     The dashboard updates to reflect the obligations that are tied to the selected parties and party role.
   - Expand the Obligation Owner filter and select a name to view obligations that are assigned to a specific user.

   The Obligations dashboard updates and the data that matched the applied filters is reflected in the charts.

   ![A screenshot of the Obligations dashboard that was filtered to show payment-related obligations.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Obligations-Dashboard_Filtered-by-Type.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Obligations dashboard that was filtered to show payment-related obligations.")
5. Review the dashboard charts: Obligations by due date and Obligations by type.
   - Hover your cursor over a segment or bar to view the number of obligations represented in that part of the chart:![A snippet of the Obligations dashboard that shows an information box for one of the segments in the chart.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Obligations-Dashboard_hover-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Obligations dashboard that shows an information box for one of the segments in the chart.")
   - Expand the More actions (ellipsis) menu and select View as table to display a list view of the obligations in the chart:![A snippet of the Obligations dashboard that shows an example of the table view for one of the charts.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Obligations-Dashboard_Table-view-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Obligations dashboard that shows an example of the table view for one of the charts.")
6. Go to the detailed report view for an obligation chart. Select the chart title or expand the More actions (three dots) menu and select the View Report option.

   The detailed report view opens for the selected chart. The obligations represented in the chart are displayed in the table.

   ![A screenshot of the detailed report view for the Obligations by due date chart.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Obligations-Dashboard_Detailed-report-view.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the detailed report view for the Obligations by due date chart.")
7. Review the table to verify that the report contains the relevant obligations.

   Use the available filters to refine the obligation list as needed or select Clear All to clear the existing filters.

   The detailed report view provides two additional filter options: Obligation Status and Agreement Name.
8. Customize the column order for the table:

   ![A screenshot of the Customize columns dialog box that shows the columns you can choose to display in the agreements table.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Overview-dashboard_Customize-columns.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Customize columns dialog box that shows the columns you can choose to display in the agreements table.")

   1. Select the Customize columns (toggle) icon.

      The Customize columns dialog box opens. These fields represent the columns in the table.
   2. Select the toggles to turn the associated field on (blue) or off (white).
   3. Re-order the columns as needed. Hover the cursor over the Reorder (dots) icon for the column that you want to move. Drag it to the appropriate position.
   4. Select the Save option.

   Continue applying filters and customizing the table data as needed.
9. Download or save the report:

   ![A snippet of the report view that highlights the More actions (three dots) menu used to download or save a report.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Report-view_Dropdown-expanded.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the report view that highlights the More actions (three dots) menu used to download or save a report.")

   - Expand the More actions (three dots) menu in the top right and select
     the Download option.

     The associated report is
     downloaded to your local Downloads folder.
   - Expand the More actions (three dots) menu and select the Save option. The Save Report dialog box opens.

     - Enter a unique name for the report in the Report name field. The field defaults to the name of the selected report.
     - Enter a description about the report's purpose in the Description field.
     - Select the Save option.The report is saved to the Custom tab on the Reports view.

   Note: **Downloading reports to CSV files**

   When downloading reports to CSV files from the Insights reporting hub, there is an agreement limit of 25,000 agreements for all IAM reports. This count includes your custom reports.

   **Suggested workaround:** Adjust the report filters to run the report in batches until all the relevant agreements are downloaded. For example, run the report in batches according to varying date ranges. Once downloaded, combine the data into one CSV file.
10. Set up one-time and recurring email reports for yourself and others. Select the Manage Notification (bell) icon and complete the Create subscription dialog box:

    ![A screenshot of the Create subscription dialog box that is used to subscribe to report notifications.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Create-Subscription.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Create subscription dialog box that is used to subscribe to report notifications.")

    Note: When you create a subscription, you have the option to add individual users and groups. Once created, the subscription is displayed in the Manage report subscriptions dialog box for any individual users who were added. Currently, the subscription is not visible to users if they were added as part of a group.

    See the [Subscribe to Receive Report Notifications](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=wfw1734545937270.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to set up one-time or recurring email reports for yourself or others to receive notifications about your obligations, renewals, or agreements.") topic to learn more about this feature.
11. Add the report to Your Favorites list. Select the Favorite (star) icon located in the top-right corner:

    ![A snippet of a detailed report view that highlights the Favorite (star) icon.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreements-dashboard_Report-details_Favorite-icon-selected_generic.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A snippet of a detailed report view that highlights the Favorite (star) icon.")Your starred dashboards and reports are displayed in the Your Favorites section on the Overview dashboard.
12. Return to the Obligations dashboard. Select Obligations from the breadcrumb navigation at the top of the page:

    ![A snippet of the Obligations by type report that highlights the breadcrumb navigation.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Report-dashboard_Review-view_breadcrumb-nav-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcwMjMsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.pLEqLRDqXURXSqdAgX-QjFt2e9mB5OyThDW751qjkHY&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Obligations by type report that highlights the breadcrumb navigation.")

    You are returned to the Obligations dashboard.

You have successfully reviewed the obligation monitoring and reporting features of the reporting hub.
