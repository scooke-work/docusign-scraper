# Natural Language Search Queries

Read about how natural language search queries, including components of the
Search Results view.

Natural language (NL) queries offer an intuitive and efficient way for you to find
agreement information. Enter a question or phrase into the search field on the Completed Documents page. The query returns a direct answer and a list of relevant agreements.

Important: Depending on the nature of the question posed, not all queries yield both direct answers and a list of agreements. Some queries may only provide a list of relevant agreements.

![A snippet of the Completed Documents view that shows an example search question to locate an amendment for a specific party.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/NL-Search_Example-question-with-dropdown.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY0MTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.k3ZQVo5kkyIE6dOh9t9Y1DSVwMkNKLacj4YstfTs69k&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Completed Documents view that shows an example search question to locate an amendment for a specific party.")

This type of search is more robust than traditional keyword searches. With this search,
you can:

- **Search in your own words:** NL processing supports a more intuitive and
  human-like search experience.
- **Find answers faster:** NL queries return lists of relevant agreements and direct answers to your questions.
- **Make more impactful decisions:** Gain insights as you search, to inform strategies and
  build stronger relationships with clients and vendors.

Tip: Ask a complex question to get a direct
answer and a list of agreement lists that match the query. For example, if you asked "What is the
value of my purchase agreements with Company A"? The search results would include:

- A direct answer to your question (the total contract value for your purchase
  agreements with Company A)
- A list of purchase agreements with Company A

## Components of NL search results

The query returns a direct answer and a list of relevant agreements. The document count is updated based on your search query.

Important: The natural language search does not support the use of filters together with questions. If you apply filters to the agreement list table, you cannot enter a search question.

![A screenshot of the Completed Documents page that shows a direct answer and a list of relevant agreements for an NL query.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/NL-Search_Example-search-results.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY0MTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.k3ZQVo5kkyIE6dOh9t9Y1DSVwMkNKLacj4YstfTs69k&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Completed Documents page that shows a direct answer and a list of relevant agreements for an NL query.")

Note: The query considers all agreement statuses when generating results - unless you specify a specific status. For example, search for all your active agreements with a specific party.

## Supported attributes and data points

As you enter a question into the search field, a dropdown of "quick" answers displays.
You can select a quick answer or view the results for your question. The search
supports questions related to these agreement attributes (fields) and data
points:

- Agreement Type
- Currency Code
- Effective Date
- Expiration Data
- Governing Law
- Party Name
- Renewal-related
  - Auto Renewal (Y/N)
  - Renewal Notice Period
  - Renewal Term
  - Renewal Type
- Status
- Termination for Cause - Notice Period
- Termination for Convenience - Notice Period
- Title
- Total Contract Value
