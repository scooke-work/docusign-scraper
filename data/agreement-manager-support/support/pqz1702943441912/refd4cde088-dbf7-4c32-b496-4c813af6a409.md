# 2024 Agreement Manager Release Notes

Look up past release notes from 2024 for Docusign Agreement Manager, including features, bug fixes, and other changes.

Note: The features and enhancements referenced in this topic are available in Demo accounts, Production accounts, or both. Availability is dependent on the ramp schedule and can vary across regions and languages.

## Release Notes | December 4, 2024 (global release)

IAM Core and IAM for Sales are now globally available with Agreement Manager, including:

- Agreement Manager AI-assisted insights support agreements in English (global), French, and German
- The localized Agreement Manager user interface supports usability in 14 Docusign-supported languages
- Localized user experience reflects global variations in agreement details, such as date formats and currencies
- Agreement Manager product documentation localized in French and German

## Release Notes | October 15, 2024

The following fixes, new features, and product enhancements are being delivered with this release:

| **Type** | **Category** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Feature | Upload and Import | - **Cloud storage upload:** Administrators can now upload agreements from cloud storage locations. This simplifies storage processes, improves collaboration, and provides agreement visibility across your portfolio. - **Agreement ingestion filtering:** Administrators can use filters to determine which Docusign historical completed agreements will be ingested into Agreement Manager. This allows for greater control over the agreements stored in the account. |
| Feature | AI/Intelligence | **Manage custom agreement types:** Create custom agreement types that reflect your organization's specific agreements. This allows for greater flexibility, enhanced organization, and more accurate reporting. |
| Enhancement | AI/Intelligence | - **Expanded AI-assisted data insights:** Automatically capture more of the contractual data points that matter to your organization with AI-assisted insights. These expanded insights drive greater efficiency and improve risk management. - **Data enrichment:** Drag standard fields onto eSignature agreement templates to capture attributes up front. This ensures agreements are more discoverable post-signature. |
| Enhancement | Reporting | **Expanded reporting:** Capitalize on additional pre-built reports and advanced reporting capabilities to gain more agreements insights and to improve efficiency. |
| Enhancement | Search | **Search enhancements:**  - Natural language searches - Download selected agreements - Export and share search results |
| Enhancement | View and Organize | **Agreement preview updates:** Experience a cleaner and more relevant view of agreement details for a selected agreement. Key attributes are prominently displayed based on the agreement type so relevant attributes are displayed first. |

## Release Notes | May 30, 2024

The following fixes, new features, and product enhancements are being delivered with the May 30, 2024 release.

| **Type** | **Category** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Feature | Upload and Import | - **Automatic sync with eSignature:** Automatically access your completed and newly signed agreements from Docusign eSignature. - **Bulk agreement upload:** Easily upload historical agreements completed outside of Docusign in bulk. - **Individual agreement upload:** All users can upload agreements ad hoc and associate them with relevant parties. |
| Feature | View and Organize | - **Centralized storage:** Store your completed agreements in one location to improve agreement organization and visibility. - **Agreement list:** See your individual completed agreements in a simplified view and sort or filter by various attributes. - **Agreement preview:** Preview any given agreement with key details clearly visible and surfaced by AI technology. - **Party deduplication:** Store, organize, and reconcile party information to access agreements associated with a specific party. |
| Feature | Search | - **Comprehensive search:** Find relevant agreements by searching for key attributes, keywords, or related terms within the content of an agreement. - **Attribute search:** Locate an agreement by searching for key attributes associated, such as the agreement type or date ranges. - **Keyword search:** Locate an agreement by searching for specific text or phrases within the content of the agreement. - **Related concepts search:** Enhance your search with an advanced technique that identifies agreements containing related terms and concepts. |
| Feature | AI/Intelligence | **AI-assisted data insights:** Rely on Docusign AI to identify and store agreement attributes, resulting in standardized data that makes it easy to find crucial information, understand key points, and efficiently search and filter your agreements. |
| Feature | Security and Compliance | - **Access permissions:** Administrators can grant users access to completed agreements as relevant to their role. - **Direct sharing:** Users have the ability to share or revoke access to individual agreements that they own. |
| Feature | Reporting | - **Out-of-the-box reporting:** Discover important insights about your agreements with an agreement dashboard that displays essential data, leading to more informed decisions. - **Renewal management:** Access a renewal dashboard and subscribe to agreement reminders to identify cost-saving opportunities and prevent unwanted auto-renewals. |
| Feature | Notifications and Alerts | **Agreement reminders:** Stay ahead of important dates with reminders for agreement milestones, such as renewals and terminations. |
