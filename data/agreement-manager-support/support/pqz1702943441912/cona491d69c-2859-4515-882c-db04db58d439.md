# Docusign CLM Integration with the IAM Platform

Read about Docusign CLM integration with the Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform. CLM customers can extract more value from agreements and use Docusign Iris for important agreement insights.

CLM accounts that have an IAM plan are integrated with the Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform. This integration helps CLM customers extract more value from their agreements and business processes. The integration also provides access to Docusign Iris to get important AI insights about agreements.

Note:

- Docusign CLM integration with the IAM platform is available for customers with CLM, CLM+, or CLM with AI Extension accounts only.
- For more information about IAM and IAM plans, see the [Intelligent Agreement Management](https://www.docusign.com/intelligent-agreement-management) website or contact your account representative.

CLM customers can select the Switch to IAM Experience button to access the CLM + IAM integration home page. You can then select the Switch to Classic Experience button to return to the CLM view.

The CLM + IAM integration home page provides access to the following IAM features and functionality:

Agreements
:   Docusign Agreement Manager provides a smart repository to centrally store, manage, and analyze agreements. Agreement Manager also provides AI assistance (through Docusign Iris) to surface key agreement data and provide insights about your agreements. Review [Docusign Agreement Manager for CLM](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con6ab4c322-b97d-4c61-bafe-4b3c9b597e7b.html&_LANG=enus "Read about Agreement Manager for CLM customers with IAM Plans.") for more information.

Insights
:   The Insights page provides centralized access to all reports and dashboards, with personalized views based on user roles, preferences, and favorites. You have access to navigation tools, such as a search bar and frequently accessed links, with real-time updates and summaries of key metrics. Review [IAM Dashboards and Reports](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=jyq1749000353004.html&_LANG=enus) for more information.

    You can create custom reports based on your CLM documents and CLM attributes. Review [Custom Dashboards and Reports](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=conac48cc6c-d92c-4187-a733-08968c218a74.html&_LANG=enus) for more information.

    Insights includes obligation management and reporting, which lets contract managers and other stakeholders identify and track contractual obligations and commitments. You can manage obligations as you work with documents and parties in Agreement Manager. You can also report on obligations. This experience also provides AI assistance to surface obligation data in agreements. Review [Obligation Management for CLM Accounts with IAM Plans](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=conc4eecaf2-14e4-4da4-bfee-e912aa4a3e03.html&_LANG=enus "Read about obligation management for CLM accounts with Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) plans. Stakeholders can use the IAM platform as a central place to capture and track contractual obligations.") for more information.

    Note:

    - Obligation management in Agreement Manager is available in English and in North America sites only.
    - Obligation analysis using AI is available in North America sites only. Supported obligation types for AI analysis are Payment, Renewal, and Termination.

Parties
:   Party management uses the IAM platform as a central place to manage parties and report on party agreements, including parties in CLM. This experience also provides AI assistance to surface party information during agreement ingestion. Review [Party Management for CLM Accounts with IAM Plans](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con40b44395-1791-4003-be9b-cf241e2caaaf.html&_LANG=enus "Read about party management for CLM accounts with IAM plans. Stakeholders use the IAM platform to manage parties, access party reports, and use Docusign AI to surface party information.") for more information.

Tasks
:   You can view and manage your tasks in the IAM Unified Tasks view. Review [Docusign Unified Task Management](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ikc1746801059132&topicId=bhd1746801493533.html&_LANG=enus) for more information.
