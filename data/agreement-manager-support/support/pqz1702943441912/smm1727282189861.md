# Standard Fields for Agreement and Non-Agreement Types

This topic answers the question: What fields are associated with Docusign-provided agreement types? Look up the standard fields that are
associated with each agreement and non-agreement type.

Each Docusign-provided agreement type is pre-populated with a list of relevant standard
fields. These standard fields (attributes) provide structure to help organizations
create custom agreement types.

A number of non-agreement types are also included with the Docusign-provided agreement types. These types are also pre-populated with a list of relevant standard fields.

Tip: The standard fields that are associated with the Docusign-provided agreement and non-agreement types cannot be removed from the type. You can add additional fields to the type, including standard and custom fields. These fields can be removed as needed.

## Non-agreement types and fields

This section lists the standard fields associated with the Docusign-provided non-agreement types.

| **Non-agreement type** | **Standard fields (attributes)** |
| --- | --- |
| **Application**  **Certificate**  **Form**  **Handbook**  **Notice**  **Plan**  **Policy**  **Report**  **Summary** | Title, Parties, Execution Date, Effective Date, Expiration Date, Status, Line of Business |
| **Invoice** | Title, Parties, Total Contract Value, Execution Date, Effective Date, Expiration Date, Status, Line of Business, Renewal, Payment Terms, Late Payment Fee, Price Cap Increase % |

Important: All the custom and standard fields that are associated with an agreement type are surfaced in the relevant documents. However, not all non-agreement types are surfaced in your documents. This includes the custom and standard fields that are associated with the type. These non-agreement types are not surfaced during agreement processing: Certificate, Handbook, Notice, and Policy.
