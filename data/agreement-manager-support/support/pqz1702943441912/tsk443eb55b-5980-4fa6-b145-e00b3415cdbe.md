# Activate Your Custom Fields

Learn how to activate the AI detection for a custom field and surface it in selected historical agreements.

## Before you begin

This topic reviews the final part of the *Test for accuracy* step, activating the
custom field. You also have the option to find the custom field in past agreements.
These steps are completed by users with Organize and manage all
agreements access to Agreement Manager agreements and functionality. For
example, an administrator or contract manager.

Complete these topics before attempting
this task:

- [Create Custom Fields from the Agreement Preview](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk0830ea60-9b29-4efa-b73f-9eb15b2d6561.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to create custom fields from the Agreement preview page for a selected agreement.")
- [Add Examples of Your Custom Fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tskcb56face-b8de-4cf9-85ce-6eac3669dd82.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to complete the second step of the AI detection set-up process, Select examples.")
- [Test the Extraction Accuracy for Your Custom Fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=wjr1740452628309.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to test the AI detection accuracy for your custom fields and refining the results to improve accuracy.")

Important: Use the Finish Later option to save your progress and finish the AI detection set-up later. Using this option ends the Track this field with AI experience. See the [Enable AI Detection for Your Custom Fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk02bd31a2-b125-4d53-b632-20d9972163a8.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to enable the AI detection for your custom fields. Once activated, the field is surfaced in your new agreements automatically.") topic for steps to finish the process.

In the previous step, you tested the accuracy of the AI detection against a group of selected documents. After verifying the identified fields, you selected the Done option. The test results were displayed.

![A screenshot that shows the results of the accuracy test, including options for activating the field.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Activate-Find-past-docs.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3MTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.dT_kIBMR_iAewZBtqqRFCSEG01NZX_uqEX8oRPJr2T0&_LANG=enus "A screenshot that shows the results of the accuracy test, including options for activating the field.")

After achieving the target accuracy score, you are prompted to activate the field. Once activated, the field is identified in new agreements and any future agreement types. For example, if you add the field to additional agreement types, it is surfaced in those agreements too.

You are ready to activate the custom field.

1. Surface the new field in your historical agreements. Select the Find in past agreements option:

   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Activate-Find-past-docs_selected.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3MTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.dT_kIBMR_iAewZBtqqRFCSEG01NZX_uqEX8oRPJr2T0&_LANG=enus)

   As part of the activation process, you can select past agreements to check for the new field.
2. Select the Activate option.

   The AI detection is published and the field is active for new agreements.

   The agreement list table opens. Use this page to search for the documents that you want to process for the new field.
3. Locate the documents that you want to process for the new field:

   ![A snippet of the agreement list table that shows the search field and filter options to locate the relevant documents.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Activate_Search-for-past-docs.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3MTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.dT_kIBMR_iAewZBtqqRFCSEG01NZX_uqEX8oRPJr2T0&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the agreement list table that shows the search field and filter options to locate the relevant documents.")

   1. Search for the relevant agreements. Enter a keyword or search string in the search field. The dropdown opens and search suggestions are displayed. Select a search suggestion or press the Enter key.
   2. Filter the agreement list table to find the relevant agreements. For example, filter by Sets, Status, Parties, Agreement Type, or AI Suggestions.

      Tip: Use the Filters option to apply additional filters to the results table.

      Expand the filter, choose the relevant filter value, and select the Apply option.

   The results table updates and the documents that match the search criteria and applied filters are displayed.
4. Choose the documents that you want to process for the new field.
5. Select the Start Processing option in the bottom right.

   Your processing job is submitted and you are returned to the Agreement preview page. The new field is displayed in the Details pane:

   A message confirms that the selected documents are being refreshed for custom data.

   ![A screenshot of the message box, confirming that the selected documents are being refreshed for custom data.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Field-actived_Message-box.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3MTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.dT_kIBMR_iAewZBtqqRFCSEG01NZX_uqEX8oRPJr2T0&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the message box, confirming that the selected documents are being refreshed for custom data.")

   Tip: Select the View Progress option to view the processing status for the job. The job monitoring hub opens, and the Processing status and Job details are displayed. Review the processing results, including any error messages.Select the Close (x) icon in the top right to return to the Agreement Manager homepage.
6. Return to the Agreement Manager homepage. Select the Close (x) icon in the top left.

   The Agreement Manager homepage opens and the Completed Documents view is displayed.

You have successfully activated the AI detection for a custom field and processed historical agreements for the new data point.
