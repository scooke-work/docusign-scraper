# Agreement Sharing and Downloads

This topic answers the question: What are my direct sharing and download options for Agreement Manager documents, including the required permissions?

Users can share agreements with other users and groups within the same account or workspace. Likewise, all users can use the Download function for the agreements to which they have access.

The Share function gives users access to share an agreement and assign an access level for the recipients.

## Agreement sharing and access permissions

Before using the Share function, or sharing agreements in general, it's important to recognize the different access levels. They are: Can Manage, Can Edit, and Can View.

![A screenshot of the agreement access permissions for the direct sharing function.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Share-an-agreement_available-access-permissions.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY2NDQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.WDhGGH-tXDj5I68-O5QPL8SsCLPH88poZ_hElyWAXm8&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the agreement access permissions for the direct sharing function.")

These permissions provide varying access to the agreements being shared:

- Can Manage:: Users can edit attribute values, and delete, share, and remove access to the agreement. Users with this access can also add the agreement to agreement sets.
- Can Edit:: Users can edit attribute values and share and download the agreement.
- Can View:: Users can read and download the agreement.

By default, all users have Can Manage access to their uploaded and completed agreements. This includes the documents that are ingested from eSignature. Both senders and recipients are given Can Manage access to their completed documents that are synced from eSignature.

Note: Users can only share an agreement with individuals and groups within their account. Users must have Can Edit or Can Manage access to an agreement to share the agreement with others:

- Users with *manage* access can share an agreement and assign *edit*, *manage*, or *view* access.
- Users with *edit* access can share an agreement and assign *edit* or *view* access.
- Users with *manage* access can revoke user and group access to an agreement.

## Notifications and "Copy link" feature

Users are notified by email when an agreement is shared with them.

![A screenshot of the Add users dialog box that shows the Notify users by email checkbox and the Copy link option.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Share-an-agreement_Add-users-dialog-box.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY2NDQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.WDhGGH-tXDj5I68-O5QPL8SsCLPH88poZ_hElyWAXm8&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Add users dialog box that shows the Notify users by email checkbox and the Copy link option.")

The Notify users by email option is selected by default. Leave this checkbox selected to notify users that an agreement was shared with them. If you de-select the checkbox, users are not notified of shared agreements.

The Copy link option provides you with a direct link to the agreement. When you select this option, a link to the agreement is copied to your clipboard. This link can be pasted into a message or email and shared with other users. Keep in mind, the user must have access to the agreement to view the link.

## Downloading agreements

The Download function is available to all users for any agreement to which they have access. For example, you may want to download an agreement to share with key stakeholders during negotiations.

The Download function is available from two locations in Agreement Manager:

- The agreement list table (the Completed Documents view)
- The Agreement preview page

The agreement is downloaded to your local Downloads folder, where you can
share it with key stakeholders or save it for future use.
