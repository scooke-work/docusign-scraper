# Duplicate Party Management and Standardization

This topic answers the question: What does party management in Agreement Manager entail? Read about the party management features designed to help you manage parties that have similar names. For example, party standardization and consolidation at the point of ingestion.

"Party management" is the umbrella term for Agreement Manager features that help you manage your relationships with various clients, vendors, and partners.

 

## Duplicate party management

Duplicate party management helps keep party data clean and consistent. Party names are surfaced and standardized during ingestion. This process reduces the number of duplicate parties due to:

- Case sensitivity
- Legal entity descriptors
- Punctuation
- Whitespaces

When duplicate parties are detected, you are prompted to Review and Merge the parties under one preferred party name:

See the [Review and Merge Duplicate Parties](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=upw1712255598663.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to merge parties to ensure data integrity across your organization. - IAM party hub: Displays on the Parties list and party hub pages

The preferred party name is also included in search queries and results.

## Opt in for party clean-up

In addition to duplicate party management, existing customers can opt in to clean up their party data.

Restriction: The party clean-up option is available only for English-language documents.

Access the opt-in message from the Insights bar on the Completed Documents page:

![A snippet of the Insights bar that highlights the opt-in message for the normalization and deduplication process.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Normalization-deduplication_Opt-In-card_Clean-Up-Parties.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NjAsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.PQ5M9BAJ-9KPEcSqx00EbQ2XLz-kPRq3xjz-WWBIwVA&_LANG=enus)

Select the Clean Up Parties option and follow the prompts to clean up your parties list. Standardization rules are applied to your party names and duplicate parties are consolidated into a single party.

Important: Party clean-up differs from reviewing and merging parties under a preferred party group name. When merging parties into groups, the individual parties retain their names and can be unmerged from the group. The party clean-up process permanently consolidates the duplicate parties into a single party.

The party name is assigned automatically based on the date that the party record was originally created. The duplicate parties are consolidated under the name of the oldest party.

Warning: When you opt in for party clean-up, the process is permanently enabled and the processing job cannot be paused, reverted, or stopped. Any parties that are consolidated as a result of the clean-up process cannot be unmerged or separated.

Once completed, a confirmation message opens to inform you that the updated party list is ready to view.

See the [Opt-In for Party Clean Up](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk63a801de-59cb-4a65-ae4d-a866d34b11fb.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to opt in to reprocess the organization party names in your account for party normalization and deduplication.") article to learn more about enabling this process.

## Standardization rules

Standardization rules ensure that consistent party names are used throughout the IAM platform. These rules are used to clean up your party names and improve data accuracy:

| Rule | Example |
| --- | --- |
| Whitespaces | Party names are not considered distinct due to spacing discrepancies. For example, "Fontara[space][space]Inc." becomes "Fontara[space]Inc." |
| Case sensitivity | Party names are not considered distinct because of case discrepancies. For example, "Fontara" and "FONTARA" are considered the same party. |
| Punctuation and symbols | Party names are not considered distinct due to punctuation differences. This rule standardizes the use of punctuation and symbols in party names.  For example, "Fontara\_Tech Solutions, NA" and "Fontara Tech Solutions NA" are not considered distinct. They are considered the same party and the first occurrence is retained. |
| Legal entity descriptors | Party names are not considered distinct due to differences in the abbreviations of legal entity descriptors. This rule normalizes how these descriptors are referenced in party names. For example:  - "Corporation" becomes "Corp." - "Incorporated" becomes "Inc." - "Limited Liability Company" becomes "LLC"  Note: When parties have different legal entity descriptors, they are considered distinct and are treated as two separate parties. For example, Fontara Inc and Fontara LLC remain two separate parties. |

## Party management tasks

Party management tasks are completed by users with Organize and manage all agreements access to Agreement Manager, such as contract managers. Common tasks include:

- Review and merge duplicate party names.
- Set a preferred name for a merged group of parties.
- Remove parties from merged groups.

These party management tasks are completed from the Manage parties page:

![A screenshot of the Manage parties page that lists all your party names, including merged parties.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-management_Manage-parties-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NjAsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.PQ5M9BAJ-9KPEcSqx00EbQ2XLz-kPRq3xjz-WWBIwVA&_LANG=enus)

Search for and select the parties or merged party groups that you want to manage. You can also use the Review and Merge option to merge duplicate parties under one preferred name.

Tip: The party normalization and deduplication process results in curated, normalized party names across the IAM platform. If the merged parties have different display names, they are added to the list of suggested duplicate parties. Use the Review and Merge option to merge the suggested duplicate parties under one preferred name.

## Party side identifiers

During processing, parties are identified as either the first-party or the third-party:![A screenshot of the Manage parties page that highlights the Party Side column in the party list table.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-parties_Party-Side_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NjAsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.PQ5M9BAJ-9KPEcSqx00EbQ2XLz-kPRq3xjz-WWBIwVA&_LANG=enus)

- The first-party designation, *Internal*, indicates the user's organization.
- The third-party designation, *External*, indicates external entities.

The Party Side column is displayed in the party list table on the Manage parties page. This information provides context to help you manage relationships, risks, and obligations effectively.

Tip: Change a party's designation by using the Set as option. From the Manage parties page, search for and select the party that you want to update:![A snippet of the Manage parties page that highlights the Set as Internal Party option for a selected party.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-parties_Set-as-Internal-Party_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NjAsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.PQ5M9BAJ-9KPEcSqx00EbQ2XLz-kPRq3xjz-WWBIwVA&_LANG=enus) Once you select a party from the list, the Set as [Internal Party or External Party] option is displayed. Select the Set as Internal Party to indicate the part is part of your organization. The Party Side column is updated and reflects the new designation, *Internal*.

Once identified, you can filter the Parties list table by Party Side:

![A snippet of the Parties page that highlights the Party Side filter.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-view_Party-Side-dropdown_page-snippet.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NjAsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.PQ5M9BAJ-9KPEcSqx00EbQ2XLz-kPRq3xjz-WWBIwVA&_LANG=enus)
