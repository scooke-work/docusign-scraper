# Review and Merge Duplicate Parties

Learn how to merge parties to ensure data integrity across your organization. This topic answers the question: Where can I review duplicate party suggestions and merge parties that are the same?

Organization party names are standardized at ingestion and duplicate party records are consolidated. This process reduces the number of potential duplicate parties, however, duplicate parties can exist. For example, if the consolidated parties have different display names, they are added to the Review Duplicate Parties list.

Tip: See the [Duplicate Party Management and Standardization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=ssf1712255476566.html&_LANG=enus "This topic answers the question: What does party management in Agreement Manager entail? Read about the party management features designed to help you manage parties that have similar names. For example, party standardization and consolidation at the point of ingestion.") article to learn more about the party normalization and deduplication process.

Use the Review and Merge option to review and merge duplicate parties. This task is completed by users with Organize and manage all agreements access to Agreement Manager, such as contract managers.

1. Log in to your Docusign account.

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select Agreements from the global header.

   The Docusign Agreements page opens.
3. Select Parties from the navigation pane on the left.

   The Parties page opens.
4. Select the Review and Merge option from the banner message at the top of the page:

   ![A snippet of the Parties view that highlights the Review and Merge option from the banner message.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-page_Review-and-Merge_highlighted-in-banner.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.qiqirVAiwB3jp7L8J0UTdHHErq831FuVByF_mghev68&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Parties view that highlights the Review and Merge option from the banner message.")

   The Review Duplicate Parties page opens and the Are these the same party? view is displayed. Potential duplicates are listed in the table.

   Note: You will not lose any agreements if you merge a party.

   ![A screenshot of the Review Duplicate Parties page that lists potential duplicate parties.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Parties_Are-these-the-same-party-dialog-box_suggestion-highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.qiqirVAiwB3jp7L8J0UTdHHErq831FuVByF_mghev68&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Review Duplicate Parties page that lists potential duplicate parties.")
5. Locate the parties that you want to review and select the associated Review option.

   Tip: Enter a party name in the Search by Party Name field to merge specific parties.

   The Review Duplicate Parties page updates and the Select
   parties to merge and the Choose a
   name sections are displayed.![A screenshot of the Review Duplicate Parties page that shows the suggested duplicate parties and naming options.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Parties_Review-Duplicate-Parties.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.qiqirVAiwB3jp7L8J0UTdHHErq831FuVByF_mghev68&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Review Duplicate Parties page that shows the suggested duplicate parties and naming options.")

   You are prompted to select parties to merge and choose a party name. The potential duplicates are listed in the Select parties to merge section. They are selected by default.
6. Choose the parties that you want to merge from the Select parties to merge section.
   - Deselect the checkboxes for the parties that you do not want to merge. This step is applicable when there are three or more possible duplicate parties.

     Tip: When there are only two suggested duplicate parties, the checkboxes are grayed out.
   - Select the Dismiss All option from the bottom-right corner if the suggested parties are unrelated and should not be merged.

   Important: Agreement Manager recalls the parties that you didn't select
   and will not suggest them again with this group.
   If you select the Dismiss
   All option, you will not see this
   specific combination of parties in future
   suggestions.
7. Select a preferred party name from the Choose a name section. Expand the Party name dropdown and select the preferred name from the list.

   Tip: The merged parties are consolidated under one name. Thi name is used throughout the Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform. For example, this name is displayed in the Parties filter and column on the Completed Documents page in Agreement Manager.
8. Select the Merge Parties option from the bottom right. The button label reflects the number of parties being merged.
9. Important:

   This action can take up to an hour before the changes are reflected in the various locations across the IAM platform.

   You are returned to the Are these the same party? view. The merged party is displayed in the table with the View Party option.

   ![A screenshot of the Review Duplicate Parties page that shows the View Party option for the merged party.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Parties_Review-Duplicate-Parties_View-Party-option.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.qiqirVAiwB3jp7L8J0UTdHHErq831FuVByF_mghev68&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Review Duplicate Parties page that shows the View Party option for the merged party.")

   Tip: Select the View Party option to view the party hub pages for the merged party. See the [IAM Party Hub](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=bpv1742428468463.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the IAM party hub, a centralized location for party-related pages. This topic answers the question: Where can I find a unified summary of all agreements and obligations for a customer or vendor?") to learn more about the party hub pages.
10. Repeat this process to continue merging duplicate parties that were surfaced within your agreements.
11. Select Close (X) from the top-right corner of the page.

    You are returned to the Parties page. The Manage parties view is displayed.

    Tip: Select Completed from the navigation pane on the left to view your agreement list. The Completed Documents view opens. The agreements to which the user has access are displayed in the table.
