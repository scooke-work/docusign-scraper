# Customize Notification Settings for Your Completed Agreements

Learn how to customize your email notification settings from the Notification Preferences page.

Update your notification settings from the Notification Preferences page. These preferences give you control over the email notifications that you receive. By default, all the notification types in the Completed Agreements section are enabled.

Tip: Periodically review your Notification Preferences to ensure that you are receiving the relevant email notifications. Additional notification types will be added in the future.

The Notification Preferences page is accessed from the My Preferences option in the user profile menu.

1. Log in to your Docusign account.

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select your user profile avatar located in the top-right corner of the
   page.

   The user profile menu opens.
3. Select the My Preferences option.

   The General Settings page opens. Your default account details and ID card are displayed.
4. Select Notification Preferences from the navigation pane on the left.

   The Notification Preferences page opens. Use this page to customize your
   email notification settings.
5. Expand the Completed Agreements section and update your email notification settings as needed:

   ![A screenshot of the Notification Preferences page that highlights the selected notification types for completed agreements.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/My-preferences_Notification-Preferences_For-Completed-Agreements_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcxMTQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.WvXYgdXlqwuasRsrqqKouEofxMiJdSinowWU2mD1fhc&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Notification Preferences page that highlights the selected notification types for completed agreements.")

   1. Select (click) the Completed Agreements section heading.

      The section expands and the available notification types for completed agreements are displayed.
   2. Review the Notification Type list and select the checkboxes associated with the email notifications that you want to receive. All notification types are selected, or enabled, by default.
   3. Deselect a checkbox if you no longer want to receive email notifications for the corresponding notification type.

   By default, all the notification types in the Completed Agreements section are enabled.
6. Select the Save option.

   A message box opens and confirms that the updated preferences were saved.

You have successfully updated your notification settings from the Notification Preferences page.
