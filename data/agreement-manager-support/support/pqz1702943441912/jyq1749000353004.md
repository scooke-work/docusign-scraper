# IAM Dashboards and Reports

Read about the Insights hub, including an overview of the available dashboards and reports.

The Insights hub helps administrators and users to gain valuable insights into their Docusign account. The hub offers:

- Centralized access to all reports and dashboards
- Personalized views based on user roles, preferences, and favorites
- Quick navigation tools, such as a search bar and frequently accessed links
- Actual time updates and summaries of key metrics

Select Insights from the global header to access the hub. The Insights menu opens and the Overview view is displayed:

## Reprocessing historical agreements

As new or updated AI models are released, the previously ingested agreements can be processed again to surface these data points. Depending on the type of new insights that are available, users can choose to:

- Opt in for reprocessing from the Track obligations badge (obligation-related attributes and insights)
- Reprocess all the outdated agreements for the account (new or updated AI model)
- Reprocess selected agreements (custom agreement types and fields)

Note: See the [Agreement Manager Reprocessing Experience](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con44590246-0f0e-43b4-9692-caadb57335ac.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about the Agreement Manager reprocessing experience, including its purpose, trigger points, and job size considerations.") article to learn more about these reprocessing features.

Processing happens in the background. While it does not impact your current experience, processing may take some time depending on the number and size of agreements. Once started, processing cannot be canceled.

## Dashboards

Each dashboard is a collection of individual reports or data visualizations, such as bar charts and graphs. The collection, or dashboard, provides a visual overview or summary for a particular focus area, like agreements, obligations, or renewals.

## Reports

When users run reports, they see information from their own account activity. Account administrators see all account-level activity. The available reports, including custom reports, are grouped under the tabs on the Reports view.

![A snippet of the Reports view that shows the report categories, including recent, custom, and downloaded reports.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Reports-view_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY4NjcsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.XA0tAlKADUumPFOJrl5_qaiVnUVsAOh6nJZHwoq-hmw&_LANG=enus)

Reports are grouped under these categories:

- Recents: Recently accessed reports
- All Envelope: All reports
- Envelope: Envelope-related reports
- Recipient: Recipient-related reports
- Usage: Activity, verification, usage, template, and web form reports
- Custom: User-created custom reports
- Downloads: Downloaded reports
- Web forms: Document and field-level data reports

Tip: The Reports view is searchable by name and description. Enter search criteria in the Find Reports field to locate the relevant report. ![A snippet of the Reports view that highlights the Find reports field and the related search results.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Reports_Reports-view_search-example-snippet.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY4NjcsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.XA0tAlKADUumPFOJrl5_qaiVnUVsAOh6nJZHwoq-hmw&_LANG=enus)

For reporting on specific content, use these types of reports:

- [Standard reports](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=cnu1642812012214&topicId=cnm1581459649387.html) that provide information about envelopes recipients, and account activity.
- [Document data reports](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=cnu1642812012214&topicId=mxa1694124283123.html) that focus on the field-level data in your eSignature agreements.

## Report limitations

When downloading reports to CSV files from the Insights reporting hub, there is an agreement limit of 25,000 agreements for all IAM reports. This count includes your custom reports.

**Suggested workaround:** Adjust the report filters to run the report in batches until all the relevant agreements are downloaded. For example, run the report in batches according to varying date ranges. Once downloaded, combine the data into one CSV file.

Note: The eSignature reports have a different set of limitations. See the [Using Reports](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=cnu1642812012214&topicId=wou1578456440763.html) article to learn more about eSignature reports for senders and signers.
