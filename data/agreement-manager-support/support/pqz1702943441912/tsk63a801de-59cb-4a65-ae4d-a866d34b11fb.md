# Opt-In for Party Clean Up

Learn how to opt in to reprocess the organization party names in your account for party normalization and deduplication.

In addition to duplicate party management, existing customers can opt in to clean up their party data.

Restriction: The party clean-up option is available only for English-language documents.

Access the opt-in message from the Insights bar on the Completed Documents page.

Warning: When you opt in for party clean-up, the process is permanently enabled and the processing job cannot be paused, reverted, or stopped. Any parties that are consolidated as a result of the clean-up process cannot be unmerged or separated.

When parties are consolidated into a single party, the party name is assigned based on the party's creation date. The parties are consolidated under the name of the party with the oldest record.

This task is completed by users with Organize and manage all agreements access to Agreement Manager agreements and functionality.

1. Log in to your Docusign account

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select Agreements from the global header.

   The Docusign Agreements page opens.
3. Select Completed from the AGREEMENTS section in the navigation pane.

   The Completed Documents page opens.
4. Expand the purple Insights bar at the top of the page. Select the Show Insights option:

   ![A snippet of the Completed Documents page that highlights the Show Insights option at the top of the page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-normalization-deduplication_Opt-in_Show-Insights_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwNTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.8bsqLFMzerS160aN6ySeXiB-HammxiebzZK9t4Ha2lk&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Completed Documents page that highlights the Show Insights option at the top of the page.")

   The My Insights bar expands to show relevant actionable insights and prompts with links to reports or next steps. For example, upcoming renewals and activity trends.
5. Locate the interactive card that prompts you to clean up your organization party names:

   ![A snippet of the Insights bar that highlights the opt-in message for the normalization and deduplication process.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Normalization-deduplication_Opt-In-card_Clean-Up-Parties.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwNTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.8bsqLFMzerS160aN6ySeXiB-HammxiebzZK9t4Ha2lk&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Insights bar that highlights the opt-in message for the normalization and deduplication process.")
6. Select the Clean Up Parties option from the Insights card.

   The Clean up your parties wizard opens and the process is explained. During processing, the party names in your account are reviewed and standardized. After the update, the process continues to check your party names with AI.

   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-normalization-deduplication_Opt-in_Clearn-up-your-parties-dialog.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwNTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.8bsqLFMzerS160aN6ySeXiB-HammxiebzZK9t4Ha2lk&_LANG=enus)
7. Review the information in the wizard to learn more about the process.
   1. Expand the What we're looking for section and review the messaging. The process looks for minor differences in the same party names. Normalization rules standardize party names around casing, suffices, and punctuation.
   2. Expand the What to expect section and review the messaging. The processing job cannot be canceled once it is initiated. Updates happen in the background and processing time is dependent on the number of party records being reviewed.

      Tip: The party normalization and deduplication process will not interrupt your work or overwrite insights that were reviewed manually.
8. Opt in for the party normalization and deduplication process. Select the Clean Up Parties option.

   The wizard closes and the process is initiated.
9. Message boxes provide you with status updates:
   1. During processing, select the View Details option to view the status and job details for the processing job:

      ![A screenshot of a message box that indicates the process is cleaning up the parties.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-normalization-deduplication_Opt-in_message-box_View-Details.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwNTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.8bsqLFMzerS160aN6ySeXiB-HammxiebzZK9t4Ha2lk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a message box that indicates the process is cleaning up the parties.")

      The All jobs monitoring hub opens and the Processing agreements view is displayed. See the [View and Download Processing Results](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=cvd1742251951627.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to view and download results for processing jobs.") article to learn more about the job monitoring hub.
   2. Once processing is completed, use the View Parties option to view your updated party list.

      ![A screenshot of a message box that indicates the updated party list is ready to view.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-normalization-deduplication_Opt-in_message-box_View-Parties.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTgwNTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.8bsqLFMzerS160aN6ySeXiB-HammxiebzZK9t4Ha2lk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a message box that indicates the updated party list is ready to view.")

      The Parties page opens in the Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) party hub. The updated organization party names are reflected in the party list table. See the [The Parties List](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=uzq1744241293689.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the enhanced Parties list, including access points and features.") article to learn more about the Parties page.

You have successfully opted-in for party clean-up.
