# Remove Parties from Party Groups

This topic answers the question: How do I remove parties from an alias group that were mistakenly merged?

Party management tasks are completed by users with Organize and manage all agreements access to Agreement Manager, such as contract managers. Common tasks include reviewing and merging potential duplicate parties. You can also unmerge parties if they were merged by mistake.

These tasks are completed from the Manage parties page. This topic reviews the steps to remove a party from a merged party group.

1. Log in to your Docusign account.

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select Agreements from the global header.

   The Docusign Agreements page opens.
3. Select Parties from the navigation pane on the left-side of the page.

   The Parties page opens.
4. Select the Manage Parties option from the top right:

   ![A snippet of the Parties page that highlights the Manage Parties option in the top right.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-parties_entry-point_Parties-view.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3ODUsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._4nScrWepIItP9dJOEfEkHzxNOWZqmqqchqLjbIW7XA&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Parties page that highlights the Manage Parties option in the top right.")

   The Manage parties page opens and a list of individual and merged parties is displayed.
5. Locate the merged party group that you want to update. Enter the group party name in the Search parties field.
   1. Enter the name of the party group in the Search parties field.

      As you enter the name, a dropdown opens and the parties that match the search term are listed.
   2. Select a party name from the dropdown or select the Vew all results option at the bottom of the list.

      The parties that matched the search term are displayed in the table.
6. Expand (click) the More actions (three dots) menu for the relevant party group and select the Manage Party option:

   ![A snippet of the Manage parties view that shows the Manage Party option for a merged party.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Parties_All-Parties_Select-alias-group-to-unmerge.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3ODUsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._4nScrWepIItP9dJOEfEkHzxNOWZqmqqchqLjbIW7XA&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Manage parties view that shows the Manage Party option for a merged party.")

   The Review Duplicate Parties page opens. The parties that were merged to
   form the party group are displayed in the Select parties to
   merge section.

   Note: The Choose a name
   section is grayed out. You cannot update the group name from this
   step.

   ![A screenshot of the Review Duplicate Parties page that shows a deselected party to unmerge.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Parties_Manage-party-dialog-box_deselect-party.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3ODUsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._4nScrWepIItP9dJOEfEkHzxNOWZqmqqchqLjbIW7XA&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Review Duplicate Parties page that shows a deselected party to unmerge.")
7. Deselect the parties that you want to remove from the group and select the Save option.

   The page closes. You are returned to the Manage parties view. A message states that the changes were saved.

   Important:

   This action can take up to an hour before the changes are reflected in the various locations across the IAM platform.

   The unmerged party is no longer consolidated under the party group.
8. Select Parties from the breadcrumb navigation at the top of the page.

   You are returned to the Parties page.

   Tip: Select Completed from the navigation pane on the left to view your agreement list. The Completed Documents view opens. The agreements to which the user has access are displayed in the table.

You have successfully removed a party from a merged party group.
