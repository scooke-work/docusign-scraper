# Benefits and Features of Agreement Manager

Read about Docusign Agreement Manager, a smart repository built specifically for agreements.

Docusign Agreement Manager helps organizations to store, manage, and analyze agreements with help from Docusign Iris. It is part of the Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform. With Agreement Manager, you can:

- **Drive efficiencies for your internal teams:** centralize agreements and empower your teams to find what they need fast.
- **Uncover opportunities to reduce costs:** use AI to handle tedious tasks and uncover cost-saving opportunities by harnessing agreement data.
- **Increase oversight and security:** tighten agreement access with rules that ensure only the right users have access to the right agreements.

 

## Key features and benefits

Agreement Manager supports these features and benefits:

| **Feature** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| Account-level activity log | The account-level Activity Log provides a centralized location to view aggregate, user-performed events across all the agreements in an account:   - Access updates (agreement and agreement set sharing) - Agreement downloads - Agreement set operations (set creation and updates) - Agreement views |
| Agreement access permissions | Administrators can grant users or groups account-level access to agreements.  - Contract managers with the appropriate permissions can create agreement sets to control access to agreements. - Users can share agreements with other users or groups in their account. |
| Agreement-level audits | Agreement-level audits are essential for minimizing errors, ensuring compliance, and troubleshooting issues.  The Activity Log records and displays agreement-level events that were performed by a user, including document uploads and access updates. |
| Agreement hierarchies | Agreement hierarchies help you gain a comprehensive understanding of how your documents are legally related to one another. With agreement hierarchies, you can more easily track down and understand related documents like amendments and addenda.  See the [Agreement Hierarchies](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con1b654754-bd6d-4fbd-ac0c-8c44a9b1d666.html&_LANG=enus "Read about agreement hierarchies, including their purpose, use cases, entry points, and common tasks.") article to learn more about this feature. |
| Agreement ingestion | Your Docusign historical completed agreements are imported to Agreement Manager automatically upon account provisioning. The ingestion limits for historical completed agreements are:  - 500.000 (North American customers) - 100,000 (International customers)   There is a 100-MB file size limit when uploading or ingesting documents to Agreement Manager. |
| Agreement list | See all your completed agreements in a single list and sort or filter by various attributes. |
| Agreement preview | Preview any agreement with key information highlighted thanks to AI functionality. |
| Agreement reminders | Stay ahead of important dates with reminders for agreement milestones, such as renewals and terminations. |
| Agreement reprocessing | As new or updated AI models are released, your historical agreements can be processed again to surface these data points. Depending on the type of new insights that are available, users can choose to:  - Opt in for reprocessing from the New Insights Available badge (obligation-related attributes and insights) - Reprocess all the outdated agreements for the account (new or updated AI model) - Reprocess selected agreements (standard or custom fields)    See the [Agreement Manager Reprocessing Experience](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con44590246-0f0e-43b4-9692-caadb57335ac.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about the Agreement Manager reprocessing experience, including its purpose, trigger points, and job size considerations.") article to learn more about this experience. |
| Agreement set management | Make sure that the right people have access to relevant agreements by organizing documents into sets. Agreement sets can be used as an organizational tool or as a way to control access to agreements. During set creation, apply smart filters to your agreement sets to create a recurring ingestion rule. When new agreements match the applied filters, they are added to the agreement set automatically. |
| Agreement statuses | Gain clarity on which agreements are active or inactive and quickly understand the state of an agreement. Primary and secondary statuses are calculated based on the current date compared to an agreement's effective and expiration dates. See the [Agreement Statuses](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con6a3bd080-da17-4d1c-a92a-b9b9340c44fd.html&_LANG=enus "Read about agreement statuses, including how they are calculated, visibility within Agreement Manager, and which types of statuses are being tracked.") article to learn more about this feature. |
| Agreement type management | Configure your agreement types and fields in one central place for one source of truth for agreement data throughout the agreement lifecycle. |
| AI-assisted insights | Rely on Docusign Iris to surface key attributes and field values associated with your agreements. This feature facilitates the standardization of agreement data and better reporting. |
| Attribute search | Quickly locate an agreement by searching for key attributes associated with the agreement, such as contract value, agreement type, and date range. |
| Auto-suggestion for custom agreement types | When uploading agreements to Agreement Manager, if 10 or more similar agreements are identified as Miscellaneous, a custom agreement type is suggested.    For example, 10 charter agreements were uploaded and identified as *Miscellaneous*. The agreements are recognized as being similar and a custom agreement type, Charter Agreement, is suggested:  A snippet of the agreement list table that shows a suggested custom agreement type for a Miscellaneous document.  Use the Create option to create the suggested custom agreement type. See the [Use Auto-Suggestion to Create Custom Agreement Types](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk2093dc24-1da8-4393-b4cd-526990bc7118.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to create a custom agreement type from the auto-suggestion feature.") article for steps to complete this task. |
| Bulk agreement upload | Bulk upload historical agreements that were completed outside of Docusign or choose agreements from a third-party cloud storage location. There is a 100-MB file size limit when uploading or ingesting documents to Agreement Manager. |
| Bulk metadata editing | Bulk edit your agreement data with a Docusign-generated spreadsheet file:  - Download a pre-formatted spreadsheet file for a group of selected documents. - Edit the spreadsheet to include the new metadata values. - Return to Agreement Manager and import the file.  Once successfully processed, the new and updated values are reflected in the selected documents. See the [Bulk Metadata Editing](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=concb493b18-adf8-4fe5-8b99-dd90cbc28399.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the Bulk Metadata Editing feature, including use cases, typical roles and permissions, and a process overview.") article to learn more about this feature. |
| Centralized storage | Store all your agreements in one location to improve agreement organization and visibility. |
| Custom fields and types | Create custom fields and types within agreement type management to track specific information unique to organizational needs. |
| Duplicate party management | Duplicate party management helps keep party data clean and consistent. Party names are identified and standardized during ingestion. When duplicate parties are detected, you are prompted to group the parties under one preferred party name.  Note: The standardization process ensures that parties are not considered distinct because of case sensitivity, whitespaces, punctuation, and legal entity descriptors.  The preferred name is displayed in various filters and columns throughout Agreement Manager and the IAM platform. See the [Duplicate Party Management and Standardization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=ssf1712255476566.html&_LANG=enus "This topic answers the question: What does party management in Agreement Manager entail? Read about the party management features designed to help you manage parties that have similar names. For example, party standardization and consolidation at the point of ingestion.") article to learn more about this feature. |
| Individual agreement upload | Users can upload agreements ad hoc and associate them with relevant parties. There is a 100-MB file size limit when uploading or ingesting documents to Agreement Manager. |
| Insights bar | The Insights bar is an interactive deck of cards on the Agreement Manager homepage, located above the agreement list table. It highlights key trends and suggests actions so you can manage agreements in one place. |
| Job monitoring | Access job details for your agreement uploads and processing jobs. The job monitoring hub provides a centralized location to view and track the status of these jobs. |
| Keyword search | Quickly locate an agreement by searching for specific text or phrases within the content of the agreement. |
| Natural language search | Enter a natural language question or phrase into the search field on the Completed Documents page. The query returns a direct answer to the question and a list of relevant agreements. Important: Depending on the nature of the question posed, not all queries yield both direct answers and a list of agreements. Some queries may only provide a list of relevant agreements. |
| Obligation management | Contract managers can take a proactive approach to ensuring that contract commitments are captured, tracked, and fulfilled. |
| Party management | Store, organize, and reconcile party information so you can easily access agreements associated with a specific party. |
| Payment obligation tracking | Stay ahead of due dates, improve compliance, and maximize contract value with AI-assisted payment-related obligations surfaced in your agreements. |
| Related concepts search | Find relevant agreements more precisely using an advanced search technique. This technique uses both synonyms and a knowledge graph to identify related terms and concepts. |
| Renewal management | Simplify renewal tracking with a dedicated dashboard and stay on top of important dates with agreement reminders. |
| Reporting | Discover important insights and key data points about your agreements with IAM dashboards and reports. Locate the Dashboards under the Insights menu. |
| Subscriptions and notifications | Sign up or subscribe to receive daily email reports and notifications to stay ahead of renewals, obligations, and other agreement deadlines. |

## Docusign IAM FedRAMP and GovRAMP authorization

For customers in regulated industries, Agreement Manager is FedRAMP and GovRAMP authorized:

- FedRAMP (Federal Risk and Authorization Management Program) authorizes Cloud Service Providers (CSPs) for federal agencies.
- GovRAMP (Government Risk and Authorization Management Program) authorizes CSPs for state, local, and tribal governments.

Both frameworks standardize cloud security assessments and compliance.

Restriction: The Agreement Manager email ingestion feature is not supported for FedRAMP and GovRAMP accounts.

See the [FedRAMP: Docusign for Government](https://support.docusign.com/s/articles/FedRamp-FAQ) article to learn more about the FedRAMP authorization for the IAM platform.
