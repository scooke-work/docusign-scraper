# 2026 Agreement Manager Release Notes

Look up past release note entries for Docusign Agreement Manager features, bug fixes, and other changes.

Note: The features and enhancements referenced in this topic are available in Demo accounts, Production accounts, or both. Availability is dependent on the ramp schedule and can vary across regions and languages.

## Release Notes | April 10, 2026

The following fixes, new features, and product enhancements have been delivered since the last release.

| **Type** | **Category** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Feature | Analyze | **Bulk metadata editing:** Bulk edit your agreement data with a Docusign-generated spreadsheet file:  - Download a pre-formatted spreadsheet file for a group of selected documents. - Edit the spreadsheet to include the new metadata values. - Return to Agreement Manager and import the file.  Once successfully processed, the new and updated values are reflected in the selected documents. See the [Bulk Metadata Editing](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=concb493b18-adf8-4fe5-8b99-dd90cbc28399.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the Bulk Metadata Editing feature, including use cases, typical roles and permissions, and a process overview.") article to learn more about this feature. |

## Release Notes | March 27, 2026

The following fixes, new features, and product enhancements have been delivered since the last release.

| **Type** | **Category** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Enhancement | Access | **Agreement access level changes:** By default, users have Can Manage access to their completed agreements, including documents ingested from eSignature:  A screenshot of the agreement access permissions for the direct sharing function.  - Senders, signers, and recipients are given Can Manage access to their completed documents that are synced from eSignature. - Users who currently have Can Edit access to their agreements will be updated to Can Manage access.  Users with Can Manage access have full control over the agreement and can add it to agreement sets. See the [Agreement Sharing and Downloads](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=hrr1722978886188.html&_LANG=enus "This topic answers the question: What are my direct sharing and download options for Agreement Manager documents, including the required permissions?") article to learn more about agreement access levels. |
| Enhancement | Analyze | **Party name clean-up:** Duplicate party management helps keep party data clean and consistent. Party names are identified and standardized during ingestion. When duplicate parties are detected, you are prompted to group the parties under one preferred party name.  Note: The standardization process ensures that parties are not considered distinct because of case sensitivity, whitespaces, punctuation, and legal entity descriptors.  See the [Duplicate Party Management and Standardization](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=ssf1712255476566.html&_LANG=enus "This topic answers the question: What does party management in Agreement Manager entail? Read about the party management features designed to help you manage parties that have similar names. For example, party standardization and consolidation at the point of ingestion.") article to learn more about this enhancement. |
| Enhancement | Access | **Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) Permission Profiles:** Permission profiles are groups of settings that determine the behavior and actions available for a user.  When creating or updating permission profiles, administrators have the option to include Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) permissions, such as Agreement Manager Permissions:  A screenshot of the Add Permission Profile dialog box that highlights the available Agreement Manager Permissions.  See the [Permission Profiles](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=pof1583277362435.html) article to learn more about using permission profiles. |
| Enhancement | Access | **Expanded administrator permissions:** Account administrators automatically have full secure access to all Agreement Manager features by default. This access includes bulk ingestion and agreement set management functionality. For example, administrators can use agreement sets to ensure that only the appropriate users have access to sensitive documents.  Restriction: While administrators automatically have full access to all the Agreement Manager features, they do not have access to view the agreements. Access is explicitly assigned to administrators for them to view any of the agreements in the account. See the [Agreement Sharing and Downloads](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=hrr1722978886188.html&_LANG=enus "This topic answers the question: What are my direct sharing and download options for Agreement Manager documents, including the required permissions?") article to learn more about sharing agreements with others.  See the [Agreement Manager Permissions, Profiles, and Roles](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=eut1712157553977.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the available Agreement Manager permissions, including how users and groups are granted access to Agreement Manager agreements and example roles for each permission.") article to learn more about this enhancement. |
| Enhancement | Act | **In-product "Insight" bar and card prompts:** The Insights bar is an interactive deck of cards on the Agreement Manager homepage that surfaces relevant, actionable insights and prompts. These cards include links to reports or next steps.  During Agreement Manager onboarding, administrators are prompted to assign a contract manager if one hasn't already been assigned:  A screenshot of the Insights card that prompts administrators to assign at least one contract manager.  Once assigned, both administrators and contract managers are prompted to:  - Create agreement sets to secure access to sensitive documents - Run the latest AI model to reprocess agreements for the latest extractions and insights  See the [Agreement Manager Permissions, Profiles, and Roles](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=eut1712157553977.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the available Agreement Manager permissions, including how users and groups are granted access to Agreement Manager agreements and example roles for each permission.") article to learn more about this enhancement. |
| Enhancement | Analyze | **Suggested reports (Overview dashboard):** The Overview dashboard includes Suggested Reports (Beta) and Weekly Insights.  - The Suggested Reports (Beta) section on the Overview dashboard provides AI-generated custom report notifications. These suggestions are auto-generated based on the custom fields in your account. When fields (custom or standard) are located in at least five agreements, they are included when generating custom report suggestions. - The Weekly Insights section displays the agreement counts for all the agreements in the account, including All agreements, Expiring agreements, and Upcoming renewals.  See the [Overview Dashboard](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con36bb809c-725e-42fa-aeae-7b7e0f4faee4.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the Overview dashboard, including key components and available resources.") article to learn more about these enhancements. |
| Enhancement | Analyze | **Dashboard bar charts (viewing options):** Depending on the number of data points, the dashboard bar charts have two viewing options:  A screenshot of a dashboard chart that shows the Stacked Bar Chart view.  - Bar Chart view: Shows a single metric per category for each time bucket. - Stacked Bar Chart view: Shows the same totals, but each bar is segmented to show the composition by a second field.  See the [IAM Dashboards and Reports](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=jyq1749000353004.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the Insights hub, including an overview of the available dashboards and reports.") article to learn more about this enhancement. |
| Feature | Analyze | **IAM Agreement Types Manager:** The *Agreement Types Manager* is the central location for configuring and maintaining agreement types and their fields. These agreement types are used across the IAM platform, not just in Agreement Manager.  This feature is accessed from the Agreement Manager homepage. See the [IAM Agreement Types Manager](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con22821364-8601-4fc5-b768-e19f07c43462.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the Agreement Types manager, including its purpose, use, and role within the Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform.") article to learn more about this enhancement. |
| Feature | Analyze | **IAM Fields Manager:** The *Fields Manager* is the single source of truth for the account's custom and standard fields. It is the central place for administrators and contract managers to define, organize, and manage the fields in their account. These custom and standard fields are reused across the Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform.  This feature is accessed from the Agreement Manager homepage. See the [IAM Fields Manager](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con7092ce57-207f-4d06-9228-c7bdd2ccff2a.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the Fields manager, including its purpose, use, and role within the Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform.") article to learn more about this enhancement. |
| Enhancement | Analyze | **New entry points for 'Add a field' function:**  The Add a field function is used to configure new custom fields. This function is launched from:  - Fields Manager - Agreement Types Manager - Agreement preview  Additionally, the Agreement preview has two options for creating custom fields. See the [Custom Field Extractions](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=condece8d7e-2da8-4e5f-8ba4-3f0175c2a625.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the custom field extractions, including entry points for creating new fields and a review of the AI detection setup wizard.") article to learn more about this enhancement. |
| Feature | Analyze | **Track this field with AI:** When you create custom fields from the Agreement preview page, the Track this field with AI wizard launches. You are guided through the AI detection set-up process:  A screenshot of the Track this field with AI setup wizard that displays when you create custom fields from the Agreement preview page.  - Define the field - Select examples - Test for accuracy  See the [Custom Field Extractions](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=condece8d7e-2da8-4e5f-8ba4-3f0175c2a625.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the custom field extractions, including entry points for creating new fields and a review of the AI detection setup wizard.") article to learn more about this feature. |

## Release Notes | January 20, 2026

The following fixes, new features, and product enhancements have been delivered since the last release.

| **Type** | **Category** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Feature | Analyze | **Agreement hierarchies:** Agreement hierarchies help you gain a comprehensive understanding of how your documents are legally related to one another. With agreement hierarchies, you can more easily track down and understand related documents like amendments and addenda.  The Hierarchies feature is managed from the IAM party hub and the party- or group-level.  See the [Agreement Hierarchies](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con1b654754-bd6d-4fbd-ac0c-8c44a9b1d666.html&_LANG=enus "Read about agreement hierarchies, including their purpose, use cases, entry points, and common tasks.") article to learn more about this feature. |
| Enhancement | Analyze | **CLM attributes in Agreement Manager:** When a CLM document is brought into Agreement Manager, the CLM-specific attributes are listed on the CLM Attributes pane. This pane is located on the left-side of the Agreement preview page.  Note: This pane is read-only and is available for documents that were brought into Agreement Manager from Docusign CLM.  Once surfaced in Agreement Manager, you can search and filter the agreement list table by these CLM attributes. See the [The Agreement Preview Page](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=mut1702945643401.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the purpose, usage, and key components of the Agreement preview page. This topic answers the question: Where can I see my agreement details and obligations?") article to learn more about this enhancement.  Attention: This feature is only available to CLM customers who purchased an IAM Enterprise plan. Also, the integration between CLM and IAM must be enabled. See the [Docusign CLM Integration with the IAM Platform](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=cona491d69c-2859-4515-882c-db04db58d439.html&_LANG=enus "Read about Docusign CLM integration with the Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform.
