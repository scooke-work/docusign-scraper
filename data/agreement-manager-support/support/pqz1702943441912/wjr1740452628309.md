# Test the Extraction Accuracy for Your Custom Fields

Learn how to test the AI detection accuracy for your custom fields and refining the results to improve accuracy.

## Before you begin

This topic reviews the next step of the Track this field with AI wizard, *Test for accuracy*. These steps are completed by users with Organize and manage all agreements access to Agreement Manager agreements and functionality.

Note: Administrators automatically have access to configure agreement data. For non-administrator users, this feature requires Organize and manage all agreements access to Agreement Manager. These tasks are primarily completed by administrators and contract managers.

Complete these topics before attempting this task:

- [Create Custom Fields from the Agreement Preview](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk0830ea60-9b29-4efa-b73f-9eb15b2d6561.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to create custom fields from the Agreement preview page for a selected agreement.")
- [Add Examples of Your Custom Fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tskcb56face-b8de-4cf9-85ce-6eac3669dd82.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to complete the second step of the AI detection set-up process, Select examples.")

Before you activate the AI detection for custom extractions, the setup wizard prompts you to test for accuracy. This step is required when using the Track this field with AI wizard. The results are a good indicator of the quality and accuracy that you can expect from the AI detection before activating it.

In the previous step, you selected documents that contained examples of the custom field. After confirming the field values and saving the examples, the set-up wizard advanced to the last step. The final step of the process is *Test for accuracy*.

You are prompted to test the accuracy of the AI detection:![A screenshot of the final step of the Track this field with AI wizard, Test for accuracy.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Test-for-accuracy_Start-Test-option.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2OTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.o9SUg-AB4_xBTE0JD8gGnS3ipSemeZsV7ocSfwzAf2k&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the final step of the Track this field with AI wizard, Test for accuracy.")

Important: Use the Finish Later option to save your progress and finish the AI detection set-up later. Using this option ends the Track this field with AI experience. See the [Enable AI Detection for Your Custom Fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk02bd31a2-b125-4d53-b632-20d9972163a8.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to enable the AI detection for your custom fields. Once activated, the field is surfaced in your new agreements automatically.") topic for steps to finish the process.

1. Select the Start Test option.

   The agreement list table opens. You are prompted to select three or more agreements that contain the custom field and at least one agreement that does not.

**Select agreements for the test**

2. Locate the documents that you want to use to test the AI detection accuracy:

   ![A snippet of the Test for accuracy step that shows the search field and filter options to locate documents.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Test-for-accuracy_search-filters.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2OTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.o9SUg-AB4_xBTE0JD8gGnS3ipSemeZsV7ocSfwzAf2k&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Test for accuracy step that shows the search field and filter options to locate documents.")

   1. Search for the relevant agreements. Enter a keyword or search string in the search field. The dropdown opens and search suggestions are displayed. Select a search suggestion or press the Enter key.
   2. Filter the agreement list table to find the relevant agreements. For example, filter by Sets, Status, Parties, Agreement Type, or AI Suggestions.

      Tip: Use the Filters option to apply additional filters to the results table.

      Expand the filter, choose the relevant filter value, and select the Apply option.

   The results table updates and the documents that match the search criteria and applied filters are displayed.
3. Choose the documents that you want to use in the test:

   ![A snippet of the Test for accuracy step that shows selected agreements for the test.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Test-for-accuracy_Test-Agreements-option.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2OTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.o9SUg-AB4_xBTE0JD8gGnS3ipSemeZsV7ocSfwzAf2k&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Test for accuracy step that shows selected agreements for the test.")

   Tip: Select the Show Selected Agreements option to review your selected documents. If necessary, select the Return to Search Results option and make changes to your selections.

**Test the accuracy and verify results**

4. Select the Test Agreements option in the bottom right.

   An agreement preview opens for the first document. The example documents are displayed in the pane on the left.
5. Review the identified field in the context of the document. Verify that the AI detection surfaced the correct value.

   ![An animated screenshot of the agreement preview that shows the user verifying the identified value in the document.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Test-for-accuracy_confirm-accuracy_animated.gif?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2OTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.o9SUg-AB4_xBTE0JD8gGnS3ipSemeZsV7ocSfwzAf2k&_LANG=enus "An animated screenshot of the agreement preview that shows the user verifying the identified value in the document.")Scroll to locate the field value in the document. The value is highlighted in the preview pane.

   - Select the Yes option if the AI detection identified the correct field.
   - Select the No option if the AI detection did not identify the correct field.

   The agreement preview updates and the next document is displayed. Repeat this step to verify the identified field in the remaining documents.

**Correct the values for incorrect results**

Provide the correct answer if the identified value is incorrect. After selecting the No option, a series of questions displays. You are prompted to provide the correct context and value for the field.

![An animated screenshot of the agreement preview that shows the user providing the correct value for an incorrect value.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Test-for-accuracy_Provide-correct-value_animated.gif?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2OTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.o9SUg-AB4_xBTE0JD8gGnS3ipSemeZsV7ocSfwzAf2k&_LANG=enus "An animated screenshot of the agreement preview that shows the user providing the correct value for an incorrect value.")

6. Locate the correct value within the agreement. Use the Search (magnifying glass) icon in the top right to search for the field value.
7. Indicate whether the field is present in the agreement:
   - Select the No option if the field is not present in the agreement.
   - Select the Yes option if the field is present in the agreement. You are prompted to provide the correct value.
8. Highlight the paragraph or clause that contains the field value and select the Confirm Selection option.
9. Enter the correct value in the text-entry field for the result.

   Tip: For longer values, use the Copy Text option to copy and paste the text into the field.
10. Select the Confirm option.

    The agreement preview updates and the next document is displayed.

**View accuracy results**

After you verify the results for each example, you are ready to view the test results.

11. Select the Done option at the bottom of the pane.

    You are returned to the Track this field with AI wizard. The results of the accuracy test are displayed.

    In this example, the AI detection did not pass the accuracy test.

    ![A screenshot that shows the AI detection did not pass the accuracy test.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Accuracy-score_Failed.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2OTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.o9SUg-AB4_xBTE0JD8gGnS3ipSemeZsV7ocSfwzAf2k&_LANG=enus "A screenshot that shows the AI detection did not pass the accuracy test.")You have a few choices at this point:
    - Start over. Use the Update Details option to go back to the first step of the setup wizard.
    - Choose new examples: Use the Modify Examples option to select new example agreements that contain the custom field.
    - Re=run the test: Use the Test Again option (recommended) to perform the
      test again. The corrections that you made when verifying the
      identified values are used to teach the AI detection. Re-run the
      test to see your new test score.
    - Activate the field: Use the Activate option to activate the AI detection for the custom field. This option is not recommended if the accuracy score is less than 80%.
12. Make adjustments as needed and perform the accuracy test again. Select the Test Again option. You are prompted to complete the process again. Return to the beginning of the topic and start again at step 2.

    When you achieve an accuracy score of 80% or better, proceed to the activation part of the set-up process.

    ![A screenshot that shows the results of the accuracy test, including options for activating the field.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Fields-Manager_Agreement-preview_Activate-Find-past-docs.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc2OTgsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.o9SUg-AB4_xBTE0JD8gGnS3ipSemeZsV7ocSfwzAf2k&_LANG=enus "A screenshot that shows the results of the accuracy test, including options for activating the field.")

    The last part of the set-up process is activating the AI detection for the custom field and finding it in past agreements. Complete the [Activate Your Custom Fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk443eb55b-5980-4fa6-b145-e00b3415cdbe.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to activate the AI detection for a custom field and surface it in selected historical agreements.") task to activate the field and find it in your historical agreements.

You have successfully tested the AI detection accuracy for the custom field.
