# Manage Upcoming Renewals from Email Notifications

This topic answers the question: How do I view my upcoming renewals from an email notification and share an agreement with others?

Renewal email notifications are sent every other Monday at 9am according to the user’s time zone setting. The email contains a summary of upcoming renewals for the selected notification time period. During notification configuration, you had the option of selecting a notification time period.

Tip: Notification settings give you control over the email notifications that you receive. By default, all the notification types related to the completed agreements in your account are selected, or enabled. Deselect a checkbox if you no longer want to receive email notifications for the corresponding notification type.

See the [Customize Notification Settings for Your Completed Agreements](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tsk155b1ed7-7b45-43cd-be92-2a5279630c0f.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to customize your email notification settings from the Notification Preferences page.") article to learn more about updating your notification settings. Be sure to periodically check the Notification Preferences page as new notification types will be added in the future.

1. Open the renewal email notification.
2. Review the Renewal Summary, including the number of agreements that are renewing within the selected notification time period.
3. Select View Renewals.

   The Insights hub opens. The Renewals dashboard is displayed by default. The dashboard is pre-filtered to show agreements referenced in the email notification.

   Note: If you are not already signed in to Docusign, log in to your account.
4. Access the detailed report view for the Upcoming renewals chart:
   - Select the chart title.
   - Hover the cursor over the chart, expand the More actions (three dots) menu and select the View Report option.

   The detailed report view opens for the Upcoming renewals chart. The agreements represented in the chart are listed in the table.
5. Expand the menu More actions (three dots) for an agreement and select the Download option.

   The agreement is downloaded to your local Downloads folder.
6. Select (click) an agreement.

   The Agreement preview page opens for the selected agreement. The Details pane displays attributes, including values helpful for the negotiation or renewal processes.
7. Expand the dropdown in the top right and select the Share option:

   ![A snippet of the Agreement preview page that highlights the dropdown in the top right where the Share option is selected.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreement-preview_Dropdown_Share.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcyOTUsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.w9u6l7PtO6CHdYOvBLNWLCY0dG58Xd5rfTHv_pVRwqE&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Agreement preview page that highlights the dropdown in the top right where the Share option is selected.")

   The Share with others dialog box opens. Use this dialog box to share the agreement with key stakeholders involved in the renewal or negotiation process.

   ![A screenshot of the Share with others dialog box.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Share-with-others-dialog-box.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTcyOTUsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.w9u6l7PtO6CHdYOvBLNWLCY0dG58Xd5rfTHv_pVRwqE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Share with others dialog box.")
8. Search for and select the user or group with whom you want to share the agreement.
   1. Enter the name of the user or group in the Add user or group search field. A type-ahead dropdown displays names matching the entered criteria.
   2. Select a user or group from the list.
9. Expand the Access dropdown and assign the appropriate access level for the agreement. Choices are: Can Manage, Can Edit, and Can View.
10. The Notify users by email option is selected by default. Accept the default to notify users that a document was shared with them.
11. Select the Share option.

    The dialog box closes. The agreement displays in the agreement list table for the user or group with whom you shared the agreement. The users also receive a notification letting them know an agreement has been shared with them.

    Tip: Use the Copy link option to share a link to the agreement with users who have access to the agreement. The link can only be opened by users with access to the agreement.

You have successfully accessed your renewing agreements from an email notification. You also downloaded the agreement and shared it with key stakeholders to assist in the renewal process.
