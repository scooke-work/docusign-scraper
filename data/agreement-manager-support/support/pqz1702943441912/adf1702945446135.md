# Agreement Manager Overview and Onboarding

Read about Docusign Agreement Manager, including an overview of how it works, key use cases, and navigation features.

Agreement Manager solves the problem of organizations not having clear visibility into their agreements. This lack of visibility can cause wasted time, revenue loss, and unnecessary exposure to risk. Worst of all, organizations are unable to take advantage of the data trapped inside their agreements.

The primary use case for Agreement Manager is that it empowers you to store, find, understand, and report on signed agreements.

When the documents are imported or uploaded into Agreement Manager, AI-assisted insights are gleaned from the agreements. These insights help you find, manage, and report on agreements, including managing renewals and obligations, and locating data to assist in negotiations.
