# Obligation Management for CLM Accounts with IAM Plans

Read about obligation management for CLM accounts with Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) plans. Stakeholders can use the IAM platform as a central place to capture and track contractual obligations.

Obligations are actions or commitments that parties fulfill as part of agreements. Contract managers and other stakeholders can manage obligations to ensure that contractual commitments get captured, tracked, and fulfilled.

For IAM plan subscribers, Docusign CLM integrates with the Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) platform. With this integration, you can manage obligations and use Docusign AI as you work with documents in Docusign Agreement Manager. For example, you can identify renewal, payment, and termination terms.

Important: This obligation management experience is available only for CLM accounts that have an IAM plan. For more information about IAM or IAM plans, see the [Intelligent Agreement Management website](https://www.docusign.com/intelligent-agreement-management) or contact your account representative.

Note:

- Obligation management in Agreement Manager is available in English and in North America sites only.
- Obligation analysis using AI is available in North America sites only. Supported obligation types for AI analysis are Payment, Renewal, and Termination.

## How obligation management works

The obligation management process includes any of the following activities:

- Capturing obligations manually or through AI analysis
- Managing obligations in agreements or by party
- Tracking obligation dates and fulfillment status
- Reporting on obligations
