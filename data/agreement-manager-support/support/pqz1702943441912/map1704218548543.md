# Docusign App Center Banner Messages and App Labels

Learn about the messages and availability app labels that you encounter when using Docusign App Center and the actions you should take.

Here are messages that you can encounter when using Docusign App
Center.

## App classification labels

These labels identify the types of App Center apps and integrations.

- AI connectors: Data-driven connectors that enhance your Docusign
  workflows.
- Approved: Approved by your account administrator to install and use.
- eSignature integration: Third-party integrations that add Docusign
  functions to your favorite software tools without leaving them.
- Extension Apps: Docusign apps that extend the Docusign platform by integrating third-party capabilities.
- FedRAMP-certified: Apps certified when used within the Docusign
  FedRAMP boundary.

## App availability labels

These labels define the App Center app's availability status.

- No tag: The app is available to you. However, there may
  be restrictions based on regional availability or product compatibility.
- Beta: The app supports a limited set of features, or
  includes features that are still being developed and tested.
- Coming soon: The app is not yet available to use.
- Custom solution: A Systems Integrator(SI) partner built a
  custom app that it distributes to select customers.

  Note: Custom solution apps are only distributed in the U.S..
- Early Access: The app is only available to a limited
  selection of customers.
- Private: A customer built the app for their own accounts. The
  app is not publicly distributed.
