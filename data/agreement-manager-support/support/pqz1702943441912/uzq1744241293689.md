# The Parties List

Read about the enhanced Parties list, including access points and
features.

The Parties page lists the individual and merged parties that were
surfaced in an account's uploaded and ingested agreements.

![A screenshot of the Parties page that lists all the parties and groups for an account.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Parties_Parties-view_Updated-table.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDIsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._HLAqed6zCTLnAqOS5E2kjff7_eIjH6W2cmvhYEhOHE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties page that lists all the parties and groups for an account.")

From this page, you can:

- Access your Recent and Starred agreements.
- View the party list table by Role View or Party View.
- Search for a specific party or group.
- Filter the party list table by Party Roles, such as Buyer, Seller, or Other.
- Apply the Party Side filter to view Internal or External parties.
- Use the Favorites (star) icon to add a party or group to your Starred list.
- View the number of active, expiring, renewing, and upcoming agreements over a 90-day time period
  for parties and groups.
- Use the Review and Merge option to manage your duplicate parties.
- Manage merged party groups.
- Access the party hub for a selected party or group.

Notice:

The data used to populate the party hub pages, including the Parties list, is refreshed every five minutes. If you've made recent changes, you will see the updated data shortly.

## All, Recent, and Starred tabs

The tabs at the top of the Parties page give you quick access to your
recently viewed and favorite parties and groups:

![A snippet of the Parties page that highlights the All, Recent, and Starred tabs at the top of the page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-view_All-Recent-Starred_tabs_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDIsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._HLAqed6zCTLnAqOS5E2kjff7_eIjH6W2cmvhYEhOHE&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Parties page that highlights the All, Recent, and Starred tabs at the top of the page.")

For example:

- Select the Recent tab to access your most recently viewed parties and groups. Up to 15 parties or groups are displayed in the table.
- Select the Starred tab to access your favorite parties and groups. Each user can "star" up to 50 parties or groups.

The All tab is displayed by default. This tab lists all the parties and groups that were surfaced in an account's agreements.

## Filter by 'Party Roles'

Party Roles are surfaced during agreement processing. These extractions identify the roles that parties play within the context of your agreements.

Once surfaced, you can filter the Parties list to view agreement counts based on the role that the party played. For example, choose to view the party list and agreement counts based on your buyer-seller relationship.

![A screenshot of the Party Roles dropdown that shows the available roles.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-view_Party-Roles-dropdown_expanded.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDIsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._HLAqed6zCTLnAqOS5E2kjff7_eIjH6W2cmvhYEhOHE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Party Roles dropdown that shows the available roles.")

Choose the relevant role and select the Apply option.

The table updates based on the selected role. For example, only those agreements where the party's role matches the applied filter are included in the table.

![A screenshot of the Parties page that highlights the applied Party Roles filter.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-view_Filtered-for-role_Buyer.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDIsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._HLAqed6zCTLnAqOS5E2kjff7_eIjH6W2cmvhYEhOHE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties page that highlights the applied Party Roles filter.")

## Filter by 'Party Side'

During processing, parties are identified as either the first-party or the third-party:

- The first-party designation, Internal, represents the user's organization.
- The third-party designation, External, indicates an external entity.

Once surfaced, you can filter the Parties list to view agreement counts based on the party's side. For example, choose to view the party list and agreement counts for your *internal* entities:

![A screenshot of the Party Side dropdown that shows the two options, Internal and External.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-view_Party-Side-dropdown_expanded.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDIsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._HLAqed6zCTLnAqOS5E2kjff7_eIjH6W2cmvhYEhOHE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Party Side dropdown that shows the two options, Internal and External.")

Expand the Party Side filter and choose to filter the table by Internal or External parties. Select the Apply option to apply the filter.

The party list table updates based on the selected party side.

## Features of the Parties list

During the upload process, key data points are surfaced within your agreements,
including the relevant parties and party roles. The Parties
page lists all the party names that were extracted from your account's
agreements.

![A snippet of the Parties page that shows the parties and party groups that matched the search criteria.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Manage-Parties_Parties-view_Acme-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDIsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._HLAqed6zCTLnAqOS5E2kjff7_eIjH6W2cmvhYEhOHE&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Parties page that shows the parties and party groups that matched the search criteria.")

Tip: Perform a keyword search to narrow the list of
parties. Enter a party or alias group name in the Search
parties field. The table automatically updates and the parties and
groups that match the search criteria are listed.

High-level details for each party or party group are displayed in the party list
table, including:

- Name, role, and status for each party or party group
- Number of current active agreements
- Number of agreements set to expire or renew over the next 30 days
- Number of agreements that are expected to become active over the next 30
  days

Tip: Use the sorting arrows in the column headers to
sort the Parties list as needed.

Hover the cursor over the
headings of the agreement count columns to view a tool tip. For example:![A screenshot of the tool tip that displays when you hover the cursor over the column headings that are related to agreement counts.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-view_Column-heading_hover_renewing-agreements-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDIsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._HLAqed6zCTLnAqOS5E2kjff7_eIjH6W2cmvhYEhOHE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the tool tip that displays when you hover the cursor over the column headings that are related to agreement counts.")

Select a party or party group from the table to view the associated party hub pages.
You can click anywhere in the row:

![A snippet of the Parties page that highlights a selected table row.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-view_NAME-column-highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDIsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9._HLAqed6zCTLnAqOS5E2kjff7_eIjH6W2cmvhYEhOHE&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Parties page that highlights a selected table row.")

The party hub provides a unified summary of all agreements and obligations that are
tied to a party or alias group. See the [IAM Party Hub](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=bpv1742428468463.html) topic to learn more about these
pages, including other entry points and reporting features.
