# View Your Buy-Side and Sell-Side Agreements

Learn how to use the Parties and Party Role filters to view your buy-side and sell-side agreements.

The Party Role is surfaced during agreement processing. This extraction identifies the role that a party plays within the context of an agreement. Once identified, apply the Parties and Party Role filters to view agreements where your organization is either the buyer or seller.

Tip: The
Party Role filter is located under the
Parties filter and it is applied to a selected party:![A screenshot of the Parties filter that highlights the Party Role dropdown and available roles.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Filter-by-party-role_Parties-filter_Party-Role_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY0MTUsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.0Qkhogg61hmywTp6y92b5PVWy6wZ-vWpT5DNLNT8mM0&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that highlights the Party Role dropdown and available roles.")The Party Role defaults to All.
Be sure to select the party first before choosing a role. At least one party must be
selected before the Party Role filter can be
applied.

**Access your completed agreements**

1. Log in to your Docusign account.

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select Agreements from the global header.

   The Docusign Agreements page opens.
3. Select Completed from the AGREEMENTS section in the navigation pane.

   The Completed Documents view opens. A list of documents to which the logged-in user has access is displayed in the table. The total document count is displayed in the bottom right. This count is updated when you perform a search or apply filters.

**View your buy-side agreements**

4. Apply filters to view all your buy-side agreements. These are agreements where your organization is the buyer in the agreement.

   ![A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows the selected party and party role.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Parties-filter_Fontara_Buy-side-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY0MTUsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.0Qkhogg61hmywTp6y92b5PVWy6wZ-vWpT5DNLNT8mM0&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows the selected party and party role.")

   1. Expand (select) the Filters option.
   2. Select the Parties filter.
   3. Enter your organization's name in the Search field. The parties list updates as you enter search criteria.
   4. Select your organization from the results list.

      Tip: At least one party must be selected before the Party Role
      filter can be applied.
   5. Expand the Party Role dropdown and select the Buyer option.
   6. Select the Apply option.

   The filter closes. The buy-side agreements that your organization has with other parties are displayed in the table.

   Next, update the agreement list table to view all the sell-side agreements that your organization has with other parties.

**View your sell-side agreements**

5. Update the Parties filter to view your organization's sell-side agreements:

   ![A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows options for updating the party role for the selected party.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreement-list-table_update-applied-filter_Party-Role_seller.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTY0MTUsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.0Qkhogg61hmywTp6y92b5PVWy6wZ-vWpT5DNLNT8mM0&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows options for updating the party role for the selected party.")

   1. Expand (select) the applied filter.

      The Parties filter opens. Your organization's name is already selected.
   2. Expand the Party Role dropdown and select the Seller option.
   3. Select the Apply option.

   The filter closes. The sell-side agreements that your organization has with other parties are displayed in the table.

   Tip:

   Use the Status filter to further refine the agreement list table. For example, choose to view only Active agreements. See the [Agreement Statuses](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con6a3bd080-da17-4d1c-a92a-b9b9340c44fd.html&_LANG=enus "Read about agreement statuses, including how they are calculated, visibility within Agreement Manager, and which types of statuses are being tracked.") topic to learn more about this feature.

   Tip: If you routinely search for the same type of agreements or agreement data, save your search criteria for future use. See the [Save Search Criteria for Future Use](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tskc38121a1-bbf8-4da8-8ed7-b364991f7e50.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to save the search criteria for the queries that you frequently perform.") topic to learn more about the saved search feature.

You have successfully viewed all the buy-side and sell-side agreements that you have with other parties.
