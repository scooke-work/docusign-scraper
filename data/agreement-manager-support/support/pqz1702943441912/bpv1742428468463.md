# IAM Party Hub

Read about the IAM party hub, a centralized location for party-related pages. This topic answers the question: Where can I find a unified summary of all agreements and obligations for a customer or vendor?

The IAM party hub is a centralized location for party-related pages. Together, these pages
provide a unified summary of all agreements and obligations that are tied to a party or
alias group. For example, the party Overview dashboard shows high-level insights for a selected party or group:

![A screenshot of the party hub's Overview dashboard that shows high-level insights for a selected party or group.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-management_Party-hub_Overview_Fontara-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3OTksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.JUb8tVkw23wmBFqgED13fn4RMrN_KeVASrAsrV7Ku80&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the party hub's Overview dashboard that shows high-level insights for a selected party or group.")

The data used to populate the party hub pages, including the Parties list, is refreshed every five minutes. If you've made recent changes, you will see the updated data shortly.

Tip: Select the Favorites (star) icon to add the party or group to your Starred list. You can "star" up to 50 parties or groups. Your favorite parties and groups are listed under the Starred tab on the Parties page. See the [The Parties List](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=uzq1744241293689.html&_LANG=enus "Read about the enhanced Parties list, including access points and features.") topic to learn more about this feature.

## Reprocessing historical agreements

As new or updated AI models are released, the previously ingested agreements can be processed again to surface these data points. Depending on the type of new insights that are available, users can choose to:

- Opt in for reprocessing from the Track obligations badge (obligation-related attributes and insights)
- Reprocess all the outdated agreements for the account (new or updated AI model)
- Reprocess selected agreements (custom agreement types and fields)

Note: See the [Agreement Manager Reprocessing Experience](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con44590246-0f0e-43b4-9692-caadb57335ac.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about the Agreement Manager reprocessing experience, including its purpose, trigger points, and job size considerations.") article to learn more about these reprocessing features.

Processing happens in the background. While it does not impact your current experience, processing may take some time depending on the number and size of agreements. Once started, processing cannot be canceled.

## The Overview dashboard

The party hub's Overview dashboard displays a visual summary of all active
agreements that are tied to a specific party or merged party group. This dashboard
contains party and agreement insights, including a party summary and recent
activity.

**Dashboard filters**

The filters on the Overview dashboard are applied at the dashboard level. You can filter the dashboard by: Agreement Type, Time Period, and Party Role.

![A snippet of the Overview dashboard that highlights the Agreement Type, Time Period, and Party Role filters.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Overview-dashboard_Filters.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3OTksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.JUb8tVkw23wmBFqgED13fn4RMrN_KeVASrAsrV7Ku80&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Overview dashboard that highlights the Agreement Type, Time Period, and Party Role filters.")

## The Hierarchies tab

The Hierarchies tab shows how your agreements are related to each other.
Whether one depends on another or they contain supporting documents, you can create
hierarchies to reflect their relationship to each other.

![A screenshot of the party hub Hierarchies tab that shows a list of hierarchies that are tied to the selected party or group.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreement-Preview-page_Hierarchies-view.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3OTksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.JUb8tVkw23wmBFqgED13fn4RMrN_KeVASrAsrV7Ku80&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the party hub Hierarchies tab that shows a list of hierarchies that are tied to the selected party or group.")

Hierarchies show how agreements are related. If there are no existing hierarchies for the
selected party or group, use the Create Hierarchy option to
build one. You are prompted to:

- Define hierarchy details
- Add agreements to the hierarchy
- Arrange the agreements to provide a relationship structure for the
  hierarchy

See the [Agreement Hierarchies](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con1b654754-bd6d-4fbd-ac0c-8c44a9b1d666.html&_LANG=enus "Read about agreement hierarchies, including their purpose, use cases, entry points, and common tasks.") topic to learn more about creating and managing agreement hierarchies.

## The Details tab

The Details tab shows the display name, party type, and party side details for the selected party or group.

![A screenshot of the Details tab that shows the display name, party type, and party side details for the selected party or group.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Details-view.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc3OTksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.JUb8tVkw23wmBFqgED13fn4RMrN_KeVASrAsrV7Ku80&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Details tab that shows the display name, party type, and party side details for the selected party or group.")

Use the Edit Details option to update the party's or group's details as needed. For example:

- Add an alias for the party or group
- Update the party type (organization or individual)
- Update the party side (external or internal)
