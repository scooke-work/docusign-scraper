# Docusign Extension App Regional Availability

Learn about finding the global regions where Docusign apps are available. These apps are currently deployed across five different datacenters: U.S., CA, AU, EU, and JP.

When browsing the App Center, you can determine the regions where a specific Docusign app is available by viewing the app’s detail page.

Applications are deployed across a combination of these five datacenters: U.S., CA, AU, EU, and JP.

Your assigned account is provisioned on one of these datacenters, granting you access to the applications deployed within that specific region.

An app’s detail page displays the app’s regional availability as shown in this example.
