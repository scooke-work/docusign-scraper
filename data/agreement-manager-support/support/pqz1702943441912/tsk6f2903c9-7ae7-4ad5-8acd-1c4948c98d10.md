# Filter for Agreements by Specific Party Role

Learn how to update the agreement list table to view agreements based on the role played by a specific party.

The Party Role is surfaced during agreement processing. This
extraction identifies the role that a party plays within the context of an agreement.
Once identified, apply the Party Role filter to view agreements
where a selected party plays a specific role. For example, they are the buyer or the
seller in the agreement.

Tip: The
Party Role filter is located under the
Parties filter and it is applied to a selected party:![A screenshot of the Parties filter that highlights the Party Role dropdown and available roles.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Filter-by-party-role_Parties-filter_Party-Role_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTYzOTQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.5ELlRbOopNq6z_vx7PCa38dEPOIGS7Or1OUcjwvrmEQ&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that highlights the Party Role dropdown and available roles.")The Party Role defaults to All.
Be sure to select the party first before choosing a role. At least one party must be
selected before the Party Role filter can be
applied.

1. Log in to your Docusign account.

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select Agreements from the global header.

   The Docusign Agreements page opens.
3. Select Completed from the AGREEMENTS section in the navigation pane.

   The Completed Documents view opens. A list of documents to which the logged-in user has access is displayed in the table. The total document count is displayed in the bottom right. This count is updated when you perform a search or apply filters.
4. Expand (click) the Filters button and select the Parties option:![A snippet of the Completed Documents page that highlights the Parties option.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Filters-menu_Parties_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTYzOTQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.5ELlRbOopNq6z_vx7PCa38dEPOIGS7Or1OUcjwvrmEQ&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Completed Documents page that highlights the Parties option.")
5. Select the relevant party:

   ![A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows a search term and the selected party.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Completed-documents-table_Parties_Tally-Inc-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTYzOTQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.5ELlRbOopNq6z_vx7PCa38dEPOIGS7Or1OUcjwvrmEQ&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows a search term and the selected party.")

   1. Enter a name in the Search field. As you enter the name, the list is updated to display parties that match the keyword.
   2. Select the relevant party.

      Important: When using the Parties filter, you must also select a
      Party Role. If you do not know
      which role to select, accept the default value, All.
6. The Party Role defaults to All. Expand the dropdown and select the
   appropriate role:

   ![A screenshot of the Parties filter that highlights the Party Role dropdown, including available roles.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Filter-by-party-role_Parties-filter_Party-Role_highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTYzOTQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.5ELlRbOopNq6z_vx7PCa38dEPOIGS7Or1OUcjwvrmEQ&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that highlights the Party Role dropdown, including available roles.")

   Choose from: Buyer,
   Seller, or Other.

   For example:
   - Select Buyer to see all the buy-side
     agreements for the selected party.
   - Select Seller to see all the sell-side
     agreements for the selected party.If you do not know the role, accept the default value, All.
7. Select the Apply option.

   The filter closes and the agreements that match the applied filters are displayed in the table:![A screenshot of the Completed view that highlights the Parties filter and lists the agreements that match the applied filters.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreement-list-table_filtered-for-party-role_Fontara-Buyer.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTYzOTQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.5ELlRbOopNq6z_vx7PCa38dEPOIGS7Or1OUcjwvrmEQ&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Completed view that highlights the Parties filter and lists the agreements that match the applied filters.")The applied filter is displayed at the top of the page. In this example, the Parties filter indicates that the Fontara and Fontara, Inc values have been applied to the agreement list.

   Tip: Update the filter to view your sell-side agreements with the selected party. Expand (click) the applied filter and select Seller from the Party Role: ![A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows options for updating the party role for the selected party.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreement-list-table_update-applied-filter_Party-Role_seller.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTYzOTQsInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.5ELlRbOopNq6z_vx7PCa38dEPOIGS7Or1OUcjwvrmEQ&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows options for updating the party role for the selected party.")

   Use the Status filter to further refine the agreement list table. For example, choose to view only Active agreements. See the [Agreement Statuses](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con6a3bd080-da17-4d1c-a92a-b9b9340c44fd.html&_LANG=enus "Read about agreement statuses, including how they are calculated, visibility within Agreement Manager, and which types of statuses are being tracked.") topic to learn more about this feature.

   Tip: If you routinely search for the same type of agreements or agreement data, save your search criteria for future use. See the [Save Search Criteria for Future Use](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=tskc38121a1-bbf8-4da8-8ed7-b364991f7e50.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to save the search criteria for the queries that you frequently perform.") topic to learn more about the saved search feature.

You have successfully viewed your agreements with a selected party based on the party's
role.
