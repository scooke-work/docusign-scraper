# View Agreements for Selected Parties and Groups

Learn how to view all the agreements that are tied to a specific party or party group.

The "party hub" displays a unified summary of all agreements and obligations that are tied to a party or party group. Access the party hub from various places within Agreement Manager, including:

- The Parties column (Completed Documents view)
- The Details pane (Agreement preview page)
- The Name column (Parties view)

Note: This topic reviews the steps to access the party hub from
the Completed Documents view. You can also search for and
select the relevant party from the Parties page.

![A snippet of the Parties page that highlights the Search parties field.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Parties-page_Search-parties-highlighted.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.AC8vVSf_-2QBmOtn31UX-GVUkVru8oVhzfnTrzhG_fk&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Parties page that highlights the Search parties field.")

- Go to Home  >  Agreements and select All from the PARTIES section in the navigation pane.
- Enter search criteria in the Search parties
  field.
- Select a party from the search results.

The party hub opens for the selected party or group.

**Access the party hub from the Completed Documents view:**

1. Log in to your Docusign account.

   The Docusign Home page opens.
2. Select Agreements from the global header.

   The Docusign Agreements page opens. The Inbox view is displayed by default.
3. Select Completed from the AGREEMENTS section in the navigation pane.

   The Completed Documents view opens. Filter this view for a specific party.
4. Filter the agreement list table for a specific party:

   ![A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows the Search field, Party Role dropdown, and the party list.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Completed-documents-table_Parties-dialog-box.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.AC8vVSf_-2QBmOtn31UX-GVUkVru8oVhzfnTrzhG_fk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Parties filter that shows the Search field, Party Role dropdown, and the party list.")

   1. Expand the Filters option and select the Parties filter.
   2. Enter a keyword or search string in the Search field.

      As you enter search criteria, the list of parties updates to match the keyword.
   3. Select the relevant party.

      Important: When using the Parties filter, you must also select a
      Party Role. If you do not know
      which role to select, accept the default value, All.
   4. Apply the Party Role filter to view agreements where the selected party plays a specific role. If you do not know which role to select, accept the default value, All.

      Select a role from the Party Role dropdown. Choose from: Buyer, Seller, or Other.
   5. Select the Apply option.

   The table updates and agreements that are tied to the selected party or group are displayed. The names in the Parties column are clickable and link directly to the party hub.

   ![A snippet of the Completed Documents view that highlights the Parties column as an entry point for the party hub.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Entry-point_Completed-example.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.AC8vVSf_-2QBmOtn31UX-GVUkVru8oVhzfnTrzhG_fk&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Completed Documents view that highlights the Parties column as an entry point for the party hub.")
5. Select a name from the Parties column.

   The party hub opens for the selected party or group. The Overview tab is displayed by default.

**View the Agreements list:**

6. Select the Agreements tab:

   ![A snippet of the party hub that highlights the Overview, Agreements, and Obligations tabs.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Overview-dashboard_highlighted-tabs.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.AC8vVSf_-2QBmOtn31UX-GVUkVru8oVhzfnTrzhG_fk&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the party hub that highlights the Overview, Agreements, and Obligations tabs.")

   The Agreements tab opens and all the agreements that are tied to the selected party or group are listed.

   ![A screenshot of the Agreements tab that shows the agreements that are tied to the selected party or group.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Agreements-tab.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.AC8vVSf_-2QBmOtn31UX-GVUkVru8oVhzfnTrzhG_fk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Agreements tab that shows the agreements that are tied to the selected party or group.")

   Tip: Use the Favorites (star) icon to "star" your most active, time-sensitive, or high-value parties and groups. Each user can add up to 50 parties and groups to their Starred list.

   Your "favorite" parties and groups are listed under the Starred tab on the Parties page.

**Filter and customize the Agreements list:**

7. Apply additional filters to locate relevant agreements.

   Tip:

   Use the Status filter to filter the agreement list table by agreement status. For example, choose to view only Active agreements that match the current search criteria and filters: ![A screenshot of the Status filter that shows the selected Active options, including Expiring Soon and Renewing Soon.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Agreement-statuses_Status-filter_Active-selected.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.AC8vVSf_-2QBmOtn31UX-GVUkVru8oVhzfnTrzhG_fk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Status filter that shows the selected Active options, including Expiring Soon and Renewing Soon.") See the [Agreement Statuses](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=con6a3bd080-da17-4d1c-a92a-b9b9340c44fd.html&_LANG=enus "Read about agreement statuses, including how they are calculated, visibility within Agreement Manager, and which types of statuses are being tracked.") topic to learn more about this feature.

   Tip: Select (click) an agreement to view details, obligations, and agreement sets for the agreement. The Agreement preview opens.

   Review the agreement and select the Close (x) icon from the top left to return to the party hub.
8. Customize the table data as needed:

   ![A screenshot of the Show or Hide Fields dialog box that shows the columns you can choose to display in the agreements table.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Overview-dashboard_Customize-columns.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.AC8vVSf_-2QBmOtn31UX-GVUkVru8oVhzfnTrzhG_fk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Show or Hide Fields dialog box that shows the columns you can choose to display in the agreements table.")

   1. Select the Show or Hide Fields (gear) icon from the right-side of the page.

      The Show or Hide Fields dialog box opens. These fields represent the columns in the table.
   2. Select the toggles to turn the associated field on (blue) or off (white).
   3. Re-order the columns as needed. Hover the cursor over the Reorder (dots) icon and drag the column (field) to the appropriate location.
   4. Select the Save option.

   The dialog box closes and the columns are updated according to your configuration.

**Download agreements and export agreement data:**

9. Select one or more agreements to download the agreements or export agreement data to a CSV file.

   ![A snippet of the Agreements tab that highlights the download and export options for a group of selected agreements.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/pqz1702943441912/page/images/Party-hub_Agreements_Download-or-export-selected-agreements.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE3OTc4MDksInNoZWFmIjoicHF6MTcwMjk0MzQ0MTkxMiJ9.AC8vVSf_-2QBmOtn31UX-GVUkVru8oVhzfnTrzhG_fk&_LANG=enus "A snippet of the Agreements tab that highlights the download and export options for a group of selected agreements.")

   1. Select the agreements or agreement data that you want to download or export.
   2. Select the Download Documents (down arrow) icon.

      A zipped folder containing the selected agreements is downloaded to your local Downloads folder.
   3. Select the Export to CSV icon.

      The CSV file is downloaded to your local Downloads folder. When exporting agreement data, all the standard and custom fields are included in the CSV file.

**View additional agreement actions:**

10. Expand the More actions (three dots) menu for an agreement and choose from these options:
    - Select the Download option. The agreement downloads to your local Downloads folder.
    - Select the Share option. The Share with others dialog box opens. Choose the user or group with whom you want to share the agreement, assign a permission level, and select Save.
    - Select the Remove from Account option. A confirmation message opens. Review the message and
      select the Remove Agreement option to remove the
      agreement and its data.

      Important: Once the agreement is permanently removed, all
      users and administrators will lose access to the agreement and its
      data. This action does not remove the agreement from its original
      envelope or void the contract.
    - Select the View Activity Log option. The Activity Log opens and a log of agreement-level events that were performed by a user.
11. Select Parties from the breadcrumb navigation at the top of the page.

    You are returned to the Parties view.

    Tip: Select Completed from the navigation pane on the left to view your agreement list. The Completed Documents view opens. The agreements to which the user has access are displayed in the table.
