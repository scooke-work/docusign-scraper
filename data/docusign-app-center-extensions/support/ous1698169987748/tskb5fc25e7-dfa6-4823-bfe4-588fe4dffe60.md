# Access AI Integrations in App Center

Learn about the AI connector apps available in Docusign App Center.

Docusign AI connectors are MCP-based integrations that expose Docusign IAM products inside third-party AI and business
platforms. These platforms can include Claude, Copilot, Agentforce, ChatGPT, Slack,
and Teams. Users can securely access agreement data and actions directly from those
tools.

You can access these AI connector apps in Docusign App
Center:

- Anthropic Claude: The Docusign Connector for Anthropic Claude provides Claude secure agreement intelligence and eSignature capabilities. See the [Docusign Connector in Anthropic Claude﻿ article](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ug3906200f-95c6-4a6b-90b1-f928c85961c6&topicId=con54692874-8229-4739-916f-fdb70778038a.html&_LANG=enus) for more information.
- Google Gemini: The Docusign connector in Gemini Enterprise acts as a secure bridge between your Google Workspace environment and your agreement data. See the  [Docusign Connector in Gemini Enterprise](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ug3906200f-95c6-4a6b-90b1-f928c85961c6&topicId=con7d418007-d283-41eb-8ea6-6d28e80c161d.html&_LANG=enus) article for more information.
- ChatGPT: The Docusign connector in ChatGPT provides an advanced AI chatbot and virtual assistant. See the [Bringing Our Leading Contract AI to ChatGPT](https://www.docusign.com/blog/docusign-contract-ai-chatgpt) article for more information.

Requirements

- Anthropic Claude: Requires an Anthropic Claude Pro, Team, or Enterprise account.
- Google Gemini: Requires a Google Gemini Enterprise, Premier, or Business plan.
- ChatGPT: Requires ChatGPT Pro, Team, or Enterprise plan.

1. Access  [Docusign App Center](https://apps-d.docusign.com/app-center).
2. From the left navigation pane, select IA Connectors and
   select the AI connector that you want.
