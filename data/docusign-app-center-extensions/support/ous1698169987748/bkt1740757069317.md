# Access Docusign Apps

Learn how to browse for and connect to Docusign extension apps from
various Docusign feature locations.

You can browse for, install, and connect to Docusign extension
apps through various methods.

## Access apps from your browser

Enter this URL in your browser for direct access to the Docusign App Center page.

https://apps.docusign.com/app-center

## Access from the Docusign App Center Featured Apps and Connectors Menu

Use the App Center Featured Apps and Connectors menu to refine your apps search.

![A screenshot of the Docusign App Center Featured apps and Connector menu.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/ac-feat-apps-conn-menu.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.u8MBLobhTcxb4jgOnJW3OJ538aqv8CZ7o-mvrXgatKU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Docusign App Center Featured apps and Connector menu.")

You can specify these app types:

- AI connectors: AI-powered connectors that enhance your Docusignworkflows
- eSignature integrations: Third-party integrations that add Docusignfunctionality to your favorite software tools without
  leaving them
- Extension apps: Extend the Docusign platform by integrating
  third-party capabilities
- Workflow templates: Workflow Builder pre-built workflows for
  specific use cases
- FedRAMP-certified: FedRAMP certified when used within Docusign FedRAMP
  boundary

## Access from the Docusign App Center Manage Apps page

Access apps from the side navigation menu in the Manage Apps tab.

![A screenshot of the Docusign App Center side menu.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/acmgttabsidemenu.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.u8MBLobhTcxb4jgOnJW3OJ538aqv8CZ7o-mvrXgatKU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Docusign App Center side menu.")

- Installed Apps: Displays apps your Docusign
  Admin has installed.
- Approved Apps: Displays apps your Docusign
  Admin has approved for your organization. These may not be installed.
- View all Admin settings: Displays the Docusign Admin homepage as
  described in the [Docusign Admin Home Page﻿](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=rrf1583359212854&topicId=ops1583359137726.html&_LANG=enus) article.

  Note: This option only displays if you are an administrator.

## Access when starting a Workflow Builder workflow

- Select App Center  from the menu bar.
- When [Creating Workflows](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yff1696971835267&topicId=tco1697826811758.html&_LANG=enus) in the Workflow Builder step builder, select
  Browse apps in the Add Action
  list.

The following example shows the App Center Menu selection and the Browse apps option to
access Docusign App Center apps.

![A screenshot showing the App Center Menu selection and the Browse apps option to access Docusign App Center apps.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/acminacmaestro.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.u8MBLobhTcxb4jgOnJW3OJ538aqv8CZ7o-mvrXgatKU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot showing the App Center Menu selection and the Browse apps option to access Docusign App Center apps.")

Follow the directions in the [Install Apps in the Docusign App Center](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ous1698169987748&topicId=ctg1698170340729.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to install Docusign extension apps in the Docusign App Center.") article.

## Access when uploading documents to Agreement Manager

Note: A Docusign Admin must install the extension
apps before you can connect to an app.

1. When [Uploading documents to Agreement Manager](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pqz1702943441912&topicId=lxo1726792845151.html&_LANG=enus) , select the upload icon
   in the menu bar.
2. Use the Select File  menu to display a list of extension apps.

   ![A screenshot of the available extension apps when uploading documents in Docusign Agreement Manager.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/select-apps-in-navigator.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE3NzcsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.u8MBLobhTcxb4jgOnJW3OJ538aqv8CZ7o-mvrXgatKU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the available extension apps when uploading documents in Docusign Agreement Manager.")
3. Upload files from the selected app.

## Access from the Micro App Center

The Micro
App Center is now available.

From Docusign eSignature, you
may see a Micro App Center panel after you send an agreement. The Micro App Center may
also display in other key workflows, such as the Sender inbox or download page.

The Micro App Center panel shows the compatible App Center apps and suggested
workflows so you can quickly launch a Workflow Builder automation
without leaving eSignature features. For example, to archive completed documents to a
cloud storage app.

This example shows the Micro App Center that displays after
sending an agreement from eSignature.
