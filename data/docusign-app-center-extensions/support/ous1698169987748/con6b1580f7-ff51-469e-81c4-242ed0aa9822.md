# Take Action on App Center Connection Notifications

Learn about App Center in-product notifications that alert you to app connection issues..

App Center in-product notifications alert you to app connection issues.

You can find these notifications in these App Center locations.

## App Center dashboard notifications

As shown in this example, a red dot displays on the Manage Apps tab or the Installed Apps tab when connections need your attention. Click the tab with a red dot to view and resolve issues.

![A screenshot of a sample App Center notification indicator.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/ac-notify-both-reddots.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE4NjgsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.urmCZGnXE3237WsrxhuBCtzvKriPW-_R60g6-PmVXHk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a sample App Center notification indicator.")

After selecting the tab, select the red x on the app with issues, as shown in this example.

![A screenshot showing a sample connection error notification.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/ac-notify-con-err-list.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE4NjgsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.urmCZGnXE3237WsrxhuBCtzvKriPW-_R60g6-PmVXHk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot showing a sample connection error notification.")

## App Detail page notifications

A message banner displays on the [app detail page](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ous1698169987748&topicId=xkg1702568563800.html&_LANG=enus "Use a Docusign app's detail page to view important information such as an app summary, features, permissions, and resources."). Select Reconnect Now to resolve the issue as shown in this example.

## Connection page notifications

On the Connections page, if a connection needs attention, a notification similar to this example displays.

![A screenshot of a sample App Center Connections page notifications.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/ac-not-conn-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE4NjgsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.urmCZGnXE3237WsrxhuBCtzvKriPW-_R60g6-PmVXHk&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a sample App Center Connections page notifications.")

When you receive an Invalid Connection notification, click Reconnect to reestablish the app’s connection to workflows.

See the [Manage App Connections](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ous1698169987748&topicId=xyw1716318604389.1.htm) article for information about correcting connection errors.
