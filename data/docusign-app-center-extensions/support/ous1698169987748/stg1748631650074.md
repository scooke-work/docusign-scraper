# Docusign App Center Regional Availability

Learn about the regional availability for Docusign App Center apps and
where to find this information in Docusign App Center.

Docusign App Center app availability can vary by region. Each app's [App Detail Page](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ous1698169987748&topicId=xkg1702568563800.html&_LANG=enus "Use a Docusign app's detail page to view important information such as an app summary, features, permissions, and resources.") includes the supported region.

Apps are available in these geographical areas and include one of these designations.

| Available Region | App Detail Page Designation | Definition |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Globally available in all regions | Global | Worldwide |
| Only the NA region | North America | United States and Latin America |
| Only the EU the site | EMEA | All EU countries and the UK |
| Only the Japan site | Japan | Japan |
| Only the Canada site | Canada | Canada |
| Only the Australia site | Australia | Australia |
| Available in NA, EU, UK, CA, and AU | North America  EMEA  Canada  AU | United States, Latin America, EU, UK, Canada, and Australia |
