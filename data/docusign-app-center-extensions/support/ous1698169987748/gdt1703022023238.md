# Switch Between Docusign App Center Environments

Learn about easily switching from Docusign App Center Demo and
Production environments.

Use the Switch feature to move between Demo and Production environments without leaving the Docusign App Center. For example, you can install, view, and manage an
app in your Demo (trial sandbox) environment. After testing, you may want to install the
same app in your Production environment.

This example shows the Try in Demo Account option.

![A screenshot of the Try in Demo Account option to change to the Demo environment.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/acswitchdemo.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE3OTgsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.8jWY9vJpFbEVYb3n_64jCqzyIokDOh6xDQ2OOA3aQ04&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Try in Demo Account option to change to the Demo environment.")

To summarize:

Try in Demo Account
:   Leaves the App Center Production environment and switches to the Demo
    environment.

Try in Production Account
:   Leaves the App Center Demo environment and switches to the Production
    environment.
