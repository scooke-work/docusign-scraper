# Non-admin Users: Work with Approved Apps

As a Docusign account user, learn how to find and manage Docusign apps you’re approved to use.

As a Docusign non-admin user, you can find and manage Docusign apps you are approved to use.

Depending on your organization, these types of apps are available:

- Admin-approved apps are prebuilt Docusign apps approved by your account
  administrator.
- Private Apps are custom apps created exclusively by and
  for your organization.

1. From the [Docusign App Center](https://apps.docusign.com/app-center) home page, select
   the Approved for you filter.
2. Select Admin-approved apps from the menu.

   ![A screenshot of a sample approved for you menu for non-admin users.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/ac-newest-non-approved4u.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE4NDAsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.spVXChUENtivPeMZZvCrSV4ywmqy564gO5_0KSzi2gE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a sample approved for you menu for non-admin users.")

   The Installed Apps approved for you  page
   displays.

   ![A screenshot of a non-admin user accessing approved apps.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/ac-app-apps-non-dash.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE4NDAsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.spVXChUENtivPeMZZvCrSV4ywmqy564gO5_0KSzi2gE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a non-admin user accessing approved apps.")
3. Select a specific app to display the [App Details Page](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ous1698169987748&topicId=xkg1702568563800.html&_LANG=enus "Use a Docusign app's detail page to view important information such as an app summary, features, permissions, and resources.") page for detailed information about the
   app.

   Depending on the app, you can:

   - Install App: Install the app for
     use with your Docusign features as
     described in the [Install Apps in theDocusign App
     Center](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ous1698169987748&topicId=ctg1698170340729.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to install Docusign extension apps in the Docusign App Center.") article.

     ![A screenshot of selecting Install App from the app listing page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/ac-aa-non-admin-detail-install-app.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE4NDAsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.spVXChUENtivPeMZZvCrSV4ywmqy564gO5_0KSzi2gE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of selecting Install App from the app listing page.")
   - Use This App: Select this option
     to go to the Docusign feature
     where you can use the app. For example, selecting
     the Smarty email verification app displays the
     Agreements page. Here you can select Smarty data
     verification fields.

     ![A screenshot of selecting Use This App from the app's detail page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ous1698169987748/page/images/ac-new-non-adm-appr-detail-use-this.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MTE4NDAsInNoZWFmIjoib3VzMTY5ODE2OTk4Nzc0OCJ9.spVXChUENtivPeMZZvCrSV4ywmqy564gO5_0KSzi2gE&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of selecting Use This App from the app's detail page.")
   - Manage Connections to access the
     [Connections
     page](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ous1698169987748&topicId=xyw1716318604389.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to create and manage Docusign app connections. Use connections to access different accounts and environments.").
