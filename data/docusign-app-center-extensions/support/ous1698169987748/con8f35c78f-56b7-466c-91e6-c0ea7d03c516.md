# Use Docusign Workflow Templates in the App Center

Learn how to select and initiate a Docusign Workflow Builder template
from the Docusign App Center page.

You can select and initiate a Docusign Workflow Builder
template from the App Center page.

Depending on your account privileges, you can:

- Docusign Admin: Access and use Docusign Workflow Builder workflow templates in the Docusign App Center.
- Non-admin user: View information about a workflow template.

  Note: You cannot initiate a workflow
  template. Contact your Docusign Admin to
  gain access to Workflow Builder Workflows.

For detailed information about workflows, see the ​[Creating Workflows ​​​](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yff1696971835267&topicId=tco1697826811758.html&_LANG=enus)​
article.
