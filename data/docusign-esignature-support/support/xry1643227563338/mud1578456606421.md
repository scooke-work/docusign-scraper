# Deleting a template with an associated PowerForm

Learn how to delete a template that has a PowerForm associated with it.

When you delete a template with an associated PowerForm, the PowerForm is also deleted.

![A portion of the My Templates page that lists templates with Powerforms associated with them.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/templatewithpowerforms.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2NjAsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.JPqyAIXmV0Txv4WPJy66raTH9fPzcCYg7WB9s4B2HLs&_LANG=enus "A portion of the My Templates page that lists templates with Powerforms associated with them.")

Any user who tries to access the form through the PowerForm URL will get an error
stating the form is no longer available.

Note:

Make sure to check if the Powerform URL is in Active distribution before you delete a
template associated with it. For example, if you have embedded the URL on your website or
included the URL link in an email campaign, you must remove it from these distribution
points before deleting the template.
