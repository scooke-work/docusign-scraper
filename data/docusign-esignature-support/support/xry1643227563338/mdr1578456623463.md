# Set Custom IDR Zones

Learn how to specify areas of a template for precise template matching.

Intelligent Document Recognition (IDR) is the process that reviews the text and information
in a document to determine if it matches a saved template. By default, template matching
examines the first and last 25 words in a document. To get more precise matching, you can
add custom IDR zones to your template documents.

Setting IDR zones is useful if you have multiple documents with similar opening and closing
texts. You can set IDR zones to focus on unique portions of the document.

You can select any region of text within a document for IDR. There is no limit to the
maximum or minimum volume of text that you can select to define a match. However, the less
text you select, the more false positive matches are likely. The more text that you select,
the longer the matching process will take.

1. Go to the Templates page.
2. Locate the template that you want to edit by searching, filtering, or simply scanning
   the Template list.
3. Click the template title to select it and open the template details view.
4. Click EDIT  to open the template for editing.
5. Click NEXT to continue to the Add Fields view for the template.
6. Click the gear icon at the top of the list of documents and
   select Add IDR Zones.

7. In the IDR Tool panel, click and drag the Add IDR Zones tool to
   the text you want to mark as an IDR zone.

   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/idrselect.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ3NjksInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.P88xVgHe1iRbXE5QjzgUyqNwQWMRvSOwAvYbzY8PLK8&_LANG=enus)
8. Click and drag on any of the sizing handles in the corners of the IDR zone to encompass
   the area you want to use for matching. Click and drag to zone to move the box around on
   your document.

   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/idrzone.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ3NjksInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.P88xVgHe1iRbXE5QjzgUyqNwQWMRvSOwAvYbzY8PLK8&_LANG=enus)
9. Add additional zones as needed. When you're finished adding IDR zones, click DONE in the banner at the top of the page.
10. To edit existing zones, from the template Add Fields view, click the Edit
    IDR Zones  link at the top of the Documents list.

    ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/editidr.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ3NjksInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.P88xVgHe1iRbXE5QjzgUyqNwQWMRvSOwAvYbzY8PLK8&_LANG=enus)
11. Edit the zones as follows:

    - To change the location of a zone, click and drag it to the new position.
    - To remove a zone, click to select it, and press **DELETE** on your keyboard.
    - To resize a zone, click to select it. Then click and drag on any one of the sizing
      handles at the corners of the zone.
12. When you are finished adding and editing IDR zones, click DONE > SAVE AND CLOSE.

Your IDR zone settings are applied and saved with the template.
