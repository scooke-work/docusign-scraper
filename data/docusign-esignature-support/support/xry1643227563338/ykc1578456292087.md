# Template Collections

Read the description of the types of template collections that display on your template
page.

The Templates page includes a set of collections to organize the templates you can access
automatically. These collections are as follows:

![Screenshot of the My Templates page that includes the side panel. It shows the template collections in the side panel.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/templatecollections.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2NjksInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.NmXF4R4H-9B5mDmncFL0EgmwUz8BgNzKQS75bndaszs&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of the My Templates page that includes the side panel. It shows the template collections in the side panel.")

- My Templates - contains the templates that you created or
  that were transferred to your ownership.
- Shared with Me - contains all the templates that are
  shared with you, either directly or through a shared folder. If you are an administrator,
  Shared with Me includes all templates on the account that you do
  not own.
- All Templates - contains all templates to which you have
  access. This collection includes your own templates and those shared with you.
- Favorites - contains all templates that you mark as a
  favorite. You can have up to 50 favorite templates.
- Deleted - contains all templates that you have recently
  deleted.
