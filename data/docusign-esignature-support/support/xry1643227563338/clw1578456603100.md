# Download a Document From a Template

Learn how to download a document from a template.

You can download PDF files of the documents in a template.

1. Navigate to the TEMPLATES page.
2. Locate the template that you want to download from by searching, filtering, or simply
   scanning the Templates list.
3. Click the template title to select it and open the Template Details view.
4. In the documents panel on the right, click the document thumbnail image to initiate the
   download. You may need to first expand the document section to display the page
   thumbnails.

   ![The documents panel on the right where you click the document thumbnail image to initiate the download.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/selectdocumenttodownload.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2MjAsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.jdTQrh6U7AhvXTKzFAqZo54j-KXXnZBfsc_AFep6MHw&_LANG=enus "The documents panel on the right where you click the document thumbnail image to initiate the download.")

   Your browser settings determine if the file opens in a browser window or downloads
   automatically.
5. If your document opens in a browser window, use the browser controls at the top of the
   window. You can download or print your document PDF.
