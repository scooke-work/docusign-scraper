# Delete Templates

Learn how to delete templates.

You can delete any template you own. When you delete a template, it remains in your Deleted
bin for up to 24 hours, and then it's removed permanently from the system. Once removed, a
template cannot be restored.

If you are an administrator on an account, then you can delete any template owned by any user
on the account.
