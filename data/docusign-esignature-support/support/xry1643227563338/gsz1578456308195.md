# Limit Changes to Templates and Template Fields

Use template and recipient modification settings to limit the types of changes that can
be made when your templates are used to send a document. Use the Sender
Permission settings to protect fields on a template from being changed or
deleted.

For the templates you create, you can limit the changes that other users can make when using
a template to send an envelope. These restrictions are useful if you want to control how your
others use your template.

This topic describes the options and steps for restricting changes to your template that
include general template settings, recipient details and settings, and template fields.

Note: If you [share
a template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=xry1643227563338&topicId=lrh1578456401532.html&_LANG=enus "Sharing a template allows other users and user groups on your account to access and use it to send documents.") with other account users, they may be able to edit your template. This
includes changing modification or permission property settings. You can protect your shared
template from being modified by other users by [setting a
password](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=xry1643227563338&topicId=xwo1578456395432.html&_LANG=enus "Template passwords protect against editing. If the template is shared with other users, for them to edit the shared template, they must provide the password."). Then, if other users want to edit a shared template, they must provide the
password.
