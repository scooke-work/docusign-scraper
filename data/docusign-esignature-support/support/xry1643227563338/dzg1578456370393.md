# Cancel Changes When Editing a Template

Learn how to discard changes when editing a template.

When editing a template, you can discard the changes and leave the template in its original
form.

1. At any point while editing your template, click the X in the
   top
   menu bar.
2. Select DISCARD CHANGES to confirm your action.

   Your changes are discarded and your template is restored to its original state.
