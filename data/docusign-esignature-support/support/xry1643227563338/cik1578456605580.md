# Delete a Template You Own

Learn how to delete a template that you own.

You can delete any template you own. Deleted templates are moved to your Deleted bin for a short time, after which they are removed permanently from the Docusign system and cannot be restored.

1. Navigate to the Templates page.
2. Select the My Templates collection and locate the template you
   want to edit by searching, filtering, or simply scanning the Template list.
3. Click the template's more actions menu and select
   Delete.

   ![Template actions menu with Delete selected'](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/deletetemplate.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2NTEsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.h9o42__XYsc5QgsQQKQouNX64QgW4nclsyfB00AwZi4&_LANG=enus "Template actions menu with Delete selected'")
4. Confirm your action by clicking DELETE in the confirmation modal.
5. The template moves to your Deleted bin. It will remain there for up to 24 hours after
   which it is removed permanently from the Docusign system and cannot be recovered.
