# Templates with PowerForms

Read about how to edit a template with PowerForms.

If the template you want to edit has any PowerForms, then changes you make to the template will affect the PowerForms.

![The top part of My Templates page with a PowerForm column.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/templatewithpowerforms.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2NDYsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.mTQ9IEuEUXP8IEcF9uk0gaXwaXttX0waROj3Yqg6lzQ&_LANG=enus "The top part of My Templates page with a PowerForm column.")

If you delete a template with PowerForms, all associated PowerForms are also
deleted. Any completed envelopes from the PowerForms remain in the PowerForm sender's
account.
