# Search for a Shared Template

You can use the search box and filter by date to locate a shared template. These controls work on
the selected folder only.

![Screenshot of the Shared with Me view of Templates with the search controls highlighted.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/searchtemplates.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ3MzksInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.-rhWIUGSUKvjtXBfXJeATaJbgLtJS0FNOPnDVL8YjJo&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of the Shared with Me view of Templates with the search controls highlighted.")

- **To search within a specific folder**, click on the folder to select it, and then enter your search or filter criteria. Folder search is strictly limited to the folder; it does not include results from any sub-folders present.
- **To search all templates you have access to**, click on the **All Templates** collection and enter your search or filter criteria.
