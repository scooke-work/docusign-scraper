# Complete Templates

Learn how to use complete templates. Complete templates have everything required to
generate an envelope. All you do is enter the recipient information.

If your template is complete, you can save even more time. For a template to be complete,
it must contain at least one of each of the following items: document, recipient, and
recipient field. When you use a complete template, you have the option to simply enter the
recipient names and review the email message. If your account uses envelope custom fields,
you can enter the field values.

Note: EXAMPLE—Complete Templates

Your Facilities
staff uses Docusign to issue temporary badges to contractors working in
your facility. You receive several contractors every month, so you have a template
prepared with the temporary access and badge request files that contractors sign. The
template is complete, with the files, a recipient role for the contractor, and the fields
for the contractor to complete. When Facilities uses the template, they enter the
contractor's name and email, and send the envelope.

1. Use one of the following methods to start an envelope from a complete template. Your
   templates and the templates shared with you are available to search and select.

   - From the Agreements page, select Start > Envelopes > Use a Template. Select the complete template you want to use and click Add
     Selected.
   - **From the Templates page**: Locate the complete template that you want to use
     for your new document and select **USE**.
     ![Screenshot of the Favorites list of templates with the Use button highlighted.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/usetemplatelist.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ3MTcsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.LYi4G5gOYwr_Pynk9etBgu0aJUFpjVCQ574CnEtrYbU&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of the Favorites list of templates with the Use button highlighted.")
2. Your new envelope is shown in a summary view for you to complete the required fields
   and review other information:
   1. Complete the recipient information for any role recipients.
   2. Review the email subject and text and adjust as desired.
   3. (Optional) Enter values for any envelope custom fields defined for your
      account.

      ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/usetemplatedialog.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ3MTcsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.LYi4G5gOYwr_Pynk9etBgu0aJUFpjVCQ574CnEtrYbU&_LANG=enus)
3. To send your envelope as is, click SEND.
4. (Optional) To make additional changes, such as upload additional documents, or add more
   recipients or recipient fields, click ADVANCED EDIT, and complete
   and send your envelope.
