# Download Template Documents

You can download entire documents or individual pages from any template that you have access
to. When you download template documents, the files are in PDF format, regardless of the
original document format when they were added to the template. The downloaded file name
defaults to the document name used on the template.

See the following guides to learn how to download documents:

- [Download a Document From a Template](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=xry1643227563338&topicId=clw1578456603100.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to download a document from a template.")
- [Download Individual Pages From a Template Document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=xry1643227563338&topicId=omh1578456603868.html&_LANG=enus)

Note: Your access to templates is controlled by your user permissions, as defined by
your Docusign administrator. If you cannot create or edit, or even use templates, check with
your administrator.
