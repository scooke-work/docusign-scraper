# Deleting Templates for Administrators

Learn how administrators can delete templates.

If you are an administrator, you have access to all templates created on your account and you can delete any of them.

1. Navigate to the Templates page.
2. Locate the template you want to edit by searching, filtering, or simply scanning the
   Template list.
3. Select one or more templates to delete by marking the checkboxes.
4. Click the more actions menu and select
   Delete.

   ![Screenshot of the All Templates view with the more actions menu, extended to show the Delete option.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/deletetemplatesadmin.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2NTUsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.J4z65BwGbEqQEfCG3km1fjjCg22kNvx5Aya_fsVa5FU&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of the All Templates view with the more actions menu, extended to show the Delete option.")
5. Confirm your action by clicking DELETE in the confirmation
   modal.

   The templates are moved to your Deleted bin. Deleted items remain in place for up to
   24 hours after which they are removed permanently from the Docusign system and cannot be
   recovered.
