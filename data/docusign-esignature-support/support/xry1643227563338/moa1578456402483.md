# Use a Shared Template

1. Navigate to the Templates page and select the Shared
   with Me view.
2. Locate the shared template and click Use.
3. If the template is complete (contains at least one recipient, one document, and one field), you can fill in the recipient information for any role recipients, and either send as is, or select Advanced Edit to further customize your document.
4. If the template is not complete, follow the standard process to use a template and complete all required information and customize your document as needed.
