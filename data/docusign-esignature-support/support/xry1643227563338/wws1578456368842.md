# Copy a Template

Learn how to copy a saved template.

Use this procedure to copy a saved
template.

1. Navigate to the Templates page.

   The Template list displays, showing your saved templates.
2. Locate the template you want to copy by searching, filtering, or simply scanning the
   Template list.
3. Click the template more actions menu and select
   Copy.

   ![A portion of the My Templates page with the Copyoption selected from the more actions menu.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/copyatemplate.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ1NjYsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.bGNLZyjdUC-fCI4DIM9MXyrmEole-R0d8vBIPcEcf7o&_LANG=enus "A portion of the My Templates page with the Copyoption selected from the more actions menu.")

   The new template copy opens for you to change any aspect, including the template name,
   documents, recipients, and recipient fields.
4. When you are finished making changes, click SAVE AND CLOSE.
