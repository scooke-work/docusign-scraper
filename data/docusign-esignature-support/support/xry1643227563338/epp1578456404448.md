# Locate Templates

Read how to locate templates that you own or are shared with you.

The Templates page gives you access to all the templates that you own or are shared with you.
There are different ways to locate templates, including search, filters, and browsing through
template folders.
The
topics in this
section
explain how
to use these methods to locate a template.
