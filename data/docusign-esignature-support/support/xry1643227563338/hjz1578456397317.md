# Share a Template With a User or Group

Read the procedure to share a template with a user or group.

1. Navigate to the Templates page.
2. Locate the template you want to share by searching, filtering, or simply scanning your
   My Templates list.
3. Mark the check box for the template to select it. You can select multiple templates to share at once.
4. Click MORE and select Share with Users.

   ![Share option in the More menu to share multiple templates](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/sharetemplate.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2OTYsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.q5Zh6vjc_Cb4CCJ82HxT7IGZuFiFnrGn6aYoWruT5sQ&_LANG=enus "Share option in the More menu to share multiple templates")

   The Sharing dialog box opens for you to select the users and groups to share your
   template with.
5. Make your user and group sharing selections - the Search function returns results for the selected tab:

   - USERS - lists all users on your account.
   - GROUPS - lists all groups defined for your account. Groups
     are listed by name only; group members are not displayed. The list includes standard
     account groups of Administrators and Everyone. By default, all templates are shared
     with the Administrators group and this doesn't change. Along with the standard groups,
     your Docusign administrator can define customized groups of account
     users and can provide further details. [Learn
     more...](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?topicId=cuv1583277363488.html&bundleId=pik1583277475390&_LANG=enus)

     Tip: To
     easily share your template with everyone in your account, on the
     USERS tab, mark the Select all
     checkbox.
   - SELECTED - all users and groups that are selected for
     sharing. Use this tab to verify your selections and make any necessary changes.
     ![Sharing dialog box to select users and groups to share a template with](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/sharingtemplate.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2OTYsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.q5Zh6vjc_Cb4CCJ82HxT7IGZuFiFnrGn6aYoWruT5sQ&_LANG=enus "Sharing dialog box to select users and groups to share a template with")
6. Click SHARE.

   The Template Details view updates to indicate the template is now shared. You can
   always check to see whom it is shared with and make changes to the sharing selections.
   ![Template details showing where to check who a template is shared with](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/sharedtemplateindicators.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ2OTYsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.q5Zh6vjc_Cb4CCJ82HxT7IGZuFiFnrGn6aYoWruT5sQ&_LANG=enus "Template details showing where to check who a template is shared with")

   For the people you have shared the template with, it displays in the
   Shared with Me and All Templates
   collections on their Templates page.
