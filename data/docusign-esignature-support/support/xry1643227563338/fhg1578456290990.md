# Add a Template to an Envelope

Learn how to add templates to your envelopes. You can add one or more templates to an
envelope.

If you start an envelope, you can add one or more templates to the envelope. You can add
other documents, recipients, and fields to the envelope separately from the templates you
add.

1. From the Home or Agreements page, start a new
   envelope from Start > Envelopes > Send an Envelope.
2. From the prepare view, in the Add documents section, select UPLOAD >  Use a template.

   ![Add documents section showing the UPLOAD menu expanded and selection on Use a template.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/addtemplate.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ3MjAsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.tlCm5cR8s-QhkPdQoPpj6BJlalVcmt9K-l4ifa9uQ-A&_LANG=enus "Add documents section showing the UPLOAD menu expanded and selection on Use a template.")
3. In the Select Templates list, browse or search through the
   template collections at left and select one or more templates to use.

   ![Select Templates window showing templates matching the search term "conference".](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/xry1643227563338/page/images/usetemplates.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ3MjAsInNoZWFmIjoieHJ5MTY0MzIyNzU2MzMzOCJ9.tlCm5cR8s-QhkPdQoPpj6BJlalVcmt9K-l4ifa9uQ-A&_LANG=enus "Select Templates window showing templates matching the search term \"conference\".")
4. Click ADD SELECTED.

   The documents, recipients, messages, and recipient fields from the selected
   templates are added to your envelope.
