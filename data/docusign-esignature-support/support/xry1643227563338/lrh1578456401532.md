# Share Templates

Sharing a template allows other users and user groups on your account to access and use it to send documents.

Sharing a template allows other users and user groups on your account to access and use it to
send documents for signature. If you have template share permission on
your Docusign account, you can share any template you created. You can also modify sharing
settings for any template shared with you.

Important: Your template permissions are set by your
Docusign administrator. You must have template permission set to *share* in order to
share templates.

## Two ways to share templates

There are two ways you can share your templates:

- Share a template directly with select users and groups.
- Share a template by adding it to a shared template folder.
