# Sending Documents for Signature

Read about how to send documents for signature.

With Docusign, getting signatures is quick and easy. Upload the document, add the signers' contact information, and specify what they must add, like a signature or initials.

Note: If you must sign a document yourself (with no other signers), follow the steps in the [Sign a Document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ulp1643236876813&topicId=dyh1578456260672.html&_LANG=enus) guide.

## Sending steps

The steps for creating and sending a document for signature are as follows:

1. **[Initiate Agreements and Templates](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ulp1643236876813&topicId=gso1578456465211.html&_LANG=enus):** You can start with a new envelope, use a template stored in Docusign, or finish a previously saved draft.
2. **[Add Your Documents](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ulp1643236876813&topicId=crk1578456412036.html&_LANG=enus):** Docusign supports a broad variety of [file formats](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gbo1643332197980&topicId=xln1578456261162.html&_LANG=enus). Select files from your local machine or from cloud storage providers like Google Drive or Dropbox.
3. **[Add Recipients](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ulp1643236876813&topicId=jar1578456351432.html&_LANG=enus):** Specify who receives your envelope and what action you want them to take.
4. **[Add Messages](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ulp1643236876813&topicId=mpf1578456350875.html&_LANG=enus):** Enter the subject and message details for your recipients.
5. **[Advanced Options](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gbo1643332197980&topicId=tfm1578456367923.html&_LANG=enus):** Set special recipient privileges, reminder settings, and expiration options.
6. **[Add Fields](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ulp1643236876813&topicId=odn1578456409392.html&_LANG=enus):** Add fields for each recipient to collect signatures and other information like title and date signed.
7. **[Preview and Send](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ulp1643236876813&topicId=oeg1578456408976.html&_LANG=enus):** Review your field setup and send the envelope to your recipients.

If you include yourself as a signer, after you send the envelope, you can start the signing process from within your account. For more information, see the [Sign a Document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=ulp1643236876813&topicId=dyh1578456260672.html&_LANG=enus) guide.
