# Make a Field Optional

Learn how to make a field optional.

To make a field optional:

1. Start an envelope as usual, adding documents, recipients, and messages.
2. In the Add Fields view, select one of the field types listed above and place it onto
   the document.
3. In the properties panel, toggle the Required
   property.

   ![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ulp1643236876813/page/images/requiredfieldproperty2.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQyNDMsInNoZWFmIjoidWxwMTY0MzIzNjg3NjgxMyJ9.o7LmpIc1nzWPHdQAfA_-N6j8UjhPmq2Bz1X_6gbDdO0&_LANG=enus)

The field is now optional and signers can choose whether or not
to complete it.
