# Read-Only Fields

Read about how to complete fields, mark them as read-only, and ensure that recipients can view but not change the values.

For some field types, as the sender, you can complete the fields and mark them as read-only for the recipients. The Read-only property locks a field from being changed by any recipient on the envelope.
