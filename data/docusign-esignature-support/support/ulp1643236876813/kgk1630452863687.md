# Log In to Your Docusign Account

Log in to your Docusign account using your email and password, and access troubleshooting resources if you encounter issues.

You can log in to your Docusign account with your email address and
password. If you have issues logging in, see the [login troubleshooting help](https://support.docusign.com/s/articles/I-am-having-trouble-logging-in) article.

1. Go to <https://apps.docusign.com/send>.

   Note: To access a demo account, go to <https://apps-d.docusign.com/send>.
2. Enter the email address for your account and select
   NEXT.
3. Enter your account password and select Log in.

   If you don't know your password, you can use the Reset
   password link to reset your password.
