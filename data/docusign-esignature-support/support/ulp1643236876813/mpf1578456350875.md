# Add Messages

Read about how to add messages to your document recipients, either as a group message for all or a private message for individual recipients.

Docusign sends signature requests to recipients through email. After adding recipients, you enter the subject and message. The subject becomes the envelope title, helping you identify it on the Agreements page and serving as the notification email subject for recipients.

For more information about adding group or private messages, explore these resources:
