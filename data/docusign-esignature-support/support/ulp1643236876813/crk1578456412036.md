# Add Documents to an Envelope

Read about how to upload documents to review and sign, and how to add documents from your local machine or a cloud storage provider.

Once you start an envelope, you add the documents you want your recipients to review and sign electronically. Docusign supports a wide variety of [file formats](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=gbo1643332197980&topicId=xln1578456261162.html&_LANG=enus), covering the popular document, presentation, image, drawing, and spreadsheet applications. When you add your files, Docusign converts them to PDF format for sending and storage.

You can add documents that are stored on your local machine. Depending on your account settings, you may be able to add documents from an authorized cloud storage provider, such as Google Drive or Dropbox. Your Docusign administrator controls which providers you can use. For more information, see "Document Sources" in [Sending Settings](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=bjh1583277352580.html&_LANG=enus) guide.
