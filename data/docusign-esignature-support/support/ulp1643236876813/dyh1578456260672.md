# Sign a Document

Read about how to sign a document from your Docusign account and send
it to anyone who needs a copy.

Sometimes, you just want to sign a document yourself–just you, no other signers. And maybe
you want to send a copy of the signed document to others. This may be a document someone sends
to you outside of Docusign or a form you must complete and send to someone.

With the Sign a Document feature, you can quickly upload a document to your Docusign account, sign it, and send the signed document to anyone who needs a
copy.

You can start a new document and sign it from the Home or
Agreements page.
