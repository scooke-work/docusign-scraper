# Welcome to Docusign

Discover how Docusign eSignature simplifies business processes for over 1.7 million customers worldwide.

Welcome to Docusign eSignature, the leading e-signature solution. Docusign brings agreements to life. Over 1.7 million customers and more than a billion people in over 180 countries use Docusign solutions to accelerate the process of doing business and simplify people's lives.
