# Get to Know Docusign

Explore a personalized dashboard with role-based widgets, a task list, and an overview of agreement activities.

![](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/ulp1643236876813/page/images/basics-newdsoverview.gif?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQxNDgsInNoZWFmIjoidWxwMTY0MzIzNjg3NjgxMyJ9.Fuu_tjwSfFWse90Bwd9PPu66NJUzJ_C3Yoa8SebyhOc&_LANG=enus)

- **Home:** Access a personalized dashboard featuring role-based widgets, a Tasks list, and an updated Overview of agreement activity
- **Tasks:** List up to 5 incomplete tasks in order of the most imminent due date.
- **Overview:** Track Open requests, Waiting for others, Expiring soon, Completed, and Upcoming renewals from the last 30 days.

Note: For users with access to Agreement Desk and Agreement Manager, the overview includes Open Requests and Agreement Manager Upcoming Renewals.

- **Smart Action Bar:** A button bar in the header area displays your three most recently used actions from the Start menu for quicker access.
- **Profile Management:** You can access and update your profile photo and signature by going to Manage Profile in the Profile menu.
