# Supported Languages

Docusign supports multiple languages, allowing users to set their preferred display language through account settings or the application menu.

Docusign is available in the following languages:

- English (U.S.)
- Chinese Simplified
- Chinese Traditional
- Dutch
- French
- German
- Italian
- Japanese
- Korean
- Polish
- Portuguese (Brazil)
- Portuguese (Portugal)
- Russian
- Spanish

You can set the display language for your Docusign account through the [My Preferences](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=jux1643235969954&topicId=kde1578456454912.html&_LANG=enus) page or from the selection menu at the bottom of every page in the application.

Administrators can also set the display language for all users through the administration view. For more information, see the [User management](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=pik1583277475390&topicId=ixb1583277365937.html&_LANG=enus) guide.

For signers, Docusign supports 44 localized languages, as listed on our [Global Business Standard](https://www.docusign.com/products/electronic-signature/learn/global) page. Languages are detected through the signer's browser settings.
