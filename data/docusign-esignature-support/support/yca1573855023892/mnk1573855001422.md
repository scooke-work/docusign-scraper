# View Comments and Replies

Learn how to access documents that include comments or replies when you sign.

When you're signing or sending an envelope and have questions or comments about a document, you can use the Docusign comments feature. For example, you may not agree with the language of a contract, or you may recommend that another person signs the document.

To view comments or replies to comments:

- If you receive an email notification, select the link in the notification.
- If you have a Docusign account, log on to your account and open the document in the Notification Center as described in the [View Comments and Replies Through the Notification Center](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=jxh1573855003469.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to use the Docusign Notification Center to view comments or replies.") article.

If you're not receiving email notifications about comments or replies, it can be that notifications are sorted into your spam folder or the sender of the envelope has disabled email notifications. Look in your spam folder or ask the sender of the envelope about it.

For more information, see the [Settings to Allow Comments on Documents and Envelope](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=odr1583277453624.html&_LANG=enus) article.
