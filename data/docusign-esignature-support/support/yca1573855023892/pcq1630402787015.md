# Sign With the DigiCert QuoVadis Trust Service Provider to Issue Qualified Electronic Signatures in the Netherlands and the European Union

Learn how to sign documents using Docusign and DigiCert QuoVadis
qualified electronic signature (QES) certificates in the Netherlands and the European
Union.

## Before you begin

Before you begin, ensure that you completed the following prerequisites:

- You installed the Go>Sign Mobile™ application on your mobile
  phone.
- You registered with the DigiCert QuoVadis team, enrolled your DigiCert QuoVadis certificate,
  and registered your mobile phone. For more information, contact the DigiCert
  QuoVadis support team as described in the [digicert and
  QuoVadis](https://www.quovadissupport.nl/) page.

Use the DigiCert QuoVadis Trust Service Provider (TSP) to issue Qualified Electronic
Signatures (QES) in the Netherlands and the European Union.

You received a Docusign email prompting you to review and sign the
documents. If you have a DigiCert QuoVadis qualified electronic signature (QES)
certificate, a sender might require you to use it to sign documents and comply with
the Dutch government root of trust. For detailed information, see the [PKIoverheid](https://www.quovadisglobal.com/nl-en/#:~:text=DigiCert+QuoVadis PKIoverheid Certificates,for your needs.) page.

1. Select REVIEW DOCUMENT in the email informing you that
   you have a document to sign.

   The document to review displays in your Web browser.
2. Select CONTINUE.
3. Select the SIGN tags.
4. Select CONTINUE.

   The Finish Signing dialog box opens.
5. Select NEXT.

   ![Screenshot of Docusign DigiCert Finish Signing dialog box.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/DocuSign-DigiCert-Finish-Signing-dialog.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ1MDUsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.QvWd0Rcy4MFLHjO8nZNz7V3TlWPv2_2KqrNkYeRnHgw&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of Docusign DigiCert Finish Signing dialog box.")

   The DigiCert QuoVadis authentication page opens.
6. Enter your email and password.

   ![Screenshot of the DigiCert log on page.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/DigiCert-login-page.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ1MDUsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.QvWd0Rcy4MFLHjO8nZNz7V3TlWPv2_2KqrNkYeRnHgw&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of the DigiCert log on page.")
7. Select Login.

   If you logged on successfully, you receive a notification to the mobile phone you
   registered during the DigiCert QuoVadis registration process.
8. Select the DigiCert QuoVadis notification on your mobile phone.

   The Go>Sign Mobile application opens on your
   mobile phone.
9. Authorize DigiCert QuoVadis to sign your document as shown in the following
   examples.

   ![Screenshot of the Go>Sign Mobile application signing flow.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/GoSign-Mobile-application-signing-flow.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ1MDUsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.QvWd0Rcy4MFLHjO8nZNz7V3TlWPv2_2KqrNkYeRnHgw&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of the Go>Sign Mobile application signing flow.")

The Go>Sign Mobile application signs your
document using your DigiCert QuoVadis certificate. When the application finishes
processing your signature, you get a notification that signing is complete. You can also
log on to Docusign to save a copy of the document to your
account.
