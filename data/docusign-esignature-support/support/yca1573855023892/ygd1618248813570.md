# Why Do I Keep Getting Email Reminders to Sign with Docusign?

Choose one of these options to discontinue repeat email reminders to sign a
document.

Docusign sends email notifications on behalf of senders. Each sender sets how often to send
email reminders.

Select one of these options to stop receiving email reminders to sign:

- Decline to sign, as described int the [Decline To Sign an Envelope](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=fqo1573855017663.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how and when to decline to sign an envelope you review.") article.
- Sign the document as described in the [Docusign eSignature Signing Process](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=lou1573854977109.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about the Docusign eSignature signing process and eSignature signing terminology.") article.
- Contact the sender by replying to the email notification and ask them to void or cancel the
  document
