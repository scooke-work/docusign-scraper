# Sign as a Delegated Signer

Learn how to sign on behalf of another person to ensure that document signing can
continue uninterrupted when signers are unavailable to sign.

Your sender may enable the optional delegated signing feature. This feature ensures that
envelope completion continues uninterrupted when signers are out of the office, or
otherwise unavailable to sign.

As a delegate signing an agreement on another signer's behalf, you sign under your
own name and signature.

1. When you receive a notification to sign, select
   REVIEW DOCUMENT.
2. Agree to the disclosure statement that details you are signing on behalf of the
   delegator.

   Once you accept the disclosure statement, the signing session displays a
   notice that you are signing on behalf of the delegator as shown in this
   example.

   ```
   |  |  |
   | --- | --- |
   |  |  |
   |  | You are signing on behalf of Tamara Morgan |
   ```

   This helps distinguish between signing as a recipient and as a delegate.
3. After completing the signing, you receive any further status notifications,
   such as if the envelope is voided or when it is completed.

   If you do not sign, you do not receive any further notifications.

   When an envelope has a signer with an active delegate, delegate information is displayed in
   the envelope details. Anyone with access to the envelope can see the
   delegated information.
