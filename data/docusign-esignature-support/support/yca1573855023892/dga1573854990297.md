# Adopt Your Signature

Learn how to choose a style for your signature and initial when you first sign a document with Docusign.

The first time you select a Sign or Initial field on a document, you're asked to adopt a style for your signature or initials.

Note: To learn how to adopt and manage your signature and initials from within your Docusign account, see the [Create Your Signature](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=jux1643235969954&topicId=lvo1578456442907.html&_LANG=enus) article.

When you adopt your signature, you can:

- Use a predefined style from Docusign
- Draw your signature or initials
- Upload an image

You adopt your signature and initials separately when you select the corresponding field. For example, you can choose to upload your signature when you select a Sign field and draw your initials when you select the Initials field. Keep in mind that the sender can disable the options to draw or upload your signature and initials.

   - Manage your signature and documents.

## Draw Your Signature or Initials

If you don't see any predefined signature styles that appeal to you, you can draw your signature and initials. Drawing your signature and initials freehand works best if you have a stylus. However, if you have a touchscreen device, you can also use your mouse pointer or even your finger.

Tip: You can use the Docusign mobile app on a tablet or other mobile device to draw your signature and initials. Use a stylus or just your finger to capture your own unique style.

1. In the window that opens when you first select a Sign or Initial field on a document, select the DRAW tab.

   Note: If you don’t see the DRAW and UPLOAD options, the sender might have disabled them.
2. (Optional) In the DRAW YOUR SIGNATURE or DRAW YOUR INITIALS field, use a mouse or, for touchscreens, your finger or a stylus to draw your signature or initials. The following image shows how to draw your signature after selecting a Sign field.

   If you make a mistake, select Clear in the upper-right corner just above your signature to reset the field.

   ![Screenshot of the Draw a Signature field and controls in the Adopt Your Signature dialog box.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signingadopt4.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQzODQsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.4namKREUoIRhuyi-w9zN873Hjpr9Y9ZIsrrs3hRZVSQ&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of the Draw a Signature field and controls in the Adopt Your Signature dialog box.")

## Upload an Image of Your Signature or Initials

You can also upload images of your signature and initials instead of using a predefined style. The image files should meet these requirements:

- Maximum file size: 3MB
- File formats: GIF, JPG, PNG, and BMP
- For the best results, use an image that is 400 x 150 pixels in size

1. In the window that opens when you first select a Sign or Initial field on a document, select the UPLOAD tab.

   Note: If you don’t see the DRAW and UPLOAD options, the sender might have disabled them.
2. Select UPLOAD YOUR SIGNATURE or UPLOAD YOUR INITIALS and browse to the location of the image file on your device.

   ![Screenshot of uploading your signature in the Adopt Your Signature window.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signingadopt5.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQzODQsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.4namKREUoIRhuyi-w9zN873Hjpr9Y9ZIsrrs3hRZVSQ&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of uploading your signature in the Adopt Your Signature window.")
3. If you upload or select a different signature, you can resize the signature using resizing controls.

   1
   :   Drag the small white squares on the bottom and right side of the signature rectangle to resize the signature.

   2
   :   Use the slider to increase or decrease the signature size.

   3
   :   Click the image and move it within the rectangle.

   4
   :   Select Rotate to move the image 90 degrees for each click.

By creating an account, you can manage your signatures through your Docusign eSignature account, including:

- Adding signatures
- Changing the signatures you have adopted
- Drawing your signatures and initials

If you haven't already created a free Docusign account, create one and manage your signatures there. The next time you sign a document, Docusign will use the signature and initials you selected through your account.

For information on how to add, change, and manage your signatures through your Docusign account, see the [Create Your Signature](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=jux1643235969954&topicId=lvo1578456442907.html&_LANG=enus) article.
