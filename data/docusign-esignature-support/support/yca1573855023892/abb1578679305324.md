# Download or Print a Supplement From an Email Notification

Learn how to download or print a document from the eSignature supplement
view.

You can download a copy or print from the supplement
view. The easiest way to access a document is from the email notification.

1. Open any of the email notifications that you received and select
   REVIEW DOCUMENT to begin the signing process.
2. Select Agree to use electronic records and signatures  > Continue

   If there's no electronic records and signature (ERSD) option, the document
   opens when you select Review Document..
3. From the opened document, find the supplement you want to download and select
   VIEW.

   ![A screenshot of selecting the option to View the supplement](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signnewviewfromemail.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0MjMsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.wMkq8irhFCjXHDgWovPPaIyxORftVKrQQgjn-oayuzs&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of selecting the option to View the supplement")
4. After viewing the supplement, save a copy of it by selecting the
   Download icon ![Download document icon.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signnewdownloadicon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0MjMsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.wMkq8irhFCjXHDgWovPPaIyxORftVKrQQgjn-oayuzs&_LANG=enus "Download document icon.").

   The PDF of the supplement opens in a new browser window. In the new window,
   select the Download icon on the top right of the PDF
   and save to your computer.
5. After viewing the supplement, you can print it by selecting the
   Print icon ![Print document icon.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signnewprinticon.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0MjMsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.wMkq8irhFCjXHDgWovPPaIyxORftVKrQQgjn-oayuzs&_LANG=enus "Print document icon.")

   The PDF of the supplement opens in a new browser window. In the new window,
   select the Print and follow printing instructions for
   your printer.
