# Use Docusign AI-Assisted Signing Features

Docusign Signing AI Summary and extraction outlines the most important
information in agreement and displays the key agreement details. Use the Ask a question
feature for customized agreement details and associated citations.

Docusign Iris is the AI engine that powers Docusign Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) and Docusign's AI features across the agreement life cycle.

Note: These features are only available for envelopes sent from North American accounts.

Take advantage of these AI signing features when you’re signing documents.

- AI Summary that outlines the most important information in agreement and
  displays the key agreement details.
- Extraction that displays a summary of the key agreement details.
- Ask a question for customized agreement details and associated citations.

1. Open the document as described in [Docusign eSignature Signing Process](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=lou1573854977109.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about the Docusign eSignature signing process and eSignature signing terminology.") the
   article.
2. When the document opens, select the Ask a question
   prompt.
