# Decline To Sign an Envelope

Learn how and when to decline to sign an envelope you review.

Here are some examples of when you might decline to sign an envelope:

- You received an envelope meant for another person.
- You’re signing an agreement, such as an apartment lease. Some of the terms you agreed to
  during your discussion with the leasing agent are missing or incorrect.
- The envelope containing the on-boarding package for your new job is missing key
  documents.

When you decline to sign, it prevents you and all other envelope recipients from signing.
Before declining to sign an envelope, ask the envelope's sender a question by posting a
comment to the envelope. For details about asking and answering questions, see the [Use Comments to
Ask and Answer Questions About a Document](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=ljq1573855001125.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to add information to or ask for clarification in the documents you are signing.") article. You can also reply to the email
notification you received.

Note the following:

- A sender can void an envelope as described in [Void an Envelope on Which You Are Also a Recipient﻿](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=oeq1643226594604&topicId=zyn1578456424164.html&_LANG=enus).
- A signer who is also the sender cannot Decline to Sign. Instead,
  they select Void Envelope.

To decline to sign:

1. Open the email notification and select REVIEW DOCUMENT.
2. In the upper right of the envelope, select OTHER ACTIONS and
   select Decline to Sign.

   ![A screenshot of the Decline to Sign option in the Signing Other Actions menu.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signnewdecline.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NjMsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.f5dYqLo1GuPgTNwYDkNTBt0_sA2Gptqhex7w5d9saIw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Decline to Sign option in the Signing Other Actions menu.")
3. Confirm that you understand that declining to sign voids the envelope not only for
   yourself, but also for all the other recipients of the envelope. Select
   CONTINUE.

   ![A screenshot of the Confirm Decline to Sign dialog box.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signdeclinecaution.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NjMsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.f5dYqLo1GuPgTNwYDkNTBt0_sA2Gptqhex7w5d9saIw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Confirm Decline to Sign dialog box.")
4. Provide a reason why you are declining to sign to the envelope sender. The envelope's
   sender may require you to provide a reason why you're declining. The text you enter is
   emailed to the sender and is recorded as part of the envelope history.

   ![A screenshot of the Decline to Sign Envelope Reason dialog box.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signdeclinereason.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NjMsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.f5dYqLo1GuPgTNwYDkNTBt0_sA2Gptqhex7w5d9saIw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Decline to Sign Envelope Reason dialog box.")
5. After providing a reason why you are declining, select DECLINE TO
   SIGN.

   A message displays confirming that you declined and that the Sender has been
   notified.
