# Change Your Signature

Learn how to change your signature and initials for a document you are signing.

When signing a document, you may need to change your signature. For a quick overview of
this process, see the video in [How do I change my signature or adopt a custom signature?](https://support.docusign.com/s/articles/How-do-I-change-my-signature-or-adopt-a-custom-signature-NDSE).

Common scenarios include:

- Fix a misspelled name
- Your name has changed since you last signed
- You want to resize the signature or initial tab to fit the document you are signing
- Users with a shared email address receive an envelope addressed to both of the users.
  They must change the signature to ensure that the correct user is signing

Note: These restrictions apply to changing signatures:

- You must have a Docusign account to implement multiple
  signatures.
- You can only have one signature per full name.
- When you adopt a new signature, this action deletes and replaces the previous
  signature.
- If you are the only signer, you cannot select a saved signature.
- If you are a first-time signer, you cannot modify your signature after you initially
  adopt your signature (as described in the [Adopt your
  signature](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=dga1573854990297.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to choose a style for your signature and initial when you first sign a document with Docusign.") article).

Certain other restrictions can prohibit changing your signature. For complete details, see
the [Why can't I change my signature?](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=tpz1620246007465.html&_LANG=enus "Read about situations where you can't change your signature.") article.

1. Browse to the document through the Docusign notification email.
2. Select CONTINUE to accept the Electronic Record and
   Signature Disclosure and display the document.
3. Select START to go to the first signature field.
4. Select the signature field marked Required - Sign Here to
   display the Adopt your Signature  dialog box.

   ![Screenshot of Adopt Your Signature dialog box with ADOPT AND SIGN option.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signnewadoptsigdb.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQzODcsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.HR58kNJUDkkDzQlmL6oKvWLpksn0Q_U5JLQmTicMRP4&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of Adopt Your Signature dialog box with ADOPT AND SIGN option.")
5. Select one of these options to change your signature.
   - SELECT STYLE > Change Style to change the signature and initials appearance.
   - DRAW to draw your signature.
   - USE A SIGNATURE PAD > Start Capture to create your signature on a signature pad.
   - UPLOAD a signature image file.
6. Select ADOPT AND SIGN.
7. Select FINISH.

## Change your Signature in Your Account

You can change an existing signature and initials from your
Docusign Account at any time.

1. From your Docusign account, select your account icon and select
   Manage Profile.
2. Select Signatures and choose:
   - Edit to display all available signatures.
   - Delete to remove an existing signature.
   - + Add New to create a new signature.
3. Confirm that your full name and initials are correct, and select a style, draw, or
   upload your signature. When finished, select CREATE.
