# Sign a Document With Supplements

Learn about working with supplemental information that you review before
signing.

## What is a supplement?

While signing a document, the document may include supplements.

Supplements contain additional information and are included with your document for
your acknowledgment.

![A screenshot of a document with a supplement.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signsuppliment.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0MTMsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.pa-0QZ4ManwbyCRW09wDu_sFF-rffOOb5vZuBxMjhdM&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a document with a supplement.")

Supplements can be (but are not restricted to):

- Terms and conditions
- Disclosures
- Financial statements
