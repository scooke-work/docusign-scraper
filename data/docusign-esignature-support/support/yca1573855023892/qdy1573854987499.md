# Other Signing Actions

Learn about the available options for signers from the Other Actions menu.

The Other Actions menu contains additional signing
actions available to you when signing documents in Docusign.
For example, if you run out of time to complete a document, you can elect to
finish later. Or you may want to decline to sign or assign the document to
someone else if:

- The document was sent to you by mistake
- The document is incorrect
- You're not the right signer

Note: Depending on the
settings the sender has enabled for your documents, some options are not
available.

Access the Other Action menu by selecting the arrow on the
Finish menu.

- Finish Later: Exit the signing process, saving any
  information you entered. Return to finish signing the document by using the
  link in the original email notification.

  ![A screenshot of a finish signing later message.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signfinishlatermsg.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NDcsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.xoDgAPnyTTT0dp92qJ3wEkb0doYvYMTsxGv5DTKDbMo&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a finish signing later message.")
- Assign to Someone Else: Reassign the signing
  responsibility to another person.
  - Provide the new signer’s name, email address, SMS information
    (telephone number), and a reason for the change.
  - The sender receives a change notification including the new
    signer's info and the reason for the change.
  - The new signer that you've identified is prompted to sign the
    document.

  Note: Add SMS
  information for the new signer if the SMS and phone authentication is
  required for your document, Otherwise, the new signer does not receive
  the document.
- Print & Sign: Print and sign the document on
  paper.
- Decline to Sign: Decline to sign the document.
  Optionally provide a message for the sender of the document indicating why
  you have declined to sign.

  When there are other signers, those who have
  already completed signing receive an email stating that you have
  declined to sign. Other signers who have not completed signing cannot
  access the documents.
- Void the Envelope You and other recipients receive
  an email notification that includes the reason for voiding the envelope.
  Recipients who have finished signing or whose turn it is to sign can still
  access the documents. A VOID  watermark displays on
  all pages and no further signing actions by anyone is possible. Any future
  recipients in the signing order will never receive any notification or be
  granted access to view or sign.

- Report Abuse: Use the Report abuse wizard to report
  fraud or abuse to Docusign. You can indicate the report
  reason, and optionally be contacted by someone in Docusign to follow up.
- Help and Support: Opens the Docusign Support Center in a new browser window.
- About Docusign Opens the Docusign
  corporate site that has more information about Docusign
  and available features.
- View History: displays the sending and signing
  history for the documents.
- View Certificate (PDF): Opens a new browser window
  with a PDF version of the certificate of completion. This PDF includes
  information about the events, and the disclosure. The PDF can be saved or
  printed.
- View Electronic Record and Signature Disclosure:
  Displays the sender’s Electronic Record and Signature Disclosure.
- Session Information: Displays session-related
  details, such as the Envelope ID, Recipient ID, and the Signing
  Version.
