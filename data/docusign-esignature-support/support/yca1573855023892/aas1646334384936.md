# Sign Through an SMS Text Notification

Learn about how to sign a document sent through a Docusign SMS text
notification.

Senders can choose to send envelopes through real-time SMS text notifications directly to your
mobile device.

With
SMS notifications
,
you
can
quickly access and complete agreements from any location.

For example, you are currently traveling, and your rental
organization
sends your new lease agreement to review and sign. You receive notification through
an SMS text message (as well as an email notification). You can immediately review
and sign the lease from your mobile device.

Depending on your sender's configuration of this feature, you may receive SMS
notifications through:

- An email notification along with the SMS notification
- SMS notification only

Note: The document mark-up feature is not available on a mobile
device.

To sign a document
through
an SMS notification:

1. When you receive an SMS text message from Docusign, select
   the link in the message.

   ![A screenshot of an SMS text message asking you to sign a document.]()
2. Select Yes at the following screen to consent to receive
   future SMS messages from Docusign.

   If you decline to receive future SMS text messages, you only see the
   messages from this sender
   through
   an email notification.

   The following message displays for an SMS-only notification.

   ![A screenshot of an SMS-only decline to sign message.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/sms-decline.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0MjUsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.u2iU5eFLFMUcV7R-l0V1TnXI7NIZv9qhNpifzqi7y2k&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of an SMS-only decline to sign message.")
3. Optionally share your current location at the prompt.

   ![A screenshot of an SMS-only review documents prompt.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signmobileshareloc.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0MjUsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.u2iU5eFLFMUcV7R-l0V1TnXI7NIZv9qhNpifzqi7y2k&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of an SMS-only review documents prompt.")
4. If this is the first time using SMS to review a document, you are prompted to
   select the Mobile-Friendly experience.

   ![A screenshot of a selecting the SMS Mobile-Friendly experience.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signmobileselmf.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0MjUsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.u2iU5eFLFMUcV7R-l0V1TnXI7NIZv9qhNpifzqi7y2k&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of a selecting the SMS Mobile-Friendly experience.")

   Selecting the mobile experience changes the formatting to enhance viewing on
   a small screen.
5. Select Start to display the document.
6. When the document you must sign displays, begin the signing process as
   described in the [Docusign eSignature Signing Process](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=lou1573854977109.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about the Docusign eSignature signing process and eSignature signing terminology.") article.
7. Complete the signing actions that you usually perform from an email message
   request.

   You can:

   - Access the signing session with or without an access code as described in the [Docusign eSignature Signing Process](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=lou1573854977109.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about the Docusign eSignature signing process and eSignature signing terminology.") article.
   - Finish later: save your changes and complete signing at another
     time.
   - Change signing responsibility as described in the [Change Signing Responsibility](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=uin1573855010064.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to assign a document to another person if you're not the right person to sign the document.")
     article.
   - Decline to sign as described in the [Decline To Sign an Envelope](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=fqo1573855017663.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how and when to decline to sign an envelope you review.") article.

Once signed, you receive a text message for the completed document that you can
download to your device and save for reference.

Note the following about SMS text delivery:

- SMS-only recipients who have existing Docusign accounts cannot have their
  completed documents associated with their accounts. If you choose to download
  the completed documents, the documents are only saved to the download
  location.
- If you opt out of Docusign SMS text notifications, any subsequent requests
  through Docusign SMS delivery are blocked.
  Manually
  opt in to Docusign
  SMS
  to reinstate SMS notifications. For detailed information about
  opting in, see the [Docusign SMS Service](https://support.docusign.com/s/articles/sms-service) article.
- Senders can also require identity verification before you sign. See the [Verify Your Identity](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=mbg1573854981921.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about using the different authentication methods to verify your identity for Docusign eSignature.") article to learn about identity verification
  methods for signing a document.
