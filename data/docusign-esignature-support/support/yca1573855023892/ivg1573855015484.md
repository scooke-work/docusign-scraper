# Fax Your Documents

Learn how to select Fax the Docusign signing Other Actions
menu.

To fax your documents as the return method, follow these instructions.

Note:

- If you already downloaded the documents but quit during a previous Print &
  Sign session before returning your documents, you must download the documents
  again and sign using these instructions.
- If you see Print & Sign: Download Document, the
  document's sender doesn't support return by FAX. Follow the instructions in the
  [Upload Signed Documents](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=ctc1573855014359.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to upload documents you must print, sign, and return.") article.

1. Select Fax and select CONTINUE.

   ![A screenshot of the Print & Sign dialog box with download for fax selected.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/newsigningprintfax.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NTksInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.j4zY0D2tniP-rzTvtCGxc3VvtcU5TR7NqUuJXcwgaZw&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Print & Sign dialog box with download for fax selected.")
2. Select DOWNLOAD to download the fax cover page and your
   documents.
3. After downloading the fax cover page and your documents, select
   FINISH to exit the signing process.
4. Print the fax cover page and documents using your normal printing method.
5. Complete and sign the printed pages as needed.
6. Follow the instructions on the printed fax cover page to fax the documents to
   Docusign.

   Note: When faxing your documents, make sure to send the
   Docusign fax cover page first. The fax cover page has
   information that links your documents to the envelope. Your documents might
   be lost if the cover sheet is not the first page.
7. When Docusign receives the fax, your documents are matched with the sender’s
   original request to complete the process.

   Note: You can
   optionally upload the documents or sign them electronically. If you sign
   electronically, you can return to the document using the link in the
   original email that was sent to you. After you open the document, select
   CANCEL FAX to cancel the fax process. Then follow
   the instructions for uploading your documents.

You have successfully completed signing your documents.

When all the signers have completed signing, you receive an email confirming this and
giving you have the option to download a PDF copy of the completed documents.
