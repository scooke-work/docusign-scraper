# How Do I Handle Supplemental Documents?

Learn how to work with an envelope's supplemental documents.

The document sender can request that you perform certain actions for each supplement
included in the document.

If the sender of the document requests that you view, accept, or read and accept a
supplement, you must complete the request to finish signing the document.

If you have questions about the supplemental documents or what you should do, contact the
person sending you the document.

## Information Only Supplements

You are not required to take any action; the document is
available to review.

1. Select the thumbnail to view the supplement document.

   ![Screenshot of a supplement's thumbnail.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signinitialsupplement.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0MTgsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.-9rDHhjU2jvk4-pEJ-lk5FBx4BnICrleUumzFaXDzTo&_LANG=enus "Screenshot of a supplement's thumbnail.")
2. Alternatively, you can view the supplement after you complete the signing
   fields.

## View the Supplement To Finish Signing

Some senders require you to view supplements before
completing the signing process.

1. Select VIEW to open the supplement.
2. Review the whole document by scrolling to the end to accept.
3. Select ACCEPT to complete the document signing.

## Accept the Supplement

To finish signing some documents, you must review and accept
the supplements.

1. After displaying a supplement, review the terms and conditions and select
   ACCEPT.
2. If the message `Read the entire supplement in order to accept`
   displays at the bottom of your browser window, you must scroll to the bottom before you
   can select ACCEPT.

## Save a Copy of a Supplement

When you download or print a document, supplements may be
included. Because supplements can be lengthy, sometimes supplements may not be included when
you download the signed document.

1. To save a copy of a supplement, see the [Save or Print Document Supplements](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=cfh1573855008547.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to save or print a supplement to a document you're signing.")
   article.
2. If supplements are not included in the document download, you can download a copy or
   print from the supplement view. The easiest way to access a document is from the email
   notification as described in the [Download or Print a Supplement From an Email Notification](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=abb1578679305324.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to download or print a document from the eSignature supplement view.") article.

   For instructions on how to download or print a document, see the [How do I download or print a document I receive?](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=sto1596228661240&topicId=wdr1573855014901.html&_LANG=enus) article.
