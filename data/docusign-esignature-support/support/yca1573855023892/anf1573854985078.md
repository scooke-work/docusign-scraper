# Use Freeform Signing

Learn how to use Docusign Freeform signing using the menu on the left
side of the signing page.

Use Freeform Signing to add information to the document using the menu on the left side of
the page. The menu has a list of fields that you can use to add signatures, initials, and
other information to the document. Drag the signature and information tags to the
document.

Note the following:

- When adding a signature, initial or other information to a document, drag the
  appropriate field (such as Signature, Initial, Date Signed) from the palette. Place the
  field onto the location in the document where you want the field to display.
- When you place a Signature or Initial field, you are asked to adopt your signature and
  initials. See the [Adopt Your Signature](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=dga1573854990297.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to choose a style for your signature and initial when you first sign a document with Docusign.") article for more information.
- You can modify prefilled information as needed. The Name, First Name, Last Name, Email
  Address, organization and Title fields use pre-filled information based on what the sender
  enters for you
- The Date Signed field uses the current date.
- When you need to add text in the document, drag a Text field onto the document. After
  placing the Text field, you can type information directly into it. You might must resize
  the Text field to have all the information fit.
- The Checkbox field places a checked box on the document.

Note: After placing your first field,
FINISH becomes active. However, continue to place fields to
complete the documents before selecting FINISH.

To manage fields:

- Reposition a field by dragging it to a new location.
- Re-size a field by selecting it and then dragging the corner of the field to change the
  size.
- Remove a field from the document by selecting it and then selecting the delete icon on
  the field.

After you have placed all the fields on the document, select FINISH
to complete signing.

For more information, see the [Complete the Signing Session](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=abm1573854992359.html&_LANG=enus "Learn how to complete signing and optionally save or print a copy of your signed documents.") article.
