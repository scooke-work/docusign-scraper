# Accessibility at Docusign

Learn how Docusign provides high-quality products that are accessible
to all users.

Docusign is committed to providing high-quality products that
are accessible to all users.

As a company, Docusign strives to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA in the design and
development of all our products. Docusign applications are designed and developed with
accessibility in mind. For example, these applications:

- Ensure that document text and tags can be read by assistive technology tools like screen
  readers.
- Provide easy navigation that allows users to skip past content with proper headings
- Have a common screen reader keyboard shortcut for form figures and tables to improve
  navigation for a reader

For detailed information about additional accessibility features and Docusign’s conformance to government and international requirements,
see the [Docusign Accessibility Hub](https://www.docusign.com/accessibility?postSignIn=true).
