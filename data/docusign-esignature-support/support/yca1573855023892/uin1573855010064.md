# Change Signing Responsibility

Learn how to assign a document to another person if you're not the right person to sign
the document.

If you’re not the right person to sign a document, you can assign someone else. For
example, you transfer to a different position and no longer have signing responsibility for
certain types of documents.

The new signer can be anyone, provided that you know their email address.

The sender's security and account settings affect the email reassignment:

- Depending on the settings the sender has enabled for your documents, this option might
  not be available
- If the sender's account settings require the signer to log in to access envelopes, the
  new assignee's email address must exactly match their user name.
- Depending on the sender's security settings, you may receive an error message if the
  email
  address
  is
  incorrect, or not associated with a Docusign user.

You cannot change signing responsibility if:

- The change signing responsibility setting is disabled by the sender
- You are not a valid recipient type for reassigning the envelope
- The new recipient is not assigned to any Docusign account. However, an
  account is required by a custom sending setting
- The new recipient already exists in the same routing order

1. After opening the documents, in the top menu, select OTHER ACTIONS >  Assign to Someone Else.

   ![A screenshot of the Assign to Someone Else in the Other Actions menu.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signnewassignsomeoneelse.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NjEsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.yFq3RHe7Po1eL4CR4FVH6vq808bmjyKWWVLflFgTpJg&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Assign to Someone Else in the Other Actions menu.")
2. Enter the new signer's email address, name, and a reason for changing the signing
   responsibility.
3. When you're finished, select ASSIGN TO SOMEONE ELSE.

All the fields assigned to you are now assigned to the new
signer. Any fields you completed before you changed the signing responsibility are reset. The
sender and the new signer receive a notification about the change. You become a Carbon Copy
(CC) recipient of the envelope documents. The notification to the new signer looks like
