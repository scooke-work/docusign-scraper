# Sign With the Evrotrust Trust Service Provider to Issue Qualified Electronic Signatures in the European Union

Learn how to use the Evrotrust Trust Service Provider (TSP) to digitally sign documents
with the same legal value as handwritten signatures.

## Before you begin

Complete the following prerequisites before signing with
Evrotrust:

- Download the Evrotrust mobile application, as described in the [evrotrust](https://www.evrotrust.com/landing/en#download) page
- Register in the Evrotrust mobile application, as described in the [Evrotrust Registration
  Demo](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZajQwY9pofY) video

Use the Evrotrust Trust Service Provider (TSP) to Issue Qualified Electronic Signatures (QES)
in the European Union when you sign a document.

You received a Docusign email prompting you to review and sign some
documents. The sender of the envelope requires that you use Evrotrust to sign your
documents.

1. Select REVIEW DOCUMENT in the Docusign email.

   The document to review displays.
2. Select CONTINUE.

   You can now access the envelope.
3. Apply your signature to all the required SIGN
   tags.
4. Select CONTINUE.

   The Evrotrust authentication dialog box displays.
5. Enter your Evrotrust identifier.
6. Select SEND.
7. Follow the instructions in the [Docusign eSignature: How to Sign a Document with Evrotrust](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Klmr97W72is) video.

Evrotrust processes your signature, and you receive a
notification that the signing is complete through an email message. You also receive an
Evrotrust mobile app notification.

Save a copy of the document by logging on to your
Docusign account.

For additional information, see the
list of Evrotrust supported countries on the [evrotrust list of countries](https://www.evrotrust.com/doc/eIDAS/Countries_en.html) page.
