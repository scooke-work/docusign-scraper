# Sign Part 11 Module Enabled Documents

Read about special requirements, including U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)
regulations, for signing Part 11 Module-enabled documents.

As a signer, if you receive an email that has the
notation Part 11 Module, there are some special requirements when
signing these documents. The signing process must follow regulations from the U.S. Food and
Drug Administration (FDA).

![A screenshot of the Part 11 Module enabled document notation.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signpart11.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NzAsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.1zeO2xOxVSUEpJhrMiaZesZUUboeqSREEuO0cU8BClU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Part 11 Module enabled document notation.")

You might authenticate when:

- You open the document and finish signing
- The sender requires you to authenticate to open the document
- The sender requires you to authenticate every signature or initial field

Note: If the sender chose the Rapid Part 11 Signing method, you
must authenticate when you open the documents and finish signing.

1. Review the disclosure and select AGREE AND CONTINUE to use
   Electronic Records and Signatures.
2. You’re prompted to authenticate. To learn more about how the sender might require you
   to authenticate, see the [Verify your identity](https://support.docusign.com/s/document-item?bundleId=yca1573855023892&topicId=mbg1573854981921.html&_LANG=enus "Learn about using the different authentication methods to verify your identity for Docusign eSignature.") article.
3. Select
   CONTINUE to begin the signing process.

   Note: Review the document by scrolling or by using the page
   thumbnails in the right-side navigation before signing.
4. Enter any required information and select a signature field.
5. Select a Signing Reason from the list, and select
   SIGN to continue. You cannot continue until the signing reason is
   selected.

   ![A screenshot of the Reason for Signing dialog box with SIGN or CANCEL options.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/part11signingreason.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NzAsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.1zeO2xOxVSUEpJhrMiaZesZUUboeqSREEuO0cU8BClU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the Reason for Signing dialog box with SIGN or CANCEL options.")
6. If you are prompted to authenticate your signature or initials, select
   CONTINUE.

   ![A screenshot of the authentication pop-up window with the CONTINUE or CANCEL options.](https://docusign-be-prod.zoominsoftware.io/api/bundle/yca1573855023892/page/images/signpart11authprompt.png?token=eyJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiIsInR5cCI6IkpXVCJ9.eyJjdXN0b21lciI6ImRvY3VzaWduX3Byb2R1Y3Rpb24iLCJleHAiOjE3ODE4MjQ0NzAsInNoZWFmIjoieWNhMTU3Mzg1NTAyMzg5MiJ9.1zeO2xOxVSUEpJhrMiaZesZUUboeqSREEuO0cU8BClU&_LANG=enus "A screenshot of the authentication pop-up window with the CONTINUE or CANCEL options.")
7. Enter your Docusign password on a secure Logon page.

   Note: If you're asked to authenticate by logging on to a Docusign account:
   - Create an account if you do not have a Docusign account.
   - The password authentication is only required per one document.
   - You have a limited number of chances to enter your password correctly. If you
     don’t enter the correct password, you are locked out and can’t finish signing.

   Whenever you select a signature or initial field, the process of selecting a reason and
   authenticating might be repeated.
8. When you add all information and signatures to the documents, review the documents and
   complete the signing by selecting FINISH at the bottom of the
   browser window.
