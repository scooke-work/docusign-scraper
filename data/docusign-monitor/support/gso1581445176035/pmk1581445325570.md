# Protect Your Docusign Deployment with Docusign Monitor

Learn about how Docusign Monitor tracks account events and sends alerts when specific
account activity thresholds occur. Additionally, the Docusign Monitor API delivers activity
information directly to your existing SIEM.

## Docusign Monitor benefits

As a Docusign organization administrator, you want to protect your
organization from outside and inside threats.

Monitor enables organization admins to receive real-time alerts about suspicious user
activities within Docusign eSignature, CLM, Workflow Builder , and
Agreement Manager.

Monitor can provide timely security notifications delivered directly to your existing
security and event management (SIEM) system or visualization tool to take quick actions
to mitigate threats such as:

- External events
  - Anomalous activity indicating a security breach
  - Access from unknown IP addresses
  - Suspicious patterns by a third party
  - Logins to an account from multiple locations
- Internal events
  - Fraudulent behavior in legitimate accounts, such as a user deleting or
    downloading an unusually high volume of envelopes
  - Disabled recipient authentication, indicating an insider-assisted attack or unauthorized
    access
  - Risk-related errors made by well-meaning users, such as too many login attempts.

With Monitor, you can actively track these types of account incidents. It provides alerts
from potential malicious activities for your Docusign agreements and
offers automatic recommendations to counteract these threats.

With Monitor, you get the Docusign Monitor API that lets you:

- Investigate third-party communication to understand whether suspicious activity occurs.
- Deliver activity information directly to your SIEM. You can customize dashboards and alerts in
  SIEM software to meet your specific industry, security, and regulatory
  requirements.

Docusign Monitor saves alert, events, and alert history data for ten years.
